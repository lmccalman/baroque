Advances of the Royal Comtois Regiment, 
and of two thirds of the 4th Battalion of the 
Regiment of Normandy.

It is said that Mr Perrot, Ordonnateur 
at the Isle de France will return, and will be 
replaced there by Mr de Chevreau.

It was decided to send back the Chevalier 
de Rabuan Together to St Domingue.

I consent that there be here the 
Pistols or a death for the 
good of the Service, therefore one must not 
navigate, the old Officers will place the Sailors there, and the New ones 
will not learn.

There is much question of the Re-establishment 
of the Marine troops, but this is 
not an accomplished Reality.

Mr de Rouvellat is not charged with it, 
we have lost several Officers 
for a Month.

Many of our people have died 
recently.
