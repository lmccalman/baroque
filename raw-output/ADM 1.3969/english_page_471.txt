We have received the sad news; that the
city of Arequipa, has been very badly damaged
by an earthquake.

The new tariff that has been established
since the peace, in all our sea
customs houses destroys our commerce and
causes the entire diplomatic corps
to turn against this government at every moment.

A writing from Pollet in Catalonia
of the 3rd of the current [month], that a mine of
Cobalt has been discovered there
in its mountains, as abundant as that
of the valley of Gistat, and of as good
quality as that of Saxony.

The Catalans discover moreover
that the skins or the surface of the
thin branches of the mulberry trees are
also suitable for producing silk,
by experiment made, from which
great advantages are promised for
the silk trade.
