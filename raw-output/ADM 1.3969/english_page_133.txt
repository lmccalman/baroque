The rumor runs here, that our
Expedition to Cartagena will not
take Place; given that the King of Algiers, and
his Regency, appear to be disposed
to accept the amicable Propositions,
that the King has had made to them.

Our Infant Heir presumptive
of the Crown, died yesterday. Don
Pedro has been declared His Successor.

An infinite World had gone to
Aranjuez to see the Carnival in
the old Spanish style, which was being held
ready there; and an Embarkation on the
Tagus on the small Isle of Duart of a [trente]
of 18 Cannons, a smaller one of 14 Cannons
a Xebec, and a Galley, which have
been constructed there; but it appears that
this Diversion will not take Place; because
of the Death of the Infant.
