in Paris the 3rd of June 1737.

It is in Alsace where the
Orders are given to assemble an
army of 30 Thousand Men, whose
purpose is only to aid and support
the Empire against the King of Prussia; the
Troops are from all Parts in Movement
to proceed there: The King's Household would
have been there it would not take two Months
to unite from all the Kingdom all the
Members who compose it, because all
the Bodyguards serve only by Quarter,
they find themselves dispersed throughout the
Kingdom, and it takes Time to unite them.
The King not being instructed perfect-
ly absolutely of all that arrives
in Bohemia, to remedy this
Inconvenience, H.M. has just charged
the Marquis de Montagut to proceed to
Vienna, Austria, then to Bohemia, in order
to instruct him exactly by Couriers
of all that will arrive, the Lord has
departed to proceed there.
