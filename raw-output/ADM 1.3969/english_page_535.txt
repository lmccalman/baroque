Brussels, January 20, 1787.

We are assured that the
disputes between the Emperor and the
republic of Holland will
soon be adjusted; however the
imperial troops do not [découvrent]
to descend, on the contrary
the regiment of Colburg cavalry
has arrived in this city, and
departed yesterday for Mons.

We are still waiting here for
the regiment of Hessanæ &c.
