Furnes the 06 December 1770:

I have spoken to you about
three Vessels that were being equipped at
Dunkirk, and that were sending casks
to go [lovir] [Flomatiers] in
Norway, but an Order has come from
the Court to Suspend the Departure of
these three Vessels and all others that would
be equipped for America.
[En paredes lombement] to the three
[Cettens] of which I spoke to you.
[Sic l'este] there is currently
[quatro] of any Work at said
Dunkirk, and the troops there Are
On the same [Ried].

Brussels the 07. December 1770:

The storm of the 17th [et fut]
in the [Reis] of [Veres] for more
than a Million in Damages.
