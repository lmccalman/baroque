In the Midst of all this, people speak of
Mr. the Viscount for Controller General
and that is possible, one knows nothing that one cannot
burden me with.

They are going to Re-appoint the Election of Rouen
to reunite the Jurisdiction and the Members
to the Châtelet of Paris.

The Court of Aides of Clermont in
Auvergne is suppressed, Its District
and the affairs transferred to the Superior
Council of the same City.

There is much question of sending the Duke
of Fitz-James to command in
Languedoc. He will hold the Estates there, in the Place
of the Prince of Beauveau, who has fallen
into disgrace.

The Parliament of Toulouse will be quite
mortified to see command in Its
District a Man who formerly wanted to
insult it, and who will be in a position to avenge himself.
That announces the Destruction of this Parliament.
