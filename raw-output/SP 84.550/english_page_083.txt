Philadelphia, March 15th 1776.

Mr. Starks.                    Sir,
                                        I have laid your Letter
in which You give an Account
of a particular Rent of Five
pounds by some Person in Ireland
which he did to continue some
very valuable qualities, before
the Lords of the Admiralty, and
their Lordships having directed
the Commissioners of the Navy
Board to cause an Experiment
to be made of the Use of that
said Tar. I am to desire You will
direct the Storekeeper to send
over, immediately a Quantity
thereof, in order it be prove of
use, that it should be advised of
to the Commissioners of the Navy
Majestys Navy, and if You will
send me the Name of the Person
by whom it was so lately discovered,
and the Name of the qualifying
Ingredients, it to be Kept
Secret, and Satisfaction may be
given for it - You being charged at the
Custom house.     I am &c
                                        Wm. Erwin.
