                    I write your Excellency the 24th Instant which
letter came safe. Wednesday evening I received the enclosed letter
& had have come on tuesday morning but the person who brought
it from Fontainebleau had the neces[s]ary for to wait from me theres
a copy of there were about.                Fontainebleau 28 Oct 1746

The Earl received as to much from Mons De la guerre & in
a Council was held in consequence of how it of late occasioned
state to Mon Van Berg. that the King insists on the agreement
made with the Republick insumers & that conformeable
thereof the Hungarian & Arch man Ministers shall be excluded
from the Congress at Aabling for Preliminaries of Peace &
in case the Peace is not made the French will be obliged
to attack the town belonging to the Republick. if this proceeds
from the Dutch altering the allies to take winter
