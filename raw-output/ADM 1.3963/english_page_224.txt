    The Letters from Madrid of the 18th
repeated that from the Meeting
of Six Knights of the order of
Charles III. that one awaits from the
News of the Death of the King
of Portugal, and that there is
no [lutte] News.
    The Ministers of our Reverends,
Mr Laboureau, and Mr Richard,
accompanied by Six men
Gendarmes, held since
last at the affairs a
very long Committee at Mr
de Maurepas's, but nothing
has transpired.
    Prince Louis de Rohan
formerly Ambassador
in Vienna, departs with all
his Household to go receive
the Emperor at Strasbourg.
