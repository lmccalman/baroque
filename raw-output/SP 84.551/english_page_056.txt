IV.

THE Lottery of the Hôtel-de-ville of Paris, the General Association Lottery & that of the Religious Communities, shall be & remain
extinguished & suppressed, beginning on the 1st of August, & the drawings of said
Lotteries, shall cease during the month of July next.

V.

THE Lotteries of the Foundling Children & of Piety, which His Majesty
confirms & maintains until He otherwise
orders, shall be united with the administration of the Royal Lottery, beginning
on the 1st of August next; for this purpose, the general Administrators
shall take possession of said Lotteries on said day, under the name
of Antoine Blanquet; & the Managers & Receivers
thereof shall be required to give to said general Administrators, access
to the registers & statements which have served for the administration of said Lotteries:
His Majesty wills & intends, that the drawings of said Lotteries of the
Foundling Children & of Piety, be made at the customary times,
& in the same place where the drawing of the Royal Lottery shall be made,
in the presence of the sieur Lieutenant General of Police, & of the Intendant
& Administrators of said Royal Lottery.

VI.

HIS Majesty ORDERS that by the General Cashier of the Royal
Lottery, there shall be paid annually, for the time of their concession
only, to the Hôtel-de-ville of Paris, & to the Bodies, Communities
& Establishments, to which said suppressed or united Lotteries
had been granted, the sum to which shall be found to amount, for
each, the average year calculated on the last ten years of
net proceeds from said Lotteries, deduction made of all administrative costs; for which
purpose, the Managers, Receivers or other Agents
thereof shall be required to deliver into the hands of the sieur Controller General
of Finances, the registers, statements & accounts of receipts & expenses
of the last ten years of their operation.
