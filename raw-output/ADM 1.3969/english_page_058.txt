The Frigate Debourse, commanded
by Mr Therens de la Robines Lieutenant
of the Ship anchored the day before yesterday Morning
in this Roadstead; she comes from Syria & from
Barbary, where she took her
Escort, and led as far as the Height
of Marseille; a Convoy of Vessels
of Commerce. This Frigate is preparing to
enter the Port, to be disarmed there.

The Frigate the Montreal, and the Frigate
the Bouillante, set Sail yesterday
morning, the former went to Marseille
to take on a Cargo of Powder
and other Munitions and the Second which
received a a similar Cargo,
directed her Course towards Naples.

it is assured that these Two Vessels are going
to carry Aid there, for the Unfortunate ones
who have suffered from the Calamity, that
has afflicted Sicily, and Calabria.

Q.S.S. At this moment Six Sails are being signaled
in the Distance, it is no doubt, The Squadron
expected from Cadiz.

Paris.
