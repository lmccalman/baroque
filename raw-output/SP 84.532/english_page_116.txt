                                                      Paris the 25th January 1778.

                                                                        We have
nothing New from Toulon. It is reported
from Rochefort, that the two Vessels are readied
to leave for Brest by the 8th.
            The [Monstre] with 3 Arms with the
Parlementarians all gone to their
families this day can no longer
alone instead of honoring them, no
more than the Parliament; it is necessary
that the Bomb enter.
            Last Night The Musketeers
presented themselves to Two and to each
Member of Parliament, and to all the
Magistrates of the Orders very supreme of the
King, that they had to declare themselves.
And Give an order, which orders
them to resume their functions
this Morning, or to refuse, the Majesty
wanting a yes or a No, that they
go or that one signs for that yes, and
she near them No.
