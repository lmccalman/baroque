Genoa the 5th January 1785

On the 5th of last last
month a vessel was launched at sea
of 16 cannons, and of 8 thousand quintals
which was sold to the Venetians.

On the 11th of the same month
two barques of 22 cannons were launched
and of 5 thousand quintals each
which were also sold to the
Venetians.
