in Brussels the 20th of June 1787.

Mr. de Pons received the day before yesterday
a Courier from Marshal d'Estrées, with Advice,
that he was Master of Bielefeld and that the Duke of
Cumberland had abandoned it, and that he had
sent a Detachment to charge the Rear
Guard of the Enemy.
The Prince de Soubise passed through here yesterday morning
by Post, going to Versailles, to reconcile himself
it is said, with the King concerning a secret
Expedition.
