17 89 Nºary

Toulon, April 17, 1812

The Vessels the Swiftsure,
the Desaix, the Conquérant, the Jélif,
commanded by Monsieur d'Arhansthause,
de la Ille de la Galissonnière, and de
Boudrines, forming Part of the Squadron
arrived these last days from Cadiz,
entered yesterday into the Port to
be disarmed there, as well as the Frigate
the Proserpine, commanded by Mr. le
Epipure.

The Corvette the Sardine, commanded
by Mr. le Petit, left this morning from
Port, to go to the Roads

[stamp/seal mark]

Paris
