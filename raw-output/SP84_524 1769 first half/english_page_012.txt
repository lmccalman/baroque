N° 32

                                          Le Havre April 14, 1789

                                                            There will remain
for us here only one single frigate, stationed
in this Port, la Blanche; it is being armed with
haste, commanded by M. de Bomeuille,
it expects to leave on the 20th for Brest,
if it receives its Orders.
     It is certified here that yesterday from Cadix
l'Expérience, built in this Port
by M. Bonne, and which [emportes] from
Rochefort for the East Indies;
[si elle esperanda], that he will be able
to send a [franche] of three R.D.S
[pureblement] to this frigate in
its [Equipant], the [Plat] that this would
render him then, would render him a
good officer, it would not [s'enforceroit] not
however, it would hold that they [ement].

                                                                                    Paris
