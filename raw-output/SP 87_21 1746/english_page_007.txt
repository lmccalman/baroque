Descent is attributed to their Plans being made with above
4000 fresh Troops. The Count had a desire of their hearts,
which but kept it so secret that Pride not known it till the
15 Inst. Had you furnished me what you promised you
I should have been much better served in this occasion.
As now the 21 Inst. I the they have received 6 Couriers the
dept publish their News but by the oath with Saverdas
Paris, & what I learn from a friend. Things go but ill there.
Morau Gelles set out Wednesday, aJournal for the Hague.
D'Sandwich yesterday, noon, in Journy persuadetion, we
Correspondent hath been purposely, well with form of all the
Medne relating to the Congress. I informed D'Sandwich
of Mons Dourville's Fleet being miscarried in their
Decizgn. and of our Actanding at Queberon, so was the
hurt advise was a App had received of either. I have the
Honour to be with great Respect

Friday evening        Your Excellency's
29 Dec 1796.          Most Oblig'd & most Dutiful
                      & most humble Servant
