Madrid November 22, 1780

They report from Malaga
on the 9th of the current month, that a terrible
hurricane which occurred on the night of the 23rd
of August completely dismasted, and caused
the shipwreck of the King's vessel the
Septentrion of 74 cannons, on
shoals at three leagues distance,
but that all the crew was
saved. It is assured that it was one
of the best sailing vessels of all our
naval fleet, English construction
from Cartagena, by the late Mr. Briant, whose
son Thomas Briant, has just been
named by the King Captain of
the high board, and sent to Ferrol
as principal constructor
in that department, to continue there
in the English manner. It is assured that he is even
more skilled than his father.
