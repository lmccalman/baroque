LETTER

TO MY LORDS

Of the Parliament of Paris.

MY LORDS,

IT IS in the name of the Fatherland that I raise my voice. Never was a purer homage brought to the feet of Justice, whose Scepter you take up, & which you console. It is therefore permitted to hope for it; the torch of our discords is going to be extinguished: you restore those ancient bonds which attach the happiness of all to the will of one alone;
