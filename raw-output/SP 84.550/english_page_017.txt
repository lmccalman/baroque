5 [inquietateurs] are employed at
[lespailles] by the Reform Projects,
that M. de Malesherbes furnished me
in the Domestic Households of
the Court; one must employ the
Master of Horses; those who
remain, will form six troops,
[repelés], instead of three.

The Minister of War, to
favor the poor Nobility
of the Kingdom, will engage by the
established Ordinances to oblige them,
to be received in any
Military Corps; those
who will not have the Means,
the King will furnish them, with the
[traité] that the Military
School comprises

As for the Edicts of M. Turgot
no one speaks of them anymore; it is believed
that none will appear

The King by an Decree of [été]
Council forbids the [Echecs] and
                                        Stagecoaches
