Brussels the 4th February 1777.

                    There arrived the day before yesterday
a Courier from Vienna, with the News
that the Empress Queen arrived there, who
gave the order to liberate Le Jeune; the
Minister must be regarded, and as
Sovereign, since he has declared to [fairs]
in the Sovereigns, and the Peoples, and
one would even believe that someone came to enlighten the
Rabble and the Protestants.
    Orders were sent to Malines, to
make the Effigy of all the Pieces
newly cast, and having
the Test of the [pleines] upon their
Effects, there are 150 of them, Namely.
        40 Pieces of 16 Pounds
        40 ........ of 8.
        24 ........ of 6.
        20 ........ of 4.
Besides 4. Mortars of 66 Pounds of Bomb
Besides the 8000. Cannonballs that there are
Besides casting, one still believes [on frs]
30. Thousands + Thousands Bombs, 6 Thousands &c
and 3000. [deniers] Grenades.
