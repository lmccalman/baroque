The Count of Narbonne has just
been made Lieutenant General,
to command in Corsica,
in place of the Count of Marbeuf
who will not return there.
    The Departure of the Court for
Choisy, is still fixed for the
4th of October, and the g... of
fontainebleau... there will
be before a small hunting
trip this Week.


                                        Letter 1st
