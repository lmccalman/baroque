    On the 4th of June we departed happily
for the Islands, but it is not yet
willing. Mr. de Boujainvillée wants
to make a Voyage to the North, and Mr.
de Roquefeuil another to the Southern
Islands.

    Mr. de l'etang will begin Tomorrow
his Inspector's Review, with the Admiral
Division, Wednesday that of Vice-Admiral,
Thursday that of Rear-Admiral.
It is After this Review, that they will examine
the proper state of each one. The
Captains will find him capable,
having the opposition, but those who
are in the Majority, find him
very well.
