No 3.

Paris, January 12, 1776

I am informed from
Toulon that the Frigate, l'Arpette,
arrived there on the 4th from Lavalle, she
certified a [relaine] at Malta.
   The Count of St Germain continues
his work with the King, but this
work is imperfect, because he burns
what one prevents from doing in the
public [offices], and is reluctant to refer
all this work in writing to the King,
and this lasts at the beginning
of the Courier
   It is well [known] that there are disputes
brought to the Council, it is even
already said that this tribunal refused that
of the Committees. The Controller
General wrote to the First President
