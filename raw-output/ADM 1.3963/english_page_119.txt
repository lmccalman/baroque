¶    The Portuguese who compose the 
     Vendes, and who were at Pontalos,
     have descended to Braga, and forbid
     despite their peace, to build a citadel,
     but war is not presently
     being waged.

¶    It has been published in Bras, which orders
     all classified [Malebrotz], to present
     themselves at the Bureau of Classes
     within three [Levins], under Pain of
     Death, which makes one strongly predict a
     coming Rupture with Portugal,
     or that one wishes to oblige this
     Power, to cease Hostilities
     in the Environs of Buenos Ayres.
     It appears that one assures, that the truce
     will be declared between this Court
     and that of Portugal, it does not prevent,
     that our troops act presently
     at the frontiers, in order to be ready

     We have also recently Learned in Naples
     of a Revolt of the Lackeys, of which
     part has made an Uprising, which
