Paris the 29th October 1704.
Vy le Mbre

The Princess has arrived well from the
Waters with a Bout of Influenza, twenty
until I told you, but one is in
the greatest Anxiety about telling it
to the Mother, and the Bishop advances
Millions of Rubles, and speaks the 20th
April, thus 25 days before her.

The Princess of Asturias was delivered
the 14th of this Month, at 10 o'Clock in the Morning,
of a Prince, who has been named Ferdinand;
the Confinement was at the Escurial.
This happy Confinement was troubled
some days later, by the Death
of one of the Twin Children.

The Return made by the Empress of the
Russians to her Capital, must be
attributed, only to the state of Apoplexy in
which She has fallen. The 16th September,
delivered to Prisoners, She was mounting in a Carriage
with one of the Daughters of the late Peter;
and ordered her Coachman to drive her
to a Place in the Park which She designated;
