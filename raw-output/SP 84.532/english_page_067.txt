                                                Paris the 11th January 1773.
                                                
                                                Word comes from
Martinique of the 15th November, that the whole
Colony is in the greatest alarm of
War, everywhere good order is being put to the
Defenses, work is being done to palisade
the Town St Pierre, Cannon is being mounted there.
M. M. de Bernetz remained in his
interior plantations [un bon], he is
armed, the rest likewise.
