One reports from Vienna of the 28th, that
they awaited there the Return of a Courier
from Petersburg, concerning the Dispatches
apparent he [songla si qua si en
lanca] in the great Affair of the
Quarrels in Poland, and of
all that has Relation to it.

The 27th [moi] arrived within three Days
at [Munig], the Majesty returned
on Tuesday early to Versailles.
Supped with the Children; She
has not left Versailles since.

Brussels the 9 March 1792.

Several Companies of [Attests]
coming from Malines with the Reports of
Miners and Cannons, all concerning
the 6th, for this [helle], foreseen but their
Route towards Luxembourg.

The same Day a Detachment of hussars
took Post at Malines, and will remain there
in Garrison, to perform the Service.
