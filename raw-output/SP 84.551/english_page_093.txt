Brest the 17th July 1776.

The following Gentlemen
are requested to be armed, being sailors
and [larcenés] on board, namely:
& Pérol de Poris
& Derc de Bouilleogne
& Mengnet son.
& Mahiable.
& Despictes
& Ailly
& Roddieu
& Covidier
& Roland.
& Alexandre
& Bizarre
& Ganophin, and
& Dudephin Royal.

This last one is to substitute, in Case
that there be one of the 12, who comes
to be absent. The first Two
[formibeges], and the 10 old ones for
the Shrouds, of which they have much
need, the last six, and them
included, that they could well
                                                           remain.
