The letters from Rennes of the 8th of the
current month, speak only of rumors
of peace.

Shareholders of the discount bank, to the number of twenty
went to Versailles three
or four days ago, to present a
memorandum to the head of the council of
finances, who was not a little astonished
by this numerous deputation.
Their representations had as their
object the last decree of the council,
relating to the bank. Mr. de Vergennes
sent them before Mr. Calonne.
