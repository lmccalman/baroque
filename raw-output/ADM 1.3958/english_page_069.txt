            Madrid January 28, 1775.

                        [Navigue] since
several [boirs] people speak more than ever
of an upcoming Resumption between
the Courts of London and Madrid, they
write to me from Cadiz that several Xebecs
Under the orders of Mr d'Esquien, Are
ready to transport to America
[Con ignorait Quand ils] 100 Pesos of Cannon,
35 Thousand Bombs, 50 Thousand Balls,
30 Thousand Muskets and Pistols, and 15 Thousand
Uniforms.
            Mr Harris departs in a few Days.
