Vienna, November 20, 1775.

The Count whom I mention
in my last, who commands in
the Capital of the Turks on the Danube,
is not confirmed, & I attach to it some news
entirely authentic.
                The
        The Pasha Aga 24 of this Name.s arrived
here Admiral Hasan-Bassa & six Officers
of the Turkish Fleet of the Archipelago; they were
[regardes avec Courage] by Mustapha
Bassa Gallixian, other Ministers, and other
Ministers; where they were very
proud; they continued their Route for
Petersburg.

        It is today that the Knights
of the Golden Fleece, named in the [Depeche des
Ordres Redevenant], were installed
with the ordinary Ceremonies. the Duke
of Arsel was installed by Prince
Charles of Lorraine in Brussels.

        Count Hosten Bozza has arrived
here, as New Envoy of the
Republic of Ragusa.
