Rotterdam Augst ye 3d 1764


My Lord


I have the honour of your Lordships letter
of the 23 past with the three inclosed proposals
from the Lord Commissioners of his Majesty's
Treasury relating to the contraband trade.

Your Lordship may be well assured that no
zeal or activity will be wanting in me, as
well in this, as in any other point of his
Majesty's Service.
