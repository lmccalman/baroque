LETTER
FROM THE PARLIAMENT
OF NORMANDY
TO THE KING.
On the current edict of the Parliament of Paris,

From February 3, 1771.

SIRE,

The subversion with which the edict of last December threatens the Laws, the forms & the constitution of the Monarchy, the dispersal of the Members of the Parliament of Paris, victims of their zeal for the important matters that we propose to develop to your Majesty. Our efforts are excited by zeal. Se, we have asked you for justice; the interest of your own glory.
