One does not include in this
Number of 200 Thousand Men,
the 6 Thousand Men of Militias,
who will not enter in time of
War, to guard the Places,

at Their Homes, to work the
land, one will complete them every
Year by Lot, in the Parishes at
the tail in each Regiment,
