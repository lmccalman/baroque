The Duke of Mahon, is expected at
any moment at St. André; he has written
from Bayonne where he had just arrived.
     The Countess of Montster, returned
with her Husband from London, died
at her Waters last.


Madrid the 5th of June 1788.

     None of the notices that we
receive here from Cartagena says
unanimously that all is ready there
for the projected Expedition, under the orders
of Mr. Barralo; of which no one
doubts here, that the Destination is
for the Bombardment of Algiers.
     Don Louis of Cordova, has
arrived at Aranjuez six days ago
where Count O'Reilly, and Count del
Aspalter are expected momentarily.
