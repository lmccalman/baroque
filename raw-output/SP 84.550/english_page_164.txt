4° Other edifice, 120 Feet

   Quantity of [Carnalies], Medals,
   Cups, [pulverale], Earthen
   Vases, of a beautiful Varnish,
   Layers of Marble and
   Copper, and some beautiful
   Pieces of Painting.

5° The Vestiges of an Amphitheater, etc.

   City of Interamna, of which Pliny
   makes among others, mention in
   Plinius Seconder, Colonia immunis
   Augusti Pici, unde Veleianus
   Pons. Castrum apud de la Motte.
