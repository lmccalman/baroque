                    Brussels the 18. April 1712

The Youth of Ypres va
left yesterday from here for Ostend, in order
to examine the Fortifications, they are
with here two French Engineers,
one named Capets, of the other
I do not know the Name.

A Battalion of Invalids passed
yesterday through this Town, coming from Luxembourg,
to go take Garrison in Bruges.
