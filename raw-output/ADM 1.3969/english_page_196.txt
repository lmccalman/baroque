R. 26 Aug. No 2

in Paris the 14th August 1782.

        The Cabinet Couriers
succeed each other rapidly on the
Current, one came from Petersburg,
& another from Vienna and here is
another, who comes from Constantinople.
According to the Reports of this Last one, the
war is inevitable between Russia and
the Porte, by all the Preparations that
he saw along his Route: the Courier left
Constantinople around mid-July, the
Plague which had already caused much
Ravage, was not diminishing at the time of his
Departure.
        Day before yesterday the Russian Ministers
received a Courier from Petersburg,
who brought them the Response, that
the Empress made to the Offers, and
to the last Intentions of our
Court. It is said that this Response is very
haughty: that Russia has refused our
                                                           Mediation
