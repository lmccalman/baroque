Hereunto you have what the Paris mail-
arrived can never brought me. —

Several letters from France continues contradicting
the declaration of the Genoeses, and yet by
all the circumstances it is not certain that they
have declared. —

All the articles of the enclosed, that are of a
maritime nature, I have sent to the Admiralty of
England, and I hope one of my fleets will intercept
the french convoys or trader. — I am —

Sir                          Your most humble
                             & most obedient servt
Robt Ed 6/19/1878.                    Woltres
