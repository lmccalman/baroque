Madrid, January 28, 1775

Whereas [ci-après] several days [o-yer], English that I [emois] of an imminent [?] between the courts of London and Madrid; one told me of this idea, that several xebecs [nous] the Orders of Mr. d'Aguirre, [ton m'éta] to transport to America & one ignores Bordeaux; 100 cannon pieces, 35 thousand cannonballs, 30 thousand balls, 30 thousand powder and pistols, and 15 thousand uniforms.

Mr. Harris [par l'an spen de l'oir].
