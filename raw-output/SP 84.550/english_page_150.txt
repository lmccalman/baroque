Second.

       Madrid the 23rd March 1776.

            My News from
Malaga of the 15th of the current month inform me,
that H.E. has disembarked the two Brigades
of the King named the St. Barbei, and
the St. Rosary, for the [entr.contraires],
being destined for Cadiz; and that
the other vessels, commanded by
Don Felix de Tejada, were destined
to cruise against the Moroccans,
and to cover our Possessions.
     I have just been told, that the King
has granted to Mr. de Galvez, Minister
of the Indies, the honors of Counselor
of State, to sustain for him the title and
of Excellency.
     Here in Summary, the Account
that Mr. de Buecareley, Viceroy of
Mexico, has sent to Court, of the
Success of the Expedition made by his
