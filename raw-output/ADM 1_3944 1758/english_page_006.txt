Of the 5th. April.
The Calapre and the Oleron which had left here on the 25th returned today with a convoy that they had gone to fetch in the Figuier and Nantes.

We learn from these two [Brigantes] that they found the France at the height of Bellisle making Route with the Bourdelois Vessels for St. Sesbourg.

The Council of the Greenwich finally held this Morning, concluded as one had always expected. That is to say, that all the officers have been dismissed from Cause and entirely placed no fault responsible for the accident purely of a man who caused the Loss of this Vessel.
