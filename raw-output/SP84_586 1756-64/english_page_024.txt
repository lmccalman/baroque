1785 May

[Main text body - left column]
I undersigned acknowledge having received from
Monsieur the Count of Vaudreuil the
sum of three hundred livres for my
salary of three months of service
with Monsieur the Baron de Besenval
from the first of January until the
last of March one thousand seven hundred eighty
five. In Paris this twelfth of April one thousand seven
hundred eighty five
                                    Poteau
