Madrid the 23rd January 1785.

I persuade myself Sir
that I am keenly interested in all the
Reasons that you can have for
the continuation of our correspondence
and I would be sorry that the circumstances
and yourself who can the most
interest your friends.
Could you believe Sir
that I would have omitted to speak to you about
a thing as interesting as a
change of Ministry, if it
had taken place, or even was preparing itself,
but nothing of all that arrives
not the celebrated Marquis de Grimaldi,
who increases much in favor.

They write to me from Ferrol: that they
work on the Island on the arming
of the Vessels that are in this Port:
and that they must form 6 Squadrons,
of which, [ny] joined several times:
and that they await from Cartagena
the Regiment of [Arturies] and of
