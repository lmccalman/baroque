Private Letter

Paris the 30th of February 1775.

There is much talk
of the Diligence, but nothing definite is said
about it up to this moment: it was not perfectly
known in this Post.

There is also talk of many Arrangements
to revive the Parliament. The general
Rumor is that the First President had
a very long Conference with the King:
what confirmed this News is that this
Magistrate was absent from [du haut des feuilles]
of the Country House, from Paris [Madame]
in the Morning until eleven o'clock in the Evening.
Without the King having granted him that he return,
and [heuroi] designs. Moreover one
[vut] by much and observation Renders
several [esclets].

It is not yet known what Course the Court
has taken on the Occasion of the famous Decree of
the Parliament of Rouen.

The Princes and Peers have claimed not to have
received the Packets that were
addressed to them in Consequence of the said Decree;
