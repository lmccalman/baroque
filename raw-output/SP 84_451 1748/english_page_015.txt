October 1709
                                                                                            by Soult the 7th of
                                                                                            December


What in Money I have received by Account deducting
from the sums the memorial of the [me] which is owed to me
or in his absence of which I hold myself by the said Count
to God my King to the Company and to go
put myself at his mercy of him as my very humble
submissive and obedient servant
