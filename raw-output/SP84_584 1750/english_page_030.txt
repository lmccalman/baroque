Rotterdam August 9th 1832.
Friday 24th

My Lord

All my attempts to procure a copy of
the Charters or grants of the New London Company,
have hitherto proved fruitless; but in the course
of them I have met with some Documents which
I think it my duty to communicate to your
Lordship.-

General Keith in a letter to Dillon informs
him, what I have had the honor to write before
to your Lordship, that Harris had first presented
his Scheme to the King of Prussia, who sent him
to the Asiatick Company; - that the said Company
has since granted Harris his wiches; and that the
grantees been confirmed by the King of Prussia.
