It is also assured, that Mr. de Maurepas is at the Moment of Retiring, on the Pretext of his poor Health.

It is very much a question of an Edict which will suppress the Offices of the Registrars in all the tribunals, His Majesty wishing that Trials be rendered free to His Peoples, and to take charge of compensating the holders, through more considerable Wages.

The Parliament of Toulouse has registered Without Difficulty the Edicts, brought from the last Lit de Justice. Bordeaux does likewise, but the Edict for the Commerce of [bris] would not pass Without an absolute Authority.
