Whitehall July 22d 1760

Sir,
Hon:ble Gen:l Yorke.

I received early on
Sunday Morning the
favor of Your Letter of
the 17th by Express, & some
Hours after, those of the
15th by the Mail; all which
were immediately laid
before the King. The
Mail of the 18th is not yet
arrived, by which I hope
will observe the Mischief of
Mispatching Posting Boats
as often as any Event of
Consequence comes to Your
Knowledge.
