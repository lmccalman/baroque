R. 1768. No 1.

                               Toulon the 31st August 1718.

           The [Gouvernement] the Commandant
     is totally finished, and in good condition.
     The Lion has been taken to the large
     Warehouse where it is being [cabanné]. Will
     presently put the [Casern], will give
     it a [Caronne] of Woods
     at [Rattraison].
           The [Fabenne Piéderie], come from
     Brest, which has masts and rigging,
     it will depart at the end of September,
     loaded with Wood from [Carafeuettes],
     and Provisions for Brest. Will
     embark there Masters of the Crew
     of the Vessels the Victorieux, come from
     Ponant, thirty Masters making
     [ramages] by the Galleys which are
     [attens].
           The Vessel the [Superieux] has
     arrived here last Saturday, by
     [Maillon] is the same as the two
     [Jabarres].
                                                                  Good.
