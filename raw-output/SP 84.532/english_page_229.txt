The King has disposed of the Government &
General of the woods & [vêlches], in favor
of Marshal de Broÿlio, & that
of La Rochelle and St Jeunio with
Marshal de Boisfan; one has united the
place of Director General of
fortifications, in favor of the Count
de Muillebois.

The King has well received the Princes of
Sweden who are here, and all the Great ones
hasten to procure for them
Amusements, and to make their
Stay agreeable.
