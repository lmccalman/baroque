From the 17th. We are informed from there, that the two
four Ships, from Brest, and from Toulon,
commanded by the Duke of Chartres,
were reunited in Sight of Cadiz,
numbering 18 Sails; and that the
Fleet, of the same Force, had weighed
Anchor the next day to support
the Destination, of a Michel des
luston, who commands it, having
orders from the Court, to open a Packet
at [istance] Height, and to conform
to it.

As for the little Maneuvers of
our Infant Don Louis, however
extraordinary it may be, it appears
that it must take place in the current
Month, all the Appearances,
and Preparations that have been made,
persuade us of it. but [le parfois] the
formal Reconciliation, as much
for him, as for the Descendants,
to all Pretensions of his Blood.
