505.

According to the Letters from Brest of the
28th, and of the 29th, the Order has arrived in
this Port, to arm with all Diligence
the Magnifique, the Intrépide, the
Sublime, the Brebis, the Oreilles, the Bigarrée,
the Fantasque, and the Breguets
the Inconstante, the Indiscrète,
the Zéphir, and the Orifeau, they
work there Night and Day. These Ten
Vessels will form five [joincts] for Twenty
others, which form the Division
of Mr Du Chaffault, Not
counting 2 Corvettes armed
at Le Havre, and 2 [Fluttes] at Rochefort.
The Squadron of Mr Du Chaffault
has not returned to the Roadstead,
but it is only 10 Leagues
at Sea. They are reestablishing the Two
Batteries of the Port of Brest.

They are arming at the Port Two Corvettes
of 22 Cannons each one, at
Bordeaux 5.

Bm.
