In the District of Shaffeld
by the Mountain of Shaston-thel
in the Isle of Iceland, there
manifested on Pentecost Day
a great Conflagration which, very
fortunately extended, and with so much
fury, that the River of Shafton
has been entirely dried up to
the Point that one sees there today
only a Heap of Stones, bare Rocks
have borne this Terrible scourge.
