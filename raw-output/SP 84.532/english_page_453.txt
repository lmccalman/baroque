The Princes that they border always try
to go to Versailles, received the spouse, and
having brought her, she was married by the
Curé of St. Philippe du Roule, to whom one gave during the
ceremony the Provincial Parlements
or at least reduced them to Superior
Councils. It is assured that everything far is
finished in a Month.
     Vigil of the 21st. The Things.
It is only the last day that Bouillé, one
never [louoit] expected it there.
     Chasseurs of the Council do not return
anymore to the Palace. The Lawyers do not
present themselves at all, there were about a Hundred
Charges that prevented them from the reverse
of leaving their fortunes.
     The rumor runs that the Duke of Aiguillon
is going to be declared Minister of Foreign
Affairs, and that the Chancellor will be made
Prime Minister.
     Mme de Soland at Brest to the island of
Bourbon, she departs the 1st October, one
gives her 80 Thousand livres of draft.
     There will only be a [Lac] of Bishopric in Paris
nor at Versailles, for the Marriage of the
Count of Provence, this day that one makes
Opera and at Marly.
                    Le figaro
                    Mayence.
