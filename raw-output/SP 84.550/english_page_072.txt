N°. 29

                    Paris the 5th March 1776.

                         It is reported from
Toulon of the 22nd, that the Capuchins for
the [incendre en Pomènade], are not
yet named there, & that they
began on the 19th to build in the
[Cuite], the most incredulous & the
most opposed to this great Project,
begin to believe in the
benefits extracted from this
Enterprise.
     The Prince de Montbarey, named
Director General of War,
will work with the King in case
of Illness, or absence of the
Minister [d'Agneray tout], with
the Exception of Orders, or
Commissions. Mr de St. Germain,
is of the elder branch, the other of
Mr de St. Germain, it is believed,
