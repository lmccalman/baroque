N° 10.

                    Paris the 18th May 1776.

                    They write from Toulon
that on the 2nd May they had continued
to receive letters there from Livorno by a
French vessel.
     That the Carolines, commanded
by M. de la Salcette, which had been awaited
for three Months, has returned there;
after the Community had not
furnished it, they will renew the supplies,
to send them back to the Levant,
under the orders of M. de Corbin.
     The same Day of the Arrival
of this brigantine, the Mignonne,
commanded by M. de Brunault,
was coming from Toulon for the Levant.
     [Le Travail de la Caisse Navale
étoient les Enfants Nouvelles des
la Porte.]
                                        N.S
