                                                    15 Dec 1777 No 1


                        Toulon the 9th December 1776

                             Our Vessels are going
          at great speed, the Languedoc, the Duché,
          and the Northumque are almost finished.
                    The Construction of the Destin
          and the Caton, is being pushed vigorously.
                    The Vessels, commanded
          by Mr de Chabert, and the Mignonne,
          by Mr de Grammmont, have
          returned from the Levant, and are
          doing their [aventure]. There only remain
          for you at Sea, the Artésian,
          by Mr de Espinouse, who is
          expected any day; the
          Gracieuse by Mr d'Esplélon;
          The Hirondelle by Mr de Bruguière;
          and Celois by Mr le Forbin.
                    Two or three frigates will be armed
          around the 15th January for the
                                        Levant,
