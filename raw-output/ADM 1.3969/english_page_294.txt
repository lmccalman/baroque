Beff. fol 27. - 1826.


            Paris, January 23, 1784.

                    Mr. de Talenne entered
the Council of State last Sunday.
                    It is written from Rome of the 31st of
last month, that the arrival of the Emperor there
caused a great Sensation. He
stayed in Rome from Tuesday the 23rd
at 20 past noon until Monday Morning the 29th.
He lodged at the Cardinal his
Minister's, but he always ate
at an Inn near the Place
d'Espagne. He had several Interviews
with the Pope. He departed for
Naples.
                    The Bey of Tunis was also
in Rome, during the Emperor's
Stay.
