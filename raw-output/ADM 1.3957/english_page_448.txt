                    Brest the 24th. October 1770.

                    On Tuesday
evening they put the frigate la Nécessaire in the Basin
of Brest, and l'Hérédia in that of Recouvrance
to careen them. [le Roi des Marseillais
était, les Corsaires repousseroient les Batteries]
of these two Vessels; much rotten
Planking will be found there, especially
on l'Hérédia; [on y mis faire Recale]
to ballast them Sunday at the latest;
and to reemploy them for the Voyageur, called
l'Henrion, which comes up to here. Other
Movements.

They are working to unload the Cannons
from the Vessels, and to work ordinarily.
There is Appearance that they want to arm
l'Actif, le Vengeur, l'Heureux, la Sylphide,
and la Punissable; they are preparing Cordage
for these Vessels; they [doit] the Masts
from the Warehouses, [on encorche] to arm
at l'échange and at Bayonne at the same time.

They have put out a Pamphlet, in which
two Officers are presented with an
[excès-]; relative to the Trial of the Commissioners of Alban
[établi] the Packets for Seydleson, and
