The South Sea Islanders say,
they see direct News from their Agents
in Lisbon, and knows nothing further
than what is at present publish'd in
England. They attribute this to the
Dispatches having been stopt by the
English in Bengal, an Intimation
while there is a great alarm among
the Directors, and frequent Meetings.
