N° 2 Spain 2.

                   Paris March 21, 1782.

          The Division of the French
Fleet in the Roads at Cadiz, destined
for Toulon must have set Sail
the 4th of this Month, if the Weather
permitted it. That of Brest was to depart
Two Days later. Mr del Estaing with
His Vessel of 74 Cannons, was going
to set sail alone, for a particular
Mission.
          Mr d'Estaing will not have left Cadix,
until after the Departure of the Squadrons.
          Mr de la Fayette was left by
the Prince of Nassau who arrived
the Evening after Him.
          We await here, Mr de la Ronchèr,
that Mr del Campo has been relieved in
London, as Plenipotentiary Minister
of the Court of Spain, while awaiting
that it send an Ambassador there
who is believed to be Mr Delmaduvars
