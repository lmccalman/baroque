N°. 23.

                                   Paris the 8th March 1778.

                                   Our Letters from
Brittany report that the Aldermen of
this Town have done at Sea the
20th April.
     It is decided that Madame the
Duchess of Chartres will accompany
her Husband as far as Toulon, And that
there one will give her a Festival
in the Dauphiné and Provence,
as Brother of the Grand Admiral
one will render him the greatest
Honours in this Port.
     The great Deputation of the
Parliament, composed of 42
Members, was Monday last
at Versailles to present the
Remonstrances to the King on the
Edicts, His Majesty received them
quite coldly, and told them.
