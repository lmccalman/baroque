[Je] left yesterday the 18th. [Geigfous] broken
with piles and Broaches, which came
to us from Malines, for Germany
under the Escort of a Corps of Miners
of 200. Men.

Mr Wensch, Chief of the [Seulerie]
aged 74. Years, left for Vienna
it is he who established in Schweidnitz.
when this City was taken by
the King of Prussia.
