172.

Correspondences condemned to Fire.
Since this Condemnation, the 4th
Part of this Correspondence has appeared,
even more Satirical than the preceding ones.
They have already arrested for this Object, a
man named Lombard, Clerk of Attorney.

They had named Four Persons
Suspected of being the [Valeurs] of the Sacred
[Passes] of the Capuchins, but they
caught yesterday the true Thief, whom they
found to be an Abbé; they do not yet
know, Whether he only has the Ecclesiastical
Habit, or Whether it is
truly so.

A Letter from Constantinople
dated February 1st, reports, that Things
appear absolutely to turn
to a general [Récification];
all was tranquil on both
sides On the Banks of the Danube.
