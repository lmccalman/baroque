Extract from a letter
from the Dardanelles of the 30th January 1775.

We received here some
time before my departure the agreeable news
of your Arrival, and of which that of
Admiral Muffuf has just confirmed to me,
and arrives in Italy.

They continue to make here all the
necessary Preparations for the Defense
of this District; which will not have for all
that any Trouble to cross, with a
Landing and the Confusion which reigned
there among the Defenders. The batteries
which have been raised to 30. men;
but they lack subjects
capable of defending them.

Besides [one] has never loved
nor esteemed the Russians, who lead him
to all sorts of cowardice and Disorder;
it is not however all, that Mussapha has,
and the myopic can say it of their arms
which undermines through towards the Rivas.

his efforts, to overcome Discipline
