Russia must have requested for some
Days, that the Affairs be arranged,
And that the Parliament raised vanity, but
this Rumor cannot be founded, the Parliament
would not return without having the same
Rights as before its Destruction, moreover
it would be necessary that the King [renvela] all,
this would be undertaking to well
descend from His throne, which will never
be seen

The Antonines who had the Design
to receive Novices, have obtained the
Revocation of this order, these Monks
will not be Suppressed, nor
will other Religious Bodies, the
System is changed

It was [achèté] the Bishop of Orleans
had Permission to go to His
Diocese, but that is not confirmed.

It is said that the Prince [d'avera] of Saxe
who is here, is resolved to make His
Residence there, And that he has there [borne] a
House and Tables.
