Madrid November 18, 1770.

From Carthage, where they
gave me the following Information.

The Chief of the Squadron Marquis de Casa Relly,
will command several Divisions, with
orders to render himself to Cadiz & arms with this
effect as quickly as possible the Divisions of the
Tenis, Minerve, & l'Engendois, of Vivre
de Canton & d'Alcantara of which it would be necessary
to Embark by the [tonsareau]. the [Diaragiume]
the Trenchant of the same Caliber, which they
sell in Cadiz & don [Retenus] de [Vayoles].

These Divisions had a
great Quantity of Cannons & other
Ammunition on board, likewise
St Ptolemée & others of the [bulles]
[Enfermeilles] with Division of the
Construction of the same [Diaragiume], and
a Frigate at [Differents], the same
as a [Baule] of [Diaragiume] the Valency.

One can Being on board of this Port the
[Diaragiume] Aquilles, & [Etouard], of 70 and 80.
Pieces of Cannons, to Exit to [guerra]
of Board, which has the [Divido] to Armed to [guenche].

The Squadron of Toleste, of Return from the
Cadies, I will [mentlran] until it [mené] of the
Board, & Also of this.
