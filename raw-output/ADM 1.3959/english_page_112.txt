N° H. Oct. N° 2

                         Paris the 9th October 1772.

                                        Nothing from Portsmouth.

     It is reported from Rochefort of the 2nd, that the King has not
made himself [humaniste] as much as the [jouisseur]
of the Oracle.
     He believes, that the Project to reestablish the
Company of the Indies, is [très], but this
Work is long.
     All the [Pollemare] that the King of Prussia
has made on the Subject of the Bohemian War.
He [sitonnait] the [asthenie] to [Clerg]: For
good citizens were in Vigils.
     The Ministers are towards their
Country estates for 8 Days, and they are
[vénèrant] to honor then until Tomorrow.
     There is published in London a Journal
of, Annual renewal, for the Provinces
of the Indies.
     The Peace of the good ones [la encevoir] good
news during the Voyages; and one
like the [benleit] of certain and [entant]
of London, had told us that [citans]
the Peace [épointer] Denise, one has arrived
to know, if one will diminish the Peace, which
is of 132. Millions. One no longer runs
as formerly, after the Mines of the
                                             Provinces.
