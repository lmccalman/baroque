Mr. Barcelo is still at Court-
where He has been very well received.
    The King has sent Don M.de Galvez
Lieutenant General, and brother of the
Minister of the Indies, to the Vice-Royalty
and Presidency of the Council of Mexico
for all the Time that His Majesty
will judge appropriate for his Service.
    The King appears very content with the
definitive Treaty of Peace.
    The Two twin Infantas,
and their August Mother, continue
to be in good health.
