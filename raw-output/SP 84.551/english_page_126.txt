Someone writes to me from Cadiz on the 9th of this month,
that they have just placed an Embargo
there on all Spanish Vessels.
That they have just dispatched to Paris from
our Chief in order to obtain the Review of
before Esbaronne. And that the
Infantry Regiments, the
Hennepe, Toledo, of Hibernia,
Zamora, Savoy, and Guadalajara,
were in full March, to
go there to register themselves, as well as
Two other Regiments, whose
Names I do not know.

From Malaga on December 12.
they inform me, that the Eve of

the four Vessels of the King,

de Lejedin, and that the Two
Regiments that remained there in
