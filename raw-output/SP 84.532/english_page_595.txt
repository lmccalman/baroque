Furnes the 17. May 1775.

They have changed the
garrisons of Dunkirk, the 4 Battalions
that were there, have gone to St. Fois, and to
Calais, and there are 4 other Battalions.
A Battalion of the
troops is working on a new Square on this side
of St. Thomas.
The entire Council at Dunkirk is
resolved on the Construction of a
[chaleureuse] Jetty.
The Messenger is daily, and there are
several families who are thinking
of settling in [ci] Berque St. Winnoc.
or at Meerynck.

Brussels the 20. May 1775.

The troops that
remain with us, work Night and Day
drawing Cartridges, filling
grenades &c., that are transported to
Germany all the Writings.
