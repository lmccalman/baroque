                                        Nothing New at Brest.

the Horwick Mines and was preparing well loaded,
it [trafiqué la froye] at very little Distance,
which would also come that thing.

        The Duke of Choiseul returned from [Chantebrey]
Friday, and the next day he went to
Compiègne; the Others left yesterday by Day.

[Daubin] return, raise at Court to stop [Bryonne]
