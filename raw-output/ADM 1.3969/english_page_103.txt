During the last Peace, Paris was
flooded with Englishmen, it is our Turn
today to return these Visits, and
all our young Officers, our Lords,
our [Artifs], depart for England.
but it is to be noted, that one does not
see among these Travelers, a
single Officer of our Navy.

The Journey of the Count of Artois
is fixed for the Month of July next.
He will go to the Waters of Spa.




Madrid.
