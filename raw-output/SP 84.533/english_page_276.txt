Paris the 22nd October 1793.

Madame the Countess
of Provence is entirely free to go where she
wishes; She is stripped of all the Titles
[Royale le porte très-bien ; on n'aura d'eux
autant la Pontine oblieux ; qu'on n'ait
envoyé à Campagne.]

The Sovereign Court of Nancy has just
been created Parliament of Lorraine;
it will be increased by 28 members.

The parliament of Paris has also been
Parliament of Metz.

The Affair of the Parliament of Rouen
was completed last Friday
28th of this Month, without Troops, without Rumors
on the Part of the People, and very tranquilly.

M. Roussel intendant had arrived from
Rouen on Wednesday evening; the Duke of
Fitz-James, and M. Bastard, arrived there
on Thursday; the Letters de Cachet were
delivered in the following Night, so that
