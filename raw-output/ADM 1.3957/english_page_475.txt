                                        the 20 Nov. No 2.

            Brest the 7 November 1770.

                    The [Quiridaires] the

Vanqueur and the Union Left the from the Bonfin
Gabrielle: they were replaced there by
the Inconstante and the Infidelle: these three [ttes]
were changed and the water was drained, there are
some Planks to change. Those which they
Leave. The Vanqueur the Palmier,
and the Desireux Will Follow: thus it Remains Full.

The Ville de Paris Will Leave the Bonfin,
and the Rosale Will Follow it will enter, she was
replaced by the Neptune which one goes to
inspect and [rallier] Its Plan. All the
vessels: as much as one could do
is finished, there are and all Crews,
the Ports and Between-decks Following on which one
has not yet worked. There is half
of the Planks to change.

Today we have
in this Port the Vessels and frigates
Following, quite ready to be armed.
