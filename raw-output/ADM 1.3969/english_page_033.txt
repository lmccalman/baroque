14 2 april N 1

Paris the 17th March 1782

        The Corvette the Rusé, entered
the 11th of this Month at l'Orient, with a
part of the Officers of the Vessel the Rélitaire
which was taken Windward of Barbados
by a Division of the English Squadron
of Admiral Pigot - Here is the Bulletin received
by this Corvette.
        The Chevalier de Borda, commanding
a Division of Two Ships of the
Line, Two Frigates and a Corvette, set
sail from St. Pierre of Martinique, the
20th November, to go cruise Wind-
ward of Barbados. The First December
M de Borda, having encountered an Enemy
Vessel, with a Frigate, gave them
chase, without being able to join them.
        The 6th December at daybreak the
French Division signaled some
Sails, which it took for the Convoy
Squadron English, which had been announced.
