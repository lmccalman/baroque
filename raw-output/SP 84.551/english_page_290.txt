N° 27

Paris the 20th September 1776.

There is this Post
no interesting News from our Ports.
The Prince de Montbarey was found
Saturday in the Council Chamber of his
Dispatches, the Ministers astonished, did not
know why; finally the King
arriving, said: M. de Montbarey
[stes-je vous], the Enigma by the fact
soon, this Prince made the Report of
Affairs, in the Place of M. St. Germain,
who was sick, the latter having
from the Queen the Cross fa Cross fa St. Michel,
It is claimed today, that the Minister
will retire [l'ivé] the Baton of Marshal
of France, & that the Prince de Montbarey
will replace him.

There are frequent Committees of
Ministers & Versailles, & M. the Keeper of the Seals
of Spain [y listoient]. Twice
one speaks of war; one makes quite
quietly march troops without
