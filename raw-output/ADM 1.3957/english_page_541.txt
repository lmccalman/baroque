Paris the 30 December 1790.

Henry still has
nothing New to report.

the list of Municipal officers, and Members
of Citizens' Battalions. The first said
5 December and the 2nd the 8th Current in [beaule]

in all the Houses, by suppressing
first the spectacles, and the expenses in
all kinds even the Purchase [en] of the Kings
This Reform has made Reduce the good

on all the objects that
