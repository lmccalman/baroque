Paris the 6th February 1781.

They are arming at Brest the
frigate the Venus, Cutters, and
three Corvettes; a small Armament
under the Orders of Mr de Marigny, is
destined for the Coast of Africa; it
is conducting a small Corps of Troops
and nine Cannons, to keep a tight rein
on the Portuguese. It is Mr Mauduit
Duplessis who commands the Artillery.

The frigate the Danae which they
are also arming, will depart from Rochefort
and will conduct Baron de Clugniac
to his Government of Guadeloupe.
