Brussels, the 20th March 1778.

In the Villages, which
are found outside the Route, for going the
enemy troops, up to near the
Luxembourg, descend in the
Patrols. The troop of the Grand
Provost of the Bishopric, as well as the troops
of Brabant, and of Count van der Nooth,
consisting of 250 Hundred Men,
patrol after the City of Brussels
to have rested in the said
Villages, which they strip and the
Patrols [Viellequieres], to avoid
the Desertions.


                                        Wellens
                                        Major
