14 17 August 1776.

Paris the 6 September 1776.

Nothing of great interest has
been received this week from
our Ports.

The rumor spread today
several Decrees from different
Parlements, and having made to the King
several Representations against
the Encroachments of the Parlements by
the Revolution, and that of
Privileges; he has just granted
a [Régence] of the King to make
various Representations.

This [Régence], in having published
the Decrees of various Parlements,
forming the Object of the Contestation,
announces an upcoming Regulation,
to establish the Competence of
the Prefectures, and the Jurisdiction,
