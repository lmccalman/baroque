Brussels the 6th February 1790.

Duke Charles is leaving,
at Easter for Vienna, accompanied
by the principal Lords of the Country.
They were [devoit] from Berlin, that considerable
Preparations are being made there in
the Artillery, just as if one was going to
enter into Campaign: All the
Artillery Corps summoned to Berlin, for
Spring to do their Exercises there,
have orders to go there as if having to
make a longer Stay than usual
[contient].
