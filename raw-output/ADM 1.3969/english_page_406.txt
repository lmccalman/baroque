On the 8th of the current [month] arrived at
Cadiz, Don Jean Joseph de Vertis, lieutenant
general, who was viceroy of Buenos Aires,
on the frigate the prophecy, with nearly half
a million strong piastres, in part
to be recovered, and many other goods.

Ships arrive every day from
all parts of America; which
[nous invitent] from Cadiz, Mr. D'olys
three from Campeche, the latter, loaded with gold, etc.

The Company of the Orient will
henceforth be called the Royal
Company of the Orient; His Majesty to
encourage it to conduct trade with
China [à Brotière], puts in a million
strong piastres of funds; and everyone
will be able to take shares in it.
