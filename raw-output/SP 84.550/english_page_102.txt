Paris the 15th March 1776.

The lit de Justice
announced, took place last Tuesday
at Versailles, the Parlement and the
Peers and Peers being there; this
Ceremony began at Eleven o'clock
in the Morning, and lasted until Five in the
evening, when all the Edicts were
registered there. Mr Seguier, First
Advocate General pronounced there
a Speech of the greatest
force, which has, it is said, displeased the King.
The Minutes of this Lit de
Justice will only appear in
a few Days.

The Parlement assembled the day before,
made a Decree, which protests
against this Place, and against all
that will be done there.

Ortner
