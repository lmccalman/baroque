                                        N° 9 Oct. N° 2

                        Brest the 26th October 1770.

                                        The Minister has been
[malades Meulon de trois Motifs], for a long time
the Vessels have their grand [haute] with the exception
of the Roy and Louis, the Bretagne, the S.t Esprit,
and the Couronne.
        The appointment of the two frigates is
advanced, the Pion lost a Lieutenant,
then Count Langlois and Mr de Donfort [le
Surprennent] the Command of Vessels,
[les et toujours aux Etats]: supplied
the two Frigates have in the Roadstead of the
Goods as in the [Saintres], the
Convicts consume 30 Barrels of
Goods per day.
        No Vessel has Departed or
Vessel of [les Vendu] Merchants
approaching the Coast.
        There are several Renewals among
the Port Officers, M. Lambert Captain
of Fireship, is made Captain of Port,
a man to be made Captain of
the Infant at the first Promotion. M. [le
Phémond] is made a Captain of Port.
        M. Montagne is named Captain
in chief of Governor. The latter
will not content [tant la Maremb].

                                        Paris
