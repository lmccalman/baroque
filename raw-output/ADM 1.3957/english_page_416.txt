193.                                    the 20 Ocb. N° 2

                        Paris the 30. October 1770.

                                    I am informed this [Soulun]
of the 17th that the [ouvras] of the Court to care for
the [Attendante] in Turin, are not yet
arrived.

I have no other details in this Packet
to examine well the [Escyennes], and I
kept them in a State of [Expédiment].

All this indicates very clearly, that
at least our Court prefers that of the [Légitres]
of the Lawsuits decided for the War in
the Moments, and that it no longer wants
to be drawn in.

Perhaps also the Spanish are,
[harais] to the Exhortations of Paris.

Our Politicians put the Portuguese
as having to submit to the Spanish.

One trembles here when one [haye] to the
War in the State where we are;
the Price of Bread increases again;
it is after Bohemia these are under
treaty the Portugal: & however from
all Sides Murmurs against the
Government.

of the Indies, armed in War, commanded
