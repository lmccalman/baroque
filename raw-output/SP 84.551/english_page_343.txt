                    Paris the 28th October 1776.

                    M. Du Chaffault,

Lieutenant General of the Naval
Armies, who commanded the
Evolution Squadron last Summer, is at
Court at the Minister of the Navy's.
He has just been named to
command in Chief the Squadron
that is being armed at Brest, the other
Commanding Officers, [S'Emploi]
in this Squadron are not named today

        It is reported from Brest on the 4th, that
the armament is well advanced.
        That the Order was served to arrive
to disarm the Ships, the
Lorraine, and the Bellone, which made
the last Campaign, they are
