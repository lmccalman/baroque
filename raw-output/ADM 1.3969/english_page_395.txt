In the last work that Marshal de
Segur did with the King for the Staff Commands
of the Army, the Count d'Elbéthézy was
named Commander of the Interior; the Marquis d'Harcourt
Commander in [Frôleme] in Normandy, and the
Marquis [d'Aprefelire] Director of the Staff.

Mr de Bougainville who has been here for several
days, will be proposed to the King this week. He has the
promise of being made Lieutenant-General at the first
promotion, which cannot delay in taking place, no
more than the new Ordinances relating to the
Navy, upon which it is claimed that the Marquis
de Castries has been working for a long time.
