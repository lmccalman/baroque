240                    Babaig ales

                        Paris the 26 April 1773.

                                        Nothing from our Ports,
we are not yet worried about the very return
from Cayenne, of which we hope that it will reappear
in Acadia, that M. Conglin will have been there
better; and that he will maintain the Corps
almost brought down.

     The three brigades armed at Toulon and
each at la Ciolle the 30th at 8. Happy if they
Beat the Corsairs without accident. If it
continues, Harris's Work will
soon be exhausted itself.

     The King who went last Monday to
Choisy will not return there soon or to
Versailles.

     We have some picturesque bits
this Week. The Chancellor has been twice
very troubled by a Colic,
he has had his hindrance of differences and of the
stones, but that in any case, he held
the bureau yesterday in Paris.

     The Procurator General appeared there again
Saturday at the Great Chamber on the subject of you
at the Parliament, but since the Bridges
do not appear there; they are the Dukes of
Luxembourg, of Montmorency, M.
d'Orque and M'gres le Roy, etc., with
seven three or [communier] names.
