Le Havre the 25th December 1777.

The Second Battalion
of the Regiment of Limousin is
embarked, that is to say, the 2 Compan[ies]
departed the day before yesterday on two
large Merchant Vessels, the last
[vaporera], they have orders to [s'envorier]
their [Autres] only at the Latitude of
Cape Finisterre.
    The 1st Battalion of this Regiment
goes to Honfleur, in Place of the Second
Battalion of Rogue Languedoc
which will embark.
    The Grenadiers of Champagne will arrive
here on the 27th. Our entire Coast is well
supplied with troops, Batteries,
and Provisions.
