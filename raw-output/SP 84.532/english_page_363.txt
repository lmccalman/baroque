That which it seems one does not do at all
by Article [ilk] in what regards the Monsieur,
and that one affects solely the Aid from
the land side, I have reason at my point to maintain,
that one seeks all imaginable Means
to [amonfler] Sums, and that there is
much Connection in the
Ministry, not so much for the Affairs
of Parliaments, as for Foreign
Affairs.

One speaks of sending French troops to
[Bouhant] to replace there
those that the Empress Queen withdraws from it.

Time will render us more informed,
and in view of [Rus], it will not hold to
[prudeur] great events.
