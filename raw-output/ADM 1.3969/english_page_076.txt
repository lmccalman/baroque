Madrid the 21. April 1782.

The Orders have been dispatched
to Cadiz, to discharge nearly 24.
Thousand Seamen, and that for all the
Ships of the Line, they return to
their respective Departments.

There is talk here of an Expedition against
Algiers; but there is nothing yet
certain on this Affair.

The Princess Regiment
Infantry, has orders to pursue, and
to destroy all the Smugglers
who are in great Number in
Extremadura, in Andalusia, and
in La Mancha.

The Ambassador of Malta, has
died four Days ago.
