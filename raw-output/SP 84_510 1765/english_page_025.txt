Reviews were held at Yaxi, and
all the Regiments depart for Brussels
for the various Regiments.

The Council finally put in Order
for the Affairs of the Provinces, and they are pursued
vigorously, we shall learn of it through
its Execution.

The Viscount de Noisseuil has just
obtained the Survivorship of Lieutenant
General of Brittany of which C.....de du
Baislin is Brother is Holder.

The Command of the Maritime
Cities of Flanders is given
to the Prince de Robecq, with 18 Thousand livres
fixed. He is moreover employed in
all the Army in the capacity of
Lieutenant General.

A Decree of the Parlement of 3 October
last, served to the Dean and to the Syndic
of the Faculty of Theology, had
forbidden them to give any Follow-up to the
Conclusion of 1st October, by which
the Faculty had declared its Duty
to adhere to the Acts of the Clergy.
