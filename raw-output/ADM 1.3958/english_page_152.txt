and of Autrices: at Rheims. He travels in short
Stages, because of the Princes of the Blood
who is not well recovered.

We have Letters from Constantinople
of the 28th of February, which no longer speak of
Peace, but of great and imminent
Preparations for the Campaign.

Our Letters from Rome of the 13th of March say
that the Pope appears very disposed to shower favors upon
the Jesuits; all the Cardinals support him.
The Courts of Madrid and of Lisbon will embroil him
anew with the Pope, it is only
a finished Affair.
