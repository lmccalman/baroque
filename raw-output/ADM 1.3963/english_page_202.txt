                    Second.

        Madrid the 18th September 1796.

                There are daily
Tantas, or Assemblies of the
Ministers at Court, and several
Couriers are dispatched Abroad.
The Ambassador of Portugal does
not miss a turn at Court,
and professes there with a very
tranquil Air, holding Conversation
with the King, as if there were
nothing amiss. Moreover, you find by the
frontiers they maintain very
tranquilly, and our Troops
come to activate our Enemies
without any Hindrance.
        The News from Galicia state,
that the King's Ship, the
Monarch, of 70 Cannons, had
set Sail, without knowing its
                                        Destination.
