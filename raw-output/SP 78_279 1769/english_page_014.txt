I the undersigned certify to all whom it may concern
today the sixteenth of December one thousand seven hundred sixty
And four be it that it has come to my knowledge at my house that
Bernard Lacroix son of Antoine inhabitant of Riviere that he committed
a theft at the said Antoine Lacroix's with a named Jean Desmarais
boy inhabitant of Baie Loup and Jeanne Desmerais sister
of the said Desmarais held a gathering at the said
Bernard Lacroix son's the fifth of May of the year one thousand seven hundred sixty
And four the said day Lacroix son and Desmarais came to
the said Bernard Lacroix this was the 5th of 1764 at named at our place


I certify                           J. Dumas ptre

                                      A. Pelletier
