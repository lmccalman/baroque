                                          R 12 Febr. N°1

                        Madrid the 25th January 1775.

                        Rest assured Sir,
that I am keenly interested in it and with the
care that you may have for the
continuation of our Commerce, and
that I lend all my attention
to providing you with what may most
interest your Friends.
     Follow therefore with confidence
your projects, [vous de vous procurer]
a [More ample interprétante] than a
change of Ministry, [le] it
will take place, in similar appearance,
but nothing [ne paroi pas éffacera]
than the Credit of the Marquis de Grimaldi,
which increases from day to day.
     One is occupied with work, which [envoiment]
in the Summer to the Arming of the vessels
which are in this Port, and which must
form the Squadron destined for the Islands;
from Cartagena the Regiment of Asturias
and that of [Permis], the same as
all the Companies of Grenadiers
which will be found in these environs,
to embark on the Vessels
