Whitehall, Nov. 11th 1780.

Sir;

I am commanded
to signify to You His
Majesty's Pleasure
That You should take
the first Opportunity of
sailing for Helvoe-
-Sluys, Wind & Weather
permitting, taking on
Board the Messenger
or Messengers that are
dispatched for Holland.
It is likewise The
King's Pleasure, that
You should wait at
Helvoed, till You are
visipatched by the Major
-General Yorke His
Majesty's Minister
Plenipotentiary at the
Hague.
