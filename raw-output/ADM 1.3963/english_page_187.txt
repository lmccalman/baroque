the 23rd Sept.ber 8

Paris the 16th. September 1776.

     The Engineer Officers
continue to work, yet
[ne sont-vend il] not at all. The Same
Obstacles are always [avenibles]
to H.M. the King. Confidence is
totally lost.
     There is much talk
of reestablishing the Corps of Berwick.
[La Viennée] is now delivered
by the Butchers, and the Police
turn a Blind Eye to this Contravention,
to satisfy the Commerce of
[Saintrie], and for the occupation of
consumers who go to Paris; who
[ambirent] the Renewal
and the request to the [Fédère] of
persons of the [Fédère] of this
Kingdom. The Deputy of a [trifle]
form the Shipments of in [Trifle].
          Merchant.
