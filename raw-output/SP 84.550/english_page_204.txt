Rotterdam the 21 May 1776.

My Lord.

     I have the Honour to send
you enclosed, the Account of the
expenses of the arrival of the Ship at this
Madrid, for the transport of which
the 8th March last I have amounted to
£446.15s. Sterling, of which the Half is
£9o. 7s.6d.Sterling, for which
Sum I have drawn at the Charge of
mine by the first of these
days Monsieur de Van Hoey with the
treasurer of Your Excellency Monsieur
William Anderson & Company.

     I have the Honour and am with a
profound Respect

                    My Lord,

                         Your very humble & very
                         obedient Servant
                              M. Wolters.

Earl of Suffolk.
