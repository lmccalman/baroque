Two Barques, of Twelve Cannons
and of Six Thousand Quintals; and
another of Eight Cannons and of three
Thousand Five Hundred Quintals.

They are working in our shipyards
on three Frigates, and several other
Vessels, with all activity, which
are all, it is said, for
the Americans.

They regard here the Defense
of the King of France; [de me paroiendre]
Store Ships to the English,
as useless, and of no Effect,
since they can well provide
at Livorno, and It is said that they
have bought there Two large Ships
which they transport to Gibraltar
to convert them into Frigates.
