Geneva the 10th July 1778.

                The Conferences,
where the Minister of France on one
side, and the Deputies of the Swiss
Cantons on the other, regarding the
Renewals of the Alliance
with France, were arranged
towards the end of the Month of May
past, and they will probably not finish
until next September,
to complete this great Work.
Nothing has been learned until now
of the Conditions, that I can
communicate to you through
my gazette.
                There are quarrels between
the Clergy, and the Citizens and
Burghers of our City,
regarding a [Cela] general School
of the Laws of our State.   The
