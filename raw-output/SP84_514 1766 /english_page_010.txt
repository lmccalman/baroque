                    Brussels the 20 November 1796

          [Le ci] that one keeps at the
Court a profound Silence on the
Result of the Consultation of the
faculty on the State of Duke Charles,
it transpires that this Result
is not at all favorable, and yesterday
the Sieur Mouton Courier of the
Cabinet of His Royal Highness,
departed for Vienna, with
Dispatches on this Subject.
