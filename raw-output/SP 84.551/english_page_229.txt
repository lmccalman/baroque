Toulon, April 22, 1776.

                                           The Je Desire departing
from Brest for this Port, arrived there
in 24 Days of Crossing, Despite
the West wind; she encountered in
the Alarm, three large English vessels,
the English Wishau, the Medaway,
and three other frigates, when
[voispere] by Cape la Roque,
and that of St Vincent.

She saw in the Mediterranean
a Spanish Warship,
mounted with 74 Cannons, and loaded with
People; having with it three pieces
of [Sull'ottes], also armed for War,
all these Vessels were coming from
Cartagena, and appeared to be making
Route for Oran or Algiers.

three [pans Tournois] of 50 Cannons, armed
