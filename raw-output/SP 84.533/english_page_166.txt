Brest the 3rd September 1778.

They are going to arm in
this Port, the Amphitrite, which will be
commanded by Mr the Count
le Rostiqueuc, by Mr de Rochent,
the Zephire, by Mr Deserans;
the Rola, by Mr de la Marteliere;
and the Gloire, by Mr Verdun de la Crene,
the first two for Martinique,
the following two for St Domingue,
and the last to go make
Observations, Mr de Borda, great
Mathematician is on the Voyage.

Messieurs Lombard, and Cramells,
Ship Lieutenants, must the
first place begin the
flutes, the Barbue, and the Etrille, they
were given order of the Renthievre,
and the Marliere at Lorient, they have just
been taken away these two Vessels,
and they are given back the two Flutes
for our Colonies.

We have only 2 Vessels
on the Shipyards, of which all has been laid
