No. 78.

Paris the 20th September 1776.

Nothing interesting has been
received from Toulon.
The King arriving from Choisy,
will hear on the 5th he will be this morning stayed
until Dispatches arrived by a
Courier from Madrid. It appears
that Spain wants to avenge itself
on the Portuguese, which would lead to
a general War.
Besides 40 Battalions that are
in Brittany, 40 others are being
marched there, and 85 in Normandy.
An Army Corps must be assembled
near Dunkirk.
The General Officers employed
in the Provinces must have received
new Orders. The Service
will resume on the 5th of October.

L.s
