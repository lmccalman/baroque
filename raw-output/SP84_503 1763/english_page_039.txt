rules the highest honours, and
his Mind Measures, so far as

to which the King has been
pleased to trust the Collection

I have met, to congratulate
you upon this happy fortun-
-e of affairs, Particularly, upon
Seeing the Ports armies, which
will put an end to that
Scandalous Spirit of Faction,
which, if Suffred to proceed,
must Soon have endangered
that Liberty, which (ever I)
affects to Support.

I am &c
Rob't Philip
