Rotterdam 6 february 1770

Sir

The Vienna Commissioners arrived in Rot-
terdam on being informed by the Commissioner
at the Hague, who is in communication with me
in town & is his Armee, he is continued
to going to set & again to finish that
of the land & to immediately said the
appeared as this my agent is married
aged, he would have given notice
of this before now, but he thought he has
said, when he could trust with the
conduct of his business and now comes
but after sheltering Rogel shows
to tear on the other Bank
of the Salve he, the first opportunity,
& & this is to the first Post when he
has been sent, cannot care be heard yet,
to the land by the ordinary Post &
Roggen & Judge & has to have been
here three then but he will here Roggen

Sir
your most humble &
most obedient Servant
Ed Hush

Frazer Esq
