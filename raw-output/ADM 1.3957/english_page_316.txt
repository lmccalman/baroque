They have the theory itself at the Chambers
a New Letter from the King. He has authorized
the [eguesque] that the Parliament of Rennes has
sent, they have named Commissioners
to inform about [Evamaj], and to render
Account of this Event.

The affair of the premiers of the
Posts and the [Clemens] of Pellenc and des
Tournelles, was judged the day before yesterday at the
Châtelet of Paris. The Judges put such
[d'humeur] in the parties, that it induced
the Families to propose an accommodation-
They have named arbitrators, it is an
Object that may amount to a Million. They have
appealed on the Spot the Appeal of this
Sentence to Parliament, which Surprises
Everyone. This affair cannot be
Subject except in 6 to 7 years.

They are printing the work of [l'Allegé] against
incredulity, it is subscribed in certain
Number to all the Bishops.

According to our letters from Breslin of July 29,
a Part of the Rebels have laid down Their
Arms, and have Submitted to the Conditions,
they are regimenting them to make them pass
into another [bonone].

They assure that finally the Pope will Decide
to give to the Courts of his demands some
aid from the House of Bourbons
