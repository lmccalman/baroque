Mr. Bigot de Morogues, Captain of Vessels,
is appointed to command in the Port of Ostend
and at Nieuport until further Order. He must go to
Brussels to take his orders from Count de Harrach Marshal,
he will go there as soon as possible. We form no body by
[la ferme], and that these two Places belong to Us and to the
Generality. It is even said that all the Low Countries are
for Don Philip in Salve retinete, in exchange for
Parma and Piacenza, and in Recompense for the Armed
Alliance that he grants us for the war in the two
Provinces. It is also believed that Prince Charles will have a
sovereign State, and that he will marry Madame Adelaïde
of France;

It is assured that the Abbé de Bernis, for the King, and
Mr. d'Affry have signed between the King and the Republic
together [d'un Affranchissement] for the reestablishment
of Senate, and that already there are 18 articles agreed
between the two Parties;

The Queen has, according to the News we
have, [d'accouché à peu] Bohemia, and always
with Loss; but it will be necessary [tant en ôt retarder le dé-
mande] that all Communication is cut off from
Silesia, and that already an Austrian General has
taken Landshut.

The Queen of [Michelius] will march from Prague
to Nuremberg, and perhaps make the Junction with the Marshal
[de] Mr. the Marshal de Richelieu is to go relieve
Saxony, and take quarters in the Electorate
of Brandenburg.

The Death of the Queen Dowager of Prussia
[pointe de notifier l'e], we will not wear Mourning for it.

The Marshal de Mirepoix leaves tomorrow for
Nîmes.
