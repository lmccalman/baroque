Brest the 10th October 1772.

They are preparing to
arm the Ships the Redoutable,
l'Artesienne, and l'Union of 64 Cannons,
the first and the last have been for two
years armed.

The Minister requests War
Council, that the Ship the
Magnifique of 74. which was to be
disarmed, would be rearmed, they are
working on it Consequently.

The Fate of the Royal Louis is not yet
decided, the Opinions for the
Demolition and the Reconstruction are divided.

Mr Fauquet, Engineer and Constructor,
has requested His Retirement and Pension,
has requested the Resignation, and has
obtained it.

There remains owed to the Officers of the two
Navy and of the Administration
the 9 last Months of 1771, the Second and
third Quarter of 1772. The Workers
