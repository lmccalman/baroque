The King has dispatched to the Princes of the
blood of the house of the Affairs of
Parliament, the Prince of Conti responds alone.
The Tribunal of Affairs of the
Princes has pronounced itself loudly, Commerce
languishes, Bills are very frequent.
Satire sells nowhere.
One can no longer wait, it must
be said that all this ends.
A thousand different rumors are spread,
seditious complaints are posted
daily.
Bread is still at the same price,
and other goods in proportion;
consumption on all things
is much diminished, and consequently
the rights being farmed out, the Farmers
General cry misery, and that they
are losing considerably.
Besides the Count of Maillebois named
General Director of Engineering, Artillery
and fortifications of the Places of
Flanders in the Kingdom, the
Marquis of Montreuil, and the Count of
Mailley, Dancourt, the brothers de la Porte de
and Briennes, and will report to the
Minister, they each have 50 thousand
livres in salary.
