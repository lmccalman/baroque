It is not yet known what will happen to the
Parliament of Brittany, for such a marked
Discipline; but it is known that certain
Persons are being watched there.

There is fermentation at Court over
the Positions to be given in the Household being
formed for the Count of Provence, where
it could be, that M.rs Lenay de Rozieres,
General [Pourveur] at the Court of Aides, and
M.rs de Kerivel, M.rs de la Roche Thevenin,
Chancellor, and M.rs de Megine, General
[Tennieur], are Intendants of it.

of the General Government of Roussillon from
the Marquis of Desfort, formerly Ambassador
to Vienna, has passed Knight of Honor of
Madame Victoire, and the Knight of
Tailleyrand to the same titles with
