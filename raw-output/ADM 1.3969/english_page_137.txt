Two Million and consequently a prodigious
quantity of Workers to establish
at Cherbourg in the fair Season, the
Dykes that the Sea will not respect as long
as they are not cast so to
speak in a single casting.

The King's Vessel Marieurs
having entered at Bordeaux, and some
young People having been arrested, the Prelate
is no longer troubled.

The Count of Cherenne Stanville has
taken the Name of Marshal of Cherenne.

The King has created Field Marshals
to take rank in the first
Promotion, Messieurs de Lauron, de
Laval, Dautichamp, and de Bretaeng,
it is therefore false, that the Latter
died at the Siege.

The Bannede de Palms died
suddenly these last days.
