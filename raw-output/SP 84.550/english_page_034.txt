The Royal Lorraine Regiment
Cavalry, is stationed at Emond Eguin
la Marne, which sent away the
horses, the Colonel bought back
those of the Mounted Grenadiers,
at the Rate of 400 Livres each;
which remounted it all at
once, with Horses accustomed
to the Maneuvers of the Cavalry.

Monsieur, & the Viscount of the
Count of Artois, has just also
made a Reform in his
Household; which makes for
each a Saving of about
300 Thousand Livres per Year.
