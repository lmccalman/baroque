Mr Huber received the Dispatches from
all the Officers of the King's Squadron at
Benderesi; who all live with the desire to
Remain under the Orders of the Brave Vice-
Admiral, & delays the Departure of Mr
Baudressi, who will not have been able to leave Borak
until the 25th of this Month.

The Deputies of the Province of
Brittany, come to offer to the King, to
put at the expense of the Province, the Port
of St. Malo in a State to receive the
King's Vessels; & the Project being adopted,
one will see no less than that of
Cherbourg being abandoned.
