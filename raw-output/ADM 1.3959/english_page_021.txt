the Officers in general will be attached

Count de la [Gorte] Prevost in
union with the Colonels and Masters
and two Councillors of State, went
the day before yesterday in a day of Ceremony,
and in the King's Coaches, to the
Chamber of Accounts, to execute there
the orders of His Majesty, it is not yet
known exactly what is planned there,
except that they had brought there three
[Bouchier] Edicts which were not yet,
the First President made
in this Session a Speech, of which
the Content must be sent to the
other Accounts, it Contained more or less
of the Accounts, after several Years
of Peace.
