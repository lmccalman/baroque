N°. 34.

                    Paris the 1st of July 1776.

                    The letters from Toulon
of the 23rd, speak only of the extraordinary
Movement that is manifesting in the
Orders of the King.
     Those from Paris of the 26th, report, that
the [Sujets en Roi de Levant] & at this
Moment [sans brilés y aller]
transport all sorts of
Munitions of War to our
different Colonies, & it appears
at least, but the Commanders
have Packets that They must not
open until 300 leagues at Sea,
which must inform them of their
true Destination.
     The fleet the Seine is equipped
from the Isle de France & L'Orient, will depart
the 20th of February. it has been in this
Country, the Duke of Noailles, the
Princes, & several Courtiers.
