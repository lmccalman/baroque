Paris the 14th January 1784.

There is absolutely nothing
new either at the Court or in the
City.

The Minister's Letters to all
the Officers of the new Promotion
were sent last Tuesday.
One counts up to present 66 to 46
Lieutenant Generals; the Nomination
of the Marshals of Camp will not pass
inclusively, and moreover, there is
only the Prince of Nassau who
was Marshal of Camp, all the
Colonels until 1745 are brigadiers
there are some from 1764, the
regiments are given but they
have not yet named the Colonels
in Second.
