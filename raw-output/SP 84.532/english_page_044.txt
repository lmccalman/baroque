The transfers throughout the entire Province
of Vienna during Marriages, have formed such
an offense of sovereignty, that the
Problem was.
            To know already justly well,
if: Mars, to that one of our [Patriques],
in Constantinople, whether it would [enseroit] more,
for the deliberation of the voyages of
Alfred Chapps, nor good Father and his
Government as much the strong [multanielle],
The [interdise] which has just [parviller]
in the orders of this Sovereignty,
through an Act made negligent,
clearly marks all the Rancor of the
Heart against Us.
            Misery in all the Provinces,
the appearances of the most
dreaded Revolt everywhere; Abbé Terray
very inclined to overburden the People who
already drags groaning its current
burden; the Lack of King & those who
have finally bent the Agent in Public;
an open War against the Parliament
that the Nation regards as the Guardian
of its Goods, of its Liberties, and of its
Life.
                                    Vuillé
