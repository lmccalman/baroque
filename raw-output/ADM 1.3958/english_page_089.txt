     The Object of this future Visit is not
precisely known, but here are the Conjectures
of our Politicians.
     The Emperor in concert with the King of
Prussia would divide a Part of
Poland between Them, and would leave to the
King only a Part of this Kingdom, with
absolute Authority.
     Others say that Leopold would
propose the Crown of Poland to the
Duke of Saxe-Teschen.
     There is no agreement on the Part
that the Russians will have in these arrangements.
     Others finally refuse, that the
King wishes, to enlarge the Port of Polices, and to
hold at His disposition in time of War
liberty to all the States; that for this
purpose the Small [Longueues] and Prussians
will be able to reconcile by Sea, and that the
