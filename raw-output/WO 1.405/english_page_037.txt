S. Pithilio       After the departure of the Red en Saturday last
6.ber (& 20.ber)  Don Don Francisco de Borja boasted his Flag
                   on board the Europa, and the Pithilio having
                   returned out of the arsenal this morning, the
                   signal for sailing has been made, and it is said
                   his small Division will proceed tomorrow morning
                   for Cadiz.        All the Ships reported to have
                   been fifteen sowte are ordered to be balasted &
