                        Breslau the 20th Xbre 1772.

of Artillery for Czenstochau;
these are [Mouchoirs] which made us
a Refuge to march these Gentlemen
of Conflans. One knows by this way

sent a famous Reliquist with a

Confederations declares to him, that he wants to
[inonnier] with the Troops the Territories

ask him to retire to a Convent.
that he has already sent twice
