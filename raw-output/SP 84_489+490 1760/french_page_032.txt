Things from Revolution
Mediate to support the
Allies & to prosecute the
War with the utmost
Vigour.
I transmit to You
herewith His Majestys
Letters of Sollicitation
on this Occasion, to the
States General, the
Prince of Orange & the
Prince of Nassau
Weilburg; which You
will deliver in the usual
& proper manner; And
You will assure their
High mightiness of the
Kings firm Resolution
to contribute & exement
the same friendships and
good Correspondence, which
so happily subsist
between the two Nations.
till His Majesty shall
send You new Letters of
Credence
