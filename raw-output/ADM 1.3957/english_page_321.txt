Brussels the 20th. April 1790.

     From the letters that the Prince
of Hohenlohe has received from Vienna, of the 2nd. of this month,
[desroient] to Continue as long as the weather will be fine
and all the Payments which are in this Country,
must be completed, by the 28th. September,
their which the end the general [Regnie] of two
all the Garrisons.
     It is doubted from Stockholm, that the Minister of
finances of this Court, has not been able to succeed in renewing
the treaty of Subsidies, and that the one of Russia
has not had enough from Russia for the Renewal
of the treaty of Alliance of this Court with Sweden.
     It costs 80. Thousand Ducats to the City of
Danzig, to come to terms with the King of
Prussia. This Monarch departs the 24th. for
Silesia, and from there to Moravia.
