Bastia the 22nd December 1759.

Order to the Corsican Legion
that is being formed here, and which is well advanced;
Mr. Buttafusco with the approval of the Court
is raising a Regiment of two Battalions, On
the French footing, it will bear his Name.
The Count de Marbeuf returns here with
Spring, all is here in order and
tranquility, the Corsicans appear content,
great Number present themselves to have
Offices and Employments, Either in the Robe,
Either in finance, and in the
Military.
