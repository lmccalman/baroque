                    Toulon the 28th March 1776.

                    The Officers who have
always waited, to be Part of
the Division, not yet being able
to all Arrive in the Roads Tomorrow
La Provence will go on the 2nd, or the 3rd of
next month.
    The Marine Council has had
Orders to deliberate on the Works
at [Vixe d'Hyenh] proposed by
a Company; the Officers
of the Administration are not
content with this Arrangement.
    The Marine Corps has decided,
to give a celebration for Monsieur
and Madame de Chartres; one is
occupied at this Moment, in
deliberating on the Nature of
this celebration.
                    Paris
