15 & May 1776.

Paris the 28. May 1776.

Monsieur Camée

News from our Posts: Awaiting Return, we have
today only from the Dauphine, without entering
into the Details of Things that are done for the
Gazettes, I will tell you the Principal following ones.

This Princess arrived Saturday evening at
Soissons, and there learned the Departure of the Two [Alles]
Monseigneur And others of more than one
The Countess at 8 hours after midnight, the King
Campaign of the Dauphins, the [voguete] [Il'etut]
Louis the Soissons, the King, the princes and
[l'embours] awaited her, and even brought her
together the Dauphine, the latter and the married
to the Castle, from where the next morning of good
world in us awaiting the pos, and She has since
very [fortifoy]; that made so much [Claires] to the
King, that He has been since that time of a Humor
Charming.

Mesdames de France awaited the
Dauphine at Compiègne, all the Family
royal and the Court followed them there; She is
[courbee], at 3 hours after noon, and She wants to come
sleep at la Muette [l'eny] some days Paris.
The King and Madame la Dauphine come
Tuesday-to by post-either at St. Denis, to
see Madame & or at l'Etoile.
The Interval that [l'espace] the Castle of la
