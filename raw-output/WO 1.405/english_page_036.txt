                                        Carthagena

Mr Walker        On the 27th inst. after the departure of the
Bothers, R. Selsey  Post which conveyed my letter to you of
                that date, His catholic Majesties Frigate
                the Borda of 74 Guns and the Fenix of
                34 have arrived here from Ferrol to
                incorporate with the ships mentioned in
                my last. They expect to be joined soon
                by one Frigate from Cadiz, and it is thought
                that the whole Squadron with the ready & not
                quite in the course of the ensuing Month.
