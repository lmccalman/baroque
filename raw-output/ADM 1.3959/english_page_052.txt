165                                R.6 ap. nº 1

                                        Brest the 28th March 1772.

                                                    The Vessels
✓   L'Hippopotame, Mᵉ Rien, have arrived here
    from Rochefort, they brought there 400 men
    from the Atalante, and which the King destines for
    this Port, we are arming the Frigates la Royale,
    la Surveillée, and the Dédaigneuse, which
✓   will bring the Rest, and the 6 old
    Vessels that we are arming in this Port. One of the
    Transports which had passed the two
    packetboats at Vedo, we believed for
    some time, that we could Navigate it
    [l'est], and from the Destruction; but today
    there is no longer any Doubt. Mᴿ de Maurville
    going there Saturday began to see
    it sinking in such a way, which makes all fear
    to give up on the Orders

    We await the Arrangements for
    the formation of the staff and
    the officers who must command the
    two frigates others are begun
✓   one can see it by the News
    above
