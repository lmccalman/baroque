Assenois Jany 23d 1782

Sir,
     I have had Your
Letter of the 4th Instant before
Lord Suffolk, and I have His
Lordships Directions to acquaint
You, that He very much approves
Your having transmitted the
Intelligence You received from
Your Friend. If You should
hear from him again His
Lordship desires You will
send over any Information He
may make that may appear
to be worthy of Notice.

I am &c
W. Frazer
