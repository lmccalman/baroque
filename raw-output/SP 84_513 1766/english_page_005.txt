People eagerly embrace every Opportunity
of putting about to some Advantage,
and it is for this Season that the
States discharging annually some
part of the Public Debt, will not be
approved of if it goes two far, as
it obliges People to send their Money
to other Countries.

The Sentiments of joy for the
Approach of the 8th of March, are
general all over the Country, and
it is to be wished that this day may
be less turbulent than we have
reason to fear that it will be in
some of the Towns.

The Magistrates of Rotterdam
being that the Mob began already
to make collections for the Rejoicing
of that Day, have issued a strong
Prohibition against it last Saturday.
