Paris the 10th June 1776.

Reports from Brest
and Rochefort state that they are occupied
only with refitting the Vessels; [Lit.
gravité des Armes], is at Versailles.

The Commissions for the
Provinces are included in the 22
Lieutenant Generals named,
with the Exception of the Prince of Beauveau
for Provence, the Count of
Mailllebois for Picardy;
the Marquis of Vauréal for
Guyenne; and the Marquis of Langeron
for Brittany. The Lieutenant
Generals will have 12 Thousand Livres
of Salary, and will be able to choose
to remain 4 Months [à l'Exception
ou Legion]. The Field Marshals
for each Division will serve
the entire Year, that is to say, Six Months
each; they each have Two Thousand
Livres of payment per Month.
