Berlin the 30th January 1773.

I am certain that
we have here War, the complaints
of our French Comedians are altogether
added to us by Poland.

They have just sent through the
Magazines 4 large Boats loaded
with Artillery, there are still 8 all ready
for the same Destination, loaded with
the same Device, and which await only
a little Thaw to depart.

The Departure of the Majesty, who left
this Capital earlier than his Custom
