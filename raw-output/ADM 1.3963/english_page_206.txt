The Minister of Finances [co]
troubled last Sundays
with the King for three Hours.
This Minister returned The
next day to Paris, suffering
more than ever from Gout.

The Rumors of War, which
appear well-founded, and the Disorder
in Commerce, continue
to lower the Houses
and the other Royal Securities.

The Revenues of the Company
of the Indies stood well at
1600 Livres.
