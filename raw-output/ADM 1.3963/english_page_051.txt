Mr de Villeblanche, Ship Captain,
is dead; they found two large
Stones in his Bladder.

The Count de St Germain is in
his bed, with fever, and a bad
Cold; which may delay the
Publication of his Operations.

The Ordinances for the Gendarmes,
and the Carabiniers, do not appear
ready; moreover the
Content is being made. The Marquis de Poyanne
remains Commander of this Corps,
under Monsieur, who is its
Colonel. Mr de Montagu, the most
senior Brigade Chief, is made
Squadron Commander in.
There are 60 reformed Officers in
the Corps, and almost all the General
Staff.
