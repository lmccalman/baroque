Madrid the 26th September 1783.

Sir Jean Nepomucene,
has not brought any money, since it
is decided that he would not be charged with
Piastres belonging to the King, which
returns to Havana, it [elles] that
[Commence] brought by Sir [deux]
A Few Days before His Departure,
our Administrators will change
their Sentiments, so much do They fear to
show too much Foresight, and
to [epouffer] their [Sent:us vidomnes]
thus one cannot expect this
Treasure until around the Month of February
One awaits here with the
greatest Impatience the News
from Algiers one is curious to know
to what Point this City has
[Jusfort]. Mr Marciel has been made
chief of the King.
