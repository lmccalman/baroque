13.3 Ungaria

Paris the 23rd July 1784.

The King of Sweden departed
last Monday at ten o'clock in the evening,
to return to Stockholm.

Letters patent from the King have just been published,
in the form of an edict,
bearing ratification of the contract
of exchange passed on 11 March 1784, between
the King, and the Prince of Condé, by
which this prince cedes to his Majesty
the rights of [Jubilles], [Salue], Aides,
Tailles, [comptrolle], and others which are
collected for his profit in the counties.
His Majesty cedes to the Prince of Condé
six hundred thousand livres of perpetual income
at the principal of twelve million,
formed at Versailles in the month of April 1784,
registered at the court of Aides on the 7th May
following.
