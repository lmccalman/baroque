They are going to repair the Artillery of the Post
of the Grand Corps.
The Officers of the Post of Rochefort
have Permission to choose Breast,
or Toulon for their Department.
We saw on the 15th of this Month with all
our Eyes a great Accident. A
Vessel from Nantes coming
from America, and estimated at 800 Thousand Livres,
struck through Ignorance, opened up,
and sank suddenly in the
Goulet, two Barks ran.
They fished out 90 Men of the Crew,
but 28 perished, of this Number is
the Captain and the Officers.
