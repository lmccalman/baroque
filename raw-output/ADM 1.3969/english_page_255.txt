to Ours, and one will know in a short
Time, what will be his Projects, his
Alliances, and his Resources, to
oppose a Dike to the Torrent, which
threatens to crush him with its violence.
A Courier from Constantinople
arrived the day before yesterday at
Versailles, but nothing has transpired from his
Dispatches; it was only remarked
that the King after having read them,
appeared long after still occupied
