Rotterdam this 10th May 1773

My Lord


The appreciation that you had the Grace to give me on
this little Piece in a Letter that you did me
the honor of writing to me in July of 1772, encourages [comment]
[mon] My Lord [pour me faire oser les accasions de mentionner
vers. Je me mon] talent as a Painter: they have presented themselves for
some Months in a happy manner, and [je vous egare]
Permit me My Lord to mark my gratitude to you for it,
and to testify how sweet it is to me to have been able to
give convincing proofs of it.
Chevalier Yorke on an order from the King of England to buy
[tter] at The Hague told me that if in a short time we had
Surpassed expectations, and that he believed that he
should inform of it this Letter with some Gratuity to our first
Correspondent of Paris, who proposes the heads of [Psts].
to encourage all the more his attention, and his vigilance
I shall await My Lord your Orders on this and [on me recommande]
the honor of your benevolent protection, I have that of
calling myself with profound Respect

My Lord

Your very humble and very
Obedient Servant
M. Wolters
