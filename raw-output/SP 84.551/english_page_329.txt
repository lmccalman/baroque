dating 08, To ss. Sailors of 18.
that there were found for a Bark
that should be loaded there with
herbs of [tailles], the 5 others
being drowned, they made
for this Bark, they have had
the Happiness to land this
morning at this Island. They are
18 Spaniards, 17 French,
8 Portuguese, 5 Italians,
one German, and one Piedmontese
to whom God of their Charity
in the Lazaretto, where they do
the quarantine.
