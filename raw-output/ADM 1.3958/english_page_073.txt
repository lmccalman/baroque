We are in Expectation, and even with
Impatience, for what the [Comtés]
of Edelvaren, the Duke of Cumberland, General
Ligonier, the most Renowned [Ables] in
[séconseillés] have to say on the whole.

They have just renewed the [Bris] of
Porto, Ostend, amounting to 600 Thousand
Pounds, upon the Demand of the [Fermiers
d'Abolir], several [tranchises] of
People of the Court and others:

It is a Fortune for the [Agothens]
that the Government seeks Money
and thrift is in place, for without that
the [aymentés] would have been suspended
& at present they are beginning to pay them
up to a certain point.

We hold more by the spirit than
the head, the Deputation of the Greeks which comes
to the Court, is charged with a Medal
struck in the Island, which represents
the Duke of [la trunné déchavant], the
[Bandeur] which crowns the Head of a Mon-
C. Arms of the Island of Corsica) Surrounded
by three fleurs de lys, which have been
added since it belongs
to France.
