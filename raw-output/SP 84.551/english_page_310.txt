Second.

Madrid the 12th. September. 1776.

There are daily
[banters], or Assemblies of
Ministers at Court, and several
Couriers are dispatched Abroad.
The Ambassador of Portugal
does not miss a Turn at Court,
and spends there more than a third very
quietly, during the Conversation
with the King, as if nothing Was.
Moreover, our three [parfins] the
frontiers are quartered there very
quietly, and we [baissons
viennent au beton] our Grains,
without any Hindrance.
The News of the [draft forte],
that the King's Vessel, the
Monarch, of 70. Cannons, had
taken three Sails, without knowing their
Destination,
