in Paris the 7th April 1784


The Rumors of an
approaching War are becoming
more and more credible, and the
time is marked as the 20th of this month,
at which time it is judged that our Armaments
will be complete; one cannot
be exactly informed of what
has just been assured to me, that is, to wit
that Orders have been sent to
Toulon to equip Ships
of the Line, which could not be
ready for a month whatever haste
is made.
There is talk of forming several
Camps, which are called exercise camps,
on the frontiers of the Kingdom,
one in Flanders under the Orders of
Marshal de Lowendahl; another
in Alsace, one on the
frontiers of Italy, and one finally
