Toulon July 7, 1775.

We have in the Roadstead

la Gracieuse, and le Récha, awaiting their Ships
since their Returns; these Ships go from the Roadstead to the hospital
The Roadstead was held here Friday [aller]
[Pénuque] came, and [Vaillé] at the Minister's [celle];
Yesterday appeared [Domaine] the Corvette la Surveillante.
The Construction of the two large
Ships [forts] at [Bregille] has been increased, [à l'auti] of the large Ships
[avons] of [Champandre], to M. Dupont and M. Charmillon,
of M. Pellerue, who have examined their own
[Mentres], so that there will be no [Battement]
in case of need, which [les district ttockel] and [forts ainsi].

We are awaiting the Chevalier de Flandaves
in the Course of this Month.

A few Days ago there arrived here
a third Barque which [fait sa], especially
On Ships [forts] from [tournai], with a
[Pénuque] of 4 Cannons and 4, and [des] 18 other Pounds,
[tant] the Crew [ttaitel fort], he sent them to
Brest of the Thames, to complain about it in London;
This is a Reference to which the [M.cher] at Toulon
owes, and to the honor he had to find thus [des]
Advantages [forts]. The [Meels] of the Chevalier de
Flandaves [font petit] the Enemies, it appears that they
derive certain Advantages from it & their
promised Promotion.
