By 29 Oct

                Madrid the 27th October 1788.

            Since the journey of Mr
Daroche to the Court it is said that he
was called there to be consulted on the
arrangements to be made for a
third attempt against Algiers, from which
the King appears not to want to give up.
    The news that we have here from Oran reports
that twelve thousand Moors both cavalry
and Infantry, had presented themselves
before the fortified town, having an Army
in sight but that they had been
repulsed with loss by our cannon
loaded with grapeshot.
    There continue to arrive many
ships in our ports coming
from America, richly loaded,
among others the ship the Galizienne coming
from Callao de Lima, which made the
journey in four months. By this ship
