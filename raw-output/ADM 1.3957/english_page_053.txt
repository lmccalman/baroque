It is reported from Rennes that the Deputies
who went to the Court, gave account
on the 28th of their Mission, one appointed a
next day to the [Etat gal], this Parliament,
not very content with the King's Response, and
without having regard to it, resumed in Deliberation
the Request of Messieurs de la Chalotais
and ordered that it would be deposited
at the Registry.

Our letters from Corsica, from Toulon, and from
Rome, as well as those, teach us
nothing very interesting yet.

There is talk of a Revolt in Portugal
but we have no Details of it, which
makes one await a Confirmation.
