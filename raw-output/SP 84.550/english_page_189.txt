Paris the 26th April 1776

It is apparently expected
to increase, Upon the News of the Departure
of the Squadron from Brest.
It is decided, that there will be immediately
a Promotion of General Officers,
but especially of Field Marshals
to make Regiments travel,
which are, it is believed, given
by ordinance.
The grand Military Circle will
only appear in the coming Month
as well as the Rest of the Ordinances
The King's Regiment is the Only
one of French Infantry, which remains
at 4 Battalions.
The King will have in recruitment funds
105 Thousand men of Infantry
and 45 Thousand of Cavalry, Dragoons
or Hussars; which will make 250 Thousand
men, which will not cost
1 sol more than at this moment
