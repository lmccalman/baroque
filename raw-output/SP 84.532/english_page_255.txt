The two Battalions of the Regiment
of Picardy, and of Flanders, which were
embarked at Cartagena for
America, [tant] at Cartagena
and are the Orders of [délbergues];
the Battalion of Navarre must, there
[veneste] at [Acigante], and that of
Guadeloupe [restôn] at Cadiz.

An Order has been dispatched from Barcelona,
to make [s.] Thousand [Viarils], which must
be ready within six Weeks; it
is unknown where they will be transported.
