le 16 July

                    Paris the 2nd July 1788.

            Nothing very
interesting is happening at this moment,
neither at the Court, nor in the City.
            It had been hoped that the Spanish
did not want to cede to us the Island of
Marguerite, for the King of Sweden:
They fear smuggling, thus
it is necessary neighbor of the Danes; for then
we would cede to them half of
the Island of St. Martin, which belongs to us.

            It is still believed that the departure
of the King of Sweden is fixed for the 11th
of the current month.
            According to the Letters from Rome
of the 8th of last month, the Plague continues at
Spalatro. The Ambassador of Venice has
warned the Pope that if the cities
