Madrid the 1st September 1789.

They write from St. Ildefonso
that the King had just received by
Express the News that the
Convoy from Havana was in
Sight of Cadiz.

The Infantry Regiments of
Burgos and of the Princess, and Two
others are destined to embark
for America; to go relieve
Four other Regiments, and
bring them back to Europe.

The 8th of last month, the Minister
Plenipotentiary of His
Britannic Majesty presented his Letters
of Credence to the King.
