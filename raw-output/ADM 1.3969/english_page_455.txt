The Fortune that already experience the

It must be hoped, that the Representations
of the King of France, perhaps even
those of some other Neutral Courts,
will prevent all Effusion of Blood.

On Our Side there are no other
Orders, relative to the Army, than those
given to the Munitioners, to compose
than those addressed to the Syndics of
the Parishes, to warn the Militias
who may be incorporated into
the old Corps, to hold themselves ready to
march.

The Courier from Vienna is expected
the Day after Tomorrow, but the one who must bring
the last Resolutions of the Emperor,
is not expected until the 27th or the 28th.
