Brussels the 23rd December 1770.

The News from Paris
informs us of the Exile of the Duke
of Choiseul.

Madame de Choiseul has just
arrived here and She is lodged at
the Hotel of the Prince de la Tour
et Taxis. Saturday She dined at
his Royal Highness's, and Supped
at the Prince of Kaunitz's.

All the Officers in the Service of
France who are on Semester in
this Country, have Orders to return
to their Regiments within the
week.
