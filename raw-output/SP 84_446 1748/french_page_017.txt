My Lord Duke,

I had late last night the following
information given me, that kinloch Mather
in the Isle of Egg has made a contract
with the court of France to furnish Seven hundred
men to recruit the Scotch and Irish Regiments
in the french Service, that a Ship is actually
lying out in one of the Ports of France to fetch
them at the said Ker, and that as a Blind Mather
has letters from his friends in france wrote to
me gentles and that the men are called Black-
Will.
