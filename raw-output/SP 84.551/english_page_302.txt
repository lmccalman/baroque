Rotterdam this 1st of October 1776.

My Lord,

          I have the honor to
send you enclosed the Account of my
Disbursements, for the Payment of
the Secret Correspondence, rising
on last September 29th, amounting
to £2000. Current Money of Holland;
which following the Exchange of this Day,
makes £1588.16.6. Sterling; for
which Sum I will take the
Liberty to draw by the next
Post, through Mr. William Davis
of the Treasury.
          I have the Honor to be with
a profound Respect
                    My Lord,

          Your very humble and
          very Obedient Servant
                    M. Wolters

Earl of Suffolk.
