IX.

INDEPENDENTLY of all the prizes which result from the different chances
above, the Shareholders holding compound tickets, whether of determined Ambes,
or of Ternes, or of Quaternes, or of Quines, shall participate in the drawings of
free Prizes, granted by reason of their stakes, as is explained
in article X hereinafter; & under the conditions stated therein.

X.

1.° EACH ticket of determined Ambe, which shall be composed of a column of
6 numbers & above, up to 20 numbers inclusively; each ticket of
Terne, or of Quaterne, or of Quine, which shall be composed of a column of 10
numbers & above, up to 20 numbers inclusively, may be taken
at the rate of each combination which shall result from the quantity of numbers chosen,
on the basis of Three deniers.

2.° Each ticket of determined Ambe, of Terne, of Quaterne or of Quine,
which shall be composed of a column of 21 numbers & above, up to 40 numbers
inclusively, may be taken at the rate of each combination which shall result
from the quantity of numbers chosen, on the basis of One denier.

3.° Each ticket of determined Ambe, of Terne, of Quaterne or of Quine,
which shall be composed of a column of 51 numbers & above, up to 90 inclusively,
may be taken at the rate of each combination which shall result from the quantity
of numbers chosen, on the basis of One twelfth of a denier. In all cases,
the Treasurer-payer, for the benefit of the fund.
