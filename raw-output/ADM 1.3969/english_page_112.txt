Mr de Cordova has fixed his Departure
for Prinquer, on the 23rd of this Month.
Mr O. Petty will follow him closely, He will take
the same Route on the 28th said.

Until the moment of the arrival
of our rich Convoy which must
restore this Town, our Port
will offer nothing interesting, since to
the addition of the disarming of my
Fleets, except that there come
to anchor in our Bay, some
Vessels of the Squadron of Mr de
Vaudrouil, of which some could
perhaps to Toulon.
