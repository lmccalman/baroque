Vienna the 14. November 1775.

                                        A [Voullée] postilion
of fifteen Days prevented me from writing to you
sooner.
    You will Without Doubt already have heard
speak of the new Machines, of
the invention of the young Count of Hersch,
by the Means of which one must
go up and descend the Rocks in
[favres] Without [Ramres] and Without horses,
and the Trial that was made On the Danube
the 28 October, which perfectly
succeeded; but I had spoken of the
box and of the [Dyalée] in the [pluque]
to a [Marie] well loaded, to
[ennuyer] to [ame] and to Miss Rogues, and
to [manager] it at the banquet of [Rains].
One needs only feeble Men,
who raise it, to hold this
Machine in Movement.

Minister of the King of Prussia, [céguté] a
