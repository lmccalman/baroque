Toulon, the 20th March 1775.

They are going to announce to the
army all that happens there, to make it
known by the Captain to report
the tin and the Arms, and to go inquire in the
towns about the Soldiers who are in our other armies
and the deserters.
Young Coste went between the Roy and Reger
and the Chamber of Commerce of Marseilles, a
part of the Russian fleet was stopped, loaded
to go to [gond], and was going to cross a river
for the wife of Mores Dubois and, continuing the
[Sébastich], and that the Dihonnell and the [Coeur] to
give Account of Conducting them: Messrs. Dubois and
Tolet went for the Vessel, when
they made the Visit to [Batterielle], and
that the Chiefs [élément] a Frenchman,
the [Baterielle] was in his [Econde]: I
respond directly, the Russians arrived
on Board the [Chabet] to demand and visit
the Merchant Vessel, or cries from the [Chabet] to
let no one come on Board, and made them
effectively descend one after the other
followed them and arranged them. The Roy and Reger
demand 500 Thousand livres in compensation
from the Commerce of Marseilles. All the family
Spanish from this River for House
Interest: the man, diamonds; that two
judges are French, she begs me
compose the [Maillons].

Brest.
