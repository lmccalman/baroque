One [synchronique] has misplaced the provisions that are
expected, and the Port is filled with Workers,
who quarrel with one another.

The 3rd Division of the Armament will be of
38 Ships and 8 Frigates, which Mr de Mailhé
Vice Admiral, will command. The 2nd of
19 Ships and 8 Frigates. 4 at Pour-
boeuf for working on the Biscuit,
[talissons] are being made, all the Boats have been chartered
to carry it [Marées] to go
to Bordeaux to fetch Wines and other
Provisions. The Expenses are enormous,
there are a Thousand Workers in the Port;
Sailors arrive from all Sides.

The Zephir and the Hère will go to Sea
in 4 days.

They are arming in Spain, and the Spaniards
are having made at Bayonne 40 Thousand Quintals
of Biscuits.
