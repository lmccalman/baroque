Rotterdam 25th January, 1769

My Lord

The Spanish Correspondent having
drawn upon me for the quarter due the first
of December last, for the account of Sir John
Ducents of Exchange, which according to the
enclosed Note makes £140.13.6. Sterling: for
the Rest of which Sum being for your Lordships
Office, I have only the next Post take the liberty,
to draw for £23.16.6. Sterling, upon William
Ouvrry Esqr of the Treasury.

I have the Honour to be most respectfully

My Lord

your Lordships
most humble and
most Obedient Servant

R.W. Otton.
