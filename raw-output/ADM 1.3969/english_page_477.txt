On the 9th of the current month there arrived at
Cadiz, the frigate named 'La
Mexicaine' coming from Callao de
Lima, from where she had departed on the 18th
of May last, with a rich
cargo, and among other things 904848 strong piastres
and 38940 arrobas of Cocoa, &c.

We are informed here that a
vessel arrived recently at Tangier
has brought the ratification of the peace
between the Emperor and the King of
Morocco.

The King has just appointed
Don Antoine Garroles general commander
of all the maritime forces
that His Majesty wishes to destine to
pursue these barbarians throughout
the entire Mediterranean, Balearic Islands,
and the entire Barbary Coast.
