XV.

WE WISH that in case of appeal, all the costs of transport, of
referral, of execution; even those of the instructions that our Judges
deem necessary, be in all cases at our expense,
without any recourse against the Lords. SO WE GIVE IN
MANDATE to our beloved & faithful the People holding our Court
of Parliament in Paris; that our present Edict they have to have
read, published & registered; & the content therein keep, observe
& execute according to its form & tenor: FOR SUCH IS OUR
PLEASURE; & so that it be a firm & stable thing forever,
we have had placed there our seal. GIVEN at Versailles in
the month of February, the year of grace one thousand seven hundred seventy-six,
& of our reign the fifty-sixth. Signed LOUIS. And
lower, By the King. Signed PHELYPEAUX. Visa DE MAUPÃ‰OU,
