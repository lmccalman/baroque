The King has just confirmed a Council of
Brevets, here is a Marshal, the Government
of Saumur and the Saumurois, object of
20 Thousand Livres of Income.

The news of Charles Clairmontuille,
who sustained these small operations, one of the
Good teach that the English do not suspect
the [Graille], of having found in this Plot,
in favor of what Metua d'Evsson had
planned to have Brest burned.

Our Effects from Rome say, that one has
received the News, that the Emperor has consented
that the Pope give an Order to the Clergy
of Bohemia and Vienna, Portuguese.

The Arrangement is absolutely final
with Poland, that of Spain is
very advanced, there remains only that of
France, which will not have Difficulty
in being concluded, the King does not necessarily
want that she alone Quarrel
with the Pope.

The Heat is excessive for 8 Days;
the thermometer Suffers from it. It is said in the Gazettes,
the Harvests Are Recommencing generally.

Brussels.
