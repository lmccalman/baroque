at Bresches 7 June 1782.

Brigade of Mlle Vaudreuil
entered Baden at 4 O'clock in the after
noon; it is composed of Eight Battalions
that Sieur le Malsergne had
announced to us; they bring back the Regiments
of Bourbonnois, with which after of my
Royal Dame Bourbon, which will have the name
of Auvergne: the Count d'Erlieux of general staff
of the Army &c. Such as Melle Boismonté,
the Prince de Broglie &c.

The Count des Sequins departed for
Versailles two Hours after the Return
of the Squadron.

Mr. Vibert de Breau was Charged
with conducting Three Battalions to Toulon
and the Frigates la Ceres and l'Amazone
escort the Clandagne, and l'Isle de France
which carry to Baden the Regiments
of Guyenne, d'Lyonnois, and de Cambrésis.
The Regiment d'Armagnac came alone
for Battles, it will return with us who
will form the Garrisons of these Islands, their
[des Tailles] who will be escorted by the Kemple.
