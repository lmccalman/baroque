Paris January 24, 1745.

People continue always to
speak of peace, and yet the
negotiations are not very lively.
The idea of a congress is gaining favor
more than ever, they are already laying the
foundations of reconciliation.
It is even said that the Dutch
are ready to make some important
sacrifices. But all of this is
still uncertain; what appears
certain is that war or not, the
Count of Maillebois will command
the army of the States General, it
appears that he will depart in fifteen
days by land, to go
to The Hague. And the permission to
