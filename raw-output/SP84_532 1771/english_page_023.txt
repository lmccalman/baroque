September c. 2. March 1771

March

                                                                                                Mrs. de Castelnaud and
                                                                                                de Maillau
                                                                                                M. Mallet

On the 15th of the 9th Day of the Month of August the year One Thousand
seven Hundred seventy-one, I the Undersigned Curate of this Parish
have interred, after having Celebrated the Service of the Dead in our Church
the Body of Mr. Pierre Louis Pelet, aged seventy-one
years, deceased the 5th of the Current month in this Parish, son of the
late Jean François Pelet and of Catherine Jouve his wife, both
from Montpellier, husband of Marie Marguerite Bosc and son Bernarde
de Castelnau. Were present at his burial Pierre Castelnau, Property Owner
of Cette and of the Parish of St. Nazaire de Ladarez, father of
Marie Marguerite Bosc his widow, and Pierre Rochemaure, resident of this
Parish, Cousins of the Deceased through their wives; Who
have signed with me on said day and year as above according to our authority, and the witnesses.
Rochemaure Father and de la Bosc his Wife, have had recorded in their absence
presence of Jean François Bonel and Pierre Maillard witnesses

Pierre Castelnaud    Jean françois bonel
J. Bosc          Pierre Maillard
                      Mallet
