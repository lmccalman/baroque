Madrid the 7th April 1782.

The King has appointed Don
Francois Ballier, Squadron Commander, as
His Minister of the Navy in the Place
of the late Mr de Castejon.

Our new Patriarch of the
Indies, died here last Thursday.

The King has ordered a general
Reform throughout all His Infantry
of 24 Men per Company.

A Battalion of Walloon Guards
arrived Three Days ago at the Camp
of St Roch; in our Vicinity,
and all the others are in full
march to their respective destination.

The Duke of Crillon is expected
shortly at Court. He has given
a Celebration to General Elliot, &c, at
the Camp of St Roch.

The Count d'Estaing has been here
for Five Days, it is claimed that he
is sent regarding Peace.
