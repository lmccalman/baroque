Toulon, October 28, 1775.

                                                The [Comite] of
[Ryche] Ships arrived here on the 28th;
the next day he held the Review
of the Regiments of Languedoc, and
of Aquitaine; he departed on the 30th.
    The same Day two Battalions
of the Artois Regiment arrived here;
on the 30th the two others arrived
there also.
    The same Day two Battalions
of Aquitaine left for
Montpellier, and the two others
leave today.
    The Sarre Regiment will arrive
on November 18th; it will be charged with
the Service of the Port.
    No News of our Navy.


                                                Brest.
