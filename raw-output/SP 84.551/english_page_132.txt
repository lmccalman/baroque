Second
economies

Madrid the 22 July 1776

Since my latest
orders were given, to conduct
general Assemblies throughout the Kingdom,
and by Provinces, to complete
all the Provincial Regiments
of Militia. To draw the Soldiers already
made, to complete the Infantry.
They have adopted the Means, to avoid
the Difficulties, of which the [Jeme Roms]
is ordered in [Payoues].

They have apprehended at Cordoba and [Bao Danyons]
from different Regiments, with all
their Equipment, to embark
for Buenos Aires, and the [Jornez]
[monds]; jointly with Mr.
[Balu etdans complextes], and for this
transport has put [D'Emblange] by very
[Estantes] in [Doyennes], both at
Cadiz, and at Malaga.

Mos
