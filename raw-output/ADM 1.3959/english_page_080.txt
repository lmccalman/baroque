Brussels the 23rd September 1772.

Here one has received some News
which informs us of an accord
between the Royal and the Ottoman Porte, and the
Troops that were taken between the Emperor, the King
of Prussia, and the Czarina, concerning the Partition of a
Part of Poland. One makes this treaty
[inde] Ryswick to be almost assured,
that this Stephen was sending one towards the epoch
of 24 Thousand Men to enter into
Poland. The Politicians ask there,
if the Poles, have the [ordes secourrables],
so that they are acceptable to them to oppose
Men as formidable as those that
the three Powers are [forteriantes] in their
Kingdoms, but Poland in seeing homage
this one this Stephen in Poland, [horste] what
to do in obtaining this Prince On the throne of Poland? in
all seeing that by the [fortunes] News,
he does not appear to have you to communicate them.
