14 y Decembr M 2

                            Paris the 7th December 1770:

                    The Council Letters from the
last post announce to me that they will finally put
in order the Bridge Buildings.
    They are in the project of determining to Mr de
Soulens, to go there himself following the Orders
of the Council, Mr de Passy, Architect of the Pont neuf
and the King have induced him to offer his services to my city,
they await from the two Projects that which concerns the two
establishments.
    The ambassador of France is still in Lisbon,
awaiting the Finance Council of France has
that the works of Intrigues the of Court.
    They confirm fortification in Paris or
Castellany of Origny, almost entirely deceased
Works.
    They say Mr. Perret, current Architect of the Buildings of the King here is appointed to the Intendancy
of Burgundy.
    They make the Opening of the 4th, that there departed
2/3 of the Workers for Brest, of which two
barges from Bordeaux; one of officers and the
fourth of Construction Wood.
    They think that the Directors of the Posts
need their Regulation. But our
Contractors will make particular ones that they endeavor
to encourage.
    The [Maraides] carry [bois Orles] to Paris
Brest, or make a free and leave all
the Properties for an upcoming Sum.

                                                    P.S.
