Every Day General Officers arrive
at the Court, both French
and Spanish.

The King has just named Don
Louis de Cordova Captain-General
of the Navy, and Don Antoine Barcelo,
Lieutenant-General, and also Two
or Three Lieutenant-Generals.

The new Tariff for all
our Customs mentioned in my
previous (letter), does not yet appear;
but one knows that the Duties for
all Foreign Merchandise
are exorbitant, which makes neces-
all Commerce, and will occasion
infinite Complaints.
