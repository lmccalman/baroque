Rotterdam June 8th 1833

My Lord Duke

this brings your grace my most humble
thanks for obtaining me his Majesty's leave
for my journey to you.

I shall leave here in orders carry home these
the affairs which I have the honour to be
entrusted with will be concluded with hers
at home, and I shall not they at your convenience
longer than is necessary to perform and explain
my errand. I have the honour to be most
respectfully

My Lord Duke

your Grace's
most humble & most
obedient Servant
R.D. Otrery
