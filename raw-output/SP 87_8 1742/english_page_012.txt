                                        Response

They are very grateful to me for it and
The Ship in a few days they are stowing the
goods that they were beginning to Embark
they completely ceased Embarking today
because the wind has blown to the North and that
the weather was threatening but however the
sailors came near our Boats and
promised to embark our goods
tomorrow if the weather is fine, one goes from our house,
and one returned right away, we Sent
our belongings to the carpenter, who
kept them, this night, Mister the gentleman
slept at the house at Mary's, Mary
has not yet Returned, we informed the
men who must take us tomorrow
if the weather is fine.
