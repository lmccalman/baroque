                        Toulon the 5th January 1770.

        [Deux qu'il's n'est desir]
That very Powerful Illustrious Lords Mabon,
Bignen, letrand the Marquis of St Etienne, we
had nothing [annové], but if it please that the
Spanish are at home in Bohonmont,
without our goats nor geese, nor [dans nus], and with,
the fleet in part to arrive at the forts
On the Coasts of Corsica.

                        Brest the 2nd January 1770.

                                    The Curious the

Johanne arrived in good State from the Isle of Ré
Rome the 22nd in the evening, the [tonnere seon allanta]
and in passing was Swept. St Raphael the
Sans Rolleta, also on arriving at the Isle of
Ré one sends him to [distr]. It must be believed that
the Curious brought back all the officers in
good Health, [d'un louifa] the Chevalier des
Roches in good health, as well as Mr Chany,
who commands the Ambulante, beautiful and
good frigate, excellently trellised.
