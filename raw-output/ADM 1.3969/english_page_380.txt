14 16 July

Paris the 5th July 1781

Monsieur Monmenie requests
resignation, until now there is
nothing decided for Health. Monsieur de
Calonne awaiting them, he will be able
to keep the controller general,
as well as Monsieur de Machault.

The principal articles of our
treaty of alliance with Holland are
agreed upon, but they will not be signed
until after the final arrangement of the
difficulties she has with the Emperor.
There are people who claim that this
treaty will be more burdensome than profitable
for us; according to them, we will guarantee
to the Dutch all their Possessions,
both in Europe and in the two
Indies. On their side they commit
to furnish us, in case of
maritime war, ships of the line and
