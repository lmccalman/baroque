The Testament of the Prince of Conti-
nent, established according to his Mother three
Days before dying, here it is:
I institute the Count of la Marche
my only son, my Universal
Legatee: I commend to him
my Two natural sons his brothers;
To all my Household, I want that
all, be treated as in my
Lifetime.
They burned at the opening of his
Body, the [Cortes] and water in the Chest,
and in the Stomach, the ulcerated Lungs,
and a Deposit of Pus in the Kidneys.
