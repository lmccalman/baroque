149.
                                          13. 19 March N°1

                    Madrid the 5th February 1715.

                         They write to me from Ferrol:
that they are drawing out the 50 [Chefs faux] of the Line, and
9 Frigates which are ready, they embark
4 men of Infantry of 20 Cannons;
and that all the Armaments
and War Equipages remain in Spain;
but one hears no news yet
from them: which makes us fear
to occasion Speculations.
    The [Payement] of Artillery which had
been taken from Ferrol for America
[que de Louis avoit la Ramette de la
Constitution de Paix], has returned to
said Port, having been dismantled
by the Catalan [Banque] which had
repatriated there for this purpose.
    [Nous] officers have embarked at
Ferrol to make it [taller] strong
at Nice the fund & the Magazine of Troops
for the Constitution of said Islands.
    They write to me from Cadiz, that they had
[petté] a Signal at [S'en d'vis], to
go near the Isle of [Trekland], one
[ne s'confirme que trois les ouvrage a
                                                   Sont]
