First.

At [lile le fonse] the 24th July 1776.

It appears that we
are not yet in agreement with
Portugal, nor the great Preparations
that the latter makes [sais] always, as much
by having troops approved
at the borders of Portugal, as
by sending them to Buenos Aires, according to
the [Billimons] at Paris of the [Belies] to
depart [deli] on the 8th and 18th of next month,
with the Regiments.

One [tirace] that even from Seville
to Cadix, [Barclots], and Cannons
in quantity, and one makes a great
Number of Vests and Breeches of
cloth for the Soldiers.

on the Coasts of Portugal, and the Motte
