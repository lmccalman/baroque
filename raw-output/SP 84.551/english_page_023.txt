Les Soines made a Voyage [onde] &c &c
of Gramois; Enrichet Kelly, she in...
a Combat [à foi tant] of another a
squadron of the Hauttes, composed
of three [Belas] of 56. to 40. Cannons
and thirty other Vessels; they
[sont économes], for 14.
Hours, Les Soines took only one of them;
the Hauttes must have [à deux lignes].
Upon her Arrival at Mahé, [il m'a voir]
French, on the Coasts of Malabar,
she found this Place besieged
by 16. Thousand Men of the Country;
she had orders to put [?]
Crew ashore, which increased
the Garrison by 200. Men;
They forced the Enemies to
withdraw with Loss of 19, to 14 Hundred
Men; we lost
forty three.
