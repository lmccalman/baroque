The King has just appointed Don
Diego Navarro, Field Marshal,
as Governor of Tarragona;
to Command in the Generalship
of Havana; in Place of a
Marquis de la Torre, who is returning
to Europe. This Mr. Navarro is
an excellent Officer, but
he is over 70 Years old.
