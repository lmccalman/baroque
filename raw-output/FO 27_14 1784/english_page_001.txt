Dunkirk the 15th January 1789.

The admiralty [lege] of the Current month,
an Engineer, to measure the
ports, and a Draining at the Port;
they are not doing the [Ravinements] that are
needed to build Houses, or to
enlarge the Basin.

There are no other Constructions
here than three small Launches,
that one makes near the Port of the Citadel.

They are arming Two Vessels
for New England.
For Two Months there have been
Seven Bankruptcies here, among others
the House of Tauris, which was
the strongest Shipowner during
the War. They have raised his
bankruptcy debts to Two Million
One Hundred Thousand francs.
