N.° 74.

Paris the 9th September 1775.

It is reported from Bordeaux,
that the frigates that had been sent back to America,
one from St. Pierre, to go relieve another
similar one St. ombré, which have gathered all Summer
in the Archipelago against the barbarians.

The Letter in German Language, which the
Commander Wasfa delivered to Mr. Durand
of Suinse during his Adventure, contained
His Condemnation; it was translated in
Paris, it states, that there is a general Law
for all Nations to defend themselves
when one is attacked, they reproached him
for not having entered into the small
Vessels of the Province; that the
Commander is not at all customary to their
belonging, nor to the Greeks and that he
dishonors it, that consequently it is not
the Russian Flag that failed the
French Flag. One awaits a
clearer Response from the Court of
Petersburg.

One awaits today the Count of the
brigands of rebels of Toulouse, it is he
Madame Donide, who bring the first
News of the Success of their Operations. One
