Mr de Ferbins was transferred from
L'orient to the Château de Pont St Esprit,
where he was imprisoned.

Mr de Maurville was likewise conducted
to the Isle of Ré. The conduct
of these two officers will be examined after
the return of our army from Italy.

Mr de La Comte was driven out by the
Lieutenant de Puissance, after having
been severely mistreated.

It is said that it was Mr de Frommelien
who was at the head of the party against
Mr de Suffren, to break the
operations.

There is no fear for the fate
of Mr de Villars, for he fought well
for two hours.

The Duke of Manchester was
very graciously received last
Tuesday at Court, by the King, and
the Royal Family.

The Prince de Marialenche is
still ill.

Madrid
