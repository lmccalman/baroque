Rotterdam January 8th 1769

Sir-


I have received the honour of your favour
of the 1st instant, upon the Subject of the
madrid correspondent, than which you
mention whose worth some prix ago I
have never received.
     In consequence, a Lord Sandwich orders
I shall by next thursday's post write to
the correspondent, that he is to be continued
for Six Month from the end of this year
which is the 1st of March. I shall not fail
