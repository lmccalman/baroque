My Reports from Barcelona of the 21st May
last state, that all is in
Security there; that they are planning 3 Thousand
Campaign tents, a train
of considerable Artillery, and all the
necessaries for the Campaign.

Three Regiments of Cavalry
namely, that of Roques, that of
the Elephant, and another, are in
full March for the borders
of Portugal; and the Corps of
Royal Carabiniers, has orders to
hold itself ready to march.

My latest News from
Cadix announce to me on the 10th, that this
last May there had arrived
the two Warships
the St. Peter of Altantera, and The St.
Julian departed from Callao of Lima
the 3rd December last. Their
Cargo joined to that, and for the
numbers, amounts to nearly 2 Millions
