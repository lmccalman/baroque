Jaques Vibert jeune

By the first Courier arrived this day from Canada, we received the news that about four to five thousand Savages had assembled to come fall upon our frontiers and that they had laid at the departure from Montreal some retreats of Huron. The day of the discovery of Holy Cross of the Royale forty Frenchmen at Montreal near the onion island, received the new Crown in a Royal field bearing the Inscription Imperii novi fines in India Occidentali a Carolis mense, in nomine Franciae a Ludovico Rege facta hac erecta 6.
