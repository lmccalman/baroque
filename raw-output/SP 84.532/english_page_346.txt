Our Minister had proposed against the
Gazettes of Holland, which permit themselves
Insolences against him; & which he has justified
in France, & then again quite recently
from the Palace. He puts himself on his side in the
to see appear an Ordinance which
forbids the Publicity of these Gazettes
in the Kingdom. The Chancellery
has made a Collection of the barbs
launched by them.

Our Letters from Rome which one sees [sties] of
a long discourse that on the 4th of March the Pope
was to make to the Cardinals; and [c']
to declare many things in his
Consistory. One awaits the News of it.

                    Brussels the 25th March 1775.

                    A Private individual has
undertaken to transport 4,000 Horses
for the Artillery train.

All the Governor Officers
[supplient le pont] to depart.

The Duke of Ursel departs today
for Paris, but one ignores the object
of his Journey.
