Paris the 16th February 1784.

Count Auguste de
la Marck Proprietor of a
Regiment of his Name arrived
the evening before yesterday at Versailles,
He brings the Dispatches from India,
He made his Crossing in Five Months
by the Frigate the Hermione which
anchored at La Rochelle and despite
the harshness of the Season, He only
took Fifty Three Hours
to come from Rochefort to
Versailles. We will have Tomorrow
the Details of the latest Affairs
that took place in India.

One writes from Marseille dated
the 6th of the Current [month], [a qui puisse].

Lez
