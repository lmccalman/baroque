There is a very great Dispute between the
first of the Blood and the Ambassadors
on the Ranks they claim at the
Ceremony of the Marriage of the Dauphin;
the King must settle this Difference.

The Duke of Bourbon will depart to go
travel, for 18 Months, and Madame
la Baume has returned to the Convent;
this Prince is only 14 Years old, he is tall
but weak, and is not formed. Madame
the Duchess of Bourbon is 20 Years old, she
promises less [atteinte] than him.

The Count d'Orpheu son and sole
Successor of the Duke de Bois lay, the sole title
with the Appointments in Russia:
the First of [So:] Counselor of State which will
become vacant, and a Regiment to serve him
well: The Marquis de Beringhen First
Equerry, the second man of the Kingdom;
Has the Marquisate of la Chere, Colonel
of his Guards.
