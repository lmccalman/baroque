Versailles the 23rd October 1776.

The Ministers of
War, Foreign Affairs,
the Navy, Manufactures
at this moment [j'avons je vous la
même, en nom] as they,
[habitant] not to do it,
I think that the News that
interests you, that the Portuguese have [confis-
culé] Hostilities in Brazil, could
overturn the peaceful
Negotiations, and bring back the Courts
of Madrid and of Lisbon, to an
accommodation. Meanwhile
our Ministers [mondent] gratitude
for the Ardor, Prudence, and
Promptitude, with which
they put our forces in a State
of War, and Mr. de Sartine has
[retenu] distinguished himself in this in his
Department.                    If
