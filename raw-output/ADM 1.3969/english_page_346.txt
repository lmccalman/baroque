27 29 March 1789.

Paris the 19th March 1784.

The Frigate Cleopatra
arrived at Brest, reports that it has taken
the lead of the Squadron of M. Jaffron,
which must be currently very near
Toulon; [?] the Winds have not
hindered its Progress.

The Letters from Brest report
that the Héropaths came to confer
with the Héros joli, commanded by M. de
Suffren, which must put in at Toulon.
On the 4th of January the Héros signaled to it
that it could dispense with It
& take the lead.

M. de Jaffron left [Ivoir de feu]
Vessels at Isle de France, and Two
at the Cape of Good Hope.

the King of Morocco. This News
