Brussels the 10th December 1770

They have distributed to the
German Regiments who are
Quartered in the Low Countries, their
tents and their [Permites], with Order
to hold themselves ready to depart.

His Royal Highness has moreover ordered
O. De Noyer and the Captains of the
Corps to not grant any
Semester, to put the Uniform
of the Soldier in Order, and to distribute
to each man a Pair of Shoes
of 15 %
