Madame the Dauphine and Madame
accompanied the King last Monday
to the great Hunt of St Hubert, &
in the Evening His Majesty Supped at his grand
Couvert; the next day the Dauphin
was purged, his State is always
the same. He is very well, and one
testifies, one always feigns between
Fear and Hope, but one
fears more than one hopes.

Mr the Controller General
presented to the Council at Fontainebleau
a Plan, to establish in Paris and
elsewhere Monts de Piété; one does not
yet know if this will be received.
