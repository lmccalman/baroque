Nothing will be said again about our
new Ministers of finance.
The News from our Colonies continues
to announce on their Part, a very
great Thirst and with the [Infatigons]
of which the vessels arrive there in
quantity, Bringing many
things they exchange for others, also
The object of our great Commerce with
our Colonies with France, in
Munitions, Arms, &c. The Ministry
continues to protest, that they have
renewed the strongest Prohibitions
of such Exportations.

It is believed, that they will make the Count of
St. Germain Marshal of France
to persuade him to retire
They also speak, of giving the Baton
to Mr. de Maillebois, an Appearance
of this General officer at the Court
which had the time [defait], announces it, and before
that [Durien] is not expired;
