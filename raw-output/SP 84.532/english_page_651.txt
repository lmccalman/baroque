    Our Letters from Rome of the 22nd, have brought
nothing interesting.
    Those from Civita Vecchia of the 28th announce
that they hang on the Scaffold for theft for
murder some Bandits.
    Our Ports are in the greatest Calm

                        Brussels June 10, 1771.

                                    Several Women
who had put themselves out of life, & are no longer so for young
men.
    The ferocious Beasts have destroyed & put
in the Peace. them, & they are not the young
departed at the first order.
    There is an Ordinance, that all
children must furnish their institution
and an exact state of the Goods they have acquired
since their institution, under Pain of
Confiscation of all their Goods. I am not
surprised at all that the Consistories
who have nothing will conform to it
between now and the 12th of July next.
