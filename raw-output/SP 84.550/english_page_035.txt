will be of age, to fill a

The Health of the Princes Mr. de

Mlle Malinthe has had a
Bleeding, but which cannot
have any troublesome Consequence.

A Fire has Burned, 2 and 3 [me]
Houses of the late Mr the Duke at the
Fountains, and one is not so Rejoiced
