Genoa the 11th August 1776.

One counts this Genoese,
for the Account of a Private Individual,
Two Brigs, of the Weight of 4 Thousand
Quintals. Two others of 2 Thousand,
One of 3 Thousand, and Two bastards
of a Thousand Quintals each one.

At Genoa the 15th left the Shipyard
A Brig of 3 Thousand Quintals.
A Two bastards of 1200 Quintals.

At Livorno, three Vessels,
of 6 Thousand Quintals.

At Sestri one has launched to the Water a
Brig of 5 Thousand Quintals;
this Vessel is pierced for 9
12 Pieces of Cannon, and belongs
to a Wheat Merchant.

The Brig which was constructed
in our Shipyard, has been
ceded by a Wine
Merchant.
