The Ball of [Couterie Vanilles] arrived [Mera]
brought him the News of the Birth of
Madame the Dauphiness, & Peace with
                  Turkey the 7th & 8th May. In the evening they
Danced Hall & [Comlique] to reach &
receive this Princess. Gentlemen this Count
will await her on the 10th in the evening & the [Menton].

We have received many Letters from Constantinople
of the 5th April, [en Laivant] all the Details of what
was happening in the World, they are very fine
confirmations the same as the preceding Couriers
had brought us the Truces; there is
great Ostentation of 33 large Warships, which
were to set Sail on the 31st or 32nd to go
observe the Squadron of the Russians, while waiting
for 30 others to come forth to attack
the Russians: One apparently awaits to see if these are
the Vessels [logy]; the Mines at Constantinople
are Dismissing all the Greeks.
