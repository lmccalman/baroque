We have always proposed to restore you
FOR THESE CAUSES & others moving Us thereto: We,
with the advice of our Council, & of our certain knowledge,
full power & Royal authority, have said,
declared & ordained; & by these Presents signed
by our hand, do say, declare & ordain;
wish & it pleases us, that all prosecutions, decrees
& proceedings which might have been made, &
all Rulings, Sentences or Judgments which might
have been rendered since the sixteenth day of December
until this day against Ecclesiastics on the occasion
of the recent divisions, remain without any consequence
& without any effect; consequently that those against
whom the said Rulings, proceedings might have been made, &
the said Rulings, Sentences or Judgments rendered, re-
enter, by virtue of the Presents, into their state & func-
tions. WE THEREFORE GIVE IN COMMAND to our beloved
& faithful Counselors, the People holding our Court of
Parliament in Paris, that these Presents they are to cause
to be read, published & registered, & the content therein
to be kept, observed & executed according to the form & te-
nor: FOR such is our pleasure; in witness whereof
We have caused our seal to be affixed to these said Presents.
GIVEN at Marly the fifteenth day of the month of June,
the year of grace one thousand seven hundred seventy-one, & of our

reign the fifty-sixth. Signed, LOUIS. And below:
By the King, PHELYPEAUX. And sealed with the great
seal of yellow wax.

Registered, heard, the King's Procurator General
requesting, to be executed according to its form & tenor;
& Collated Copies sent to the Bailiwicks & Sene-
schalsies of the Jurisdiction, to be read, published &
registered therein; enjoined upon the Substitutes of the King's Pro-
curator General to see to it, & to certify the Court
within the month; & Collated Copy likewise sent
to the Suffragan Bishops, to be read, pub-
lished & registered, in conformity with the Edict of the month of
February last, following the Ruling of this day. In Paris,
in Parliament, all Chambers assembled, the nine-
teenth of June one thousand seven hundred seventy-one.

                          Signed VANDIVE.
