It is claimed that a Courier dispatched, by
the [Gour], arrived at Rouen last Friday,
that the Chambers were assembled there that
Day; but one does not know either why,
or what the Result was.

Those who do not want to admit, that the
Negotiation for the Return of the Parliament
is underway, claim on the contrary,
that the Chancellor is very Seriously
occupied with constructing a new one.
What is Certain, is that the Parlements
of Douay and of Pau, which he had founded
to have them preferred here, have refused
his Entreaties.
