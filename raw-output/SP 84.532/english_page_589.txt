   The Marquis de Monteynaud has just
issued an Ordinance, by
which he gradually increases
the Soldier's Pay, according to
Seniority in Service; he grants them
a Mark of Distinction, which will
make them equivalent to those of
St. Louis... The order for this
honorific Plaque, it may be acquired,
but for the Regent, by seniority
of Service only; which has been
for a very long time seemingly
impossible, to provide for this
Increase in Expense.
   This ordinance may Serve
to wait until more fortunate times.
