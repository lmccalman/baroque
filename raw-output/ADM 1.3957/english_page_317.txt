What we saw of the Turks and the
Russians, is so contradictory that one
cannot establish a good [vians] Judgment.
We always have the finest weather
possible, but the Heats are
exceptional. They are doing the Harvests
everywhere, the Price of Wheat is already a
little diminished.


Brussels the 18. August 1770


The Disease of the large-
Cattle continues to manifest itself in
several Places of Brabant; and
[dis] that there is a sick Beast in
a Stable, the [Lanssier] kills all of them, and
receives from the Magistrate of the Place 28 florins
for each Beast thus killed.
