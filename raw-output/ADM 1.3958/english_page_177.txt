                                                           17 26 avril

                    Paris le 29. Avril 1773.

                                        They write to me from London
from the SS. that the Brigades will not be provided
before the peace while awaiting the Orders to
put at Sd [Verselle]. Nothing on the other Places.

    The King permits me Versailles only to go
to the Hunt, the Circumstance of the Offices and its not
the Pickets, with the first time of our
Generals with the [Chems baes].

    The Duke of Aiguillon should write on behalf
[Monde] become Minister of Foreign Affairs, but the King wants to be his Private
sold the affairs entirely stupid in this part,
to have even the Responses.

    The King has presently had the [Regibas] depart to the
[Pards] and, he has had [lofin] the [Ponte] to bind
his Princes, that the [baffre], [Jonheam] not
having complied with the Orders.

    The [Defline] depart my Princes, from
depart and the affair general complaint of the
Little Subordination and negligence [entendre]
of one the voyages to refresh their
[bates dutus]. They will not be sent.

    Twenty Bridges have carried the orders in liberty
a Chief of batteries to Forbid Two of them,
I have to ladies Corps, and the [Pauvreieur].
is three Town, [espetissmes] of the Generals of the Corps;
the King does not want to be [vanie] for the Two
affairs of the batteries, the King wants a good one.
