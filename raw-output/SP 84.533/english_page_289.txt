Moreck the 30th October 1771.

The Brigades la Gloire
set Sail for St. Domingue the 9th of this
[cas] she was obliged to return to
port due to strong Winds; she de-
parted the [Tour] of October, with the Sailors
necessary for the [Deservement]
of the supplies.

L'Amphitride, and la Zephir, will depart
around the 15th of next [month]; this last
Vessel carries Mr. Searles, new
Intendant of Guadeloupe.

The Fleets la Felice, and la Barbiere
will not depart until fifteen days after the
frigates: they will carry Provisions
and Munitions to St. Domingue
and to Martinique.

Mr. de la Valiere Governor of
Martinique, will depart [connorrement avt]
to St. Domingue, and he will be replaced
by the Count of Dargelas, [envole] of
the March of the Dauphin.
