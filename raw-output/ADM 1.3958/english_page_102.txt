A few days ago, that of Besançon
charged with Remonstrances during the
same Object. It would like to prove
that the King is wrong, and that the former
[Polticulté], has only done what it should
do. The King responds to no
Letters and does not return them. That is
where affairs stand, and there they are in
the Parlement of Paris, without the Response.
One wishes to hear from them in the Province
only a Letter, that a Parlement, like
that of Besançon to the King.

These Two and More than all assembled
