                                                    R 29 April 1761

                        Paris the 16th March 1772.

                              [OVAL SEAL]

                                            The famous Bretonniere
for the Navy for the printing presses, it
contains 39 Articles. The Officers, that is
to say the Master Printers will be
in the School of Evolution, legally
recognized, in Report of the Chiefs
and School. There will be a similar
School each Year, it will be the turn
of the Port of Toulon next year.

They will augment the Proviseur
of the King's Rochelle, with Cannons,
of 16 inches of ball, 400 men from
Rochefort, for the Guard of the Port.

Until the 8th of June, time at which the
two Regiments of Marine will be
formed, then it will be Rochefort who will
have the Port of Rochefort guarded.

They will bring from all our Colonies
the regular troops who will be there, they will
be replaced with troops of the
Marine.
