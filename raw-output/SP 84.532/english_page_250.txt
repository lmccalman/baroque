One does not know precisely the Object
of this [denne Surbee], but here are the
most Political Conjectures:

The Emperor, in concert with the King of
Prussia, would share a Part of
Poland between Them. Then would leave
to the King only a Part of this Kingdom,
and a weakened Authority.

Others claim that the Project is
to transfer the Commerce of Poland
to the Duke of Saxe-Teschen.

One is not in agreement on the Part
that the Prussians will have in these
arrangements.
