At the Moment that we offer
our Mediation, we united the King
ourselves to Restore Peace between
our Family with Portugal;
and it is the King of Spain, who has
been willing, to take charge of settling
a Dispute, of which one barely speaks
here, but which appears quite
important in Lisbon, to
send the former [Envoyeur] of
the Ambassador of Portugal
in order to see it soon terminated.
   Some new Dispositions
made to the Edict of the Loan, have
delayed the Sending to Parliament:
it will be presented there Tomorrow, and we
shall have it this Week. It is
as we have said, of 125. Millions,
repayable in 25. Years.
