The three Intégrales for America,
commanded by Messieurs Beauffier,
Dones, and Vanier, and the [Camps Bréves
pompant], they are in Port, and
only await Wind, [il n'en
qu'après, ou du contraire].

La Renommée is ready in time,
but has no orders, she will join
the Squadron, to carry orders there,
and from there, an unknown Mission.

Nothing has been decided by our
Intendant, it is still believed that
it will be M. de la Porte, and that M.
Manthais will be made Intendant
of Toulon.

There are still several armed Vessels
there.

Yesterday we learned from the Gazette
[du Ventre d'Augure], dated the
[troye? manyné?], at Caure of the
Illness.
