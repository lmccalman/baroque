Private

Rotterdam, 5th January 1775.

My Lord

Altho' the Vessels is quite at Ease,
I should be glad that it is not improper Sale
but my Letters may be in time for
the Days Packet Boat, and I have
in Consequence sent them off.

I have the honour to be most Respectfully
My Lord

your Lordships
most humble and
most Obedient Servant,

R: Wolters.


Rt. Honble Earl of Sandwich.
