The Partisans of Mr. O. Reilly
claim, that shortly this high [fonale]
will be named Vice-Roy of Mexico,
and that Mr. de Buccareley, who is it
currently, will come to replace him
at the Post of Ste. Marie.

The Government of Ostalrich
in Catalonia, has been given to Mr.
de Villers, Captain in the Walloon
Guards.
