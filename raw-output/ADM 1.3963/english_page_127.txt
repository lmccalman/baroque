34.

Orders to Perrolle, to keep ready there
all the [Cheifzeaux] of the King, for
three years at least; and that in the
meantime besides the exercise of [Cellix]
in town that one will make fall all tombs
of 15 Christians furnished, to
count the frequent Desertions.

Order [florille] for the lower Court:
and departed the 9th of the Current, for the
Destination.

Three Letters from Alicante of the 28th of the
Current, announce to me, that a
Dutch Merchant Vessel
which had just entered there, coming from the
North, had encountered by Cape
St Vincent, the Squadron from Brest:
after Cape Gata, 4 French Warships
from Toulon:
all making towards the Strait.
