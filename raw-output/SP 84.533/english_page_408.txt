Baron de Riedeslèm, formerly actual Privy Councillor of His Imperial and Royal Majesty, and First Councillor of the Chamber of the Court, and of the State Chancellery for Foreign Affairs. And finally the Privy Councillor Baron de Creutz, and the Councillor of the Superior Court Mr. de Lofter, have been named Members of the State Council.

Next was [supposé la vice-devise]: Mr. d'Hommond, Ambassador of His Britannic Majesty, had Frederick of the Empress Regnant, and the Count de Storkheim, Minister of the Duke of Württemberg, had Audience of the Emperor.

On the 18th in the Afternoon the Duke of Saxe-Teschen and Madam his Spouse arrived at this Pressburg in this City.

Today in the Afternoon Mr. d'Hedérique brother de Carlowitz in [Courtle], who has Sojourned here for several Months, has departed for His Residence.
