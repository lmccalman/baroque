agreement with the said Sr de Voisin for a covered chaise with two Horses for
the price of fifteen livres or twenty sols per league in a time when money
was very rare in the country (following the resolutions taken with the said Sr de
Fay my brother the Saturday 25th 7ber 1722) We set out on the road on Sunday
twenty six September one thousand seven hundred twenty two the said Sr de Fay my brother on horseback
and me in the said chaise That sufficed according to the resolutions and the weather treated
us all the better during the journey. On Sunday having left a bit too late or after Midday
we only went to sleep at Beaucaire distant from this place by about two small
leagues. On Monday twenty seven 7ber we crossed the Rhone to go to Tarascon
and from there to Orgon where we slept that evening. On Tuesday twenty eight 7ber we
departed from Orgon and went to sleep at Aix en Provence distant from the said Orgon by seven
to eight leagues. On Wednesday twenty nine 7ber we departed from Aix and went
to sleep at Brignolles distant from the said Aix by eight or nine leagues. On Thursday thirtieth
7ber we departed from Brignolles and went to sleep at Muy distant from the said Brignolles
by seven to eight leagues. On Friday first 8ber one thousand seven hundred twenty two we
