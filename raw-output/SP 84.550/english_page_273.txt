Madrid the 20. May 1776.

My Advices from Cadiz
of the 10th of the Current (month) arrived there by the
same as the previous ones, with
the Vessels; however they appear
well founded, probable, that there would
be nothing yet decided, the Bills of Engl:
having had no effect, judging them
insupportable to the Nation, she wants
to obtain with her [sa] of the peace from France,
who does not want War at all, she
[va seroit] with the Rebellion, I believe of it
badly begun the [fils] that there is
a change of Minister. [d'ame]
