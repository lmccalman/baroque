It is supposed, that through the Credit
of the Regency, and others who are very
fortunate, they will obtain the said
Ministry.

There is talk again of a
Marriage of the King of Poland
with Archduchess Elizabeth,
but for that it would be
necessary to have the consent of
the Empress of Russia, and
who could have Reasons
to oppose it.

One promises many
Consequences of the artillery [Dictae]
general of Poland; The
civil Confederation, formed
under the Auspices of the King
appears to have averted all
the troubles, so that, it
is no longer necessary to
bring in a Corps of
Troops
