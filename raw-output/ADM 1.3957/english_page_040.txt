Brussels, January 25, 1777

                    The Count of Cobentzel the
to [la Hievre] my [haut Deurger].
   There is a rumor circulating here that a large Body
of Russians has been beaten on the frontiers of
Poland by the Turks.
   The Duke of Ursel has left for Paris
with Letters for the Minister of the
Court of Vienna, which arrived here by a
Courier from Vienna.
   It is assured [d'un Colleque] the King of Prussia
has left suddenly for Silesia,
and that several Imperial Regiments
have orders to approach Poland.
