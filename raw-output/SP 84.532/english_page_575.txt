Madrid le... May 1785.

                                    The situation in
which Spain currently finds itself [nous
princepaaux] Ports of Italy, if there are, we must
engage several Algerian Corsairs,
not having the means to Renew
to communicate to us, which [menache]
your attention.

The [Belles] of the high seas and of Galicia
are [obojines] in the British navy.
I am informed that all the arrangements
are made to not disarm at all,
until we have the Ships, that this
be done in England.

The Merchant community of Cadiz [revive] very
far from content with the Establishment
of the [Canasto], which absorbs all the Rights,
[s'entretenois] in this Kingdom: but which
obliges them to pay a Duty of one
piastre as the Equivalent, by reason of
the extent of their Commerce. This
Establishment extends today over the
lands of the [Indiens], [d'un des Particuliers],
Which considerably increases
the Revenues of the Crown.
