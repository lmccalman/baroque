Reflections on my Arrival

1769

I embarked at Nantes on the 20th of June 1769 for St Domingue, the voyage was a happy one, I had the good fortune to arrive in good health at my place of destination on the 19th of the following month of August, from my departure from France until my arrival, I experienced no discomfort, I was not ill for a single instant, I was always in good health during the entire crossing, I had the pleasure of making acquaintance with several persons of merit who were on the same vessel as I, I was very content with the manner in which I was treated, both by the Captain and by the other persons who were on board, I found upon arriving at St Domingue everything that I could desire for my establishment.
