198.

One works, one goes very slowly
at the Rebuilding of the Towns and Municipal buildings
of the Flames. It is ordered that the Houses
will be rebuilt in Wood, to be well
inside, and [magasines] outside, to
prevent the Excesses of Bombs or
iron

Despite our Misfortunes, they have just
increased by one Million the funds
of the Lottery, that is to say, that one [Lisa]
of 4, we pay 5. Millions.

The Militia is entirely reestablished
and Uniformed.

The General of the Castle, that is to say
the Chiefs of the New [one], are still
in the Prisons, awaiting their
Judgment.

Despite our Misfortunes the
Apprehensions of a coming war,
the Island remains Situation.
