Brest the 4th July 1785.

On the 29th June arrived here Mr d'Albert-
Rions, commander of the Evolution Squadron,
which departed from here yesterday the 3rd July.

Here is the State.

On the 11th June, the Frigate la Révole, commanded
by Mr de Barras, Ship's Lieutenant
anchored in the Roadstead, coming from Martinique.

On the 28th June, was launched into the Water with Success
the Frigate la Cybèle of 40. Cannons, of which
26. of 18. in Battery.
