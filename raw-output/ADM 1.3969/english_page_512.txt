The prince arrived in triumph on the 27th of October
in Barcelona.

The warship the Prudhomme
richly laden, is expected at
Cadiz imminently, and there is
news that the other ship, and
other vessels also heavily laden,
which had been separated from it by a
strong storm, after their departure from
Callao de Lima, were all in a place
of safety, and would arrive later,
there had already been much concern about
this rich fleet.

The King has named lieutenant-general
of his armies, the chevalier de Croix, of
the Teutonic order, and currently
viceroy of Peru.
