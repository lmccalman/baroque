The Finance Committee must have assembled
yesterday Evening, they must have decided there the Loan
of 30. to 36. Millions, by means of Lottery;
it is claimed that there is still the Question
of the Postal Administration, which they want to put back
into tax farms.

Mr. de Fleury keeps nothing, the
King gives Him 50. Thousand Livres, to
compensate him for the Loss of his Offices,
which yielded Him approximately the same
Sum, before he entered the
Ministry.

The Nomination of Mr. Dormeslon,
will be followed by some Change
in the Intendancies, it is true
that Mr. de Crosne, will have the Offices
of Mr. Dormeslon. They give Him as
Successor in Rouen, Mr. de Vergennes,
who is in Metz, they have the
Latter replaced by Mr. Maurot.
