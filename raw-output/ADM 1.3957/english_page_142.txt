     The Duke of Richelieu is advised,
that it was resolved for him in the Council of the Duke
d'Aiguillon, the Marshal de Richelieu
explains, that the Prince de Condé being
Related through the same Alliance, he would
therefore be placed to recuse himself: The Prince de
Condé speaks to the King, that the Princes of the
King had here this Conduct published
at the King's, and that by the Quality of Prince
he should not be recused. The King
confirmed what the Prince
de Condé had just said, and all was finished.

     The Assembly is continued to Saturday
the 5th of May, it will be decided then if the Affair
will be sent to extraordinary [court], or if
it will be judged; this will depend on the
new Depositions of the Witnesses
that will be heard
