R° 5 fol. N° 1

Paris the 26 January 1776.

The letters from us
have announced with the smallest
Particulars.

The Minister of finances has
returned to Paris, on the Subject of the Edicts
that are at Parliament.

The assembled chambers Tuesday
26, examined and read
these Edicts, they have named
Commissioners to examine them.
The one concerning the corvées, the Militia
and the taille [tonne], The one concerning
the corvées, has been sent back, to
make some changes to it.

People speak variously about the other
Edicts, it is said that there is one, which
suppresses the Banalités, that
is to say, that in the Kingdom
there are Banal ovens, where the
People are obliged to bring to bake
