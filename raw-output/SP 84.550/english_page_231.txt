    La Courberelle, commanded
by Mr le Bat, has returned from
Martinique. Mr de Rosiere
who was Governor of this
Colony, has returned to France
on this Vessel.
    One awaits the Corvette commanded
by Mr de Marigny; all his
Staff has perished, having been
poisoned, one does not know how
one thinks that it is by the Wine,
or by the flours.
    There was much putrid
fever, 500 Persons have died
in a Month.
