October, in the Year of 1775
along the North Coast of
California, inhabited by Savage
Indians.

Having departed from the Port of San
Blas in New Galicia
the King's frigate, the St. Jacques
the Galley the Sonora, and the Packet boat
the St. Charles, with different experienced
Naval Officers, all
destined for Monterey, from there to
continue their Course and Discoveries
The said frigate reached
30 Degrees of Latitude; the Galley
at 58, and the Packet boat at 37 and 40 Minutes,
in which Voyage they
discovered all the intermediate
Coasts, the great Port of
St. Francis and different Rivers
whose Coasts are inhabited by
Indians very docile, affable
of a well-disposed humor
