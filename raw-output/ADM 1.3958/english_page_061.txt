70.

                           Brest the 25th January 07

                           I Order to a
[endetté] that our Batteries of the Port
of Châlet and of the Point, and C[?] Ord[?]
given to suspend the March[?]
of the Brigs is a Counter-order which
reveals our Politics.

One [reflete] of a Good bom[?]pri[?]
and qui[?]q[?]ontra its Squadron Chiefs.

The turn reveals nothing by
The Jaffir, the Brave and the Belle Poule
always armed awaiting the
Distribution.

For our Vessels and Brigades
are [mutés].

One does not doubt that the Union and the
Seine went to the Isle de
France.
