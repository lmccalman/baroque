The Controller General persists in wanting to
obtain a new Reduction of 6 Millions
on the funds Department of War; and
has had [égraber] this Project to the King, to present
the conjectures to the [Battelethe] of Royal
Counsel, and them to enforce by the Chambers,
and to postpone to another time & to another
occasion, when the Court has the facility to
[tenter] the Regiments. It is agreed, that these
Lieutenant Colonels and these Majors at
a thousand livres each, without favor,
are useless, and that this expense is from
the imagination of the Duke of Choiseul.

The Minister of War worked Tuesday
