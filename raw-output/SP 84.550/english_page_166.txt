Toulon, April 7, 1776.

The Atalante set
Sail on the 4th, with a good Wind,
for the Levant; she is to touch
at Malta in passing.

The Playette is careened, she
will then go to join
the others in the Roadstead.

There has been an Order from the Court, to have
disembarked from upon the Provence,
M. de Capelles, the Ship's Ensign,
one knows not why.

The Duke of Chartres will not arrive
here until the 22nd.
