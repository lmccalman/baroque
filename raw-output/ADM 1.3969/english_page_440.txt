also his Reflections are all the more

The Imperial Minister at The Hague has been
recalled, was he provisionally given
Leave, & Count Belgioioso has
notified the Dutch Plenipotentiaries
who were at Brussels, that their
Masters, by the Peace that they made
under the Emperor's Pavilion, having
declared War on His Imperial Majesty,
their Negotiation was finished.
Three or Four Captains of our
best Corsairs depart today
for Ostend, if they are going to arm for
Privateering; The one among them who knows
perfectly the Waters of the Indies,
will go cruise by the Cape of Good Hope,
and at the Strait.
