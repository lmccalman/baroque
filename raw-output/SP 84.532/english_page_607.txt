Madrid the 10th May 1773.

                A Courier from London has
arrived here charged with Expressing
to London, concerning the Disarmament
of the Fleets in the two Kingdoms;
orders have been dispatched in Consequence
in the different Ports of this Kingdom.
It is said, that they will only disarm in
Proportion as the English will do so;
It is the only of Confidence in
England, or else to put its
Spain. It [?] two to
receive funds in this Regard from our
Ports, which I will also communicate to you
at Carington.
        Order has been received by troops,
quartered in S. Ander the Isles;
to hold themselves ready to return
to their former Garrisons.


                                        Toulon,
