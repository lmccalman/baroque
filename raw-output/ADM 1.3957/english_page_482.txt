1326 Novem: 1701

Calais the 24th October 1770.

A Worker of
the late M. dommel Ker, brought this Order
to [desbler] the Workers, which puts the
pieces in good State of Defense, 24
Pieces of Cannon were placed outside the new
Domain, the [Ouvades] of the Capuchins were furnished with Mortars,
Port St Sebastien is being fortified, a
Battery is being established at Puerto Ruco, another
where the [Reige] will be placed, and another
on the Mole, all the
things are being furnished to Ports Louis, la Pintade,
Chatoynes, and the land Gate.
There are already 2 Regiments in the
Town, C. others are expected. The
Duke d'Abbe commands as Chief,
the marshal him the Lieutenant General
Mr Seelly, and Mr Pertuis as
Marshal of Camps.


Rochester B.
