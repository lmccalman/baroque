                Brussels the 20th January 1791

    The Duke of Aremberg arrived
the day before yesterday from Parliament of Vienna in
this City, and after a Conference of
several hours with Duke Charles
and the Prince of Stahremberg, Minister
of State, he left immediately, it is believed
for London, but nothing transpires
of the Mission.
    The same President of [Olleniverité] and
The [Curé vil] on the 18th of this Month.
The work of a Bridge of Planks, at a half Quarter
of Leagues from this City descending the river, is
finished in one hundred twenty days. The new
[bridge] is temporary, the Pedestal transported
to [Chalermont] is temporal.
