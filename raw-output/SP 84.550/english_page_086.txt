The same Day 3, after Noon,
the Princes and Peers were assembled,
to hear the Affair of the
Marshal de Richelieu, the King
having ordered that this Affair
be judged in full Lit.

The First President went
Wednesday to Versailles to bring the
representations of the Company,
by Virtue of the Decree of the previous day.

The same Day the allied Princes
were again assembled
with Mde for the Affair of
Mr de Richelieu.
