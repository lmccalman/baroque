129.                                                R. 10 Epre N.1


                    Madrid the 18th March 1770.

                              The Dispatch of the Carmen
arrived at Cadiz on the 28th of last Month
after a long and dangerous voyage.
     There arrived at said Cadiz in 60 hours
from Havana three Swedish Hookers
with the Payment from Lisbon. The Rest
of the Convoy put to sea with them,
has not yet arrived.
