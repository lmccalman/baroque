They do not name the Vessel
François-Daniel the Letters of Ladies
one believes that it is the Crown
which is anchored in the Bay of
St. Jean.

Last Monday the Ambassador of
Prussia, received a Courier from the Court,
& Couriers told me that one arrived
from Petersburg, where some Post had
arrived 36 hours to Berlin. One claims
that his Dispatches announce the
pacific Dispositions of the Court
of the Empress, & the Divan having
granted him the principal Demands.

M. de la Porte, Intendant of the
Navy having requested his retirement
because of his Health, M. Dutrône
formerly Clerk of Finances for
M. Necker succeeds him in the Part
of Funds, and Accounting.
