Rotterdam the 5th October 1778.

My Lord

I have the honor to send you
enclosed the Account of my Disbursements
for a Part of the cargo of the Ships
[Sustes], the 29th last, from the Port of Liverpool
to 1769. Norwich. I have delivered on the rest
which has been Charged here this day, for the
of Â£769. Sterling. For this sum I

hands Messrs Thomas Munro & Comp of Middlesbrough
Wilkson Daviesson & Company I, in
sending at the same time to Monsieur
Deles my legalized Receipt for the said
sums.
