To join on the 3rd of the Current, of 75
159 Transport Vessels
this day including of the King; and that
they on the 5th of the Current one would fight
and load them; and that one would calculate,
that the total of the Embarkation Troops
would amount up to 32 Thousand Men;
Infantry Dragoons, and Artillerymen.
     One awaits from hour to hour some
Attacking Vessels from Carrol,
with Troops, and that the
Lieutenant General, Marquis de
Cene d'Hly, had the order, to make
[Briller] Commanding General
by the Viscount [le Ponifant], of Mens.
     There comes Squadron Chief, Dom
Michel Carton, would command
them only the [flainne], and [antienroit]
the Conquest, by the [Tires] of Portugal.
     The News from [l'excol], report,
that the Spanish fortify themselves
more and more, and that are forces of
[fortis froise] three times in the days.
