Toulon the 3rd July 1776.

I found the [Armalle],
its Maneuvers well done each yesterday,
[à son le suppose à pouvoir être]
attempted and Small inconsistently.

The Vessel the Lion has been put
since yesterday Evening.

The Commander has everyone contribute
to the Machine to [étemetre] Dominus

They have emptied almost Entirely
this Morning the Vessel the Marseillois,
to [claver] the new and
interior Pieces, the Careening of this
Vessel is done, and they have
begun the [Armengammes]
of the Hold.

The Instructions for the Faucille
arrived yesterday. They await in
some those for the Aurora,
which was put this morning to
take on its Water.

The work of the [Cargne] of the [Bâffons]
continues always quite [traire].
