They have been obliged to cut off the Carrice
and the Oats from the ordinary Food
of these Makers of hoots; but they have been
relaxed on the [foix].

The Chamberlain has summoned the Knight,
it is expected that there will be something
New at this weekly Assembly.

It is agreed that the Parliaments, the
Bourgeoisie and the breeched Populace of
Paris, with the Dependents of Paris itself
have orders to proceed to the Lands
where there is Parliament, to make
enter; By the edict of Abolition of
all Monies except the [ternie],
their offices will be not only
confiscated, but they will be degraded
from the Assembly, they and their Posterity,
and excluded in Perpetuity from professing
ever any office in the
Kingdom...

The Provost who will render the Ministry
more firm; the King is resolved to
not recall, if not to re-establish
the Parliament, unless he submits it.

The Parliament of Rouen will reconvene
on the 2nd of February and deliberate on the Edict;
all absent Members will be
warned to attend this Session.
