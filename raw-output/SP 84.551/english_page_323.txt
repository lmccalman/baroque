According to the Letters from Brest,
of the 29th, and the 30th, the Order has arrived
in this Port, to arm with all
diligence the Magnifique, the Intrépide,
the Terrible, the Achilles, the Bizarrs,
the Fantasque, and the Brigadon,
the Aveuglante, the Indiscrète,
the Zéphir, and the Affiquain; they
work there Night and Day. The
Ten Vessels, will be [4 joints]
by Fifteen others, which will form
the Division of Mr. Du Chaffault,
not including 3 Corvettes
armed at Le Havre, and 2 Flutes at
Rochefort. The Squadron of Mr.
Du Chaffault has not returned
to the Roads, but it is
only Ten Leagues at Sea.
They are reestablishing the Batteries of the
fort and the Gouletz.

They confirm the Letter of 22 [huict]
of August brought to Bordeaux.
