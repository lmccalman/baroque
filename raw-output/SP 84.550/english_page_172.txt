There appeared yesterday Afternoon,
some Ordinances for the Military.
one has not seen them yet, but
one knows, that there is one which
approves the General Inspectors
of troops, conferring to them alone
their Appointments.

Another one, which suppresses the
Venality of Charges in the
Military, that is to say, that at
each Transfer one will diminish them
by a Quarter, so that, at the
Reforms, the Places will be free,
and meanwhile the Heirs will
no longer lose the Price of these
Charges, in case of Death, as
formerly.
