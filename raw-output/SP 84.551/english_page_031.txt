From Barcelona it is reported, that
War Preparations were continuing there,
and among others, Quantity
of ladders, of Rocks, of Shovels, &c.
    My latest Reports from Malaga
of the 7th Current state, that all
was very tranquil in our
[Projedes], and that the Four
Xebecs of the King of this Department,
had departed for Algeciras,
and that the famous Barcelo was
to be found at Majorca.
