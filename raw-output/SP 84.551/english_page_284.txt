They are building on the Side of the Cooperage
a new Enclosure, to
close the Port. They are finally
building through the Heights new
Ramparts, which take route
from the new Wall of the Arceaux
to the gates of the environs of the
Brosserie. All that will cost,
they say, 4 Million.

There has just appeared a
new Ordinance, which [fait
quand l'hiffer] to the Mariners:
It [tend] the Batteries at
Toulon, and Obligation of the
Comedies. It orders this
printed against Art 30. Articles,
it fixes the Service of the Bourges
of Land and Sea, in the
Town and in the Port, to
avoid in the future all
disputes.
