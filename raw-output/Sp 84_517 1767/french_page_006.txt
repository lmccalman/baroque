Septemr 29th 1774.

Sir:
    I beg with the deepest affection
am now to acquaint You that on
Sunday last Capt Woolsey arrived
from Menace with the melan-
choly account that H.R.H. the
Duke of York died at that place of a
malignant Fever on the 4th inst
after fourteen days severe illness.
I give you the earliest information
in my power of this unfortunate
Event; the in all probability You
will have recd it before the
arrival of this Letter
    I am &c
        (H Stoney)
