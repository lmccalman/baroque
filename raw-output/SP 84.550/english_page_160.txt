    The Count of St. Germain has had
delivered recently by last the State followed
with the King for all the Military
Arrangements; this work lasted
nearly Four Hours. The
Commanding Colonels, that were
had from all the Regiments, have
been all named, and they are only
given 5000 L. of [Frayer]
of Salaries.
    It is assured from good authority,
that Mr. de Malesherbes has
resigned from the Position of Minister,
and that he will leave it very
soon.
    It is assured likewise, that the Cabal
against the Minister of finances,
which has caused him to lose his Credit; his
other Projects will not take place,
and that perhaps in a little while they will do
less, but that is only a Rumor.
