to contract with the Court of Vienna
for the Delivery of a certain
Quantity per Year, to be sold
on His Part to those who would have
need of it: As such an Arrangement
would suit well enough the Court of Vienna
the Negotiation on this Object is
begun, and has scarcely any other
Difficulties, than to agree on the Price
which naturally must be lower
than that of Retail Sales.
