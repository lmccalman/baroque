Mr. d'Ogny, General Intendant
of Posts, is named by the King
General Inspector, and Chief of the
Messengers and Stagecoaches of the
Kingdom.

The King determined yesterday several
of the order of the Spirit; the Ballet de Soto
is the principal hero of it, His Majesty
said he would receive there the Knights
named at Candlemas.

To name two vacant white Colonels;
it was said at court yesterday, that it
would be the Prince of Lambesc,
Grand Equerry, and the Duke of Cagny,
First Equerry: this is what one
will know shortly.
