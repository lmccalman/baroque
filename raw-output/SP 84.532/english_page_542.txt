They have published with a theory Decree of
the Councils. The 1st breaks and annuls the
Decrees rendered by the Parliament of
Rouen against the Superior Councils
as [autérieurs], reckless, injurious
encroaching upon the authority of the King
tending to upset the People
and incompetently rendered, and
forbids under Penalty of Disobedience, the publication and execution of similar ones in the future.

This Parliament has still done nothing
regarding the Decree of Paris, which had its
Decree burned.

The 2nd orders and regulates the
Reimbursements of the Offices of the Parliament
of the Grand Council, and of the Court of Aids
a Million per year will be employed for this
By reimbursing beforehand the Debts
of Provisions, and while awaiting the
other Reimbursements, they will pay
to each Holder the interest of the
Sum at which his [Établissement Retenu]
is Set at a rate slightly higher than
what existed at the acquisition and [lors de divers]
of the said Offices
