Genoa the 30th December 1784

In one of my
previous ones I told you, that
our Regency had forbidden
having Frigates or
other Vessels Warships
built for foreigners, for fear that
France might take offense at it; but
since then, the Court of Versailles has
declared, that It did not intend
at all to prevent the Contractors
from having Frigates built,
and other Warships,
but that It could not
approve the Sale to the English.
The Americans are buying
here Vessels, and in the Course
of this Month there departed for
North America five
Tartans, armed with five Cannons,
