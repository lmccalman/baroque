As for the present state of the most
sensible part in their Situation, and as
there is to stand no nothing that way
without having the Opinion & Concurrence
of the States and of France, I you will talk
to our three friends upon that head, & be able
to know their Sentiments, what plan may
be most proper to be formed for annoying
Spain in those Quarters, where good use
might be made of our Superiority by Sea;
And you may let them know that the King
is now desirous a Reinforcement of the
Men of War from G.B. to come into those Seas.
