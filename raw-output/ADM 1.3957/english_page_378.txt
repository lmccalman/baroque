                                        fx tocket N°2

            Private Letter

                        Paris, September 21, 1770.

        The letters from Spain
of the 8th of this month [des anciennes] the Events
that had been announced [la Faire] for the King
of Navarre, it is said that this Prince
Embarrassed does not think of attacking
Ceuta.

    The same Letters say on the Subject
of the Expedition of the Spanish Squadron in the
Coasts of Brittany.
    There has arrived at Rochefort a Ship
from St. Domingue, which brings Letters
from the Ports to the Prince of S. Guillez, According to
which they were experiencing daily
Shocks of earthquake,
which maintain Terror in
Spirits.
    I believe all Sorts of Rumors for
& against the Russians, too uncertain
to Report them.
    It is said from another Country & [Cruisement]
that they were needed [chez lettres], & it is said that they
had, as soon as we Response from our
Minister in London, a Courier to [calligié].
                                        Brussels.
