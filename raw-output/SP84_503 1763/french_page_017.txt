Rotterdam august 16th 1763.

Privat.


My Lord—


The enclosed paper is a copy of a letter—
instruction which has been sent by the Duke—
of Choiseul to mr Brown the french—
minister at the Hague, and to mr Ashley—
the french consul at amsterdam; the latter—
to my knowledge is taking great pains to—
fulfil his orders.

The person from whom I had this paper has—
