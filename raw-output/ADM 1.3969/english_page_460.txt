The March of the Troops, from the Borders
to the Low Countries, is regulated
at 35 Days.

Those who wish to arm for Privateering
against the Dutch, must apply
to Trieste, or to Rome, to receive their
Letters of Marque; and in case of Need,
they will be given gratis the Artillery and
the required and necessary Munitions.

There are People who still flatter themselves
that the War will not take place, and that all
will be arranged during this Winter by
the Mediation of France.
