Extract of a Letter from His
Wolters His Majesty's Agent
at Rotterdam, dated the 10
of December 1740 N.S. to
Mr Corbett.

Private Letters from St Malo, give
us an account that four large
Transports laden with Ammunition,
Arms, and some Men, were ready to
sail on the 4th of last Month, and
only waited for a Man of War,
that was to Convoy them; it is said
they are intended for Great Britain.
