17th of August 1770

Brussels the 9th April 1770.

For some days
there has manifested in several Parishes
in the Environs of this City, a new
Disease of Livestock, which has begun a
species of Gangrene, but very little [ininte], &
in 5 Days there died 627 of them in Three
Parishes.

The President of Franse obtained on the 4th April
the transport of 400,000 [Bassieres] of wheat.
