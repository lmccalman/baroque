No 75.

Toulon the 4th September 1776.

I will seek from Mr de
Montolry, my host, news in Order,
to take from Bresnes.
     That one will continue its change
for Brest.
     The Minister has written, that as
the Vessels designated to arm
at the first Epoch, are ready,
he wants that one take advantage of the fine
Season, to prepare as many
as possible of the others, and in
consequence not to send back
the Workers from Lorient who have arrived
in the Port.
     One will work on the Vessel
the Bourgogne, which needs a
good Careening. C. One will have
the Foudroyant pass to the great Basin,
to change its Stem
One will continue the Rodeur at two
