                    Brussels the 4 March 1773.

            The Orders are given
to all the officers both of the Infantry and of the
Cavalry, from the Colonel Proprietor
to the Ensign, and the provision of a
Cart and Horses as necessary.
the officers of Cavalry are obliged for
one year the Corps, and two blacks more [Heaves] to
a Pair of Shoes, two Pairs of Breeches
one of Cloth, to another of hunting deerskin
leather, and two Hats for the Regiments
of the 38 [Acoult] next.
