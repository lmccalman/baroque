     2°. The Intendant, is the Lieutenant
of the King, to exercise & the First
at 9. Thousand livres of Capón; and the
Second at Thousand livres. They
do not diminish the Appointments
of the Governor.        There were
24. Grey Sisters to care for the
Sick; they add only three of them.
There were 16. Priests; to
dispose of 6. it is necessary, they cut
only four.
     3°. I have observed, that the King of
Poland has remained imbecile since
his Illness, and that in Consequence
Your Excellence, with that of Spain
must go to Arbonne, to
contribute to the affair; and to have the
Queen Regent.
     I will signal at length here a
