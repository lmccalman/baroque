Pendre de Mêlée Carvalho, Minister
of Portugal, who will go there, to
settle our Differences, with that
Crown, if any.

The 17th has just named the
Knight de Croix, Field Marshal,
and Lieutenant Marquis de
Croix, and former Vice-Roy a
[clairyue], as General Commander
of all the newly
discovered Provinces beyond the three Northern
[provinces] of Mexico, namely to discover,
California, Sonora, New
Galicia, and New Biscay;
it is said with 20 Thousand Sols two
as Salary.

The 14th of the Current [month], died here
the Count of Fuentes, formerly
Ambassador of Spain in
France.
