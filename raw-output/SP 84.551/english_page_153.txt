Since [Rigultes] hours they have been removing here
and in our Surroundings all the Vagabonds
and People without Avowal; and they have had them
dispatched, to make general Levies
throughout all the Kingdoms,
to complete the Armies.

This same Wednesday, our Politicians
flattered themselves that it would not come
to an open Rupture; that they
claimed by the [Defferiture] at the
Congress, while they struck
the Blow in America. But some
News are of Sentiments,
that the Port should be attacked, and
with all our Forces; but it
appears that they prefer to avoid
a general War.
