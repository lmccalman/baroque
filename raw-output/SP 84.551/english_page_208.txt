Paris the 22nd August 1778.

N° Letters from Toulon
of the 18th have announced nothing of ordinary actions,
that there are as before.
    Those from Brest of the 17th state, that
the Squadron of Mr. Du Chaffault, after
having conducted the Division of Toulon
as far as the Orkney lands, has come
to put in at Quiberon, to take on
water; it will remain there eight days,
after which it will put back to Sea, to
confirm the Provisions, and will not return to Brest
until the month of September.
