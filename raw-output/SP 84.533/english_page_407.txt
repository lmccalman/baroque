Vienna December 29, 1775.

The Promotion, in which
you were in the last one, was
published last Sunday by the Grand
Master, Prince of Kaunitz Rietberg, it
is as follows. The Count of Strafford
is named Minister of State, and in the Place
Count Henry Cajetan de Blumagen
as [Prishire] Chancellor of Bohemia
and Austria, Count Leopold des
Coloredo, formerly Vice Chancellor
of Bohemia and Austria, is named
President of the Court Chamber
of the Bohemian Deputation, and of the
[Alliés de Camerere], Count Antoine
de Borgen, formerly Minister of State,
is made [Supointé] to the Prince des
[françotin], as Marshal of the
Austrian Post, in the Country of the
Governors of the Country. Count de Wrbro
is now President of the Court Chamber
and of the Bohemian Deputation.
Baron de Raigersfeld is named
Vice President of the [Alliés de Camerere].
