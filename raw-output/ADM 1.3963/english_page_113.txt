The two Brigades of the Commands of Mr de Lafourcis, which are annexed to these troops; the small Brigades of Franceries, near the von d'Heyer, and the others united to the two Regiments for the others, have entered from the [Jointure] to the list [Escandre].

The Council convenes the [Playsant] of the [stête] Marinis of Flanders with troops from Deluquetourt, [dont aux Inroites pas andres].

The Regiment of the [stête], its Garrison at Valencia, and that of the Irish [Ermin], which were at [l'amplaine], as well as that of Beaulysigue, which were in full March to go into Garrison at Barcelona, all three of Infantry, are currently on the March for Extremadura, as well as the Cavalry Regiment of S'Luyule, and that of Dragoons of Siguense.
