I am grounded to believe by strong
Reasons, that Cardinal de Bernis will be
Recalled from Rome, and put at the head of
Foreign Affairs: He was already informed
of the Fall of Choiseuls before it
happened, which I know conjectured as certain from
a Letter dated from Rome the 28 December,
which arrived from one of my Friends.

The English [font] is still undecided
in France, and the most clever to transport there.
