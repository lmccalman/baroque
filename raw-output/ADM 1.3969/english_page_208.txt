The Council for the new
Formation of the Army was held
the King's Ordinance on this Subject,
will appear soon, and already several
Officers employed in the General Staff
of the Army, have received their Letters.
The Army is distributed into Three
Divisions, there will be more Inspection
of.

As for the great Promotion,
it will only be declared at the entrance of place,
or at the latest September 21st.
It is said on the Subject, that henceforth
one will no longer reach the Rank
of Lieutenant-General by Rank
of seniority, but only when
one is employed. The Rank of
Brigadier is suppressed, thus the
Officers of this Rank, will be by
this Promotion elevated to the Rank
of Field Marshals.
