Whitehall Sept 9th 1768.

Sir
His Majesty having been
pleased to lay His Commands
upon the Earl of Halifax to
act in the Southern Department,
It appears me to be His
principal Secretary of State
for the Northern; I am to shew
that you will be pleased, for
the future, to address your
dispatches to me, which I
shall not fail punctually to
lay before the King. &c.
Transmit to you such Orders &
Instructions, as His Majesty shall
think proper to give for your
Guidance & Direction.

I am &c.
Sandwich
