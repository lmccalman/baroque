                        Paris the 2nd September 1720.

                             Word is sent from Tortosa,
of the 26th that Vessels will be proposed in
this Port to carry [St Battailles] into
the Place, and that if the Siege continues they
will be brought back to France to refit
them and that matter of Armament.
     Six Battalions have been brought back before
to hold the Siege of Alambrar with four
of those to The army of Chevalier Binette;
who returns to his Embassy; this
Brigade makes an advanced Line of 30 hours.
     By [le Sentiment] departure on the 18th from
before Beth Maria was made, some Details of the
troops that he has at this said Place.
     These states 180 Bombs on Brigade, which
have fired without Success, after which they went
to Beth Maria to bombard and complete
the Plan, but the Wind being Calm,
[Drapier] on Raza, they took advantage of the Vessels
[en detant] the [Casses] of this Castle of
Maria, to prevent the Corsairs from
Going out; Raza was bombarded with all
possible Success; the Town was reduced to
ashes. The Bombardment lasted three
Days, in which Mr. le R de Montelgh Ensign
of Vessel, who was carried away by a
Cannonball from the Battery of the Place of Raza.
