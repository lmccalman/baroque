Vienna the 55 January 1773.

The Observations of the
Peers at Bucharest & at Grimustadt are
slowly. One is always here
of Opinion, that the K. [fastient] them sworn
for the Turks, to gain time,
and put in order their army of 40
thousand men, very scattered apparently
in the circle of Perfchin Stepanvali
in the Exercise of Artillery,
and their new foundry goes
very well.

One speaks here of five Corps for the
Spring, of which two in Italy,
one in Bohemia, one in Bavaria,
and one near Lascndorf. One does
not want to [juquer] until following the [Pragertes]
which could Arrive something
extraordinary. The
future Operations depend only
on what will pass
at the Sanatas Consilium which will be
