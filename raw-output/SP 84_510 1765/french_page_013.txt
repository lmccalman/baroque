Rotterdam the 25 July 1763.

My Lord Duke,

As I am drawn upon by the
Madrid Correspondent for the Quarter
due to him the 8th of June last, & take
the Liberty to inform my self if it is
your Graces Pleasure that I should
go on in the Method which I have
followed since the Year 1746, by
standing Orders from the Secretary
of State.
I sent the Account of my Disbursements
to the Secretary of State with acquainted
the Treasury of it, and by the 1st &
following, I drew at 25 Days Date
upon William Davis Esqr. of the
Treasury.
This same Method has allways been
followed for my Disbursements for the
