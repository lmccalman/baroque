13. 21 June

                                    Paris the 9th June 1783

     You are probably unaware
of what is currently happening in Russia
it is known that in all Despotic
Governments, it is hardly permitted to
speak, even less to write; thus
one does not know to what extent it has been
forbidden in Petersburg, the Secretary
of Cabinet Operations, and of the
Movement of Troops, and to com-
municate them to Foreigners who have reported
to the Agents of the State. We think
indeed that Russia alone will hardly
be able to undertake to make the
Porte consent to what is demanded of it;
but one is sometimes Guarded with regard
to the Emperor. It is known that the Russian
Troops were already well advanced so
that the Rupture could be prevented.
However the Letters from Constantinople
want to make it believed, that the Settlement
is concluded.

     The Persons of the Court who come
from London returned 4
or Five days ago -
