     On the eleventh of April they began
to give the example of Reform
in the Swiss; what is called
the Common, composed of a great
number of Officers, has just been
reformed; those who finding themselves [nez]
for [plus nonnes], they
give them so much per year to
spend.
     Monsieur works on the same
Reform. One will see executed, soon
that of the House of the King, and of
the Queen; and it is claimed that that
of the tables which are made at the King's,
form an Economy of more
than 40 Millions per Year.
