The Regiment of Vietoris of 1400 Men
Had Embarked, and set Sail from this
Port with the first favorable Wind,
to reinforce the Garrison of Porto
Rico.

At Cartagena one is likewise
occupied in taking all imaginable Measures
to put it under shelter from an Invasion,
and one works there to arm 12 Ships of the Line
of different Burden, one assembles
there also troops.
