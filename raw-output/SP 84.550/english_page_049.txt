                    Paris the 19th February 1776

                    M. Bellues from Toulon
of the 10th present, that the Brig. of la Sayette,
returning from the Levant to this Port,
was to depart again on the 18th, to
go disarm at Brest.
They continue to load the Quiffe,
and they will not be able to begin
to unload for Two Months
           The armament prepared at
Brest, & at L'Orient, for The
Indies American Islands & to
depart.
           Nothing still transpires
of the operations of M. de St Germain.
He has however spoken, that the Corps
of the Gendarmerie would furnish
only officers, but that the
