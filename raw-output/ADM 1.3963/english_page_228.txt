drawing one Lot of 50. Thousand Livres
of Life Annuities; one of 40,
one of 30, and the others in
decreasing, the smallest
will be of 150 Livres of Annuities.
The 15 Thousand Tickets will remain in
the Wheel, until exit of 2 Hundred
Livres of Capital, at 5 per Cent
of Interest in perpetual Annuities,
which will make a Contract of 400 Livres
of Annuities for each Ticket.
