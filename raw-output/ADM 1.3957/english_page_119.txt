This is what is reported,; but one can hardly believe
that Royalty would admit to its Council
a Man who nearly was judged to death
by this same Council.

The King went the day before yesterday the grand [Monarqi]
to the Chancellor the briefs [destens tontes] against
the Duke and [l'aquillon], it is thought that this affair
will have no consequence. The 4 Councillors of
Rennes who presented a Petition, have withdrawn
the letter, and have returned to their lands.

We mention by letters a Decree
against the Countess of [sequeville], the Duke
of Bourbon profits from it, in which
[l'akon], this Lord is not spared.

The King went for [l'aquelles] first expenses
yesterday from Italy, and [me le quitter qu'avère] before Easter.
