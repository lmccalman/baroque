The King will give Audience
Tomorrow to the Ambassadors
and Foreign Ministers. He will
give them also to the Deputies
of the States of Languedoc, who
will present their Cahiers,
following the Usage. His Majesty
will depart after Dinner, to go
spend the meantime at Choisy.

The Queen will not make this Trip,
because she may at any moment,
the King must make the Reverence
judicial.
