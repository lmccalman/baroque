Toulon the 29 May 1793

The frigates
l'Impérieuse, and la Mignonette, have
anchored the 25 April at Malta, and have
departed the 24th for their mission
which is to protect our Commerce
in the Levant.
    The Grand Master had sold to
Chevalier Deyanne the [Mégaliens] taken
last year by a Royal frigate
flying the Flag of the [Rays].
    Mr de Gebaret has departed, to
give an Account of His Conduct to
the Court.
