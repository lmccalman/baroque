    Rome is the Country of Pasquinades,
one sees there a Print which represents
the General of the Jesuits, holding the Equilibrium
on an agitated Rope, by the Ministers
of the Bourbon Courts, the Pope is
below, to hold him if he comes
to fall. &c.
    The Letters from Bartie of the 7th July,
announce nothing New, all is
tranquil in C. City, with C. exception
of the Bandits, who still make
Honors.
    The Russian and Ottoman Armies are
in Presence, one expects a
Battle between Them.
