Madrid the 29th May 1782.

Here are the Letters that are
delivered to me from Cartagena, in accordance
with our arrangements, for Montre Algiers, two
Corvettes of M. Barrulo.

Eight Gunboats.
Two Bomb vessels.
Four Doubles.
Six [Chalupeaux] of the Line, and
Forty Vessels including Frigates,
Xebecs, and Transports.

Don Louis de Cordova is expected imminently at
Court, having been
summoned there.

It is written from Aranjuez, that our
Minister is extraordinarily
occupied with great Affairs, but that
nothing leaks out.

The King has conferred the Title of
Duke of Sedano and Grandee of Spain on Million
who is his Departure for France.

Under Press a Decree of the King
carrying capital Punishment against Tobacco Smugglers.
