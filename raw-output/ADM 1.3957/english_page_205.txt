The Nobility always in Combustion, until
the Abbé, [Frédeur] of Bugles, for a decree,
in connection with a Ballet at the Gate that he made in
Combray to extinguish the fire, that he will set there.
The Lover of another girl and one will go to the Police that I
give; On the Boulevard of Amboisse at Versay.

One marches from Rennes where the Officers make
a fine sojourn in the World. There are two
Cornets of Dragons at Pithie at the lodging, of which they normans
present a Petition for their Lodging.

Our People always united as much as to the State of what there
is, did not inform them of the Marriage without
letting them know; but one speaks of making the
Second Marriage of the late Dauphin.

Madame la Dauphine announces a great
Desire for pleasures. She is at Versailles as
She had been there all her Life. The King takes her
today to St Hubert for two days, and
The King, The Queen and Madame Adélaïde
come by the Engagement and for the
Glasses.

It is a question of giving Letters Patent
to absolutely extend The Affair of Brittany
so that there be no more talk of it, but this King
works on it without one speaking of
Registration. The Duke of Aiguillon St supports.
