The Governor has it supplied, but some
Grains which would not amount to much,
and one cannot [foncier] for the Chain, they have
need to earn money.

It is wished that no one from the Town
will go anymore to visit the Duke and Duchess
of Fitz-James which marks them,
and would prove that Yesterday's Assembly
was without order and [degeurement]. After the
last one, the Bretons by this Conduct
will not obtain a Session of the Court.

The operations are done, today it
assembles, it is known that this
Parliament will be verified: and that the
Procureur General is made Premier
President. It is also Dijon's turn
for today.
