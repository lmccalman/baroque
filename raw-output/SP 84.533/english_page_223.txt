According to all Appearances, and despite
the opposition of the Jesuits, they will proceed
with the Beatification of Palerina. The Pope
has rendered a Decree, which declares Just the
Cause of this Servant of God.

Letters from Constantinople of September 30
inform us, that the Turks have been in
[rerus] of Ruentberg at [Lux] the Danube. The
Russians lost there 2 to 3 Thousand men
and 6 Pieces of Cannon; their General the
Prince Répning was there grievously wounded from
the Campaign [Navana], which is not Far
From there: there is no question of Peace, and the
Grand Vizier has begged the Ministers, [jusqu'à
ce sçu à monter à être plus doux].

The Russian Squadron has not burned this
Year, nor done any ravaging; it is known
that it lacks Provisions and Money,
that the Crews are falling ill
and that there is great Division
among the Chiefs; thus their great
Maritime Preparations have served for
nothing [jusqu'à l'huis] the forces of
