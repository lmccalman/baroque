R 249 2 N.24

                    Paris the 9th February 1776.

              They are not annulling at all the
[interessantes] of our Parlements, and still
nothing substantial for the finances.
     The Grand Council has made the
strongest Remonstrances
Against the Undertakings of the Parlements
(not authorized) these Remonstrances
will be carried and presented to
the King the day after tomorrow, by a
Deputation of this tribunal.
     Through the Parlement begins
to distinguish itself, and to make felt
their Ambition. The King had
caused to be registered at that of Rouen
Letters Patent, concerning
some Arrangements for the
Province; Where the registration
destroys the Letters Patent.
Has made the King. This Registration
                                          comes!
