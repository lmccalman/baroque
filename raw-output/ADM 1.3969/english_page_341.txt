Madrid, March 8, 1782.

At last we are informed
of the arrival at Cadiz of our fleet
from Mexico, departed from Havana
at the beginning of last
January, rich, as they say, with 30
Million strong Piastres, both in
Silver and in Effects, which I accord
with what they write to me from Havana
dated January 14 last.

Our National Bank of
St. Charles is going at great speed, I was
assured that since Christmas, there had
entered more than N thousand shares, and
that every day, there is more
eagerness to place funds there.
