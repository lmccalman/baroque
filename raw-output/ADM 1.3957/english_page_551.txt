Word comes from Rome of the 26th, that there are
some troubles in Sicily with regard
to provisions & that they sent there,
they were made to transform in different
Communities.

The Cardinal [Stere] which was to take place
on the 26th, was not held.

The King of Poland has made
Complaint to the Pope against M. Davini
Nuncio in Poland, in that he favors
the Confederates &c.

P. S. A Friend who arrives from the Court
tells me, that as for the News
of the War, they weaken,
at the same time that the Rumors of an
Accommodation strengthen.
