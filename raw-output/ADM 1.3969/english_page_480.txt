The Horses for Transport, and those for

Equipment is in the best State.

The General Officers are not yet
appointed, at least they are not
known. Some Powers
claiming, not wanting to arrive
Marshal de Broglie, that he be refused
to the Designs that one may have upon him.
It is added, that the Count of Maillebois,
who also took refuge there a Dutchman,
is finally determined to accept the
Commands that they have offered him.
He will have the Grade and Rank of Field-
Marshals, the Difficulty of
investing a Catholic Officer with it is fixed, but
it was finally decided, that the Difference
of Religion was no longer a Mean
of Exclusion. The Count of Maillebois
will prepare everything for his Departure.
