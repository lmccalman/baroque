    Finding myself, a few Days ago,
in the apartment of M. de Puisegur,
where M. Dubucy and several
other persons attached to the
different Offices of War and of the
Navy were then present, in the general
and animated Conversation which was
held there on the affairs of the [Antique] and of Asia,
and notably of the current [Brition]
of France, it was said that France
could not and should not today
wage War, but it was necessary to avoid it
[repondre], that there was perhaps
foundation in it.
    The [tictif] to draw Conjectures
from this Anecdote, Time will
instruct us. The redoubling of Care
and Activity for [d'etendroit] of
[Touze] the most important, to
instruct you of it in advance, as much
as it will be possible for me.
