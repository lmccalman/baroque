I have done everything possible to succeed well; but I have the misfortune of not having succeeded in it. However that does not discourage me from [laisir] all the occasions which could give the marks of my true zeal for the interests of your State.

As there is still some time before the summer quarter comes I will not yet say anything to Sr de St Pierre, touching the designs that one has to seize it; but I will wait about an ordinary before the Deadline to [promenir] them; not that I fear that he would not [s'entenrât] to commit himself; the dowry that he requires, for I know him as a man of probity & incapable of failing his commitments, but that perhaps free at the Court where he promises [intervele] that something which could engage your principals to continue it, & you will be able R. to [laisir] it to you at less, when I present the affair to you there; & if they are [suffir] & the offers, so that they can see that there are no better [Sels] are not the facts of the honor that I wanted to attach to them.
