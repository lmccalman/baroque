Geneva the 11th April 1778.

There is effectively nothing new
happening in the Towns or in the Surroundings.
As I was conveying to you
the preliminary ideas, touching on
our disputes with our Magistracy,
I hasten to send you
the enclosed printed matter, which is sold
everywhere, searches are being made
to discover the Author, and especially, because this
work makes a great impression
with the support of the Citizens & Bourgeois.
Its Content is an abridged
History of the fundamental Laws
of our Republic, and clearly shows
that our Government
is a Democratic Regime,
and that one must demand from it
the code of our Laws, and of the Edicts,
