being troublesome upon this Matter
but I thought it that at the beginning
of my Correspondence I had to lay open
the whole Affairs and then kept
throwing in the rest. De Bernis from with
Amsterdam; who is very anxious to
give me all the Supplies that he
can come at, had wrote me that a
small Quantity only of Gun Powder
