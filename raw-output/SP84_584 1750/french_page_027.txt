Mr Wolters.                                    Whitehall June 16th 1752

                           Sir

                           I have communicated
                           to the Lords Justices,
                           Your Letter of 23d Instant,
                           And their Excellencies are
                           pleased, very much to
                           approve, Your procuring
                           Intelligence of the
                           Proceedings of the Indian
                           Company & your Attention
                           to watch Forbe's motions.
                           I must desire you would
                           Continue to Exert Yourself
                           upon this point, & Send
                           me Notice, of the Said
                           Forbe's Should come
                           over to England.

                           I am &ca

                           Holdernesse
