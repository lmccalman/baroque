composed of Young men of the Regiment,
carrying the Sword for the Corps. One
[siecle] Gentleman per Company,
and one Sergeant [l'aivonier]. Which makes per
Regiment of Infantry, 75 Officers,
nearly 1800 Soldiers. One does not include
in this Number the King's Regiment,
because it amounts to 46 Battalions.
It must be doubled. It amounted
to 2174 Men. Which makes with
the Staff nearly 22000 Men,
in the French Infantry.
         The Active Captains, have by the
2nd Edict, 1200 liv. of Pay, and
the Reformed Captains, 1400 Livres
only. The Captains of
foreign Regiments, draw 2400 Livres
of Pay.
