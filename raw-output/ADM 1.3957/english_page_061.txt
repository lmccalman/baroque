                    Brussels February 18, 1790.

                                Duke Charles is
summoned to Vienna.

All the Generals and Regimental Chiefs
distributed in these Countries, have Orders to be
there on the 28th, with a Vedette in front,
from which a [monique] in Flemish:
Arms, Tents, and Horses, to supply them,
and ready to enter Campaign
at first order.

The Court has renewed Order to pay the
Debts of the deceased Prince Minister.
