Vienna the 14th December 1775.

You will accuse me of
Negligence, and will say, that the English
Gazettes announce to you the events
of which I make no Mention; but
it is a matter of seeing, if these events
are well founded, or pure Conjectures.
I want first to cite you an Example of it.
The Gazette of the Hague of 6 Nov. us
under the Article from Vienna of 30 October,
gives us exclusively news,
where we should see, the Austrian
Gazettes. I had however
counted on my zeal, and was even
[suffisande] to have been faithfully.

Here are the Advantages brought back
since the Porte over the Turks, while the Cases
Russians [vailés] since recently: but
we have, that the Troops have made themselves
Masters of Nicoporte port near
the mouth of the Danube.

culière], that one transported to
