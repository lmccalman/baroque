Since the 12th of the current month,
three to four foreign extraordinary
couriers have arrived here, who have
only changed horses to
go to the Escurial, but until now nothing
has transpired.

The Duke de la Vauguyon, new
ambassador from France, is expected
at any moment, part of
his equipage having already arrived.

Work has begun
on the preparations for the double marriage
in Portugal, which will take place, it is said,
next May.

The Princess of Asturias was
happily delivered of a prince,
on the 15th of the current month.
