Names of Ships for which the Masters made
Affidavit at Rotterdam, in order to procure
Mediterranean Passes.

The Minerva Thomas Amos Munster, for Philadelphia
                                    15 June 1768. (Cleared out)

The Storks, Penhorse Nilson, for Virginia, 15 June 1768.

The Town send, Arthur Reddy, for Carolina, 4 August 1768.
                                    Cleared out at Portsmouth

The Britannia, Alexander Hardie, for Carolina
                                    the 16th Sept.r 1768.

The Mary, John Fourrier, for Carolina, 25th October 1768.

The Prince of Orange, David Dunlop, for the Streights
                                    25th October 1768.

The Hope, John Robertson, for America, 30th Nov.r 1768.

The Speedwell, Robert Wilson, for the West Indies
                                    the 30th November 1768.

This last Vessel has not received her Pass as yet,
for Want of a Persons appearing at the
Admiralty to join in the Bond; and She is
Sailed without the Pass.
