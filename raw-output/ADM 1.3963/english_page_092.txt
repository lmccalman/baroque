H. 28 may N°2

                          Toulon the 16 May 1778.

                                    Le Chevalier le Roy

sent to the Town this Morning for Corsica,
where the Command is charged with examining
the Woods of the Forests, the most suitable for
Construction, & the manner of being able
to deliver them in the future.
        There is an order to careen the Carcabon.
        & the [archine] with permission the 19.
        We sent today to Corsica,
the Official Report of the Marine Regulation
who leaves tomorrow to examine the Project
of a Company, which I propose to
compose, of the manner & number
that the force of 80 pounds & Sixteen the Project
[Cabre], which on one side can be, [contrevoit]
almost less a King, than those who
make them for the others.
        The torrents that we have from [Allège] to make
[fondeller] to the leash, carry poorly until here;
that it is necessary to always avoid some;
we are providing Means of remedying
these Abuses.
                                                    Brest
