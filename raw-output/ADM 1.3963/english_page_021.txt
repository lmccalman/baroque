N° 25. N° 1

                    Paris the 12th January 1776.

                                        It is reported from Toulon,
that the frigate d'Arzette arrived there
on the 4th, bringing back [l'elle a fait don
d'ennemi à Malthe].
    The Count de St Germain continues
his work with the King, but this
work is impenetrable, and
all that one can say about it in
the [Nôller], is pure Conjecture;
all this work will come at once,
and that between now and the Beginning
of Lent.
    The Sr Peau, that there are Edicts
brought to the Parliament, because even
already, who has good
reasons to doubt it.
    The General [Lamprotateur] has written
to the First President, on behalf
of the King, that concerning those Edicts
which were not registered, the
