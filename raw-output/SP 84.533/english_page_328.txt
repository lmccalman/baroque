4°. The voluntary liquidation of the Offices
will be viewed in the present Moment
as an express Renunciation of the
Retention of Registries, concerning
the King of the improbabilities; as having an
striking inconsistency with their
Conduct for Two Months; as being
perjurers, then against the Oath which
essentially binds them to the Deliberations and
petitions by their Companies; as being
Considered for what they are in
the hands of the [Desoateurs].

5°. The chancellor himself is internally
Convinced of this Truth, that instead of
preparing a Confiscation he aims to pronounce,
If he acts for the relief of the Debts
of the State, there is no Intrigue, no
Ruse, no oblique Detour, that he does not employ
to succeed in wresting their
Consents.
