App:                                 Whitehall May 9, 1706

Dear Sir,

        I am directed by the Duke of Grafton to
desire You will acquaint Your Spanish Correspondent that
His Grace approves of his Continuing at the same Time
He desires wishes You would recommend to them to
endeavour to render his Correspondence with You as
authentick & interesting as possible, & that He will
be careful to give the earliest Intelligence He can
of every thing that may appear to be worthy of Notice.

        I am &c

                    W. Travis
