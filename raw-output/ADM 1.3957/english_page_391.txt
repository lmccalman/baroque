H 10 Octob. N°2.

Geneva the 26th September 1772.

They are spreading rumors here
that besides the Reports of the Proximity
of War between England and Spain,
§         there is no doubt that being allied in
it, France will also enter into it.
There is much talk of Armaments
in the Ports of Spain, but there is
as yet only question at Toulon of
gathering all the old Uniforms
§         of this Port, and for this Effect they have suspended
the new Constructions.
They say that there must depart from Piedmont,
or Savoy a Corps of Troops.
There is no question at Genoa of any
§         Construction of Warships.
