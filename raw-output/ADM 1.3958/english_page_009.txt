They write to me from Cartagena, that the
Triomphant had arrived there from Reuple
[s'y] after some Repairs.
It will join M locados from Cadix.
At Rerral they continued to hurry
to arm the Squadron of Don Reggio;
that the abundance of Sailors there
was so great, that they had
sent away nearly 6000 of them, until
further Orders.
It is reckoned that in all Spain
can assemble 42 Thousand Sailors
in condition to Serve.
Several Private individuals have offered
in Case of War, and to equip at
their own expense d.Warships
