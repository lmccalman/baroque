It is said that Mr. de Gravina, who
commands all of our xebecs, has
encountered in the Mediterranean
three Algerians, of which he captured one

Mr. Barcelo, who was summoned
to court, is still detained there
for nearly three weeks: which
leads to suspicion that there is
still question of some other
expedition against the Algerians
for next summer.
