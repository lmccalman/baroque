                                      Paris the 12 February 1776.

                                                    [ It is reported from
Toulon of the 4th, that there is an order to
prepare for Armament One
Ship, 4 Frigates, and 2 Chebeeks;
it is to form a Part
of the Squadron that must be commanded
by the Duke of Chartres. In the
Number of these four Frigates,
there are Two destined to
go relieve two others
in the Levant. ]
         Thirteen Officers from Brest and
         Rochefort have obtained their
         Retirements, the Rol du Rol of Toulon
         have just obtained theirs.
         [ They will send to Brest ], it is from
         Rochefort, a certain Number
         of Vessels, to form there
                                                          the Squadron
