Whitehall July 29th 1761.

Sir
   Having received theKings
Majestys orders for present of the
memo's inclosed, marked
No 1, 2, 3. from hence at time
of the last messenger, I loose no
time in transmitting them to
you.
   In the executing the Commis-
sion, I ought upon the most
part requesting, asking for his
Attestation of the late Order of
the Marquis of the hullish
ceremony, are those, which
particulars or were the
continuation of his Office
servant, I cannot
recommend his Zeal too
strongly to your Serious
consideration, nor doubting but
you will, with your usual zeal,
ability, take the best &most
immediate Steffes of putting it
immediately into execution.
