Ny 24 Janv.

Paris le 17 Janvier 1785.

Nothing at all
interesting is happening here, all is
quiet, and there is no
movement that could indicate
a war. Also one no longer doubts
that an accommodation is
underway.

But it is otherwise in
the Low Countries, they are recruiting there
with all possible activity, they
are filling the supply magazines,
continuously currently already 800
thousand rations, the troops are
arriving there successively, they
have even received orders to hasten
their march. Thus the peace with
Holland, which several gazettes
announce to us as certain,
is not very certain yet;

Louis
