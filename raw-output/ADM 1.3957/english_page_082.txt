58

Regard: A great [éventage] of these will be taken
Hope when she returns.

The King will give Bellienne Tomorrow an
Audience, as well as to 15 or 16 other Foreigners; and he
will receive the others the following day.

It is claimed here that to avoid for the Bishop of
Mury several Heads of decision, the Queen
has not yet determined to name him to the
Place of Pamier Spain, nor to unite the two
Bishoprics together.

The King thought today of working with the
Council which had business to do and He retired at 4 o'clock
contenting both at once the [Pré-visiteur] and
the Controller General.

The Parliament is working on its new
Remonstrances, and awaits this new Project
to include it in the Disputes.

They want to send to Marseille the young Man
who had the Portfolio of the Agent to have
Tickets, the Funds in all of these sorts of
Wheels, but Only the Dispense, or [relieque]
in similar Circumstances. The
Directors to attract the Customers, do
themselves this fraud, by placing there a
Certain number of Men [affidés], who draw
Lots of money of which they make the Remittance
Soon after. The other people are the Dupe of it, who
wait by following the order, & remembering
that they fool them. The Public which had the Press
knows that it is a good affair, and hastens
