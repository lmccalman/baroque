                    Madrid the 5th January 1790.

                                [Si trois de Clent] of my
Health is always my Doctrine in my
Correspondence.
     I write to you today to inform you that
there are absolutely no [Crebis] for
the [Mes] of the South, that [delays] the Warship
of War the St. Laurent, and that there is no
Order to send any for these Voyages.
     It is [true] that from time to time there are posted
at the Portal Warships of War, to
be armed there on Occasion! They have
promised me Notice of it, and when I obtain it you
will have it immediately.
     The Aguila arrived from Callao de Lima
to Lima carries 3 Million Piastres.
I am informed of News of the St. Michael
departed with it, it brings 700 Thousand Piastres.
     The Aventura Carrier of, as they say,
2½ Million Piastres, should follow
in all the Month of April.
