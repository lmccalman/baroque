18 2 May

                    Toulon the 5th April y 92.

         The Squadron which has had Orders
to proceed from Cadiz here, and whose
arrival we await from Day to Day, must
arrive at this moment yesterday at Cartagena, it
with about 60 Transport Vessels,
loaded with Troops. This Squadron must be
composed of Eleven Ships of the Line, of
Two frigates, and of Four Corvettes, namely

for Four Days because of the Illnesses which they
have caused the Rumor to spread.
