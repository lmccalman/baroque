H 2e trag N° 8

                        Paris the 13. March 1776.

                    News was received from Toulon
of the 5th, that on 2 March, the
Squadron continued the Cruise of the Hyères
Islands for a [fauvis] Wind.

            The Sardines, commanded
by M. de Castellanne, which was
extended and for three Months,
has returned, that as soon as the
weather would permit, one
will renew the [livres], to
arrange it in the [Carquois],
under the Orders of M. de Rortlan.

            The same vessel of the Squadron
of this brigade, the Mignonne,
commanded by M. de Grancourt,
set Sail for the Levant.

            The work of the [Carffe] advances
here is the new depth [news] of this
Port.
