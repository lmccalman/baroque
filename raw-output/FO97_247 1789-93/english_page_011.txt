Rotterdam 16: Novr: 1790

Sir,

In Consequence of Orders from Lord
Auckland to Messrs Crumhard, to take my
Bills upon You, for the Arrears of the
Allowance granted to Mr: Dollars, and
myself, and regularly for every Quarter
as they shall become due, I have this
day taken the Liberty to draw upon You
their Order, the Sum of Three Hundred
Pounds Sterling, being for the Quarters
due the 25th July, 25th of June, and
29th of September last; and shall continue
drawing upon You Quarterly, beginning
with next Christmas Quarter the Sum of
One Hundred Pounds Sterling for Quarter.
This Affair being thus arranged and
fixed, I think it needless for me to trouble
You with a Letter of Advice every time
