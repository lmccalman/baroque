Vessels arrive each day from
America in our different
ports, but especially at Cadix, and at
Corogne, coming from Buenos-ayres, from
Havanna, from Caracas, and from Carthagena,
with rich cargoes of Sugar,
cocoa, Indigo, cochineal, logwood,
and two Million strong piastres
have arrived for the King.

The King has just named Don
Bernard de Galvez, Captain General
of the Island of Cuba, of the two Floridas, of
Louisiana and of [Couloir] our Allies
[ou vont et fous le vent].

The viceroyalty of Peru, has been
given to the Chevalier de Croix.

It is said that Mr de Bassadale,
ambassador of Naples is recalled
by His Court
