That day, MY LORDS, when you
received the sacred deposit of sover-
eign power, was the day of fidelity, the
national [oratère] which distinguishes us,
was growing obscure amidst our ideas;
this People idolatrous of its Kings, car-
ried far from itself, seduced by 
false maxims, floated uncertain, and
seemed to misrecognize the hand that
governs it: you have fixed it for-
ever under this paternal hand; in pre-
serving it for its legitimate Masters,
you have restored it to its own virtues.
