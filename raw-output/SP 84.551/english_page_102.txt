Last Saturday there arrived here
an extraordinary Courier from
France, who was sent back to Madeira
having been preceded by another
on Monday.

During all of last Week
there have been continually
extraordinary Councils,
and from it to the Court at the time of the Departure
of the Dispatches of Two Packet boats,
of the Infante, and that of the Eugenie,
arrived at Corunna, from Montevideo
on the 19th and the 20th of this last.

The Courier from Spain of the 8th here,
which is if one can has dispatched
Orders, to bring back
the Majority of the troops that
we have on the frontier,
of which 4 Regiments of Infantry
must go embark
at Cadiz, with two others, for
Bueno[s]
