The 28th June, the Dorade, commanded by
Commander de la Bourdonnaye, Ship's
Lieutenant, arrived from Bayonne

The 4th July, the Serpent, commanded by
Mr de St Pierre, Ship's Lieutenant
anchored in the Roads, coming from
Martinique.

                    At Rochefort.

The 2nd July they launched into the water the
Vessel the Genereux, of 74 cannons.
Some days before they had
launched the Frigate la Bonone of 36 Cannons.
