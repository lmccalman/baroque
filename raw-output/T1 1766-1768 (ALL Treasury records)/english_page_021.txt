Nameur Jan'y 29 1763
                                                                                                    305

Sir

    Mr Wilkins, His Majestys Agent
at the Hague, haveing drawn a Bill on
Mr Davies for two hundred thirty three
pounds & seventeen Shillings for
Disbursements for Secret Service; Mr
Secretary Conway has directed me to
desire you will be pleased to move the
Lords of the Treasury, that they will issue
my give the necessary Directions for
his Payment hereof.

    I am Sir, Your most dutifull
                    humble Servant
                            Wm Frazeer
