Rotterdam this 2 June 1778.

My Lord

        [Pray] & the honor to send you
enclosed the Account of the Coal of which
Correspondent of [Muscain], for his
Quarter, as well as the 3rd of the Month of March
last, the amount is 127. 18. Sterling.
and if it please You to give order, by
which Sum I will be reimbursed in the Receipt
of all by the Bearer, for [choix de Tran]
Mister Williams Davisson of the Treasury,
at 21 Days from Date, to the Order of Miss
Willemijn Davisson and Company.

I have the honor to be with profound
Respect
                        My Lord


                        Your very humble and
                        very obedient & devoted
                        W. Wolters.



My Lord Halifax.
