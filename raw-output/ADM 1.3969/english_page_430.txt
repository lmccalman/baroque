I heard a Dutchman say to
a Dutchman. 20 Thousand Men of
our troops will suffice to put to
flight all your Mercenaries, and conquer
the disunited Provinces. The Dutchman
replied: They need only appear
these 20 Thousand Men, we will buy them
on the spot. This Response is
not a Boast, for the Emperor
can expect, if he sends an Army
against the Dutch, to lose of it
in the First Campaign, at least
half through Desertion.
