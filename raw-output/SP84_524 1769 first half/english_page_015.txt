Army of Corsica
the 6th Kerguentain 1769.

On the 4th May arriving the Division of Messrs
d'Arcambal [tiret] from the head of the Corps of
the army; and the gates of the entire right of the Camp
of Aletto, to cover the Resupply of this,
while maintaining on this Side only
posts part of Biguglia, so that it Resupplies,
and has carried in Detachment in the depths of the
Gulf, to go around Bavgco.

On the 5th at 2 Hours in the Morning, all the Army
marched forward, and at 9 Hours Messrs
d'Arcambal [tiret] temporarily the Villages
Venzolasca, the Volunteers took the
Viladeculles, and the Rgt of Provence the Camponne
had already taken with the Brigade of Saint-Pierre
d'Ornetto; and we made the night and the
to the same Rgt to go [Debande] of Zafera.
the Count of Vaux made only Bouvons at the head.
The Column of Bonaveri and the depth of the Corps
wanted the outside the 5 Arms of Msr de Boufflers
and of Messrs de Drolonbert; this month had taken
Murado.

The Count of Marboeuf, the Left, at the

the Posts of the enemy more the [terres avisantes]
