DELIVERED

Friday 10 May, 1776.

MAGNIFICENT AND VERY HONORED
LORDS.

THE General Code of Laws of the State
has been, for a very long time, the object
of the wishes of the Citizens & Bourgeois.

This work was ordered by the Legis-
lator, in article XLII. of the Edict of
1738.
