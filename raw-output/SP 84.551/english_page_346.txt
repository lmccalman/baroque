Madrid the 26th September 3776.

My News from
Cadix of the 17th of the Current month reported,
that the Squadron of Don Michel Gaston,
which had been destined to cruise
on the Coasts of Portugal, had
[ilots] departed, without anyone knowing
its destination, and that the Squadron Chief
did not even have it, except when
he would open the sealed Packet
from the Court, at a certain indicated
Distance.

And that the Expedition of Mr. de
Sovellos (which I had very problematic
as to the truth of its destination)
appeared to be real only for
Michebergen.
