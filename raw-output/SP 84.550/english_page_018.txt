Stagecoaches by Cuidans to the
Kingdom, to the Market, thus these
Regules of Penna.

The Baron d'Ogny, Intendant
General of the Posts, has obtained from the
King, the approval of the Chamber
of Grand Master of Ceremonies
of the Order of St Louis; in
Consequence he will wear the
Grand Red Cordon, and the
Badge, He surely swears
into the hands of the King.

The Intendant of Bordeaux,
M. de Clugny, has gone as
interim to govern the Intendancies
of Pau and Auch; in the Place
of M. Fournell, who has been
dismissed; the latter in
Despair, strikes at the
Throat.
