171.

The Soldiers of the Company of Soldiers
are sent, upon [novram] to the Company
of Soldiers of their Garrison, and they will be
Commanded with the Director of New
Account to the Ministry, at least Half
of the Clerks of this Company are going to be
dismissed in the Month of July.

My Letters from Berlin of April 30 say,
that despite the Executions that are being carried out,
there are more Bandits than ever in
these Islands, it has been resolved to make Boats
in the Woods, to arrest and to kill all
that will be found there.

There is presently that Madame de Barry
desires to go with [Deux de Barilges] during
the wedding festivities, but that is not certain.

We have had by a Courier that the King
of Prussia had been very ill with the Gout,
but that he was better. His Death would make
a great Event in Europe.
