Rotterdam 5 February 1798.

My Lord

        Being drawn upon by my Madrid
Convoy pursuant to His Majestys Orders of the 8th of
December 1797, I have the honor of this
to hand enclosed to your Excellency an exact
Account of the said Draft.
        In consequence I beg leave to take the liberty,
as usual to draw by the next Post for
Â£80: 4: 6 Sterling, being one half of my
Sallary, the other half being directed
to the Admiralty, upon Mr. Trotter in
Davis Bay of of the Treasury, by my Bills
of Exchange payable at Sight after Date
to the order of Messieurs William
Davidson and Company of this place.
        I have the honor to enclose
two letters respecting the disposal of
the Spanish Navy, and State of
embarkation of Troops in the Course
of last year.
        I have again wrote to my Spanish
Correspondent to engage his Attention
this year on the 8th of September next.

&c

To the Earl of Malmesbury.
