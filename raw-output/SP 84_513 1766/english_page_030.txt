Rotterdam Juin 6e 1766

Sir -

I have advised from my correspondents at Paris, upon whom the intelligence from the Port all rest, that a Project for taking containing part of the copy of the new loan of the french Navy, has been intercepted in the Paris post Office, & which the consequences may be traced to a correspondence that has been so useful for many years, and particularly in his successor Waa.

I have directed my clerk not those, in my absence
