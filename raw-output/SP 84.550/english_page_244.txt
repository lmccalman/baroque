While awaiting the Arrival of Messrs
de Clugny, Messrs Bertin, [Peyve] all
the urgent Dispatches.

The same day last Sunday
Mr de Senac, was at the instance of the Minister,
was named Keeper of [Leur 9] of War
in conformity with the Government in
[Valedictorien].

The same within a [paye], Mr the Sr
Germain, who was only Secretary
of State S., was entered into the Council
as Minister of State, and after
to the government, which holds at the same time place
of a Controller of War, composed
of 4 Field Marshals, and an
Intendant, and 4 Commissioners.

One assures likewise, that there
will be a Council for the Controller of
Generals, and that Mr de Maurepas
will be the Chief of it.
