The General of the Ombre de l'marcq:
the prince of Cantelmes.

The N***, and the family, I have the arms
carried well to Fontainebleau, and
that is the only News that I can
still give you of the Court.

        Brussels, October 22, 1772.

        There arrived in
this City two Companies of
Cannoneers, of 60. Artillerymen, and
of 40. Laborers per Company,
making together 240. heads, coming
from Malines, and departing today
for Luxembourg, and from there further
into Germany.

There remain at Malines,
only One hundred men, who work
continuously in the Laboratory,
and another Hundred men, who are called
the Founders; these are all
Germans; they have orders to complete
all the works begun.
