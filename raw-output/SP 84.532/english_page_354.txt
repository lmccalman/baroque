Brussels the 28th March 1793.

All the troops in
the Low Countries are occupied with the preparation
for Departure, and with forming an army for
the service of the King of France, under his States
incapable for the March, which
will guard the Country.

A Rumor [tomber le fruit entendres]
that some French Regiments
will come there to take the place of the
Subsidized troops, to guard
the Country.
