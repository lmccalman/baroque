Rotterdam July 14, 1765

my Lord Duke


In obedience to the commands
contained in the honour of your
Grace's letter of the 12th instant, I have
the honour to transmit to your grace
the intelligences, which will be
continued every post.

With these I send also a copy of
the state of the Trade carried on
between the British Ports and those
