The Prince of Soubise departed presently [bourg],
of the small Army must be assembled on the 11th of this
Month; it will immediately set out on March, to move
into the upper Palatinate and Bavaria, and from there
proceed either to Bohemia, or to 100 M from Ratisbon.

Our Army of Westphalia was
still at Hols Minden; there is to go to Hameln
a forest of 5 leagues, and Defiles, the Marshal has
sent Detachments to clear this forest,
arrange Roads, and take Posts forward
on the Reine; All is ready for the Siege of Hameln.

The Duke of Cumberland has the right at this Town, and
the left at Steinbock; The Marquis de Bereur [se]
Marshal of Camp, was detached from Cassel with
a Corps, to move on the Reine at a
Height that our Army; he took Göttingen
in passing, where he made 200 Prisoners, and took
4 Pieces of Cannon: We shall have soon the
News of a Battle from that Side, or that
of the Retreat of the Duke of Cumberland.

There is not great Risk to the Royal Treasury
for the loan of 40 Million, it [fêe] [se]
will fill up however.
