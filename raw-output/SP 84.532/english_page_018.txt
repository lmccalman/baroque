Brussels the 25th December 1770.

The News from Paris
inform us of the exile of the Duke
of Choiseult.
   Madame de Choiseult has just
arrived here, and She is lodged at
the Hotel of the Prince de la Tour and
Taxis. Saturday She dined at
His Royal Highness's, and Supped
at the Prince of Stahrenberg's.
   All the Officers in the Service of
France who have been on Semester in
this Country, have Orders to return
to their Regiments in the
Military.
