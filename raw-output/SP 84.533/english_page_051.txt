Rochefort the 11th July 1775.

                    We would have good
company here the 19th of this Month, as The
Count d'Herouville must arrive to
make His Inspection, several General
Officers will be here to
receive Him.

Mr de Boynes has sent order to
Mr de Voltdée, to go to The Court,
to account for His Conduct,
this General has given so much [Regissons],
[demonté] Mr de La Brisoliere, Lieutenant
of Vessels, and Mr de Guimond good
Second, whom he sent back to Touraine
after having given The Command
of The frigate to Mr de Pardines.
These two Officers arrived in Touraine
Have Constituted themselves Prisoners to
The Admiral at Brest, and have made to the
Memoirs of Their Conduct towards Mr
de Voltdée, one has been sent a
Copy to the Minister, [Jeune à Charger]
