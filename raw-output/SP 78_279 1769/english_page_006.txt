multitude of carts loaded with wine that
passed through. This town is situated on the Loire in
a beautiful plain at the foot of hillsides covered
with vines. We embarked there to
descend to Nantes. the first day
we slept at Ancenis. The next day
we arrived at Nantes where we stayed
some days. This town is large well
built and filled with an immense population. It
conducts considerable commerce for the Islands
of America and for Europe. It is from this
town that most of the Ships depart that
go to St Domingue. We went there to see
the vessels that were being prepared for this Island.
We saw some of them that were on
the point of departing. We embarked
on one of these vessels that was to depart the 15th
of the month. We made our farewells to all our
friends and acquaintances and we departed the 15th
