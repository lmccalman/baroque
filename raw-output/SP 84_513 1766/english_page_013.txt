The Parliament has been expressly summoned to Versailles
with the Projectors, the King wishes to [entretée] it.
The Parliament of Tounefels is summoned
from today in eight to the King for the same
treaty as the others, the Projectors as
[conge] the Parliaments, and the payments until the month
of August do not change at all.

The King [de est demence entés] of the Parliament
of Tounefels to return; and she rejoins
Bordeaux; She was [admeengratié] on Tuesdays
at the Wood; however the [Mailleurs] assure
that She is out of Danger; They make in Paris
prayers of 40 Hours for
Her; the Hunt at M. Genevoire has
recommenced.

Brussels the 30 March 1768.

There arrived from Bohemia
100 [Revues], all fine Men, for
the Regiment of Stampach being part
of the Regiment of Champagne and this one [plustions]
out of State to serve.

Saturday General Ferraris who to make
Review 400 Artillerists and Artificers;
he was very content; and yesterday Sunday
he examined Twenty four Pieces of
Campaign conducted there from the Regiments of Poitou.

[Il vostien] Baillet Journal that the Queen
of France is dead.
