687                                  Bordeaux 1781


                                First

                    St. Hildefons the 28th August 1776.

                                News has just arrived
from Cadiz, of the entry
of the Ships which come from
Cartagena with 2 Million
Piastres for the King's Account:
not yet knowing what they
bring for the Account of
Private Individuals, nor that
the List has not yet been
received.

        These Ships have brought
the News, of the arrival at Vera
Cruz and at the Port of Honduras.

        Mr. de Souville is totally
deprived of everything, and destined
to die Deliberately, leaving the Assets
that he had either in Cadiz for
some Time, or in Cartagena
