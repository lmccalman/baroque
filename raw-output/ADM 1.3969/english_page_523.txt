But the Negotiations are not
yet sufficiently advanced to
be able to promise oneself a Success
as prompt and as easy [s'ensuit].
What also persuades that the
Court of Vienna is now inclined
to a suitable Arrangement
is that Count
Mercy is almost every
Day at the General of Hostilities' and it is
possible that he stops there and that he
prepares the Ways for an
Accommodation.

Despite the intimate Relations
that exist between the Emperor and
the Empress of Russia,
despite all that Friendship
has been able to dictate to this last [Instance]
in favor of her August Ally,
one is well persuaded that the Empress
will not make great efforts in his favor.
