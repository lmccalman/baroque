The Affair of M. de Sennes:
Intendant of Valenciennes, is
concluded. He was at the same time
Intendant of the Mints; He has
confusedly refused to work
with the Committee of the Mints.

He was sent to Fort
l'Evêque in the Province, and M.
de St Germain who served, to
know all the Papers.

Mde Houaine does not count
at the Court.

The Marquis d'Esgriquetot at
the Camp of the Colonelle Generale,
made felt the Appellation of the Exactitude
of his Regiment, to the marquis
de Boutellé, as only my
Quarter, and that makes the [brelatte].
that the Right of the Charge fulfills it
to the Appellation: This Refusal has been
well received at the Court, and it is said that
the Right will be suppressed.
