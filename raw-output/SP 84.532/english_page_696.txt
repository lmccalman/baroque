One infers that several Councillors
in the Parlement, continually
looked upon unfavorably by the Procureur General,
who desires [l'eclateux], want either to
make him retire, or retire him themselves.

One always speaks of a Lit de
Justice, and of the Suppression of three
Parlements, one even says, that the
horses who were proposed to the Council,
have from several opinions observed,
that the Parlement of Paris was
not yet well established, and that the
King had approved it.
