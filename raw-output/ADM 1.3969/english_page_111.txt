By 17 June 1789.

Cadiz the 19th May 1788

Two Warships
and a Bilander have received Orders
today to put themselves in
[Erriviere] between the Capes. It is believed
that they will go there to await the Squadron
and the Convoy from Havana which
it is hoped, will appear in
these Waters before the 20th June.

Several Xebecs with Two
Frigates, set Sail at
the same Time to go into the
Mediterranean, to seek a
part of the Convoy, which for Two
Months swarm there, and where They have
captured several Catalan
and Italian Ships.

M. des Valin commanding the
French Ship the Reflechie of
64 Cannons, will set Sail Tomorrow.
He received today Packets
from the Court. He goes to Martinique;
