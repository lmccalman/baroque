203.

The Assembly concerning Vienna yielded yesterday
some Fabrics that it [manoit] to receive,
there was no more Reality in the World.
Our letters from Rome always announce
a great Discontent of the Cardinals
of the Court, about the Influence that the Nuncio
has and they consult no one, also the affairs
hardly advance.

All is quiet in Malta. The
Knights make many efforts to arm
some Vessels which in 200. Masts. This Order
is quite intrigued that the King of Naples
has deported [Mon Boulligue] who was at the
Nomination of the Grand Master, it is believed
that this favorite will not be replaced at all,
As one does not [déguillle] from the Order of the
most beautiful Rights.


Brussels the 9th July 1770.

The Prince of Starhemberg
was Friday with the [bryonieur] of Vienna,
to visit the works of the Citadel of Antwerp,
as well as the Magazines.

The said Minister [justifié ses succées] to
the Order Saturday, that all absent Officers
and the [talents] who have Leaves, must
be returned to their Corps within 10 Days, under
Penalty of Dismissal.
