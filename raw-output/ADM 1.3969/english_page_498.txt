By Order

Genoa, December 5, 1784.

Various Commissions and Merchandise
have come to our Entrepreneurs
and Builders, since the time they
had, the Number of Vessels that have
been sold, has not been considerable;
among others, Mr. Odriella, from the Riviera
of Arenza sold a Polacre of 18
Cannons, and of 3 Thousand Quintals,
to some French; Mr. Parodi sold
Two [Balottes] to the King of Naples;
Mr. Durotti from Savona sold
a Vessel of 24 Cannons, and fully
equipped, to the King of Spain;
In [Domaine] last were purchased
at auction four Transport Vessels
for the same Sovereign, with Two
Gunboats. The Antonin brothers,
from St. Remo, and Mr. Borea, sold
to some Americans Two Hookers.
