They are all found in poor
condition, the Glorieux is less so.
the Bretagne must be refitted;
there will be a shortage of Wood for all
these Refittings.
     They were obliged to send away
Spinners, for lack of Hemp.
     They are preparing at Rochefort the
pendant, and the Refleshi; there
is a shortage of Oakum there, it is
Wretched.
