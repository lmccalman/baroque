XII.

THERE shall be deducted from the net profit of said Administration & Management, & after the payment of sums which shall be fixed for indemnities due for the suppression of other Lotteries, Two sous per livre of the fund which shall be formed, of which His Majesty has reserved the particular disposition; for which purpose said Two sous per livre of the profit.

Which, shall be paid by the general Cashier of the Administration, into the hands of the Treasurer who shall be appointed by His Majesty.

XIII.

THE decrees & regulations rendered concerning the Administrations & Management, both of the Lottery of the Royal Military School, and of that of the Foundlings & of Piety, shall continue to be executed for the Royal Lottery of France, as well as for said united Lotteries, according to their form & tenor, in what is not contrary to the present decree.

XIV.

THE sieurs Intendants & Commissioners dispatched in the provinces & généralités of the kingdom, & the sieur Lieutenant General of Police for the city & suburbs of Paris, shall have cognizance of all disputes relating to said Lotteries & to the Administration of the same. His Majesty attributing to them all Court & Jurisdiction necessary for this purpose, save appeal to the Council: His Majesty forbids all Courts & other Judges to take cognizance of said disputes, & parties to seek recourse elsewhere than before said sieurs Commissioners, under penalty of nullity & cassation of proceedings, & of all costs, damages & interests. DONE in the Council of State of the King; His Majesty being present therein, held at Marly the thirtieth of June one thousand seven hundred seventy-six. Signed BERTIN.


AT PARIS: FROM THE ROYAL PRESS. 1776.
