Paris the 12th January 1784.

A Courier departed from
Constantinople the 16th December, and
arrived at Versailles these last days,
with the Dispatches of the Count de St Priest,
they respond to the orders of the 28th October,
and of the 11th November, sent by two
extraordinary Couriers, the Divan felicitates
H.C.M. in [perspection], and acting ordinary
Donor, asked for Time to
respond to these pressing Insinuations,
and three Days after the Departure of the
Courier, It was to assemble to
take into Consideration, the important
Object that was to decide War,
or Peace.
