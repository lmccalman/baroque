Although the Peace Treaties
have appeared in the English Papers,
they do not yet come out here from
the Royal Printing House.

It is still said that Mr D'Ormesson
will be retiring, and they even name
the Archbishop of Toulouse to
succeed him.

The eldest son of Mlle Vigentives
has been to be named the day before yesterday
Captain Colonel of the Door Guards,
Mr de Selle having given the Resignation
of this Position.

Captain Royle has arrived
here; He is going to Fontainebleau to thank
Mr de Vergennes, for the benevolent
recommendation to which He
owes his Life.

Madrid
