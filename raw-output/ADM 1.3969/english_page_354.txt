The west and northwest winds
burden the sea as it would be
necessary to enter the passage
without a pilot who knows the
instantaneous situation of the bar, one is forced
in such a case to put in at Passage
Spanish port. This anchorage is very
advantageous, and one must not
neglect it. For it is the only one for a
loaded vessel, being off Bayonne,
if one finds oneself burdened by winds
from the south to the west, one must gain without
hesitation Bordeaux, or the Pertuis,
