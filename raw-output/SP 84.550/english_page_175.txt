The Second Suppresses the Chiefs
of General Inspectors of the Troops,
while preserving their Salaries
of 8 Thousand Livres, [des en avisans]
12 Thousand more for their Tour.

The third orders, that there will
be withheld only 2 Sols per man
from the Soldier, for the Ration and Munition
Bread; which increases
his Pay by nearly 2 Sols.

The Fourth concerns, the
Tours to provide to the [Paroles]
of the Corps.

The Fifth Suppresses the
Venality of Military Employments;
at each Transfer these Employments
will lose a Quarter of the finance
[cy-forte], that at the Fourth, the King
will find it Free, without it
costing him and in the meantime,
the inheriting Relations have Military

Employments
