                                                B 30 Sept: No 1


                        Brest the 30th September 1776

            The Ministry has just
sent Order, to increase the
Companies of the Marine Troops
by 20 men. These troops in the
three Ports were numbering 99
Companies at One hundred men per
Company. There are found on this occasion
120; they were previously
only at 80, that makes 70 more
since three Years.
            Messrs Boscawen, and Keppel,
command the two Combined
Squadrons currently our
Colonies; they are heading for
Ste Marie de Vennes.
            One awaits the day of the order
of Evolution in the war, or less
for these Evolutions one had doubled
the Crew, and the two Masters, one
[décoûtent] them by Success in proportion
by other Vessels.
