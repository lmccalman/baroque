14th of August

Paris the 8th August 1782

The Works in the Port
of Toulon are in the greatest
Activity; they received the order to prepare
several Millions of Pounds of
Biscuit, as if there should be
some upcoming Embarkation.
The Sovereign to give the [augeau]
that was expected at Toulon, or which has
arrived there, I do not know. The Armament
made from Cartagena, the States have
remarked that it would appear a Matter
X se X before Mr Barcelo would be
being before Algiers or the contrary
Winds that he had.

All the Vessels that were waiting-
at Brest, have likewise returned
    Few Days after the arrival
of the Sagittarius, which came from the Coasts
of Africa, one saw arrive the
Vessel the America of 74 Cannons given
to the King by the Congress.
