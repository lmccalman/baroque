This affair gives the Magistrates more
uneasiness than even the Russian treaty, as for
a great number of years they have had the dis-
position of places which every magistrate given
by turns, by whom ever he pleased.

The Zeal of our Burgesses has carried this
still further, and I hear that an other petition is to be
presented at the same time to desire that the
Regents of this Town may please to propose to the
Council especially to make the Stadtholdership
Hereditary and to fixe it in the female line
of the house of Orange in case the Prince Stadtholder
should dye without male issue, this being handed to
magistracy by some of the Burgesses I advised them
not to be hasty in this affair but to consult some
of the Regents that they could most depend upon.
