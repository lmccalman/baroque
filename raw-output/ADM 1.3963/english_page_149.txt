N°9 Sept N°22

Toulon the 22nd August 1778.

If she carries [brodyeurs] this Party arrived in
24 hours of crossing, and very fortunately.
She brought up D.Hlerarde, Duffour, the
[Hyglenirs], the Vaughan Higgliffe, the
[Mediatags], N'bois and three [Holgueres], 1 year 7.
in [Assire] and by the Capt. le Gogues
and another person.

She believed she owed the Mediterranean to
a Vessel of the [Inemes] of 74 cannons of 4.
[monthe] of 14 Cannons, and three large
[bondes]: having with them three Vessels
of [Balestates], uniformed in Wars,
all these Ships were leaving
Cartagena, and appeared to be making
Course for Gibraltar.

I raise at her arrival the [Sagittaires],
[l'arcjsambre] N°2, 82 Horses [Salambres],
2 the crossing from Marseilles, and
[Surrescant] from America, That is
to say, from the Cape.
From the Church.
