    The Ship the Bourgogne was
lost through the fault of the sailing Pilot,
the greater Part of the Crew
saved itself.
    There entered here yesterday a Polacre
named the Conception, departed from
the Isle of Bourbon on the 26th of December.
She reports, that Mr de Bussy had
departed for India with 3 Ships
of War, and 10 Transports.
    One adds that she left at
the Cape of Good Hope the Dutch
Squadron, which was still
anchored there at the beginning of
the Month of February.
