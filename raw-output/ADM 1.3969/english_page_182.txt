Madrid the 24th July 1783

The Letters from Alicante report
that our [Escadrivion] is the Mayer
under the Orders of Don Barrolo, continues
in Sight of this Port, struggling against
the Winds. And that Two Vessels
[anonimosos] had entered there, to
repair the Damages suffered from
a Gust of Wind.

The Letters from Havana
of the 7th June report, that Mr Solano
was ready to depart with his Squadron
to return to Europe
with the Troops of the [Rag], but that
