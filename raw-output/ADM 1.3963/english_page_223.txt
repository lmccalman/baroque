There is News from Lisbon
which reports, that the King of
Portugal still unwell, having
lost the Use of his Senses, has been
declared in dementia, and his
Confessor the Queen has been
declared Regent. Since as
She loves the King of Spain
her Brother very much, it is hoped
that She will profit from the Circumstance,
to give Satisfaction to
Spain, and arrange the
Affairs.
