Rotterdam the 2nd February 1776.

Mylord.

I have the Honour to send you
enclosed herewith the Account of the [Frailes] of the
Consignees of Madrid, which
amount to £146. 18. Sterling,
of which the Half is £73. 9. 6. Sterling,
for the Quarter [d'en entre] the 3rd of
December last. By the next
Post I will take the Liberty
to draw on the said M. Herries,
through Monsieur William Davis
of the Hague, at 6 Days of
default William Davidsonn
& Company.

I have the Honour to be with
a profound Respect
Mylord.

Your very-humble and
very-Obedient Servant
M. Wolters

Earl of Suffolk.
