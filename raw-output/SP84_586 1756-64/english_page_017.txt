Of this tenth                                                                                             Received. For. M.on The 19. 1823

                    today eighth day of March one thousand seven
                    hundred ninety two before us municipal officers
                    of the Commune of Bordeaux
                    and undersigned notables appeared Sieur Bremontier one of the
                    four Engineers of Bridges and Roads of the
                    Department of Gironde who declared to us that he
                    was bearer of the Sum of fourteen thousand five hundred francs
                    amount of a warrant from the General Paymaster of the
                    Department of Gironde on the Receiver
                    of said Department, which warrant dated
                    first March last, to be employed to continue
                    the project of works of the Digestion, Drainage
                    and improvement of the marsh of Bruges, we have
                    delivered to him said warrant to make use of it
                    Conforming to its destination -                                            Definitive Copies
