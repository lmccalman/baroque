Rotterdam 2 February 1798

My Lord

Here is the Honour of sending you enclosed the Account of the draft of the Correspondents of Messrs. for ten Grontter received the 30th December last, Amounting to £97.8 Sterling, of which the amount is £105.14.8 Sterling; for which Sum I take the liberty of drawing for the present Letter on Monsieur Denis of the treasury, to the order of Messieurs William Davidson & Company.

I have the Honour of being with a profound Respect

My Lord

Your very humble &
very Obedient Servant
M. Walters
