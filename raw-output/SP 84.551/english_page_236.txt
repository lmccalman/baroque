                    Toulon, August 23, 1776.

                    The Ship the Victoire
returned to this Port yesterday; work is being done on
its disarmament [by] half of what
[en part] found in a very
condemnable State.

The Minister sends word that the
Mission of Mr. De bon, being finished
in the Strait, he should arrive
shortly in Toulon, she will remain in
the roadstead, and will live [au jour nalen], until
new orders.
