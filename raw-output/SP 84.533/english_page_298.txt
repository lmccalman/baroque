The King has named two New
Presidents à Mortier, for the Parlement,
they will be received at the Reopening, it is Mr
de Mesangon Master of Requests,
son of the Chancellor, and Mr des Raiseaux,
son of the former President at Connaiguer.

There is an Edit rendered in the Council
which [L'see] in charges all the Places
of Municipal officers of the Towns
& Boroughs of the Kingdom.

Mr le Ryaulles former President
à Mortier, has obtained the [Bonneffe] of
the first [huiron] who will voyage in
the Gendarmerie. The son of Mr
Hoquart Counselor of the King's Parlement,
also bears the Port of Arms;
he is chief [d'Erré] of the Corps.

One still speaks at the Court of the
upcoming Retirement of Mr de Monteyoard;
he will be given a Governorship, and the
Blue Ribbon, and the Duke of Aiguillon
will have the two Departments.
