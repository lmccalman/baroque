☙ The ordinance for the [N'afforme] of Men
of Province and Compiègne, the [Comite] of Horses,
the [appetisseur] and the Infantry, is changed and it
deprives the Deputation; It is Tomorrow that it
will take effect at Court.

☙ It is said Tomorrow that no more than 104 will be established,
and that 20 Thousand Men in French Companies
of the Marine.

☙ The King will not leave Compiègne until Monday
to be on the 29th. The Masters of the Court have already
returned to Paris.

☙ I have received news about the Fate of the two lower
Parliaments to the E.G.C. of the [deffartissements]
in a Council of Dispatches held Saturday
at Compiègne, in Consequence Marshal
de Richelieu, and M. Bertrand, are going to
Bordeaux, to examine it and render a
Parliament at Versailles and that of Beaune
which is nothing other than a Superior Council
which receives the Registrations of the Parliament
of Paris.

☙ That in this Council they have transported to Port
the [suffit] heard in that he comes to his home
only in [nomelli].

It is said today that the Comte de Roy E.G. will be
commander in Bretagne, in Place of the Duc
de Duras.
