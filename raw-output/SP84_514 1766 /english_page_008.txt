The Chevalier de Lastic, First
Gentleman of the Duke of Penthièvre,
prepares to leave for Chambord
after receiving and putting in possession
[fait] the Princes of Condé and Conti;
Madame the Countess of Marche will
conduct him and will have moreover to be [enquête]
near His Person.

M. de Mainçon Counselor and Squire
in the Constable and General in the Place of
M. de la Porte Banker of the Court,
who relinquishes it, M. de Longchamp is
appointed to the Place of M. de Mainçon
for the Treasury of the Stamp of Spain.

The Marquis de Marigny, Uncle
of the [Frue] Marquise de Pompadour,
will marry Mademoiselle Filleuil
daughter of the Concierge of the Château de
Choisy, who is very pretty.
