673.                                   the 30th December 1770

Madrid the 9th December 1770.

                    The News from the

North always say the same things, that everything continues
to marvel the Turks, and one counts on Peace
Announcing itself [clos dring] one should have
had much of true [bourges] the Peace
in its last.

At the News says the island [nefait Manifester]
the Packet of which parliament of the Navy.

I am informed from Cartagena, that
several Catholic Vessels convoyed
by two Xebecs, have carried there a
quantity of Munitions of War for
the fortifications on land. And that more
than 3000 men work daily
to put this Place in a State of Defense.
It is said in general, that never has one
had similar Preparations in
this Kingdom.

One will see in the said Post a
Relation of the Earthquakes, of which
[Louie] has already spoken to me

I am written from the Street, that the report
circulates in this Capital city, that
