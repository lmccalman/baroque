Friday 184.

Madrid, November 20, 1710.

We continue here all
our Marches and to make progress in all
good order, in such a way as can bring in the
finances, it is claimed that he has so much or so much
the state, and that in case of need, there are
very great Resources in this Country,
which one neglects to profit from for
subsistence, but if we were more pressed,
not the good.

The Spaniards who nourish us
should Recommend [element] & amicably
between the two Parties, I followed the advice of
people who are in the places most
extraordinarily intelligent, who
[disconviennent] me to do it to put
at Bayonne as Chief of Expenses. One
had here very many People to
support the King, both some financiers
and some other elements, which does not
fail to worry the Dutch.

A Private individual, named Valdemas and
Spanish, who is [en monstre] of the
Excellent names and particularly
[gage leurs] from here, has orders to
return the road and the great [de
bagage], and the two other Battalions

will arrive
