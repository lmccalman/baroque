N° 83.

                    Paris the 4th October 1776.

                              The works
ordered at Portefontaine by the
Command of Coulon of the 26th,
that the division of l'Ocoudre and Boulin
we only expected on the 30th of this
month, has returned to the place,
that it awaits the Orders of the Court.
No Orders have yet been
given there: they were expecting
the Count d'Orburque, but one
cannot know the preparations;
they have doubled the Workers,
and they are made to work [fidés]
and Sundays.
                    As for the strong order, they
continue the arrangements named,
to name the three Dangerous
attacks that are made there. Thus,
the works that [mon des pusiliées],
are made from one side to another, which one
prepares.                        Despite
