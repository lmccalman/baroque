                    Brussels the 18th February 1770.

                    All Officers
must provide themselves with Horses for
the 25th of March, the time fixed for them for the
Review of the Commissioner of Wars
in the Garrisons. Those which
are lacking for the completion of the
Cavalry, must be furnished
for the end of the present Month.
   One must also furnish for the
same term to all the Infantry
Regiments two Chevaux de frise
per Company.
