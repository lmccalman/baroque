One sees the brave Officer in Despair of
the Expedition Denounced against Bergen;
if [de viriume] many Foreigners
carry out a Bombardment which,
according to him, for a Number of Years
Spain had not undertaken.

     I can tell you, that there has not been
question of assembling Troops at
St Koch, nor of the Cordon formed by the
troops of Spain on the frontiers
of Portugal.
