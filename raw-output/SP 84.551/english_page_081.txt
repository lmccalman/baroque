N.58.

                                        Paris the 30th July 1778.

                                        The works that
continue without Pause in our Ports;
one awaits from Batavia that one has had by the
latest News.
        Our Squadron of Evolution has received
Order at Lisbon, to return to France,
it has returned, as well as one of our Vessels
on Cruise to Brest at the end of this month.
A Brigade of this Squadron is
detained at Brest, one does not know why.
        The Minister of the Navy has had
Construction Wood brought from
Brest, for 2 Billions, a Forest has
been sold, one awaits the English.
        There is still rumor, that the
K. of Portugal were married, but
that does not appear fixed.
        A Letter from Bilbao, announced
the day before yesterday, says, that the Portuguese
have taken from the Spanish, Saint-Cathale.
        Monsieur Lebrun from Province;
and that they have massacred the Governor
and the two daughters; that this Affair,
