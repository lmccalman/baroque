We have Letters from Cartier dated the 28th
of October, which report that the Commerce of
Marseille, which is very considerable for the Corso,
was expecting a tour of two
Months in all the Provinces.
The Intendant on his Side had departed
to conduct an inquiry there.
One learns of a Complaint that the States
of the Ministry have sent to the [Culture]
of Paris, concerning a new [Impôt Terreur], by what
was in progress, in the Council where it
is found, that since 1769. there have been cleared
368000. Arpents of Land, not counting
the [Denier altâts] of Languedoc, that of
Montauban, and of Dauphiné; where one
