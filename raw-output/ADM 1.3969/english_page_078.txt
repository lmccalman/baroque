One will soon see the [Cèlliers] of the
King the Hirondelle, the Argus, the Guarmeur
the young Henri, the Epervier, the Schelft, the
Milans, the Emeraude, the Expedition, the Tonaix,
the Tartare, the Aigle, the Triomphes and
the Espiégle, with some other Vessels
of Transport - they are all worn out from service.
The King will soon have constructed Twenty
Cutters or Luggers of the first Rank
