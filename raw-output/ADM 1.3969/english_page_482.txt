N 28 Decr

Paris the 6th December 1784

Since the Letter written to the
Colonels, nothing has come out of the War
Office, and there is still no
question of the Choice of General Officers.
It is claimed that the General Staff of his
Armies will only be formed after
the Emperor's Response has been received
to the Second Letter of the King, by
which His Majesty informs him
of his Dispositions, and of his last
Intentions. This Response is
expected only around the 10th or 12th of
this Month.

It is hoped more than ever, that
every Path will not be closed to a
Conciliation, and before the
Armies are assembled
Perhaps an Accommodation will
