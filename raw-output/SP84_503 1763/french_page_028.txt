Rotterdam the 31st October, 1783

My Lord

I have the Honour to be advised
to your Lordship a Copy of a letter from my
Correspondent at Madrid containing the
Estimate of the Expences of the Intelligence
which would come to £1050. a quarter, which
at par makes £250.#.

By a Remittance made to this Correspondent
the 31st of May & of October last, & orders of 1780
and by the Draught from me in Consequence of
the £1050. which by your Lordships orders were
paid me by the Admiralty, the 9th of July last
the Correspondent is paid down to the 31st October.

I most humbly beg to know by your
Lordships if the His Majesty's pleasure
that this Correspondence be continued and
the above said Terms. I have the Confidence
of this Corresponding declined hitherto
to give us exact informations upon the
Navy, Army, and Ordnance of Spain.

I Judge that no Copies of this
Correspondence is to be divided between the
