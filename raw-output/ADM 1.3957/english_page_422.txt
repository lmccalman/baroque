St. Ildefonse, September 27, 1780.

I am informed from
Barcelona, that they are loading from the Arsenal
of this City 300 Pieces of Cannons of
different Caliber, several Mortars,
Cannonballs and other Munitions of War,
to be transported to the different
Ports of Spain, as well as to Ceuta.

The Regiments the Crown and the
Bourbon left on the route from
Barcelona to Cadiz.

They are embarking the [Evangile] of Murcia
and they are marching with the 1st and the two of
Soria [Milicellannes] to accompany
this last Place, Ceuta.

I am written to from Cadiz of the 18th current,
