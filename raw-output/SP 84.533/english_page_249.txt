One Senses surely, that an
English Column ad hoc, near
Manila combined, cannot bring
Aid Against and [s'ingenie] of the Countess
du Barry, and that the Alarms which She
gives to the King, are inspired to
Him by Herself.

This Anecdote, justified by the
Events, is attested by two
Courtiers, whose testimonies
Are of great Weight.

There is no appearance today
that the Position of the Duke d'Abbe was such
as had been announced; one speaks
not at all of Reconciliation of the
Princes, there is no question that they
should go to Fontainebleau; They
prepare themselves to continue to play
between Themselves the Comedy of Chantilly
where they Are.
