The War Bureaus have been working
all day since yesterday. They are no doubt
preparing there the new orders
for the Colonies, & the Regulations
for the voyages.

There is a third Article
of the Dispatches: it is that the Athenians,
that one was not so 40 years ago! Then!
which leaves an open field for
those who wager on War:
and for those who claim it to a
Congress, charged with pacifying everything.

Mr Maillebouis formally took yesterday
the Request of the Count of
Maillebouis the States General
defense to place at the head of their
Armies: this Proposition having
been examined in the Council
of State, was approved by the King.

Mr Necker sent to the King a
Copy of a considerable Work
on finances, of his Composition.
