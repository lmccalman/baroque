Spain is intriguing strongly for
Peace; unsigned Notes but
always arriving nonetheless from Paris and
in tranquility.

The King, at Mass, last
Sunday in the Salon
de la Paix, the Duc de Choiseul found
himself exposed there; the King gave him
no welcome; but the King having withdrawn
backwards, [le qui après] the Duc de Noailles
fell to his Knees, [un sa entre autres]
the Ambassador was to return to
the Ministry.

The Chevalier de La Luzerne,
Nephew of Mr de Malesherbes, has
just been named, to
replace the Minister of France,
Mr de Bailli, at Ratisbon.

Mr Dupré de St Maur, passes from
the Intendancy of Bourges, to
that of Bordeaux; and Mr de Bonni,
Master of Requests, is named
to the Intendancy of Bourges; but
he is ill received at Versailles.
