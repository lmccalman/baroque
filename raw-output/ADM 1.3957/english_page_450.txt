Brussels the 5th November 1770.

By Order of the Government
they are making in this City and in Ghent 30 Thousand
men, and 2 Thousand [Bertons] of [Gros
Wane], to be delivered on the 5th of February
next.

They have also ordered as much in
this Province as in those of
Flanders, Luxembourg, Limburg,
Namur, 10 Thousand tents, 80 Thousand
Pairs of Shoes, as many Pairs
of Stockings; 4 Thousand Pairs of Boots,
to be delivered the 5th of March next.
