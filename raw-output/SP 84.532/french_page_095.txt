private                                Rotterdam 20th January 1781.

My Lord,

        I have to acknowledge the Honour
of your Lordships Letter of the 18th Instant,
and have the pleasure to have it in my power
to inform you, and also that Central
friend, whom I had your Lordships
orders to communicate for one day the longer,
having in the interim taken in & Rich.
and St. Eustatius Rum and Sugars as ordered
with Raphael, I have this day the pleasure
finally to discharge him; and granted
your Lordship will see by the Artilery
Clothing, is an Assurance cloth worn with
the Artist of Spain, and by that out of a
Series of Attention with the Public
upon His Majesty's Secret Service and
Purposes, which is to be communicated.
The occasional Recompences to my
Correspondents, will be managed
with great Oeconomy.
        The Arrival of Peter Le Coppier,
to the Hock Islands at Amsterdam
with the News of Troops' Mutiny, had
a Message had been to her Sister Majesty
                                        I remain
                                        Your Lord
                                        

Hon'ble Earl of Sandwich.
