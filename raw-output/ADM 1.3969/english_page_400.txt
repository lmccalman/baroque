Paris the 26th of July 1784.

                    The order was sent
to Brest, to arm a frigate
with celerity, which will go directly to
the Isle de France; Mr. de la Croix des
Castries, who commands it, has seen
a minister to conduct investigations,
and to take knowledge of
all the depredations that [vos bas]
committed in this Colony, [plutost]
the Bankruptcy of an individual
named Da rifal, formerly Lieutenant
in the Regiment of Pondichéry, who
having gained the confidence of the administrators
and of the principal inhabitants, has just
spread desolation in the Colony.
[Le] has [mangué] for nearly [deux huit]
millions, the King is for three millions
in this Bankruptcy, it takes away
five hundred Thousand livres from the Fund
Of Invalids &c, and the last of the
