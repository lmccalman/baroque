                           First.

                     Aranjuez, the 29. May 1778.

                           The fleet, composed
of 18. Merchant Ships, and 2. of
War, departed the 8th of this Month in the Morning,
with a small favorable Wind,
which changed two Hours later,
so that on the 9th at 3 o'clock in the Morning,
it was only 30. Leagues distant:
it apparently cannot move farther
away until the Tailwind, when it
took to the Open Sea.

The Commander of this fleet, three
Ships for Cartagena, two
for Buenos Ayres, and three for
Havana, which makes in all
24. Vessels.

On the 11th of this Month the Squadron from Cadiz
a War Brigade, with a
Court Packet, equipped for Several
Months of Provisions, it was learned that it
is destined for Buenos Ayres.
