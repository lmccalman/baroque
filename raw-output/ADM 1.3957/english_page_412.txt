A French Officer who returning from
Constantinople since [quarantieme] to Senec,
in Hungary, writes to M. de St Priest, that the
Senate is with the Turkish Army, but that
the Turkish Cavalry and the artillery is weak.
Reports from Constantinople
say, that the Chevalier de Tott foresees & fortifies
the Point of Buyuker, which is the ordinary Anchorage
of the Castle of the Dardanelles.
The King left from Choisy Saturday at 2 o'clock, all
has returned to Fontainebleau. He enjoys good
Health there, the Count of Provence had a Gravel this
month which had no Consequence.
The King goes Hunting every Day, the
finest weather in the World continues for
the Season, there will however be little wine
in France, but it will be good. They already seek
it in Paris for there are People
who have gone to buy all the Burgundies.
It is at the Castle of Tournele in Beaun
that they have confined M. du Paty, Advocate
