IX.

The assignats for the amount of said liquidations will be paid
to the Holders of said Offices or Proprietors in the same manner as the others, at the rate of five percent counting from the day of the remittance of their titles, into the hands of the Controller General of our
Finances.

X.

Will be moreover our Edits & Declarations concerning
said Jurisdiction, notably our Letters Patent in the form
of an Edict of the month of August 1768, executed according to their form & tenor, in all that is not contrary to our present Edict,
We THEREFORE GIVE IN COMMAND to our beloved & loyal Counselors
the People holding our Court of Parlement in Paris, that our
present Edict they have it read, published & registered on its day therein to keep, observe & execute according to its form
& tenor: FOR such is our pleasure; & so that this be a thing
firm & stable forever, we have had our seal placed upon it.
GIVEN at Versailles in the month of May, the year of grace one thousand seven hundred seventy-one, & of our reign the fifty-sixth.
Signed, LOUIS. And below: By the King, PHELYPEAUX. Visa
DE L'HÔPITAL. Visa at Council, FERRAT. And sealed with the great
seal of green wax, in red & green silk laces.

Registered, heard the requisitory of the Prosecutor General of the King, to
be executed according to its form & tenor, & collated copy sent
to the Bailiwick of the Prosecutor General of the King at said Châtelet, to be
read, published, registered & executed there, following the decree of the Parlement of
Paris of the Parlement, of the assembled Chambers, the twenty-eighth of May
one thousand seven hundred seventy-one.

Signed, VANDIVE.

IN PARIS, at P. G. SIMON, Printer of the Parlement;
rue Mignon & at Barrois', Quarter S. André-des-Arcs. 1771.
