Madrid the 5th May 1782.

All the informed men
of Cadiz and of the new Tariff that
they want to establish there, as well as in all
our other cities, all the Merchants from
Abroad, equally for all
Nations without any exception
and that this Tariff brings a considerable
increase in Duties to pay which becomes
ruinous for Commerce.

Despite the Opening of our
National Bank since the 15th March
last, our Paper Money
continues to Lose still Eight
per Cent in Commerce, although
it is admitted to fund the
Bank at the Price of Ready Money.

The King has named the Marquis
de Matallana, for his Minister
and Plenipotentiary at the Court of
Parma.
