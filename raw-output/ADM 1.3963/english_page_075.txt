17 20 may N°2

                        Brest May 8, 1776

                        The Transport Ships the

Tamponnes, commanded by Mr.
Verdelin, the Betzeul, for St. Jean de
Villebon, set sail from this roadstead
Tuesday, the 7th current, loaded, they
                         one before having changed, they
[touvoye] were very contrary the
next day. The Tamponne
was dismasted of its small Topmast
at the base of the [Choque] and.
it was noticed, since, that it
was rotten.
        The Betzeunne was relieved
on the 2nd, from it in the storm; it
made much [tic] the damage
moreover is not considerable.
Mr. Duchée, who was to pass at
the cape of [la frasse] for the [Régrade],
said, that there was no reason to
                                    attempt it.
