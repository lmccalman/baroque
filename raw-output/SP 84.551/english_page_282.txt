Toulon, September 8, 1776.

[Recluse] in order its
[Rargement] in Wood and in Cordage,
is to depart to return to Brest.
We await from L'Orient the Vessel
the [flameau]-
M. de St Agueur has received Orders
to have the Vessels tested
of the Port.
We still await the Division
of the Squadron, there is still
order to keep it in the Roads
without disarming.
We have had Notice, that the frigates,
with the Agreement of the King of
Morocco were forming an establishment,
and a good Port on the Coast of
Barbary, near Tangier.

Brest
