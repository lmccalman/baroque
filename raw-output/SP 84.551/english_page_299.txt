The Divisions will be doubled,
& the number of Lieutenant
Generals who command them,
will be reduced to 16, instead of 80,
all the troops are in
Movement.
     The Count d'Arteing, Lieutenant
General of land, and of Sea,
has just left for Brest with some
from the Court, one does not know where, nor what
is his Mission.
     They want, it is said, absolutely
to oppose the Establishment
of the Russians at Tangier, even
so that the English at Gibraltar,
they would not control the Masters
of the Strait.
     They speak of two Loans of
90 Million, for the 66 First
Generals, that is to say, that the
66 First will make Loans, and
will jointly answer for the
Interest.
