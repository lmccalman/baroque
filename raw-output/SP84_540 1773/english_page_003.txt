Rotterdam this 8th November 1792.

My Lord

        There is no Thomson this had believed necessary
to join the Copy of the Letter from Doct.
Hoste The Repetition of Correspondences
by their Letters of 28 December last,
Amounting to L 939.7. 2 Sterling, that
from Holland, which at the Current Exchange
Rate amounts to L 939. 2. Sterling, for a
Credit which the [Legel] would have to Consider
to draw for the Value name me
Mister William Davis of This Sum
for my all payable at 3 Days after
the presentation of the Letter, William
Davis on The Shoppers William
Davis son of Company in London.

        I await a moment in the Course, some
Orders from the Provinces of Vienna, and The
Regulations of Holland for one could not
perhaps in The Region with The Regiment
of this province, which appear to take
[artigement] in my Journal of Accounts.

        I send you then My Lord my sincere
Congratulations on Renewal of
[Gasteele], and On Your Continuation in
all Your health and Joining The Objects
of my Wishes The Respect that you, Gentlemen
from here                             My Lord
                                        Your very humble and
                                        very Obedient Servant
                                        M. Wolters

His Power the Registrar.
