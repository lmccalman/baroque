N 29 dep: N: 1

Madrid the 8th September 1782.

     They must embark at Cadiz
Four Regiments of Infantry, to
send them to Havana; but it is necessary
that a new popular uprising arrive
in different Places of the
Indies, because of these, news &
Contributions.

     I Expect at any moment
the arrival of the Convoy from Havana
to Cadix, of which a strong Frigate at the
same Time, has already entered the
Bay. M. de Galvez comes, by this Convoy,
we are awaiting a Council
of War requested by Messrs
Solano and Cagigal, who find themselves
under Arrest.

     It appears that the Tribunal
of the Inquisition, is very occupied in
discovering several Concealers,
concerning the atmosphere of certain
