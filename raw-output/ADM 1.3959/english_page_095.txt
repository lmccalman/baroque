592.

There are few Officers who present themselves
to advance.

The Queen, commanded by Baron
de Cheynes, has arrived from the Isle de
France. He said that Mr. de
Roquefeuille's voyage is not at all a Mystery
in that Country.

Mr. de Bourjonville has arrived
here for some Days; He is going to
command a Vessel to go
make an extraordinary Voyage.

The Minister has informed that Mr. de
Roquefeuille [lani] was paid by all
the emoluments of his Command.
Mr. d'Estaing had requested this
Arrangement before coming here.
