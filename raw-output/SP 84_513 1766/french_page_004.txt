Rotterdam the 4th February 1786.

My Lord Duke

The enclosed Paper is the account
of the Spanish St. Eustatius incident & Draft
upon Mr. Clifford's for his Quarter due
the 8th of December last, amounting
in the whole to £419.12. Sterling;
for the half of which Sum, being
£209.16.14. I shall by the next Post
take the Liberty to draw in the usual
way upon William Davis Esqr. of the
Treasury.

The moneyed People in this Country
do not seem to take great Confidence
in the new french Loan, neither have
they in general a good Opinion of
the present System of french Finances:
But Money is in such Plenty here
and the Interest of it is fallen, that
