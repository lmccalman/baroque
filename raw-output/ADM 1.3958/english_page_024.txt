Paris, January 24, 1778.

Nothing is read from Paris.

Those at Versailles to have Commerce with
My Lord the Count of Artois who is in the Country, are the
King and Louis, the Dauphin, the Solitary, and
the Mystic. There are others who have the
Liberty to see him, but he Receives them
privately & [séance] in Cabinet but later,
[Peine] begins to hunt, if that
continues, she will soon be taught, which
will finish losing the Capital for
the arrival of books.

The King upon arriving from Choisy, at
the fall of his Mistress, [s'est precedé]
that presents [a l'affeteux a Sortens] of the 1st
of King [lui est de s'y naturés a l'ame est]
to have if he has for.

This Response has consisted of offering
three or four Bracelets loaded with Diamonds
worth forty thousand [deux en], but, that
intrigues the Parliament, it is said even
[à Branchel à qu'il], for a [Bret] of Council.

The King charged the Duke of La Vrillière
to carry [à action sous l'Emire sous la bien]
until he said according to the [Stre] as
[bien] of a Banker of Paris who having
proposed to the King in the world sent to
charged his same in the [Ctre] of
[Descard], his First [Marape] and said to
give the Dinner.

[L'S]
