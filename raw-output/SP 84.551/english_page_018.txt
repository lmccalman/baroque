Toulon the 19th June 1775.

The Courier arrived
the day before yesterday from the Court, brought
express Orders, to arm 8 Galleys
and good Vessels, 1 or Frigates, and
and four Xebecs, to keep them
ready to set
sail at the end of next Month:
Consequently, the Commander
and Intendant are having work done,
there has been the greatest Activity,
everyone is therefore working
diligently.

The Musfel at Boulli, is
totally finished.


Brest
