Paris the 21st July 1782.

We see from the Letters
from Toulon and Brest, that they are
seriously thinking of preventing the
Mediterranean from being troubled by
foreign Corsairs, and that
Two French warships,
always ready to sail towards Tunis
& Toulon, and a Body of Troops
sufficient to man them
will bring to Duty, the
Declaration of our Court.
The First Courier who will come
from Versailles will bring us the
Response of the Empress to the
Declaration that France has
made, that its Commerce and the Interest
of other Commercial Nations
did not permit that armed
Fleets enter into the
Mediterranean.
