Whitehall. July 12. 1760.

Major General Yorke.     Sir,

                                        Two Mails are
                                        still due from Holland,
                                        so that You have a
                                        further Proof of the
                                        Utility of dispatching
                                        Fishing-Boats, as
                                        had You not taken
                                        that Method, the
                                        King had still
                                        remained uninformed
                                        of the Return of the
                                        21st past. In this
                                        Sterility of News,
                                        which does not
                                        agree with our
                                        Impatience in this
                                        critical Minute, I
                                        have no Trouble to
                                        give You but to say
                                        You of the great India
