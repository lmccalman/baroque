N.° B.

                    Paris the 29th July 1778.

                    The Letters from Brest
speak only of the Continuation of the
works of the Port.
     Those from Toulon of the 24th report, L,
that [le eq. a Vioneles des Mutins], the
[très a la Ordonnez des] has been transferred from
Ollean, with great Success, they are found
very Satisfied.
     An [Oden], arrived from [Mogon], has
brought Projects for the Court,
which were sent there by an
extraordinary Courier. The
Departure of the Ordinaries has been delayed, until the Return
of this Courier.
     They are going to construct [la Mayorienne]
of St. [Ouene]. [Les Arbelles] continue their
course: they are going to [exhaler les rélais] of
[Sols]. [Les Coffaillous] have been raised.
     Arrived at Toulon [Ed. Catolas]
from Bordeaux.
     A Captain of [Reine Marchaud]
reports [le ec.] at Toulon, that there arrive 3.
