Rotterdam, this 11th October 1771.

My Lord

[Vous] the honor to send you herewith the appreciation &c [fuy] from the Correspondents of Vienna, [quoi] the Gazette of April even [des nous etes utile], the [faulcon] received by the model that you me.

I have [encoremallion allus] to Return My Lord, by putting the Projects in [Endendes] in a temporary form, but I [les poumets] to the other sheets ordinarily in [Vous poumettant] some [qu'etres] that I return from the [Prohes], on the contrary.

[Suy] the honor to be with profound Respect

My Lord

Your very humble and very obedient servant
M. Wolters


To M. de Stiftich.
