There appeared since some time
Two Natural Ordinances.
By the First the King attaches 18 Gentleman
Cadets to Each Infantry
Regiment; His Majesty takes Charge
of their Maintenance, and grants them 18 Lt.
per Day, to feed themselves.

The Second orders the Sending of
Students to the Military Ports, of
which one has already spoken.

Here is an Extract of the Ordinance
for the formation of Each Infantry
Regiment.

Major Staff.
A Colonel in Chief, A Lieutenant Colonel,
A Lieutenant Colonel, A Major,
A Quarter Master, treasurer of the
Regiment, An Adjutant, An Ensign
Bearer, A Surgeon, and Two
Flag Bearers or standards.

Companies
Eleven Captains and The Lieutenant Colonel.
