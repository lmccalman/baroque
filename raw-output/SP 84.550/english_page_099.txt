and although the subject is unknown
there is reason to believe, that it is by
the latest News that the
Court has received from Lisbon, and
which could intrigue us.
They say the King of Portugal is very
ill. And that even if
he were to recover from it, he is no longer
in a state to reign: and that in this
case Mr. de Carvalho, in agreement
with the Princess of Brazil,
would want to be the Master of
the Regency, to the Prejudice of
the Queen, although that
does not appear very probable,
it is at least quite certain, that
by order of the Court, work is being done
with activity in all our
frontier Fortresses of this Kingdom
to put them in good Condition,
