51.

They were not far from assembling the [avenrienne] Guard, & they notified about Fifty [carneaux de haies], but they were alerted that a considerable party was marching on Tellier towards the Chatelet. It is supposed that there will be something new at the [Paige Sabatisme].

It is feared that the Provincial Parlements will fashion themselves like that of Paris, & that the Provincial Commanders, who no longer had power, will take it back, & that they will restore themselves, to make it pass as Presided by Authority. The Ministers are doing the Service, their Charges are, not only confiscated, but they will be stripped of Nobility, them & their Priors up to the sixth degree, if ever such a prince were to restore the charge in the Kingdom.

La Roche having provided the Ministry with a great Sum, the Regent will not hesitate to yield, & if this is what has happened: the Parlement which has to place itself there.

The Parlement of Rouen has restored to the 4. Governors [abattus] upon the Bishopric, all the Chambers by acclamation, but sealed from the [tournelle].

They are still occupied with the provision of the Court, by printing a Mark of the Chambre des Comptes, & a Part
