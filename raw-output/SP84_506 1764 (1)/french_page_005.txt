Wachsale 1st May 1764.

Sir,
Mr Mollen.                    The Earl of Sandwich
                              being extremely employed,
                              has desired me to acquaint
                              You, that Your Request
                              for going into the Country
                              for some part of the Summer
                              having been laid before the
                              King, His Majesty is
                              graciously pleased, to allow
                              You such Time of Absence
                              as You shall find necessary
                              for the re-establishment of
                              Your Health, not omitting
                              but that the Business for
                              which You have it do
                              will be left in good Hands
                              & that You will be ready
                              to return in case that
                              any particular Occasion
                              should require your Presence.
                                        I am &c W Smellese
