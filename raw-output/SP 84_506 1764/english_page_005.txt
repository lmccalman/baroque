Willembrand knight

We Pierre Francois Willembrand knight lord of Marchenoire, 
bailiff and governor for his Majesty of the county of Grandpré by virtue 
of our provisions dated the twenty eighth of October one thousand seven hundred sixty 
and eleven, signed Louis and below Phelipeaux and sealed with the great 
seal of yellow wax. Certify to all whom it may concern that 
those named Jean Baptiste Mahuet, and Jean Louis Renault acting 
for Louis Joseph Lelievre farmer of the forestry office of the woods and 
forests of said county of Grandpré, who have represented to us 
two fines exploited by them one of six livres against 
Claude Roux son of the late Jean Roux residing at Montcheutin 
for having cut an oak of twenty feet in circumference in the 
wood of the lady of Marchenoire situated in the territory of said Montcheutin 
upon which judgment was rendered by us the fifteenth of March last, 
and the other of three livres against Jean Gilon also of said 
Montcheutin for having removed dead wood from the wood of 
the lady of Saint Fergeux situated in said territory judged by us 
the twentieth of March also last. We declare that the aforesaid 
fines could not be recovered given the insolvency 
of said Roux and Gilon. In witness whereof we have 
signed the present certificate to be valid and serve as 
is fitting. At Grandpré this nineteenth of April one 
thousand seven hundred and seventy three.

Willembrand
