Brest the 30th March 1783.

They returned all the men
from the Port People, however we still
have many remaining;
The three Delegates are still in the
fort eating, however we were told that the
Ships of the line of the Americans and the
Coastal Armaments have never served, that
the [étrangement] long in the Port the [compt]
costs and becomes exposed, they were granted
Compensation.

They launched last March the Républicain
and the Révolutionnaire, they immediately put to the
Chamber and the Slabs from above and below,
which are borrowed from Baye and Royan with
they also count the [Bord] of town
for the State of [carme] to arm the vessel
and give the details? of these ships Battery,
etc so that it has the complete [jettements] of a
Town of first Rank before a line of other
Stature, to go to Cap. d'Esp. and to Cap
[Panchéme]; there are about a hundred others.

M. de Montazer captain in first
went to Port Louis with the Director
of hospitals the son of [moi] the Count
but whose expenses are [de Payen] of the other
