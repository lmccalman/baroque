Estates of Languedoc; these Estates
will open their Sessions, their
Assembly at Montpellier, the
28th November.

The King did not go this
Week to Choisy, as he
should have; His Majesty postponed this
voyage by three days, so that
the Queen could be part of it.

Our Letters from Rome of the 28th say,
that on the 16th September there would be a
Consistory. That the Cardinals
named as [Imperos], will continue
their visits to the Cardinals.

That the Pope did not forget that many
young Ecclesiastics could not
enter into the Ecclesiastical State,
for lack of Titles. His Holiness
assigned them Pensions, to
take their place.
