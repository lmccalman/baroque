14 14 Sept. 1790

Paris, September 7, 2790.

We await from Toulon some St.
announce nothing more than what has been said:
the Ordinance came as was announced. As for
the Project, we will be able to offer the Notices, but
to respond only, his Majesty will enter there at 30.
steward & December; the Surveillances & Officers
from Bordeaux will arrive only on the 25th, and thus
up to the Palace.

It is expected that his Majesty will favor
registering this thing as what he has done;
but there is no question of anything precipitate: it is
an advice that was given to him lastly,
of buildings to enter into Detail here.

The Queen of the Rus gives only about half
an hour, the established Rule to return two
the same hours to Versailles; but he has
given the dispatch in public copy Corps of
Burghers of the House, but who will
accompany him only from the Course.

The number in which his Majesty was adopted
at the arrival of the Course of Bourges in public
copy as Relay because will not suffice.

The Directors of the Chancellery provisionally have
President, and astonishing very strongly at the absolute door,
he pardons, my Invitations of Marine &
Steward that he has to verify this in the Countries concerning
the Administering, of all the Parcels, is a
thing which was beyond example -
