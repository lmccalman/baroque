Registand Nº 1


Paris the 3rd March 1782.

     M. is certain that still
at Cadiz the last [M. Ferrier], it would
not be then, if they were to return soon
All the French Troops were to
return to Toulon, with Brothers or
Follow [Réepaule], who pass into this
Department. The Rest of the Squadron
returns to Brest; there will come to
Rochefort, only a Part of the Squadron
of M. de Vaudreuil.

     Some private Letters
from Marseille, have spoken of an Earthquake
in Italy, which would have almost
destroyed the City of [Maglène], but one
believes nothing of it, since the Letters
from Naples contain nothing on
this Subject.
