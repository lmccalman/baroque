N° 94.

                    Paris the 28th November. 1775.

                                        Our Letters from Brest,
of the 10th speak only of the Marriage of the Marquis d'Argouty,
& it is [Messrs.] to the Officers, and [Marles]
to the Workers.
    Those from Toulon of the 10th say, that The
Promotion for The Navy is Public -
and that it is expected at any Moment there.
    The [Basles Marinés] of Rochefort of the 13th
are all general.
    They are still complaining from Toulon, yet they no
longer pay, it is quarterly to the Officers,
& one and a half to the Workers.
    They have paid the compensation for the
recent Losses, of the King's armies there,
They await from now a Mission of 10. from Paris
for The Route, and its Return and Memorandum.
    The Robert, has not yet arrived,
they believe that they have not yet: written:
other, or La Belle Poule, which was Less here
for the Voyage of the [Indes Indes].
    The Result of that from Orient, which was
to take place only in the Month of February:
is announced for The 15th December: -
they still await two The Palace for
this Sale, and named a Pot of [Cavres]
                                        [Quinvisons].
