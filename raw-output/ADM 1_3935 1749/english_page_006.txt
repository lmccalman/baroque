Mr. Justice of the Peace and 8 7bre 1790

Sieur Aubert master wig maker, following the declaration of said Sr.
Aubert son who declared to us that the furniture and merchandise
belonging to his said father, must be sold after the death and that the said
deceased was Owed several sums according to what may result from
the statement of active and passive debts which said Aubert son has undertaken to
provide, we made the description of the movable effects and utensils
