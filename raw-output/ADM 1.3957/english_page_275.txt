They speak only of Riots in all the
Provinces, I believe I mentioned that there had
been one at Fleurus, 5 or 6 days ago,
the People wanted to get rid of the Colonel
and perhaps the Nobles who had not at all
conformed to it.

Brussels, July 19, 1790.

Sir, the Government
has published an Ordinance, that all the [Dansiens]
from Foreign Provinces, even if they were
Citizens of this City, as well as all the
Belgians in Antwerp, married or not married, who
could prove it there, [en descendant leurs an
anceôtres ett leurs, tous leurs de Foreet et
de longue]. This Order is regarded [à raance]
as a [toubble Reditque], because it is believed
until they are Rendered, they are threatened with [boul],
order to prevent service, and that
they will not find lodging for a good Number of these
Men for Military lodgings, these
[?] sent back from the other, which is order the beings
[comploit], for the End of this Month.
