Sir—

You will undoubtedly have heard by my—
favour of the Service Sam upon all presents, which
has prevented my answering home the honour
of yours of the 26th of July, O.S.

I shall use my utmost diligence and exactness
in transmitting to you constantly the accounts
of what is doing in the Parts of France, and I have
left the strictest orders at home to have any—
Such accounts as shall be received, transmitted to
you regularly. —
