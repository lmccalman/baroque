Madrid the 5th April 1776.

     Since the day before yesterday
the Confraternity has returned from
Pardo, until after the Easter
holidays, when it goes to Aranjuez,
until the end of June.
     From Malaga they inform me on the 22nd
March, that the Two frigates of the King,
announced in my previous [letter],
had departed from there the day before, for
Cadiz, with a favorable Wind.
And that elsewhere nothing New.

one has not ceased to find Medals
and ancient Coins, along
