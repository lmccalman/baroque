Extract from a Letter from Paris of October 27
                                                                    1763.

He has received a Letter from C. G. which I
do not judge to be for me to forward to you, since
it refers me to other times, given the
present circumstances. Here is the Explanation
of the Enigma, it is that there is a singular
Revolution in France concerning Finances.
The King as the Father of his Subjects, has finally
yielded to the Instances and Solicitations of the
respectable Magistrates of the Kingdom, in order
to adopt a System that he can while [ronrissant]
relieve his People. All the First Presidents
of all the Parlements are summoned with all
the Procureurs General, assisted by the most
senior Presidents Ã  mortier and Counsellors,
to scrupulously examine different
projects that have been presented to Him. They are furnishing
the Chateau of Vincennes to hold this
celebrated Assembly there; the [batimens Liberaux]
are ordered for the 8th of next Month in Paris
under Penalty of Disobedience, it is to be believed or
assured that they will all be suppressed. The First
