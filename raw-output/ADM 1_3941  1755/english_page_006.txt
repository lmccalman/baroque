in Paris the 3rd March 1759

Upon the notice that the Court
has received that Admiral Anson
is preparing to put to sea
with 20 Warships
to cruise in the Channel,
the Court has sent various
Couriers both for various
Sea Ports and for Spain,
Naples and the other Powers
friendly to our Crown.

This news joined to
that of the other powerful
armaments of the British
Court will make our Court
undertake Operations
to try to counter
the English.
