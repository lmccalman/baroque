                    Cadiz the 25th October 1770.

        The Marquis de
Vanvout, formerly Governor of this
Province, arrived here the 20th of this Month in the
Morning, since that Instant one is occupied
every Day with furnishing with Cannons and
necessary Munitions the Vessels to
establish some news, finally to
take all possible Measures
to put this Place in the best
State of Defense, one expects Incessantly
a reinforcement of troops for the Garrison.
        One has also embarked on the Batavian
Ship 8[-pound] Cannons and Infantry
Soldiers for Buenos-Ayres.
