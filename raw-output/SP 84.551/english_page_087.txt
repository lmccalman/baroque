The [plus Majors] of the Vessels, and the
Crews, are in good Condition.
[L'ience] will continue to conduct
Maneuvers in the Vicinity, and
will return in September. [La Parle]
will Rejoin.

The squadron of Mr. Du Chaffault
spent three Days with a Spanish
Squadron of 7 Vessels and 3 frigates,
who exchanged Civilities.

The Spanish and the Portuguese rendered
the greatest Honors
to the Duke of Chartres.

By a new Ordinance,
the King grants to the troops of the
Navy the same Increase
in Pay, as to the land troops,
and creates a Company of
Grenadiers in each of the
Battalions of the Navy; there are
Six in this Port.
