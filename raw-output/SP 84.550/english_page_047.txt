The Court of [Elumbre], and the Parquet,
were able to prevent the Chambers
by the prompt Aid that was
brought there; but the Court of Aids,
the Chancellery of the Palace, the
Petitions, and the Waters and Forests,
and the Marble Hall, were
consumed. The Loss
of Buildings for the King is estimated
at 4 to 5 Million; Without counting
the Loss of Private Individuals, who
could save nothing of their
Merchandise. This fire having
started at Night, and the Merchants
being fortunately at Their Homes
in the City.

It is here that this Species
worked secretly
on this fire, with an indefatigable zeal,
