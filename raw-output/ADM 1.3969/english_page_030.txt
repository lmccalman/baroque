Paris the 14th of March 1783.

They do not know how to give us
Reports of the Catastrophe
of Italy, and Cailes.

We have learned that the
Count d'Estaing stopped in Madrid
while returning to France. As soon as
the Departure of the Fleet, he must leave
all at once, to return to Cadix
no one knew the Motive of this
new Voyage. They also said other
things, that the King of Spain wanted
to send the General to the Indies with
Denis Vaudreuil de Ligne; & the English
announced the Armament that they prepare
at Portsmouth which has also occupied the
Minds of our Ministers; but we
learn from London, that this
