N° 68.

                    Toulon the 4th April 1776.

                    They are working on the
Vessel the Zèon.
     They will [vivera] Tomorrow in Gratings & Grated,
to break B. [Jonover], in our opinion
rotten.
     The Chevalr [Comelier] [en avielle]
yesterday in the Grande Royale, and he
left from it this Morning. The Chevalier
[le Duge] mounted to mount there once.
     The Minister responded On
the subject of the Matter, that
[she/it] will warn where & when it will be
time. For the rest everything goes here
as usual. They do not
believe in War, at least
for this Year.
     The Buffon goes always very well,
and with the greatest
Hopes.
                              Paris
