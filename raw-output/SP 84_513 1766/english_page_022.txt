Paris the 19th May 1756

                                The King held yesterday
Chapter of the order of the Holy Spirit, it is
not believed that he named new
Blue Ribbons. His Majesty distributed
sixty eight to the Curates of Montpellier
and of St Brieux, and to some vacant
Boroughs.

    They write from Rennes, that the First
President, who was to come here, had
Counter-order, that one has summoned to the King
all the Attorneys, and ordered them
to give him the List of all the
Peers that are found in the hands
of the Counselors who make them [d'erits].
