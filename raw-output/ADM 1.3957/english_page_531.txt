They are going to increase the Number of
hussars, which are trained in Charges, so
that in no Case these Gentlemen
can refuse their Services.

They write from Marseille, that despite
what was said about Beef that was thrown
Wood to the Adriatic Sea, which is said to the
News that we have, that the Export is
permitted in the Kingdoms of Naples
and Sicily; and by the [Tarasta] of our
Commanders, who requested, that
Marseille can continue to be
regarded as a Foreign City,
we can provide our neighbors
to do a Commerce of which the other
Provinces of the Kingdom are deprived.

They write from L'Orient, that despite
the Ban on the Export of Grains in
Brittany, the Price is rising.
