Madrid the 22 April 1776.

The Papers of the
Ordinances of Puyeras, mentioned
in my previous one, have not
appeared until now; the Secretary of
Finances, who has been extraordinarily
informed of it, does not know if one of my
Friends, whom he wrote to [Pomme] for the
affair today much of the
[Tranquilitès], by spreading
the Rumor, that these are only
Papers of [Mafrique].

Talavera, has been arrested, and brought
here to Prison, for having given
Post Horses to a
