The frigates the Amatiste and the
fleur de lys received orders the day before yesterday
to go out to go on Discovery;
but they were obliged to remain
in the Goulet for lack of wind to
pass it.

All the Preparations announce
an approaching War; there has
arrived to us a Battalion of Militia;
the Coast Guards have orders to Assemble,
They are working vigorously to put in condition
the Batteries, the Officers Are
appointed to command there.

They are training the Officers and the
embarked Soldiers to make
Musketry discharges, as
is done during boardings; They also
make them do the watch and the
basic maneuvers.
