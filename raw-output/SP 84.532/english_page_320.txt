Duke of Orleans, First Prince of the Blood,
to beg him to carry to the Feet of the
throne the Complaints of the Bourgeoisie.
Consequently several Persons
of Distinction have already conformed
to this Invitation, and have addressed
Letters to the Duke of Orleans.

The King's Men of the Parliament, who
should perhaps have been there [écartés], [Poursuive]
to the assembled Chambers last Wednesday,
took a Counter-
Order in the Morning; which leads one to presume, that the
Ministers wish themselves to remove
all the Difficulties that one would wish
to bring to their Registration.
