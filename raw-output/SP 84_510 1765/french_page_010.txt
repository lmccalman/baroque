Copy
of a Letter from Richard Hollis
to John Pownall Esq.
Secretary to the Honourable the
Lords Commissioners of Trade and
Plantations, dated

Rotterdam the 16 July 1816.

Sir

In obedience to the Commands
of the Right Honourable the Earl of
Sandwich, I have the Honour to send
you a short State of Particulars of
the trade carried on between the Ports
of His Majesty's Dominions, and
those of the Moor.

If I have been dilatory in this
Point, it has been owing to the great
things of more of other Merchants
in answering my queries, and to the
Uncertainty of the Informations.
