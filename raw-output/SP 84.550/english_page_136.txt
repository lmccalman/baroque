    In a Royal Road that is being opened
in the Environs of here, through
the Mountains, the [forgeaieur] has
discovered a Sort of Precious
Stones, of the Size and Growth
of the opal, of three Colors, Blue,
yellowish and reddish, of which
a good quantity has been worked,
they are very beautiful;
Madame the Princess of Asturias
already has a Ring of them, and they are
busy working on an
Assortment of Buckles.
The King has had a
Box of them sent to Paris and to London;
to have them Analyzed and
valued, and it is believed that
the extraction will soon be
increased.
