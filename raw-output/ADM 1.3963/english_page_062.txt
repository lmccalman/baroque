A Ey. Son. No 1.

                                        Second.

                            Madrid the 29 January 1776.

     The 25th arrived here an
Extraordinary from France, but
until now nothing became known to me
of the Object of his Dispatches.
     The 25th there was a great extraordinary
Council of War, but
it is known that its Object was to
complete the Army for the [Pays
du Sort], since the Regiments
of Spanish Regiments are in
the Pay of their Court, without
finding a Man to recruit.
such is the Nation's Disgust
for the Service.
     The Promotion of General
Officers, promised for so
