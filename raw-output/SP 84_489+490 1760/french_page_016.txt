Whitehall July 1747

Hon Superior Yorke, Sir,

I received on Sunday
in the Afternoon the Sheets
of Your letter till began
of the 23d Insta: Verbatim,
Naming the three Mails
that were due, arrived
here, with Your Seven Ch
- Sallest of the 18 22, and So
So that You will observe
the Fishing Boats from
Schevenning constantly
make their Paflage
sooner, than the Packet
Boats - All the letters
I am acknowledging
have been laid before the
King; and You will
believe with what
Satisfaction His Majesty
