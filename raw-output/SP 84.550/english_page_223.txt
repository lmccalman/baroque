A Rebeckt is being armed for Mr. de
Martelley, charged by the Court, to go
to Corsica to survey the Woods that
can Serve for the Construction
and Equipment of the Navy.

D'Estaffe goes, His way, good things
are hoped from it.

Paris the 30th May 1776.

The King made yesterday
the Review of the infantry of the Household
in the Plain of Sablons; the Regent
and all the Royal family having Followed him.
Moreover, the fine weather determined His
Majesty; it was near 2 o'clock when he
left; there were not found there two
Thousand Phaetons than three or four times.
Half, this Month than three times.

Now that it has been said
of the other Bad [demarque dortant]
for the Bureaus of War;
but not a [neige]; it is only a
[brode], which has not yet been
carried out.
