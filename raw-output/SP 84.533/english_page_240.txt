Paris, October 11, 1771

Our latest Letters from Brittany contain a detailed account of the Session of the Court, and of the Comedy, and of the Means by which a New troupe is being brought back.

Nothing yet positive, the more we [timons] it is said that it is during the Journey to Fontainebleau, that it must be finished these days here.

The Absence of the Court, and the Vacancy of the Parliaments make the News most sterile in this Season.
