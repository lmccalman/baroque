At Orien the 5th November 1783

                    Mr. de Bournay one of the
Directors of the Company of the Indies has
arrived here to proceed with the Sale of
Provisions, Rigging, and all that is in the
Warehouses; this Sale will take place on the 6th.
[Regulation du Monde] here to buy.

     The Vessels will only be sold by
new postings. The Brigston that is
expected, has not yet arrived.

     The Horses are being brought hastily
the Pondichery, and the Duke of Duras;
they will depart at the end of this Month for
the Isle of France. They must transport
troops and Munitions of
all Kinds.
