A 30 Novemb. 1780.

                        Toulon the 30th November 1780.

                                    M. de Broves is
still in quarantine, the chevalier d'Oppede
has finished his, M. des armes, as well as
la Reynanne, who all arrive from Naples.
    We in the Cabets the Rajasa & the Cameleon
Are at Gulf Juan, we await them;
as well as the Bordelois at the island of Port-cros.
    M. de Piles, we await him with 4. of 50.
[gros Corps de Cout] at 2. Frigates of the Corps J.B.
Longe; who came from the island of [Son] M.r D.
de Mazargue, who by the Charter & another
[demitée] of [Son] M.r de Beauregard, M. des
Songrand have requested liberty to be
able to return to their Country Alexandria.
                        The Petitions of Roux at Audibert's, [font]
The two M.r we are the two the
greatest Serviceable, [pointés et l'Orchées]
to arm. We have 20. Vessels,
and on board the Chambers, which board the
Destins of 74, and the Caton of 64.
there are 68. and 8. frigates in State to Go
[bindes], and we have noise that which
makes them: our Port is in good
State.

                                                           Brest.
