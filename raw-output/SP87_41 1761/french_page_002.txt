St. James's 11th Sept: 1764

Marquess of Granby.          My Lord                    129
Ed. Monfort.
Cholmondeley.                I have the highest
Ligonier.                    Delight & Satisfaction in being
Albemarle.                   able now to acquaint your
Fitzpatrick.                 Lordship that the Marriage
M:Wallace.                   between His Majesty & Her most
M:Gary.                      Serene Highness the Princess of
Waldegrave.                  Mecklenbourg now Her Most
M:Matthias.                  gracious Queen was solemnized
M:Brunet.                    in the Chappel of this Kings
                             Palace at St. James's, on
                             Tuesday last the 8th Inst:. And
                             I most humbly wish You Joy
                             of an event so ardently desired
                             by His Majesty's faithfull
                             Subjects & from which, with
                             the Blessing of God, the
                             happiest Consequences may
                             be expected to this Crown
                             and Nation.
                                                I am &ca:
                                                    Buck:.
