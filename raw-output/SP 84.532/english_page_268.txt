X I I.

THE Prosecutors in our Parliament of Paris, who will wish to establish themselves with our Superior Councils, will exercise therein said functions of Prosecutors, without new commissions nor receptions; only on condition that they obtain our approval; & deliver to remit to the registry of said Councils a formal copy of said commissions; upon which mention will be made of our approval & of the decree of their reception, & the excess of the finance of their offices over that of the offices created for said Councils, will be reimbursed to them by us.

X I I I.

MAY likewise the Prosecutors of our Parliament of Paris, sell their offices to subjects who would desire to establish themselves with the Superior Councils, & the excess of the price of said offices will be reimbursed to the purchasers as it would have been to the holders.
