289.                                R 5-oct. no 1.

                    Brest the 21st September 1772.

                              The Minister informs me
to Mr d'Estaing, that the King warns me by
[approuailles] that he is informed that until now
the Captains of the Marine Brigades and that the
Cadets have had themselves raised by 2 Deniers from
the Ration. He asks him not to grant them the
same favor.

The General of the Army will call upon
the Captains and the last Captain of
each Regiment, he will have extracted there the
young [Vivisions] of all Marine Regiments,
which will bear the Name of
Army, Retz, France, Vermandois, Spain,
Normandy, Touraine; will bear
the Name of Brigades by Divisions;
and the Chiefs thereof will be equally divided into
three [Batles], attached to the Brigades.
The Captains will be attached to
three Brigades even with their
mentioning, which will bear the Name of the
Commanders of Brigades. The
Lieutenants will have the Companies,
under the Name of Commander of the
Company. The Continuation of two
of the Brigades among all the Brigades
