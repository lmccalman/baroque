169.                                                B 31 avril N°1

                    Toulon the 16th March 1772.

                    One had hoped that
we would have some Movements this
Year, but the Departure of the Squadron, and the
Depots have made us know that the
destinies will occasion only One
to change the Rules, and the Discipline itself
that maintains good order & the [Emancement] of
some Royals for the Regiment.
This is not thus affair I near,
to send some
One likes in [delaigue] the [Haulente]
✓      as people having the Best;
✓      At the [Richo], for [Aiole atandelles].
        One has ordered two Dutch
for the supply of Provisions
        One expects here to see appear
an Interpretation of the Ordinance
of Marine.


                                                Paris
