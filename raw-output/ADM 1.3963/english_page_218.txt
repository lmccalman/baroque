365

In the Month of October there was
in our Port a [Pelure], bound
for St Servans, of the Value of a Thousand
Livres tournois belonging to a
Merchant of Languedoc who
carried 1650. Quintals, paid
for St Servans, which was seized
by Merchants of Languedoc of
Two [Centennes], amounting to 325. of
[Pinel], of 1680. Livres, thus, which is
of subsidies by Two St Servans
[Rillieux]. A Brig, of 4500.
Livres tournois for St Servans,
amounting on Account of a
Merchant of Languedoc.

Presently we have,
in the Port, a Brig, amounting
at [Vames], of 4 Thousand Livres tournois,
for for St Servans, which
belongs to a Merchant
of Languedoc.
