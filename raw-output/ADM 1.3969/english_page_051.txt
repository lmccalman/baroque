ANNEX

                    Madrid, March 24, 1782

                    The Letters from Cadiz of the
10th of the Current [month] report, that the
[Mats des] of Gibraltar [quoiqu'ont]
almost all, through the Refreshments
that were distributed to them. And that
the Duke of Crillon and General
Elliot, had had Interviews
together.
                    On the 10th of the Current [month] died
here, the Marquis Gonzales de
Castefon, Minister of the Marine;
& the King has ordered the Count of
Florida Blancas, to take charge of
this Department, ad interim.
                    The Swiss Regiment of St Gall,
[voit participer peu] from here for
Galicia. And the first Battalion of
Walloon Guards having left the Camp of
St Roche, is expected here on the 11th or 12th
of the Current [month]; I say of next Month.

                                                           Cadiz.
