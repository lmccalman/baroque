                                                N. 27 October 1882

                            Toulon the 9th October 1770.

                                        The Commissioner
between ordinary warrior and correspondent with the
Minister of the Court of Versailles left from Paris,
returned here on the 7th, and brought the
Ratification, in Consequence the Frigate
of Altalaute, commanded by the Chevalier
d'Espagne [suppléa] [batamentes] in
Tripoli in the first decade of October.
The Minister had announced by this
same Courier the Arrangements, for
the Officers and Men of State; M. Bavent
[reçant] Commissioning Commissioner at
Bastia, M. de Batailleana [revisandrais]
Commissioner at Calvi, M. Rigoles
will go replace him at Calvi with order to
Deliver and to Provision; M. Sallane
Chevalier and Treasurer also receive from
the Court of Toulon.
Department of Marine of Bastia
provisioned in Hall of Toulon,
Marseille [Quarterniern] Clerk; Calvi one
will return to Bastia.
In the Letters of announced News from
this Port, where one still receives no Order
for the Completion of an Equipment,
one continues the Arms in the Magazines,
When they [onntâtre] to offer to the principal measures,
Received.
