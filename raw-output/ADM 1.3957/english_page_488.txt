    Mr. le Currey has the Command
of the Dauphin Royal, Dunkirk of 96, [demis],
Mr. d'Onas has orders to take The
[Rolle Paula] of 80. [Cavois] of 82, in
place of La Reine who is leaving.
    The Dauphin Royal, Le Bizarre, La
Barillon, le Cassandre, and Le Gros Ventre
are going to come from Rochefort here; Mr.
Traversay will command The Dauphin
Royal, and Mr. de La Mathonnie The
Bizarre.
    They are sending to L'Orient all
that is needed for the [Ans troisfomme]
that are being armed there, and which will return here.
