as undermining the Authority

one will see well, that the Grand
Council was not created, to
have Peace.

There appears a two Volumes;
entitled, Letters of the late King
Stangealli; they are very well
written, and of a very light Style;
that the Connoisseurs say;
that they were composed in
Paris, for they even say the Author.
I know that it is perfect, and I
tear myself away from them to read them.
