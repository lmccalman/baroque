Whitehall, Janv: 4e 1777.

Sir,

Mr Yeldert.                    In answer to the Question
                               proposed in your Letter of
                               the 15th & 26th Inst., I am directed
                               to tell you, that you may
                               promise an occasional
                               Gratuity to your Correspondents
                               whenever they give you
                               material Information; &
                               with regard to that Correspondent
                               who Letter you enclosed,
                               it may be continued another
                               Quarter.
                                        I am &c &c
                                             Fanvrick.
