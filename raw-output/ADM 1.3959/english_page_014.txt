                        Paris the 6th March 1792.

                                            The Letters from Brest
state, that the officers are no longer
required to send back their Journals to
the [Chefs-naux], as well as the General
which is declared. M. Lavilleurbois
& Rolland & Rochefort 2 of Instruments
ordered

The Courier from Touraine has not
returned. I received from yesterday to warn;
I was told to return
They are going to continue at L'Orient a
Vessel of 64, and a frigate, on
the Plan given by M. Brun,
Engineer.
They are going to reestablish the Position of Paymaster
of the Colonies, which was Suppressed
5 to Months ago: they will give nothing
in addition, but they will not be able
to [se tourner] more Aid, than from
[allées] which for their Revenues will
