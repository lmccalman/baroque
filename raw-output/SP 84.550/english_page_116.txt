   The Letters from Malaga of the 5th of
this Month report, that there is nothing
troublesome, touching our
Accommodation with Morocco.
all the News from our Refugees
in Africa depicts, that these Barbarians
are very tranquil.
   The famous Mr Barcelo there
was awaiting [ting] xebecs, to go
refit them at Cartagena,
and that it was added there, that as much
at Cadiz, as at Ferrol, one was arming
20. Vessels or Frigates, to
divine the Object of it; but here
at the Secretariat of the Marine,
I am assured from good authority;
that it is only [Se. ou Si.] for
one of Prevention, to be
