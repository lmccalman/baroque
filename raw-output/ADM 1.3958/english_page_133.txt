There is therefore no other refuge against the
Fugitives and Crimes of what remained
of the Sansons against all that remained
in France, and principally all the
Party of Parliament, one should not be
surprised to see appear an Ordinance
which would declare the Publicity of these
fugitives in the Kingdom. The
Chamber makes the Article in Procedure of
all the city that goes.

Our Letters from Rome which were delayed
by one day, say that on the 9th of March the Pope
should make Cardinals, and
declare many things in a
Consistory. They await the News.

Brussels the 21st March 1791.

Individuals have brought in
since then 4000 Horses for the train
of Artillery
all the General Officers are preparing
to depart.
The Duke of Ursel leaves today
for Paris, but the object
of his Voyage is unknown.
