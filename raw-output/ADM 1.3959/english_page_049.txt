Others claim that it is a House
of the Controller General, to obtain
Two Million more from the Assembly
of the Clergy, which will hold it in the Month of October,
by making them hope for the Revocation
of the Declaration, which makes so much Noise.
Since he is there, the Archbishop of
Paris has resolved to establish it in his Diocese,
and has made an agreement to give from
Madame de Chantal in the Religious
Houses of the Daughters St. Mary, so that
the Bulls do not go as far as
the Parliament. There come prophets this
latter as much to make flourish
the said Bull, that's all the Ceremony.
The King named last Sunday
the Abbot de la Rochefoucauld Bishop of Amiens
of Revenue, and the Bishopric of Beauvais, vacant
by the Resignation of Cardinal de Forbin,
they have placed 36 Thousand Livres of Pension on
this See, of which 10 Thousand for the Cardinal,
besides the Abbey of l'Aiples, which is worth 50.
The King has fixed the Domain Ferret the
Distributions of the Count of Artois and the Lists
of the Troops, they are done in the Courts
of the Castles and Versailles.
