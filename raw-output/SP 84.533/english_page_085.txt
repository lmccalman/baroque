The Tax imposed on the new Nobility
has been reduced by one Third, that is to say
that instead of 3 Thousands, they will only pay
2 Thousands, without including the 3 Sols per
Livre; the Ennoblements will pay as for them
[à les Belles de Lyon les Hommes]; it is
counted that there are 20 Thousands to draw to judge
at the same time the other 4 to Thousands, drawn;
that must produce more than 60 Thousands.

Work will begin to establish the Offices
Established by the edict of December for the [prises]
constantly from the borders of the countries that would pay,
so that one can finally Pass from being
[dechu] & restored to the Rank of [Notoire] to
be imposed to the taille & to other Charges.

Work is being done to establish the Offices
for the Administration of Mortgages; the King has
[fait l'acis pour l'acis] the grand Hotel of
Bullio, near the grand Post.

There was published the same day an Arrest of the
Council of [Monsegas], to establish the Profit
that sends, that the Captures of 3 Sols [allévies] &
[à mandats] to Rio de la Plata.

It has been reported for some days, that
[l'emprisur] in his finances, and Chagas and du
Royer, and that the orders anew; It is
[aogue] Mr. Portail, State Councilor, and the
Masters of Requests have gone to Besançon.

On.
