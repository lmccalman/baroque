Gregory

Paris the 18th January 1785.

The same uncertainty still
reigns over the consequences that
the disputes of the Emperor and
Holland may have. The military abuse
no less a Congress, although
however it alone can
appease the salutary remedy that
the state of living requires.

No new order has been
issued for the army destined
to camp; which makes one believe that the
congress will be assembled before it.

A frigate has just arrived
from India, with dispatches from
Mr de Bussy, who informs our
ministry of the anxiety into which
