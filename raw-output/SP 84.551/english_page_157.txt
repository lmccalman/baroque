Tuscany, of Archduke Maximilian,
and of the Duke of Saxe-Teschen, who
was proceeding to the Camp of Maneuvers
at Two Leagues from here, composed
of 14 Battalions, and of 56 Squadrons
of carabineers.

Around mid-April this Monarch
will pass to that of Pest in Hungary,
consisting of 18 Battalions, and
56 Squadrons.

At the beginning of September
His Majesty will pass to the Camp of
the troops of Bohemia, which must
assemble near Prague.
And toward the end of said Month, to
that of the troops of Moravia.

The Emperor has no plan
this Year for the Artillery Camp,
which they form every Year at Mellet
near Budesch in Bohemia.
