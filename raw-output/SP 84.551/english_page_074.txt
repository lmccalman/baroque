EXTRACT
FROM THE COUNCIL REGISTERS.

Of June 19, 1776.

THE COUNCIL having deliberated in several
sessions on the Petition submitted to Messieurs
the Syndics on the 10th of last month by
Citizens & Bourgeois, the opinion was to respond:
That the COUNCIL recognizes no other Edicts
having force of law in the State, than those which
were printed in 1707, & thenceforth succes-
sively until our days: that the printing
which was done at that time included the Edict of
1568 & all subsequent Edicts; as being
the only ones which were then in force; that this
Collection was distributed nearly a month before
the MAGNIFICENT & SOVEREIGN GENERAL COUNCIL
approved the Regulation of the MAGNI-
FICENT COUNCIL OF TWO HUNDRED which had
ordered it; that by this approbation, given
without any objection, it received the sanction
