One will see after the Holidays,
the War recommence between
Him, and Mr. de Jacomman.
    The King will give Audience
Tomorrow to the Ambassadors
and Foreign Ministers. He will
give it then to the Deputies
of the Estates of Languedoc, who
will present their Cahiers,
following Usage. His Majesty
will leave the day after Tomorrow, to
go spend some Days
at Choisy. The Queen will not
make this Journey, but She
will make Her own, which the King
must make in the coming
Weeks.
