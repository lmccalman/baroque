Brest the 4th September 1778.

                                    One works with
      very little ever at the port works,
      there are still [metaux perus]
¶    very [aller] with the walls of nine
      & Buildings [reponds cher chez] in
      almost new.
                                    Fourteen Vessels, and Six
      frigates are [malades], and ready to
¶    go with at the first Order.
                                    One has increased by 38 the Number
      of ovens that make Biscuit.
                                    The Bavin has just arrived from
      Martinique, the Officers of this
¶    Vessel are very sick;
      for having eaten a [largue]
      where there was Verdigris.
                                    One occupies oneself, at [la Bale], with
      all others to leave squadrons of Mr
¶    De Chaffault, to go to the
      Colonies.
