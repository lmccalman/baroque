On the 5th of the current month arrived at Corunna
from Havana the King's frigate the
Cattet with correspondence, some
little money, 233 crates of sugar,
tobacco, and other goods.

The same day arrived at Cadiz
from Montevideo the frigate St Francis
de Paule with a cargo of
28 thousand untanned hides, and other
goods.

All the Spanish Infantry is going
to be reduced to forty Men per
company, as it was.
The general levy being unable to suffice
to fill the void, this proves our
lack of population.

There arrived these past days
two extraordinary couriers from Naples
it is suspected that the dispatches are
related to the Emperor's claims
on the Status of the Presidii of Tuscany.
