Teylingen June 10th 1966.

Sir,

I began to hope that the storm which threatened my chief Paris correspondent was blowing over, as for three posts past I have as usual received his intelligence, but I shall not be quite easy till I know that the man at Toulon who was of such infinite use in this affair is safe.
