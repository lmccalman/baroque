P.S. Since writing this letter I am informed, that it is
judged by some that I endeavour to establish a gallery;
for fear it should hurt the interest of I.B--- Brothers;
as this is the land of my habitation, and if that should
be attributed to ill designs I cannot but be concerned,
and submit to the Judgment and Decree of my
Superiors, humbly aiming to recommend myself and
approve the duty, without bartering with other Brothers.
