N° 60.                                    Paris the 26th July 1776.

                        It is reported from Toulon
on the 18th that the Frigate l'Aimable was to
be launched to Sea on the 20th. The Three
armed frigates have departed; they want
to continue to work on the
future works begun.
Biscuits are being made in force.
    The Duke of Chartres is expected
at Tours for the 15th of next [month], having
to leave the Squadron, which will not return to
port until September. This Squadron will
perhaps from time to time fire the B.de
of Brittany, where it trains heavily
with weak fire. The same
Preparations continue in the Port of Brest.
    The Execution of the Ordinance
for the Reforms of the War Offices
encounters Difficulties,
the co[?] do not go. Serve the R.né in them
were, the King said to the C.te de St
                                                   Germain.
