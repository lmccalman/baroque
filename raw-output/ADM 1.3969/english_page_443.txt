The Count de Vergennes
was not present at this Council,
having been kept at home by a
Catarrh, accompanied by a rather
strong fever. He charged the Baron
de Breteuil with his Portfolio.
We shall know in a short time,
by the Rest, or the Movement of
the Army in Flanders, if this
Response is favorable or contrary
to the Interests of the Dutch.
