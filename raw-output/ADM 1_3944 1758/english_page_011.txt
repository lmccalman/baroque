All our Officers exalt the Maneuver
of Prince Ferdinand: he is positioned in such a Manner
as to be able to cross back over the Rhine, and to be able to engage
or avoid Combat, and it was a Matter
of Honor to beat the Hanoverians
   They write from Hanau on the 21st that the Army of
Soubise is going to march to go to Bohemia;
there are 70 Days of March, and it would arrive there
in the Month of September, to take up
winter quarters. I refer back to what
I told you in my last letter, and I am
persuaded, that in whole or in part it will descend
the Rhine to come toward the Side of Wesel to
Support the Army of Clermont.
   There is daily talk of several
