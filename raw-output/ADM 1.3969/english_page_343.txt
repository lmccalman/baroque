Genoa the 16th March 1784

Since my last I have
sold here to Spanish Merchants
a Ship, carrying two hundred Thousand
Quintals, and armed for 480 Cannons.
Another of the same caliber, was sold
to a Dutchman. Five Transport Vessels
of 330 Quintals were also sold
to the Spaniards, without Cannons,
A Barque of 5 to 6 Hundred
Quintals, Two small Frigates of
22 Cannons, Four Barques or Tartans
of 300 Quintals, without Cannons.

At the end of last Month,
Four Transport Barques of 480 Quintals
and 14 Cannons were sold to Russian Burghers;
A Corvette of 48 Cannons,
Two Barques of 600 Quintals, A
Ship of 1.5 Thousand Quintals, which one
