                                                A 30 Sept. No 2


                    Paris the 29th September 1776.

                    Nothing interesting has been
received from Toulon
     This King was arriving from Cherbourg (an entire
Artillery Corps(?) below
Dispatches arrived by a Courier
from Madrid. It appears that
Spain wants to avenge itself on the
Portuguese; which will lead to
a general war.
     Besides 40 Battalions that there are
in Brittany, 40 others are being
marched there, and 30 in Normandy.
An Army Corps must be assembled
near Bayonne.
     The General Officers employed,
whose Divisions have the new
are called Orders. There are
20 appointed for the
