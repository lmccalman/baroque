547.

To put into service a 4th fine Squadron
[leger] the Suite & the Service of the War
   of the [Reveres] of Sailors of Normandy &c.
✓ The Portage has not yet arrived.
   The Commander of the Deffinir is not
yet decided. The Review of the Crew
of the 2. Ships which was to be done yesterday,
has been postponed until tomorrow.
   The Orders commanded for Brest are
made, [années] for several
✓ Ships for the Relief of the [Régies],
and departed from here all at once, by a
favorable Wind!
   The Return of the [Enseole] Lieutenant
of Vessel, was made happily from Bort:
M. de Blangier Ensign of Vessel,
was made Ensign of Bort.
   The three of June was taken out to the
Workers of Brest, and the three of October
to those who are new. The active sum
of the Suite for the month of August 150. Thousand livres.
   The 30. first time to seal the Water and
to See the stowage in full, much
Money for this Month.
   One does not speak of the Return of Mr. des
Closny, But people say that he
will return near the Ministers, and that
even he must be joined to the Ministers.
