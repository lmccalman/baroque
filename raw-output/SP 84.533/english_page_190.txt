Toulon the 10th September 1793.

                    The Frigates [luo]
the Egypt, and the Topaz have returned
into the Port, the first after 40 Days
of Quarantine, and the Second after
47 Days, they are being disarmed for the
last [enquêtes] for the Levant.
   The Règale has been sent from the Army
that the 14 to 15 Years, that one has furnished
of the Rule of Toulon taken by this
Frigate.
   We have had from the [Reye] finally
enough to make Woods of the Vine
to preserve what remains of Olive trees.
And which gives still from this Year
in Provence.


                                        Brest,
