We do not speak of all our Ordinances,
one speaks then without making all the truth
only of Reforms.
     The Ordinance announced for
the Defenders, and that carrying a
general Amnesty, although indeed
well printed, but they are
not yet published.
     That which establishes [Juilliers]
of Serre, appears a bit harsh;
it will be able to retain the Deserters,
and it will make them less frequent,
because one will fear less the
Deaths, than this new Chain.
     That carrying Amnesty,
grants Two years to the Deserters
who are in foreign lands, to
return; it does not oblige them
to serve, makes them absolutely
free to withdraw where they wish.
It sends Three livres to the
Deserters, who will repent,
and who will return to their
