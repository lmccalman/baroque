Accounts Mus. Phisic. Sept 1757

I certify having made payment of the
sums hereafter to Mademoiselle 
the Countess of Liedekierke in the capacity of Guardian
of the minors of the late Monsieur the Count of Liedekierke
namely six hundred Brabant florins for the six months
fallen due on the last of July one thousand seven hundred fifty seven
of the Capital of Twelve thousand Brabant florins of which the
Domain of Brabant is indebted to the estate of
Sieur and Madame Lecompte de Liedekierke for
acknowledgment of a Contract in six hundred florins here . . . . . . 600

Furthermore for the redemption at the rate of twenty of the annual
interest of the Sum of Twelve Hundred Brabant florins
at the rate of five per Cent making twenty four
thousand florins here. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 24000

In which Domain I have given the present Certificate
under my Signature and the Seal of the arms of my Office
At Brussels this thirteenth day of the month of August one thousand Seven Hundred
Fifty Seven

Received Villee pr
