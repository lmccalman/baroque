Trumeley March 1779.

The present is only
to ask you that from Cries
proposes absolutely nothing at all
New at Dunkirk.
A Director is ready and for the fodder
prepares the exchange for he will return
to the purses in the Environs
remains in the same State.

Brussels the 31. March 1779.

The Direction has Returned
and frequent in France, that it arrives here
for Twentieths at &c. strong, and as it
has the power to employ as many
Tax Collectors as it judges Proper
and makes them return from where
they have been signed, one the most fruitful
put down, and they are transported
to the Minister of France.

P.M.
For M?
