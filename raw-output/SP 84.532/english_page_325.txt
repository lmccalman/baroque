The current Position of the Parlements cannot last long, after Easter nothing is still done there. The Peers are [differen], however a bull from the Pope has been placed in Provence with the date of 1769 to demonstrate that it was confined to Martinique.

The young King of Sweden has not yet departed, the Voyages are not ready, besides his brother is ill. Others claim that this new King is detained here by a [Regentchen] from Valencia by his dear Ambassadress.

It has been said that Villeroy had the honor to [se demettre] the day before yesterday a thesis at magnificent at Bucelle, in the House that the Cardinal de Richelieu had built there, whose [vot] remains in Plato.

This debt includes Furniture Precious Stones, [Jorhommes], that the Duke of Aiguillon had been declared Minister of Foreign Affairs for some time, the King was forced to Appoint him, and appointed Evening at the Hotel of this House before taking it there to leave for the Embassy of Sweden. He takes from here Place, he sends nothing at all. He is on very interview with the Chancellor.
