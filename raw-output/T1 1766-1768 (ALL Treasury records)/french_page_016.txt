St. James's Jan'y 10th 1767.

Sir,

     Mr. Wolters, His Majesty's Agent
at Rotterdam, having drawn a Bill on
Mr. Davis for Two Hundred and Seventy
Two Pounds, Five Shillings, for Disbursements
for Secret Service, Mr. Secretary Conway
has directed me to desire You will be pleased
to move the Lords of the Treasury, that Their
Lordships may give the necessary Directions
for the Payment of the Bill abovementioned.
          I am,
               Sir,
                    Your most obedient
                         humble Servant
                              Wm Fraser
