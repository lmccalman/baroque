the 17th of June


                    Pradt the 17th of June 1782.

                There has just arrived news
in the Port three Regiments
of the Congress, one in Rhode Island
there comes the Duke of Lauzun with
a Part of the Legion; the Three
Frigates are the gloire, the Astrée, and
the Danae.

                We are waiting for the Astrée, which
was to leave Two Days after, she
will be passing through, and what we
cannot see arrive the
Bonalius, the Guadeloupe, and the
Laurun, Vessels chartered by the
Congress, to bring back the Troops
and a Part of the Administration.
One does not know if the America
has yet arrived [de filé].

                Mr de Lauzun will leave Tomorrow
Evening for Pradt.

                The King has taken in the House
of a Director of the King's Warehouses,
and has all the Effects of
several of the Employees.

                                                    Pradt
