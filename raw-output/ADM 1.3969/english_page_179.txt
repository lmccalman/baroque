Mr de la Porte continues to 
procure affairs of the Navy
perhaps, until the Accounts
are entirely cleared up. He
retires with 20 Thousand Livres.

The King has added four new
Administrators to the Administration
of the Posts. Mr de Bourgade is among
the number, and it is not believed that he
leaves for that reason, the Controller-General.

The Marquis de Broglie returned
from America, where he had been recalled,
disembarked at Brest, and died in that
Port.
