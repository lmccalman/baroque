Kingdom, two Lords
Land Rules

The Baron d'Ogny, General
Intendant of Posts has obtained from
the King the Approval of the Office
of Grand Master of ceremonies
of the Order of St. Louis, consequently
he wears the Grand Red Ribbon,
and the Plaque, He took yesterday
Oath in the King's Hands.

The Intendant of Bordeaux,
Mr. de Clugny, went on an interim
basis to govern the Intendancies of Paris,
of Auch, and the Position of Mr.
Soumak, who has been dismissed;
The latter, out of Despair,
cut his Throat.
