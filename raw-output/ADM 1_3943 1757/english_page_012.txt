The King wishing to give to the Duke of Berry the
Dignity of Chief and Grand Master of the Order of
St. Lazarus, requested for this a Brief from the
Pope, his Holiness granted it, and put therein, that
this Prince would bear arms, and promise to
wage War against the Infidels in person, provided
that the Constitutions of the Pope, and notably the
last one, there was made a Brief of Letters Patent
which were addressed to the Grand Council to be
registered; this Tribunal refused them, alleging that
the Pope's Brief was contrary to our Gallican
Liberties.
