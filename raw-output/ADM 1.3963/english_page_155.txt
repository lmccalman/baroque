                    R.7 Sept 8bre 3


                    Toulon the 22nd April 1778.

                    The Chevalier Collaire
entered into the 1st [Skien], work is underway
on his Disarmament; the Vessels of
this transport are sent back to
their Ports as requested.

                    The Minister reports that the
Divisions of Mr Dabon, in order
in the Strait, will be traced
shortly to Toulon; it will be
in the Roadstead, and under a Flag,
until we have Orders.

                    The chevalier de Demis Lentazy
sent from Malta to our
Commander, the following
News:

                    [Illon], the Vaillant, a Vessel
arriving from Marseille which
announced to us some of
our knights, deposited, are
                                        encountered
