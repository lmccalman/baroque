Paris, October 7, 1796.

News from Toulon
on the 30th, that they have disembarked from the Excellent
the Crews of 6 fireships.
The Sultane was dismasted while
leaving the Pâles d'Hieres where
[le fort grand Mal des Mânes];
They put another one from
Le Pino, shorter, and she is
better. The Caméléon was to
leave that same day from [armez].
The Lingo was to do the same
three days later. [L'Escu des
Voyages les Bannières] of Brest,
of Malaga.

The Esfini is finished, they are likewise finishing
the Patelaine, and the Hector;
they continue to inspect the other
Vessels.

The Spanish Consul was expected
in Toulon, [sur-le-besquet], until the end
of this month [pour l'obten pouva faire
ces Monuments].
