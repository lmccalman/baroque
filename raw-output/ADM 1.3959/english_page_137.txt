Our Letters and Dispatches, always come
that you are making Amendments in all the
Ports of the Monarchy. But as
I have no trusted Correspondent there,
I do not guarantee you their exact News.
I [tenis] you to believe it, If these Reports
are true, favorably.

They launched into the Water these past Days.
Two other Brigs, of the Capacity of 4 Thousand
And Hundreds, these Vessels belong
to various Private individuals, of whom most,
Mariners. They have a Number of large
Vessels, rigged as Brigs, and
Thereupon, they conduct a great Commerce
in the Levant.
