The Marshal and Armentières who command
in Ostend and this, also had likewise
in the Morning Arms taken up by a Part of the Garrison
of Nieuwpoort, 10 Leagues from there, for the same purpose
and the same day, when the Councils of Flanders, of
Chambers and were notified, one has as much caused
to be registered the Patent Rules for the Chamber
of Visitations, and afterwards he presented a
Decree of the Council, which quashes and annuls the Decree
of the, concerning Messieurs de Calonne and
de Harlay in two words which, as well as the other
Registers, in the instant one has notified them
to be withdrawn, the First President is the
Chief Clerk wanting to keep likewise
Mr the Marshal gave them back a Letter of
Cachet, bearing order to remain, and to cause
to be registered the Registers, which was executed.
Mr Nicolaï had agreed in the Margin
who could not resist such fear
destined there of Messieurs de Calonne and of
d'Harlay

After that the Regulation was notified
at the First President's, he was still there
at Midnight of the same day, one does not yet know
what Course he will take.
