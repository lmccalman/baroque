No 26.

                    Paris the 25th March 1776.

                         For the Colonels,
[chantres] Officers, it is ordered to you
to provide for their lodging on the 30th
of next April, to await there
the orders of His Majesty.
Your Orders for their
lodgings are not, but the
Secret is inviolably
kept.
     It is supposed, that there is a
very strong request presented to the
King to request the
establishment of one of the
Companies of Musketeers.
Establishment I have at the Gendarmerie,
& unworthy the King to pay all the
world thereby, that it is the Marquis
de Ceries who made the Order
