Paris the 5th August 1782.

A Courier from Spain has
informed us that the Armament of
Cartagena under the Orders of Don Barcelo
has set Sail, most of the Vessels
were still believed on the 10th and 11th, but on the 16th
of the Month, they were all aligned, and
as the Wind was good, it was hoped
that the Bombardment of Algiers would have
begun on the 19th or 20th of the same Month of
July.

M. Amelot, has been obliged to hand over
his Portfolio into the Hands of M.
Vergennes, - A serious Illness
has been the cause, for three Weeks.

The Baron de Breteuil, entered
last Sunday the King's Council of State
this new Minister does not
yet have a Department.

The Marquis de Noailles, will
replace him in Vienna.

M.
