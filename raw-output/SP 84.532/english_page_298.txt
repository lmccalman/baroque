From Petit to the Prince January 9, 1778

The land no longer has any
hope, that which [quirint] to the Rumors of
War, troubles much.

The Affairs concerning the New
in the Palatinate, regarding our
succession, proceed well by degrees.

We have been afflicted since our
Departure, by a pestilential Disease,
which has put two-thirds of the Officers
in the tomb, the troops are almost
annihilated by this Misfortune.
