One infers, that the Military
Promotion that has been expected for
a long time, is completed; but
that it will only be declared at
Fontainebleau. It is claimed
that in this Promotion
there are three Marshals of
France, namely, Messieurs
de St Germain, de Maillebois,
and Mauby Daucourt.
