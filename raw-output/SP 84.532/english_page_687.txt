The castellany is in the greatest
difficulty, more than 600 Cases have
been judged there in 9 Months: The Attorneys
count on not being well Paid; they are full
of Zeal and Ardor.

The Masters of Requests are making
Remonstrances against the Nomination
of 4 Presidents for the Requests of
the Household, and demand that these Positions
be merged with the most senior
among Them. There is still no
Response.

The Rumor runs that the King wants
to appoint the Bailiwicks and [Chausées] in
temple and the Seneschalsies. The Grand Paris,
is already the Inquiry of the Household Paris,
in that the Privileges of which all at
the beginning, and that the temple is a
Retreat for all the Bankrupts.
This Priory is a House of 3 Hundred [Mèdes]
or at least the Revenues of the [Prône]
of 3 Hundred, as Grand Prior of
France.

The Count of La Marche, the Only Prince
who goes to Court, was Lodged yesterday Morning at
Meuily, will announce to the King the Death
of the Count of Clermont His Uncle.
