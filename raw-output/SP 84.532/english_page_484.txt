GENTLEMEN,

HIS MAJESTY deposits in your hands the most noble & most extensive portion of power; it is in his heart & Justice of simple faith, He exhorts you to occupy yourselves & to contain under the afflicted penalties, & to contain by your subjects to watch over the maintenance of the Laws, for their duration to all this faithful to the King's example, loyal, just with it, as much as it is only with grace that He communicates to you does not permit, Justice, & justice to communicate Justice, that It will not be being in your hands, if you can forget a single instant that it is the King's Justice & not yours,

Sure of the probity of the Courts, you will always render true Justice, to homage the most sure & most faithful, & you will give to the people the example of submission & to foreign Sovereigns

You will not however make blind judgments & the victim of an absolute will, & which could be any other.
