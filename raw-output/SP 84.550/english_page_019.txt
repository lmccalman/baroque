Second.

Tuesday the 23rd December 1773.

In my last
I had advised, that our
Chief and Squadron, Mr Ullee, was
appointed to command and
escort the Expedition that was
being prepared at Cadiz, for Buenos
Aires; but this is an Error;
for the said Ullee is destined
to command and escort
our flotilla, with twelve
Warships, to Vera
Cruz, in the Month of May,
or next June.

arrived here from Lisbon, has
suddenly changed the face
