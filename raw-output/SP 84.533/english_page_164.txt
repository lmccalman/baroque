Rotterdam 30 Sept.r 1773.

Sir

The french Vessels Engaged in m[?]
only prohibited bred in France, as you will
see by this days Intelligence. but
the Distribution of them thro this Country.
is likewise suspended for the time
of two Weeks, but we are promised
that we shall have them after the
expiration of that term. Besides
this punishment, he has been condemned
to a fine as...

The Reason of all this is, because he
had put in his Gazette, that Madame
the Dauphine had [far-some] Recommended
[Sun des Ames], and had greatly
rejoiced.

I have the Honour to be with due Respect.
                                                          Sr
                                        your most humble and
                                        most Obedient Servant
Mr. Frazer &c &c                              L.C. Hake.
