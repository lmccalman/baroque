They were not pleased at Versailles
with the fact that the Dutch have inundated
the Flat Country in the Environs of their
Forts via the Scheldt; If they
had been threatened, they would have
been pardoned this Operation.

Paris the 19th November 1784.

It is not at all surprising,
that the Orders for the Departure of the Imperial
Troops have not yet been given;
the March of an Army in this
Season, and through the bad Roads
of Germany, would prove very
hazardous and reckless, to say
nothing more: There are therefore until
the present only the Troops from Flanders
and Brabant who can garrison
these frontier Posts of the Dutch;
