Vienna, November 16, 1805.

Finally, and after all the peaceful
Appearances, our troops must march,
the order is given, according to which
the Regiments of Bender, Meyrzki,
Cubing, and Warrafor, must depart
on November 9.
Presto on the 20th
Jablunkowsky on the 24th.
Leopold Teronne Dragons, on November 18.
Cellier on December 10th
The Warasdins also on December 30th.
Lattermann awaits his orders.
The Miners, Sappers, &c, have already
departed.
Four Companies of Artillerymen and Obriste,
who will arrive on December 8th, at Linz,
will be distributed among the Regiments.
The other free Companies, and the
Regiments both Infantry and Cavalry,
will remain in the Empire, of the Princes
of Nassau - Usingen, the orders for
their Route.
