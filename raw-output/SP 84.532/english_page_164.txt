Toulon the 22nd January 1793.

                    We have received a [détenu]
our Ships and Frigates, they are in
a state to be armed at the first Order;
as well as the Frigates: The Armament
of our Batteries is finished.

The expense and the Magazines have been
put at the [Valée] of more than the bourgeois the
[Levants] of the Necessary Corps been
necessary.

There are Russian warships
at Mahon to refresh themselves.
Three Dutch warships
which had put in here, departed
the day before yesterday to go
to the Levant ahead of
their Merchant Ships.


                    Brest
