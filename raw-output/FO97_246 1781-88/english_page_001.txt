Rotterdam the 25th March 1785.

My Lord,

I have the Honour to send you
enclosed the Account of my Disbursements for
the Quarter of general Correspondence, due
today, amounting to ƒ2975. Current
Money of Holland, which at the Exchange
Rate of this Day, makes ƒ2976. 14. 6. Sterling;
for which Sum I will take the Liberty
to draw by the next Post, on the
Treasury, to the order of Mr. J. B. Montigny.
I have the Honour to be with profound Respect

My Lord,

Your very humble and
very Obedient Servant
M. Wolters.
