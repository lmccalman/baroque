First

Pardo, February 28, 1776.

A [Mefame] of some hours prevents me from writing to you, and I only do so today to give a sign of life; nor do the affairs [les bien mes] give me more matter for anything else.

The Minister of Marine [Efourth] died, they have named Don Galvez as Minister of the Indies, and Don Castegon, who commanded the [Ordennce] of Algiers, as Minister of Marine. The first is a great man, according to general opinion.
