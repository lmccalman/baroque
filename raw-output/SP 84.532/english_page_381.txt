From the 8th to the 9th. the 8th January 1773.

The contagious Diseases are not
continuing. The [moins loujours] was
giving us good from day to day. A
Report that I received from the chapter, 
convinces me [ont été pelle] & Remois.
The fair continues always on the
rise, but the Poor are not
considerable at present.
One must consider only the objects
of work of lands [la font particulies],
which causes great Damage
to many dwellings, which are
obliged to go 2 and 3 leagues to
seek Water. But what
one notices is astonishing,
is to see in a River that flows
one, small Mountains of Sands,
come from the Entrails of the Earth,
in grains of [Reubles] and Woods,
very long and powerful [Hommes],
[d'Hygrois] of the Beaches that extend
up to the Hill, the quantity of it
is so greatly that I ignore
that this thing is a great and strange
Movement.
