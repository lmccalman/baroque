War'y Office 13. Sept 1118

Sir

In return to your Letter of the
8th instant, enclosing Copy of One
you received from Mr Stokes of
Rotterdam, I am to acquaint you
that if the King of Sl Mercantiliers
to sent to the Peary Board, They will
cause an Experiment to be made of
it, and Report the result of it with
their Opinions thereon. I am

Sir

Your most hum[ble] Obedient
[signature]
