to request Permission, from His Royal Highness
this Minister, who had worked the
previous days with the King, did not expect
this compliment there, he was dismayed by it;
but soon he recovered, and did what was
asked of him, saying, I do
not regret my Position, but I regret
the Fate of the Unfortunate, whose
Moment of fortune I followed: he was
Threatened, and sent back to Brussels to Paris,
and since then his family
has not been to see him. There are still some of his
Partisans, but in small Number;
