at St James this 9th August 1771.

                                                Madam,
Mr Wilton:
                                                The Proposition that You
                                                made to me to make the [l'Eglise]
                                                of a Correspondent in Vienna, at
                                                the Rate of 200 Florins per
                                                Quarter, having been approved by
                                                You yourself, Madam [de duc
                                                même, la plus et le meilleur],
                                                I I will not fail to
                                                give You from Time to Time
                                                my Sentiments on the Revolutions.
                                                It only remains for me at present
                                                to inform You that the Desire that
                                                You have shown on this Occasion,
                                                to see our Our two as useful
                                                as possible [à] merits the greatest
                                                Approbation.

                                                I am &c.

                                                                Lespoth.
