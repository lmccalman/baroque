Toulon the 4th September 1778.

                    He has finished his finishing
loading in Wood and in Rochefort,
He is going to leave to return to
Brest.
        We await from Orient the Chevalier
le Desmazures.
        He has orders to take at Toulon some Orders
to go there for the purpose of Vessels
of 74.
        We still await the Division
of the Squadron it still has Orders there
to make it stay until new
to arm.
        We have had notice, that the French
with the Hyacinthe with Payment of the King to Thomas,
form an Establishment. It
is good If to go the Coasts of
Barbary, near Tangier.

                                    [SEAL]  Brest
