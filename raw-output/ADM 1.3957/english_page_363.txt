Brussels the 10th September 1770.

Monday until around Noon,
all the Streets of this City are filled,
by Order of the Court, without
anyone being able to Leave, not even the
Stagecoach; during this Closure one
saw Patrols who crossed
all the Streets, and made their Visits
in all the Houses, asking in
each House the Names of the People who
were lodged there, of whom several
Foreigners were led to Arrest,
Until the next day to the Object of this [Manie].

The Disease among the large Cattle is
taking strong Root, that it has
communicated up to the Interior of
this City and in the [Eaux bourges].
