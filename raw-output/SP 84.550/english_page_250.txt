Versailles 18 May 1778.

The Pardon, the of
Invokes the persons, seeing from a
Brillon and all the [Pères] of Grace
were put in Place yesterday, they will
peg them, put a false
Keel of 4 Inches, after balancing
the Vessel, they will next do the
Caulking and the Fittings
it is expected that everything will be finished at
the end of this Month, no time is lost
On the work of masting the three
frigates which must be fitted out
they have substituted the Bourdonnée
for the Indiscrette, because of the
Repairs made to the [Carthage]
of this latter, which one
cannot do afloat
