13 29 Nov. No 2


Vienna, November 6, 1786.

On the 3rd in the evening, two
Couriers arrived here, one from Berlin, and the other
from Paris; the Rumor spread
immediately throughout the whole City, that the Emperor
had been displeased with the Dispatches from the
Former, that His Imperial Majesty
had given him 300 Ducats.
It was added that the King of Prussia
had declared: That, if the Imperial
Court reclaimed its ancient
Rights of free Navigation for
the Scheldt, and that it demanded from him the
[rien de plus] of 1648, and of 1713, Holland,
Switzerland, Germany and even would not meddle
at all in this Affair, and would remain
a tranquil Spectator.
From the side of France, one declared:
that one would offer a proportionate sum;
