14 May

Paris the 4 August 1782.

                    We have nothing
interesting from our Sea Ports.
They are sending many Merchant
Ships from Bordeaux. The word
of Summer but all for the Account of
Private individuals.

        It is assured that at the Hour that it
is, that the English are in Possession
of Crimea.

        The Commercial Letters saying
that this Peace departed on the 16th.
From the port of Kronstadt, but it is not
believed that she actually departed.

        One is not yet sure, if the
Council of War for the Affair of
April 12th will take place or not.

        M. de La Porte requests a
position of Master of Requests. He
is still too young to renounce
all Work.

                                                Madrid
