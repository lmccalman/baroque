Brest the 8th June 1778.

        The named one is being
Armed, she will depart from the 18th to the 20th,
to go join the Squadron.
Mr de Montluc, after having left
the Squadron, has a Mission that one
does not know.
        The two other frigates being
Armed, commanded by
Messieurs de Beaufier, and de Rinier,
will depart at the same time, the
First for St Domingue, the
Second for the Windward Islands.
        The frigate that Mr Dombe
commands, will also depart at the same
time, one does not know its Destination;
[avant] des Brigands de la Cour at
approximately high tide.
        The 4 other Vessels have all
Been Supplied with Provisions.
