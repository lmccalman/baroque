My Lord Duke—

[faded text - partially illegible]

In consequence of his grace the Duke of Newcastle's
orders, I have herewith begun his'ness my intelligence
to your grace.

I have herewith received my commission from
the Post Office as Commissioner to the Duchess Dowager;
I hope then the choice which I shall make of a
Deputy and the measures that I shall take in the
discharge of this additional trust will meet with
your grace's approbation.

I send by Lord Ancram the Catalogue of medals—
                                                    Twickell's—
Duke of Bedford. —
