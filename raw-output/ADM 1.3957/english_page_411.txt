475

Bourbon Court [aurienhbleis] well illuminated
their Palaces, which astonished.
    Letters seen from Constantinople of the 30th
September report, that there was a
Dreadful Fire in Constantinople, which was
reduced to Ashes: that after the Populace
demanded the Sultan to show himself, the Chiefs
were arrested and put to Death; it had
also besieged for a long
time the Emperor's Palace.
    The respective Armies are quiet
on both sides on the Banks of the Danube.
Bender does not appear to surrender.
    The Senate of Naples has Obtained from the two
delegates of the Pope two Charters of
current rights to force them only on a single State.
Thus they have already [rhabités] in a
Hundred on these Ports. They are Masters
of Liguria, and they are greatly so of
several Ships loaded with Provisions,
which has caused Scarcity and High prices in
Naples. It is said that following Ostia and Salé
detached to judge at the Alley to put [Saberesque]
under Contribution; this is a Thing scarcely probable
without.
    Other letters received by Courier say
that the Sultan had sent the [tracaus]
to [Burcoun] Asia, where he intended to
withdraw himself if the Danger increased.
