The Number of the Sick diminishes in
Corsica, and that of the Bandits increases.
The Regiment of Buttafoco is named
Regiment of Bouillon, and it is formed
of two Battalions; the foreigners who had
entered this Corps have been recalled
from it.

Nothing at all interesting
is happening at Court; there are
alternately Opera, French Comedy,
and Comic Opera. These
Spectacles attract People. Ah
it is a single object that costs much
one must see all the Wagers all these
Histrions, Dancers, and Musicians,
and finally defray their expenses, and pay
for their voyage.
