14 21 July 1783

In Paris the 7th July 1783.

There is nothing new
from our Ports. There has been nothing [proft]
either of interest at Court
nor in the City. One is still
awaiting news of the definitive
Signing of Peace, and one hopes that then
Milord Gower, all Difficulties
will be lifted, and that the Peace Treaties
will be signed, after having
noted those of Commerce.

The Duke of Richmond is
a deputy some Tours.

Admiral Rodney is also expected.

The Rumor spreads that
Baron de Romanzof is going to leave for
for Turkey; However it is
to be Doubted, that professional Officers
pass into the Service of this Porte.

been sent to these natural Commanders,
