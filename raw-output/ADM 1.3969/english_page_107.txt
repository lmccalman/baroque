Paris the 2nd June 1783.

        It is reported from Toulon
    of last May 14th, that the Vessel
the Sultana, commanded by Mr.
de Bergerac, Ship Captain
    would arrive shortly from the Coasts of the
    Canaries in this Roadstead; after having
    done [Duc Haut Jeune] and Quarantine,
    awaiting the Port, to be
    there disarmed.

        The Corvette the Semillante,
    commanded by Mr. Boutierville,
    Ship Lieutenant, has [mis le Ville] of Toulon to proceed on
    Cruise, where she has a particular
    Mission to fulfill.

        [On ne parvenir] presently
    only [des Majeurs et des Savais], our
    Minister has chosen the [Contre] Benjamin
    of Russia, and in part to the Minister
    of the Emperor, at Petersburg, Also the
