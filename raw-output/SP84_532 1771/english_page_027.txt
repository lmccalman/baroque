Extracts from the Gazette of Berne.

Whitehall, August 1707.

Milord, upon his arrival here the Duke of Burgundy
made known to his Majesty that he had received notice
that a Danish vessel named the Christ loaded in part
with goods belonging to the Subjects of France had been taken
in Sweden towards the end of last January by
three English warships, which had
conducted it into the port of another [hamen] of Scotland
where it had been treated as a prize vessel by the law
of the admiralty of Scotland, the consequence of which is very prejudicial
to the Interests of the King of Denmark & His Subjects
Louis ordeit.
