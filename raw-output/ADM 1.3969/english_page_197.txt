It is also assured, that the King of Prussia
having discovered that there was a
Treaty of Alliance between the Emperor
and the Empress of Russia; it could not
be kept hidden any longer, and
it was communicated Ministerially
to the Minister of His Prussian Majesty
at St. Petersburg, at the same time that
the Ambassador of Russia at Berlin
was informing the King of Prussia
Himself. One may [contenter] on the subject
that it was the same as that drawn up
in 1781, that it contained nothing
contrary to the Interests of His Prussian
Majesty, nor to the Peace of Teschen &c.
