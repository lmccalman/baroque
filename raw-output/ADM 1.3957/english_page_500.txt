N° 29 Nov.re N° 3

Paris the 29th November 1770.

Our Letters from Toulon
of the 18th speak only of the Returns of the
Ships from Provence, the Sagittaire, and
the Atalante, they are doing the quarantine
of 18 Days, after which they will enter
into the Port to disarm there.

Verjus has Gone [en Pere] to Marseille where
he has made some purchases of Letters
of [la ce bonance].

There is absolutely no question in
any Letters from Provence at
the Present Moment.

The Parties of Peace or arrangement
take to the Peace the counter measure the
Spain acquiesces to the Propositions
of the English, which have been sent to London
by a Courier, and one hopes for a Reply.
