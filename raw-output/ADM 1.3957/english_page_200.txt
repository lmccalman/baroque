                        Brest the 16 May 1770.

                                        They are going to arm
Two Flutes for Toulon, and six Months of October
an Armament will be made for the Islands of the
Indies.
        There have arrived here 30 chosen Workers from
the Port of L'Orient, 20 have been retained at L'Orient,
Remainder of 7 Thousand four Hundred fifty from the Indies
have brought to L'Orient 50 L Depart with
Two of the current Brigs For India, one
England to Seek Woods.
        There are said Eleven Months also Officers of this
Department, and the Months follow Workers of the
Port.
        The Chevalier de Bonnescue, who was the
in Prison at the Chateau of Brest, made a
Turn, He burned himself.

                        Paris the 21 May 1770.

                        There are Letters from Toulon
of the 10th last, that the Frigates L'Atalante
carry [Bentivl] for the Levant, and the two
xebecs for Corsica, after they become them,
protect the [Toros] of Bonnescue.
