And Mr Tilly, who has the Command
of the Squadron, is also there;
it is composed of 56 Vessels,
without including the Regates
which departed from Lisbon under the
Command of Mr Gastron;
and which are affirmed to be for
Cape St. Vincent.
     Nothing is yet known of the
preliminary Affairs of this
Court with that of Portugal
which still has its
Ambassador here, and goes almost
every Day to the Court.
     The [Conflit] of Our Court
is already in Lisbon,
from where nothing
particular is written.
