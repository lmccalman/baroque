First

Aranjuez the 21st April 1776.

The News from the
Journals, that the Fleet must depart
from Cadiz today; If the weather
permits it, Are the Ships,
which compose it, are in the Bay.
The President must deliver to them
their Register on the 28th.

There are three Ships of the Line,
only frigates, which must
convoy the Fleet; they have
Provisions for Five Months; it is assured,
that they will only go as far as the
Canary Islands, and from there they will
return on the Side of Lisbon,
to join there another Fleet, which [se]
in five days at Cadiz, Two in
[Bonne], and one at Cartagena
