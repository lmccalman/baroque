Sir

I have desired mr Dayrolles to make an
apology for me to my Lord Duke for my not waiting
his Grace at the Hague, but the landing of the
voyage to Helvœr and the expectation of the orders of
the former to regulate his Majesties Journey—
from thence to Helvœr puts it not in my power to stir
from hence.—

Inclosed you have this day's intelligence
of his grace, as to the news of this country I give you
