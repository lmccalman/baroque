The Count of Narbonne, Marshal of his
Armies, is named General Commander
of the Land, & of the Place of the King of Martreuff,
who has requested his Recall, because of
his advanced age.

The Count of Chabanne has obtained the
Survivorship of the Office of First
Equerry of Madame Adelaide, which
the Marquis de l'Hopital possesses.

The King has acquired from M. the Duke of
several Towns of Alsace, opposite
placed at Paummers in magnificent
Barracks that are going to be constructed there; this Corps
is going to be placed on the Footing of the other
Regiments of Cavalry.

The Parliament has resolved to make very
humble Remonstrances, to
beseech the King to withdraw his Declaration
& to Restore by an Edict the Pensions
[Raines], which suffer also the astonishment of those
of the King's Relatives, & as much also these
[Rersentes] of the Court of Rome considering
that this leads to no Propriety;
and that Spain has no Right
to demand from his Majesty a Retraction
of his Word. The Ministry Believes itself quite
