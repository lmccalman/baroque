The Comptroller General has just
given an Adjoint to Mr de la Garde,
first General, it is Mr de la Loge
Director at Toulouse.

The Duke of Chevreuse, following the
Intentions, was buried the day before yesterday
in the great Ceremony of Uzege,
he took the Duchess [Loguien], his Brother,
Universal Residuary Legatee, he left much
in Legacies, and among others 50 Thousand Livres
in Pension to his People, of which he charged
the Sum acquired the Distribution.

The King has not yet disposed of the
Government of Paris, the Position of
Colonel General of Dragoons which the
late Duke of Chevreuse had, is given
to the Duke of Logne, and that of Master
of Camp General, which the Duke of
Logne had, is given to the Duke of la Loge and
Son of the Duke of Chevreuse.
