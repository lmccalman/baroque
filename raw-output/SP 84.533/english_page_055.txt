Another Hyppomene that one of Blois, and the
reverses of [nouvenenu].
    The [Buillages] of the [Hprunages] of the Duke
of Orleans, have recognized the new
Parliament.
    M. d'Heymes, former Counselor President
of the Parliament, has obtained Permission
to come to Paris for the Confinement of his
wife; he has bought at Vic a very
beautiful House on the Boulevards, near
the House of Théré.
    The Minister of the Marine wanting
to preserve the Sea Officers of the Young
Company of the Indies, has just had
distributed to them 20 Commissions of Louis,
there will be established for them a Marine
particular at L'Orient, and have them
navigate, under the name of the Marines
Pelée, to distinguish it from that
Royal, and [provenir] the [Kivalités]
and, them.
    M. de Vieuxville, Field Marshal,
accompanied by 15 Officers of
his Legion, has left for Poland
to join there the Confederates.
