The Ministry of Finance has
worked the last Sundays
with the King for three Hours.
This Ministry returned the next day
to Paris, suffering more than ever
from Gout.

The Rumor of War, which
appeared founded, and the Discredit
in Commerce, continue to
lower the Stocks and other
Royal effects. The Stocks of
the Company of the Indies were
yesterday at 1660 Livres.

It is certain that the Promotion
of General Officers is made;
but that it will only be declared
at Fontainebleau.

It is assured that if
troops are made to march to Spain
that the Marquis Ossun d'Higuerson
will command them, and is employed
in Suyenne for this Winter.
