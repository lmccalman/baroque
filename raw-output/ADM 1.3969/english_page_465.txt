Paris, November 29, 1784

The famous Response of the Emperor
has not yet arrived; but the evening before
last came a Courier from Vienna
dispatched by the Marquis de Noailles,
after the reading of the Dispatches that he
brought, the King, who had been in
very good humor at the Comedy
appeared worried and Pensive at his Bedtime;
and during all of yesterday's Hunt
He spoke to no one. The Orders
given to the War Office, were
the consequences of these Dispatches: It is said
openly, that Letters are being prepared there
to recall immediately to their
Regiments the Officers on Semester leave,
and that the Colonels will have Orders to
join in the Month of January.
