Paris July 5, 1785.


On the 29th of last May there was signed an
Alliance, or rather a League, to maintain
the Liberty, the Constitution, and the Indivisibility
of the Germanic Body, between the King of Prussia,
the King of Sweden, the Electors of Saxony, of
Hanover, of Trier, the Houses of Hesse,
of Brunswick, of Ansbach, &c. Holland
will be solicited to enter into this League,
as well as France.
