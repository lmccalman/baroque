Volunteers of Catinat, remained at Oran.
There was to arrive the embarkation
of another Battalion [en fer], the Dauphin
the Princess.

Up to the 5th of the Current month, there
were 29 Transport Vessels
departed on the King's Account, Namely:
14 Brigantines with Cannon, Provisions,
to load them, There are Cavalry &c.
that the Total of embarkation Troops
will amount to nearly 12 Thousand Men,
of Infantry, Dragoons, and Artillerymen.

They were sending there with some
Vessels and Brigantines from Carreaux,
with Troops, and that the Lieutenant
General, Marquis de la Ville,
had left for Brullon the
commanding General for
Vaijean the [Refort], of the 20th Current,
and that the King must arrive there.

From The Hague, the 2nd June Commanding
It appears and the Queen. One
is preparing a Convoy, for the
Coasts of Portugal.
