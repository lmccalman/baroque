Abed Pacha, and the force having killed more than 500 men from them, from Monfguir, and having him abandon his Camp and the Place; and he found Eleven iron Cannons, which they threw into the Rivers; with 14 of Cast iron, and a Howitzer of 8 Cannons which he brought with him, having set Fire to the Magazines and the towns.

The Expedition on Orsowa, was to be in charge of Lieutenant-Colonel Serbowitz, at the same time; at the News of the [Reddonde], [Clement] all the Town and the Magazines himself, [pendant] the Castle, which was in very good Condition. Mr Serbowitz threw [Co] Cannons which he found there, the greater Part into the Water, since they were of iron, a great [Nonbrope] [Abbe], and brought back with him, only some of Bronze.

General Effon in His turn, after having defeated near [l'Argiels], and having [Furer] in general Mongloch Agla, in which commanded Mehemed Pacha of three tails, with two other Pachas.
