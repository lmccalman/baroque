R 20 bru No 1.

Geneva the 6th March 1778.

One permits me to
say, that the Council of extraordinary States
are as extravagant, as the court of the Dowager
Queen of Sweden until 1772, with regard to
what is decided on this subject.

By letters from Paris of the 2nd one learns,
that the Ambassador of Vienna has requested
from the Court the men, that the 24 Thousand men
that His Most Christian Majesty must
furnish to Her Majesty the Empress Queen,
have ready as first troops of
this Sovereign.

One expects, that the States of the Court of
Vienna make more and several Studies
of different Politics and of their Commissions
following the Councils of Mr. the Cr. de Mercy
who always wanting the Reg. to succeed.

The infinite power in the Subjects,
the Empress Queen makes of it various
sciences, to balance them, in
which one [sout], and always a
Power of Peril.
