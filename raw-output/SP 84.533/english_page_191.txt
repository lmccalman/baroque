Brux[elles] the 30th September 1771.

We always speak
of Novelties to come, but we
await them.

There is still nothing well decided
about Rochefort, several Marine [Endes]
of this Department are already here. Mr.
de Beauchene who will communicate them
to Rochefort, will command them here.

We are arming seven Vessels, which
will depart for their Destination
at the end of October.

The Brigade of Orjouska that we had
here in L'Orient will take Mr. de
Lorme to transport him to the Isle
de France.

Mr. Decour who commands the Zenith
[parfois] the Rendezvous at the isle of
[moussant] Army of Montbazon, at
Martinique.

We will work on frigates Incessantly
for the Town, which will try to affirm themselves
of Port.
