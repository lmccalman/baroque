The latest News from Bern
say, that the [Environ] to receive
a Commander & Two Captains
[Portée] from America, M. de Watté,
[ne font que de paix plus du lieu des],
as one supposes, Hostilities
& execution of the Portuguese, which
always shows, that the Danger
always goes from that Side, while
one keeps the defensive in peace
[de fus] our frontiers, & one does
not discontinue having an eye on
the troops.

I am assured, that the Marquis
de la Torre Governor of
Havana, has passed away, & one has
named [à pardon à l'écoute]
of [Havieu].

The King of Spain, it is said, has
[l'effreur de l'entrée] orders,
The Duke of Monteliano, & the
Captain of the Halberdiers, to
Count de Crill, & his [Maire] of
Infantdón Louis.
