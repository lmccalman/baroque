It is added, that the great Interest which
the Bank of England takes in the progress
of Commerce; and in the security of
Merchants' Funds, has caused it to
unite the Tribunal of the Admiralty
and the Consulate, under the Presidency
of Dom Nicolas Niventis: the President
will always have as Assistants Two
enlightened Merchants, to examine
thoroughly with Him, all that may
concern the Commerce of Naples,
