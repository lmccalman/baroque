The King who almost lacked Artillery, ordered
that he be furnished from all the Churches with
Martin funds of the House St Egnidee and from
the others of their Domains and By the
other Ecclesiastics eventually with
Cannons for the Army. It must not make time too long.
They will take in their Opposition
The King has declared the raising of the Pages
of [Linaments], by which we were
inscribed the Protestation of the Princes,
and adds a good.

The King is instructed, stable, and does not yield
not. At present that he not become too
firm, which could occasion some
many Evils. The Parliament having been heard
in the Spirits, but they
Will register the People as Royalist.

Several Councillors of the Grand
Council who had been installed in the
Parliament, carrying for their
Families and by their Friends, have
taken the Party of retreat. The King
signed by this Model, has exiled them to
different Places of the Kingdom.

Our new Parliament of Troyes
is soon at the Palace, but the
Lawyers do not present themselves there to
plead.
