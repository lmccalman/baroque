The said decision in the last
Council, to bring a great Part
of the Land Army to war
complement, this Operation will cost
Eight Millions.

The King has disposed of vacant
Regiments through the Promotion of
new Field Marshals;
the Prince of Berglie, has obtained Dautemont;
the Marquis of Rouge, Chenois, and
Mr Duttemann, Saintange, the Viscount
of Rochambeau having left the
latter to take Royal Auvergne,
The Lieutenant Colonels, named at
the same time are Messieurs de Grasse,
de Chabannes, and de Fauban.

The King's Orders depart
this August for Corsica, one must
there raise his Regiment of Cavalry;
his Voyage will only be Twelve Days.
