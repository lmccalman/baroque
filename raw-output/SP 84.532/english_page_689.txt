DECLARATION
OF THE KING,

CONCERNING the recall of Priests decreed against or banished.

Given at Marly the 15th of June 1771.

Registered in Parliament the nineteenth of June 1771.

LOUIS, by the grace of God, King of
France & of Navarre: To all those who
these present Letters shall see; GREETING.
The Clergy of our Kingdom having
several times beseeched Us to cast a favorable regard
upon the situation of several Ecclesiastics who have
been pursued on the occasion of the divisions which have
agitated the Church & the State, We have deter-
mined Ourselves all the more willingly, as We have
reason to hope that by annulling all that has been done
