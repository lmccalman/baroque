247                                B 20 Liv N°2


Paris the 20th December 1778.

                It is certain, that
three new vessels are being armed
✓    at Brest, as has been said.
It is at L'Orient that the
✓    Bien-aimé of 74 Cannons is being armed.
Mr. de Bougainville has left
to go take
Command of it.
It is affirmed, that
✓    three Vessels are also being armed at Rochefort.
All is calm and quiet
at Toulon. One of the frigates
that was in the Levant, has
✓    arrived, and makes [l'éventraine].
Here these [bruyapets] are circulated,
that the Spanish fleet must [devenir]
Tangier, and that it had taken
this Place, in order to take away from the King of
Morocco

✓    747
