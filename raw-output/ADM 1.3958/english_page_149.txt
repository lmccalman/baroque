                                                                14. 6. cap.873

                                    Paris the 29th March 1773.

                                                        Nothing new
from our Parts, nor from our Indies or England.
Our Parlement has not been restored, as
was said, to the Châtelet. It had been [amis]
there was strongly a Month of proceedings between
us I have not learned that there has been
any Resolution.

In the Châtelet had appeared
& [Malade] Surgeon a Letter de Cachet,
which ordered them both to Retire,
to make a new Parlement which they
have always done for the [Canien]. Which
has been executed.

The [portionnaire hierarque] Parlement
& [Mesage], one calls half Class, there
are found in it neither Lawyers nor Attorneys,
to Plead, one [fait Semetre] the
[Vielle] of the Parties for [Enforme de l'Amerio le],
and some fears, Six Weeks the Palaces
during Closed and then Mondays & [Mariez].

The Châtelet increases only by ten Cases
a Protestant, who does not recognize
not the Peers of Council for the
Parlement, and could one recognize
no other than the dispensed Parlement.
