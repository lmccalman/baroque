N° 34.

                                Paris the 21st February 1775.

                                        Sirs Letters from Toulon,
from Brest & announcement by That which you
Order to cease the Preparations for War
when we Course that were all in
progress here; but finishing all the
Vessels, and leaving the smallest
Things in a state to be quickly put forth
the Magazines well supplied with all
the Warlike
     A Corvette has been dispatched from Brest
to go announce to our Colonies
the Convention Signed in London
the 6th January:
     [Propleteres] of Correspondence of 27th January
bearing that while all is tranquil there,
there is a quantity of notices received by
Vessels, all our Commanders of
Pilots giving [Buls] and
Casks in this dark grey and, this, announces
[paturages] in all the
new quite remarkable of these places there
filled that from the day before yesterday, it is a
maneuver Negotiation which has [sondécrét]
at [avigner], it is the finest Trifle
that has been made the Duke de Choiseul.
