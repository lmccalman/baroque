Rotterdam 10d December 1780

My Lord

I have to acknowledge the following
your Lord's Ships Letter of the 6th Instant.
No Pains will be Spared by me to obey
His Majesty's Commands contained in
the same, relative to the Information to be
given occasionally to the Lords of the
Treasury, of the Illegal trade that may be
carried on from hence to the Ovillines &
of North America.

It will be difficult, if not impossible,
to ascertain precisely the Nature of such
Cargo, as this can only become by an
Inspection of the Custom House Books here,
to one at which I have hitherto made
frequent and unsuccessful Attempts.

I hope to be able in a few Days to give
some Light into the Nature of the trade
carried on between Rotterdam and
