Head Quarters at Philadelphia 3d Day 1777

Major Chester fixed since my last of the 1st. Nothing new has moved the Enemy are lodged encamped, but their Camp is faced towards Middleton & Darwon, with the causeway between their two lines.

Monsieur de Noailles arrived ten Days ago at Versailles, as do the French King, the 1st is every day reported that he is setting out & that they have several times over fixed the Day for his arrival, at Brest while, yet, it is in my believes he will hardly come to the Army this year.

Last Wednesday Stoas al tier lowered our advanced Posts & saw four Troops while Corps under Arms, & has keep saying, that the two Battalions of Hessians are the two Guards Battalions I ever saw. We have sent six hundred regular foot from the whole Army, to garrison Lier, that Troops may have more use of the irregular foot, the night before. Every
