St James's 10th May 1765

Weston                                Sir;

                                      Your Letter of the 8th
                                      Inst: being arrived, I have
                                      communicated to His Majesty
                                      Your request of being absent for
                                      a Month some time in this
                                      Summer from Rotterdam for the
                                      Sake of Your Health; & have
                                      now His Majesty's gracious
                                      Permission for so doing, the
                                      King not doubting of Your
                                      Care to continue the usual
                                      Intelligences during such
                                      Absence.

                                      I heartily wish You all
                                      the Benefit that You can
                                      desire from this Excursion, &
                                      am &c &c:

                                      E: Weston
