92.

Mr Mélée Garrag has taken this Course to
have ready Money and to [lettes] made
to urgent persons, such as the Regency
of Finances, and the [Leverages] of the Routes of
[Recrutcertain gardag] has a [Camelle]
of increase among the Other Letters.
[A Dieu ne chaisse Macelo de reforme].
[Sa Macelo, Sa table eea].

The Chancellor has not yet entered
the Council, His Friend Mr Garrag [demeure]
that for him, and one thinks that he will
enter there soon.


Brussels the 5th March 1770.

The States of Brabant
and the [Jours Etat] assembled [Venetelli]
have granted the Request of which I
spoke in my last, but, for
once only.
