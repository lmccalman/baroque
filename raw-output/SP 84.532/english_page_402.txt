IMPORTANT NOTICE
FROM A
GENTLEMAN
TO ALL
THE NOBILITY
OF THE KINGDOM.

1째. IT is evident that all the ancient
& true Nobility is interested in not
allowing its principal Members to suc-
cumb under frivolous accusations, & in de-
manding justice from the Chief of Nobility &
of Justice, whose House has been on the
Throne for more than eight hundred years.

2째. That in the affair of a Peer, the
Parliament of Paris could not (especially without
the intervention of any Peer) act against

in his Council, upon the very documents of the
trial, exempt from all suspicion, & having
faithfully served.

3째. That it is as absurd as it is unjust,
to call Letters of Abolition the Letters
Patent of the King, which establish the justifi-
cation, the innocence & the services of a
