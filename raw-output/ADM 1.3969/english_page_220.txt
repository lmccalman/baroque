                    Paris the 12th September 1782.

                    The News is most barren
at this moment.
    We are waiting at present to see
the Peace Treaties, which will be printed
in a Fortnight.
    The Preliminaries of Holland will appear
before, it is claimed that the Dutch
in general, are very discontented with
the Peace, because of the Cession of
Negapatnam, but we know well
that England would have rather wanted
to war always, than to back down in
this regard.

    It has been learned at Versailles, that
the English Admiralty, has just given
the Name of the City of Paris, to one of the
Vessels of 100 Cannons, and that of
Rodney, to a Vessel of 74 Cannons.
    Pride is always in the
same State.
                                                    Madrid
