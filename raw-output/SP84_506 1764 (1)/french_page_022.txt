Rotterdam the 5th July, 1764

My Lord

The Madrid Correspondent
having drawn upon Mr James Crawford
for the Amount of the Quarter due to
him the 5th of June last, Your Lordship
will see by the inclosed Note that the
half of the whole Sum amounts to
£88.19.9 Sterling, in consequence I
have by the next Post dated the Liberty
to draw upon William Davies Esqr at
the Treasury to the order of Mr James
Crawford for the above said Sum
of £88.19.9.Str. at 21 Days Date.
I shall be particularly happy to know
that the Intelligence I give your Lordship
Some


Rt. Hon. Earl of Sandwich
