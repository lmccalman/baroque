Toulon the 4. May 1782.

The frigate la Bendeuse,
commanded by Mr. de Laperouse,
Captain of the Vessel, entered the day before
yesterday in the Evening into this Roadstead, coming
from the Coasts of the Barbaries, where she was
on Cruise for some Time to
protect the Vessels of Commerce.

La Previeuse which has been disarmed
since her arrival here from the last N.re will
be rearmed, under the orders of Mr.
de Caperne, Captain of the Vessel.

The frigate la Sultan and the
Corvette la Blonde, commanded by
Messrs. de Sengleton, Lieutenant of the Vessel
and Mr. de Marguerie Ensign of the Vessel,
[me seront remis] these last Days in
this Roadstead, the former coming from the
Coasts of Barbaries, where she was in
Station, to protect Commerce
the Second being returned from
Malta, where she has just fulfilled a
Mission, with which she was charged.

                                        Cedix
