18 30 nov 5

Paris the 22nd November 1784.

Everything is in the same state
concerning the Russian Kings. It is claimed
to know from [bafielle], that the Austrian
troops must set themselves
in March. Until this one the 21st,
or the 22nd, when the
Emperor's Response will have been received.

The [larifpuiis] of Lines not arriving
at all. Those among the Ministers who are
[jusqu'a-priinis], have not
found the great storm, nor
Gales in the Passages
of Cape Horn; which gives reason to
believe, that the expected
[Equipaux] have not been delayed by the
constraint of the Winds, even less
that they have perished; it must be that
