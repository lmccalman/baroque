     It has been enjoined to the Attorneys
to bring within a Week, under Penalty
of exemplary Punishment, all the
Papers of the Affairs that they have in
their Hands; to draw from them [contellat] that
it is in order to send back to the Superior
Councils the Cases of their Jurisdictions
and to execute the Declaration
of the King registered in Parliament,
the 2nd of this Month, which restricts the
Right of committimus, whose
abusive Extension excited
continual complaints.
