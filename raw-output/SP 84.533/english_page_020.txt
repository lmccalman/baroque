Madrid the 27th June 1773.

The latest Letters
from Cadix state, that the 4 Warships
aforementioned in my last,
and a frigate, from the Department of
Cartagena, Under the Orders of the Squadron
Commander, the Marquis de Crofts Beley,
have set Sail from this Bay, having on
Board two Battalions of Walloon Guards
& Spanish, to transport them to
Barcelona, and the Rois of Cartagena
of the 18th Current, say, that this fleet
had already passed the said Port, where they
were awaited on return, to disarm there,
