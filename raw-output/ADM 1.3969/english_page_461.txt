14 a Droit

Paris, November 26, 1744.

For Ten Days there have arrived
here three Couriers from Vienna; the one
who arrived yesterday Evening, is
not only for some others;
one sees therefore at the Court, more than
ever: However what concerns [us]
are the Emperor's Responses to the
King's letter, which will decide this
great Question. One flatters
oneself always that it will be such as His
Majesty desires it; for how
can one believe that he dares undertake a
War at the Extremities of his States,
while his Frontiers are
threatened by the Swiss, indignant
at his Pretensions, and the Evils that she
dictates to them; while Hungary
is ready to revolt; that the
