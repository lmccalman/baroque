Toulon the 31st March 1776.

                The Nomination
of the head of the Division of this Port
has finally arrived, it is Mr
Dabon.
    The Minister has ordered, that
the Honors of Prince of the Blood
would be rendered to the Duke and Duchess
of Chartres.
    The Division finished yesterday
careening, thus it will be ready for
in the Roadstead the 33rd of this Month.
    The Duke of Chartres will lodge at
the Marine Commander's residence.
    The Battery of the Cross is
continuing, and could be finished
marvelously.

                                        Brest
