   Mr Barbaud, Chancellor
for the Seals of the Count
of Artois, and Superintendent
of the House of this Prince,
being too overburdened with
work, has just sold his
Charge of Superintendent
to Mr Radix de St Fois, treasurer
of the Navy.
   Mr Bourret, General Farmer,
and farmer of the Posts, has dealt
with his Position of the Posts with Mr
Lendon d'Honnecourt.
Mr Bourret retires; his
successor gives him great
Advantages, and they have given
as Deputy to this successor,
Mr Prevost de Chantemeile.
