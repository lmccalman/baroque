a whole company, and they believe that they

Saturday, at 10 o'clock in the Evening, they have
the King and Queen leave Versailles,
to retire to Marly, where all the
Court went yesterday, Madame,
and Madame the Countess of Artois
will go this Morning.

On the Eve of the Illness of the Count
of Artois, this Prince was preparing to
leave with little Retinue, to go
visit the Cities, from Dunkirk
to L'Orient, to Brest, he will make
this Voyage when he will be cured.
