                                                B 7 Lyno 1

                     Second

                     Madrid the 13 August 1776.

        Mr. de Zavala departed
from here for Cadiz, the Night of the 5th to 10th
of the Current month, and Mr. des Rivieres
departed the 12th. It is the Lieutenant-
General, Mr. de Gayos, who exercises
the position of Governor of this
Capital, ad interim.
        His orders continue here,
to bear upon the Kingdom, with
Rigor, and all the Officers,
Without distinction of Rank,
report to their orders on time.
Despite the considerable losses
[de l'Equibre à Carthagene à sus dans Avee],
having weakened the Honor that one
has here of the Name of [Suinties], the King
wants that they be made throughout all the
Kingdom, up to the Number of
16 Thousand Men, from what is said.
