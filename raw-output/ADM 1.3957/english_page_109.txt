                                          Bastia the 2nd March 1770.

                  Account of Marshal de Canteny they continue
to work on the fortifications of this
city and on the roads from this City to Bastien,
but it will not be finished for three Months.
                  The workers of the troops of Dauréliée
repair everywhere, he has received from
[dépositaires] very frequent.
                  The Corsican [Zepin] is almost complete,
the other Corsican Regiment has not too
much advanced.

                                          Paris the 28th March 1770.

                  They sent the day before
yesterday from Paris the Equerry for the Departure
of the three Highnesses who are ready in the east,
the Pantheon has arrived for the Account of
the King; and the Provost for Monsieur the Dauphin
and Mesdames the Daughters of la Rochelle.
                  The President Prince having been partly
Sunday at Versailles, the King to him extremely
the disadvantage who feared the Bombardment
during four of the [tettes] of the [fites] of Order
But two who [Morisiont] been as until
the foundations Wednesday 14.
