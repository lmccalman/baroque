17 7 January

Paris, October 27, 1782

     The Administrators and
the Shareholders of the Treasury of Tuscany
assembled several times to
consider the Means of reestablishing Order
and Confidence; which this Treasury has lost
in different Sessions, some of which
were prolonged well into
the Night, several interesting Memoirs
were read. Mr. Dufrenoy, Notary, among
others, was the Embarrassment of one of these
Clients, who having made an acquisition
for the Sum of One Hundred Thousand Livres,
of which he paid 60 Thousand in Money,
and the Rest in Notes from the Treasury.
These 40 Thousand Livres were refused, and
one must fear that at the Reopening, the
Building may still be full
of similar affairs. Mr. Beauchamp
