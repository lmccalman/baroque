62.

Twenty-one Aldermen have been established in each
Parliament, who provide for all that needs
and circumstances require. They assemble and likewise
take on all the Charges of the Kingdom.

The Comptroller has three Months to
work on these Objects of Beneficence. It is
known that they procure more than One Hundred Million
per Year. The great Seal of alienated Domains
is delivered to be put up for
Auction.

It appears certain that the Credit of the Duke of
Choiseul is declining considerably. One
sends to Dunkirk, if I am not mistaken to
observe the Landing of the Court to
observe everything along the Way.

The Affair of the Company of the Indies
still continues pending in fact
that one knows, it is so Vague for
all parties.

It is doubted that England will go to this
Lottery, if there is still some Confidence
to Come, it will be better not to buy Effects
that the productions will have reduced very low.
