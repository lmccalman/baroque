Middlebottom
B day 27

The following named Ships appear on
our out in the Road, and nearly ready
for service
                from
Ste Esther    -    Commodore Goldfinch
Elephant  74  -    Capt. Thickin
Mars   68     -    Capt. Trider
Defension 64  -    Capt. Hollowell
St Lewis acquia 64    Capt. Rosenbalm
Thomas    36     Capt. Hoven
Omberburgh 36  -  Capt. Lubbock

The Danger, Groeland & Tienen are moved
as Guard Ships.
The Hastings Bellinus, the Nyborg, Rensberg,
aggershuns & the bomberburgh Ship "the Krone"
together with the Bomkeholder "Wandsgid"
and "Haroldsholm" are also stationed for the
defence of this harbour.

By the latest accounts the Swedish
fleet was between the Isle of Gottland
and Oego - and its destination was
supposed to be make an attempt upon
Revel.
