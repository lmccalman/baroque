Preparations are being made in Spain
that announce a War against
Poland, France will do what it
can, to prevent this
Rupture.

The Peers were assembled
at the Palace yesterday evening and last
for a lit de Justice intended
to register the Affair of the
Marshal of Richelieu, the King
wants this Affair to be
concluded and judged. The Marshal
gets angry and Swears against a Letter
from the King, written formerly
to the Countess of Vence, Sister-in-law
of the opposing Party. It has been
resolved, that the Letter, and the
Protestations, will be joined
to the substance of the Trial. Several of
our Peers were not found
at this [l'ance], the Prince of Conti
[Jommement], a sixth one died
during two Hours the day before yesterday.
