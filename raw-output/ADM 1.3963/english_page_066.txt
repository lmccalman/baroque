    Parliament assembled
last Friday, heard
the Reading of the first
Sheet of the States of the Minister
of Finances. That bearing
Suppression of the College of
Blois, was registered with
some Modifications, and to
take the place of the Sol per Livre
on the Purchase of Livestock that
this [Crise] collected, one puts
Eight livres per Ox at the
Entries of Paris, beyond what
it already paid. The other
Edicts are put to the Examination
of Commissioners, of which the
Prince de Conti, despite his
State, is of the Number.
