The Duke of Crillon, Lieutenant-
general in our Service, has just
obtained from the King, for Voyage of
Donation, 4 Leagues of land in
Vivant, in the Island of Porto-Rico,
on Condition of cultivating this land,
& of making useful Plantations there,
this Duchy proper for [fsy] to go
in Person.

Here finally is the Marriage of Our
Infant Don Louis published at the
Chamber of Castile, without The
Conditions already mentioned,
after the illustrious Spouses
have agreed as a private Lord,
at Saluera of the Queen,
& while waiting for the Palace
that is being prepared for them there, [puissh] to be
ready, They will depart to a Castle to a
Beautiful Country House or Place
Duke called Arcos at Soixoyo, Village Two
Leagues from Toledo.
