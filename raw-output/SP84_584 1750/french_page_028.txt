Rome d: lunep 30./19. 1582.


My Lord,


It is a great satisfaction to me, testified by
the honor of your Lordships letter of the 27 instant
that their Excellencies the Lords Justices are
pleased to approve of my zeal in promoting
Intelligence of the Proceedings of the East India
Company, and in Watching the motions of Forbes
and others engaged in these schemes.

Your Lordship will find enclosed with this
letter, what I have this morning received from
my correspondent at Angiers down in the
neighbourhood of London.

Forbes is still in this City, and I am assured
