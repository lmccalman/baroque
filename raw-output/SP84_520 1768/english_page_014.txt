The First Resident of Ostris to the other Residents
had been summoned to go to Versailles; the Re-
porter defended the Refusal of the of the Peers;
advised by the R. Same gave the orders.
   For several days, there has been no Assembly,
because one did not know what was to be heard.
It appears however, that the Magistrates
will come to Sit in Parliament one day of the
next Week; the Lawyers and the
Prosecutors are frightened in advance of a vengeance
and exile from Parliament. Other well-
claim that everything will have time to be arranged.
   The Princes offer no [Baillement],
Parliament, but it is of arrangement a new
not a general approbation.
   The Same-[Marecha] the Queen the [Sautrait] to
this Princess is known in France;
the Fever is always considerable; one
claims that She can go on still for a long time.
   The Duke of Choiseul left on Wednesday
after Noon for His Castle of Chanteloup,
near Amboise, and will return from there
Tomorrow to Versailles.
   For some days now, it has been
much the most admirably possible
for the Goods of the earth, and if this
continues, there will be a year
abundant in all things.
                                                    Turnes.
