Be it for Economy. There were formerly
Nine Regulators of Stablerie, who made
but a number of ten children of honor, they were
given by the King 500 L. per year. Those who
changed in their Department did not call
[ni blans] that which concerned them, and came to render
to render Account to the Court. The Minister of
current wishes to oblige them to come there only once
during the entire year, and to assign them
a Chief. It is in their Correspondence,
from which they would hold everything in their hands;
This would diminish their Carriage expenses
and their [Soinces], and they would also be received
from the Court, and could not return to
Paris except by Leave. This Arrangement
would not suit several among them,
who would reject it as much more
expensive, and would request an
Increase, be it of Salary,
Aid of Gratuities, but one does
not think that Monsieur de Monteyrand
will acquiesce to their Representations on
this Point, being more occupied with reducing
than with increasing.
