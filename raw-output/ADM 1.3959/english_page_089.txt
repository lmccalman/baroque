Mille does it at St Denis with Madame
Louise, then hunts in
Flanders, Turin and arrives at Marshal
de Soubise's at St Ouen, the next day
Tuesday, between 3 and 4 hours after noon:
the Queen and he go to see Mondy at
Merilly, where they arrive there on Wednesday.
On Thursday everyone will go to la Muette
and the King and Queen will return for
the night of Kings, in richly
decorated tents. Four Carpenters
of the Bridge have been engaged, but for
dangerously. After the
Carpentry was down, the King will hunt
in the Plain of Ruelle, and will have
supper at Lucienne at Madame
du Barry's, who awaits them from long
Hand. Mgr la Muette went
the day before yesterday to Choisy, for until
Tomorrow after dinner. The Royal
Family must go there with her Majesty.
The three young Princes, and the Two
Princesses went yesterday to Meuray
to do [l'eutenque] at Madame
Countess de Marsan's, and they all
three go, and Mgr amuses them
very much.
