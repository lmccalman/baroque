The Return of the Court is fixed
for the 20th of November.

One is in the greatest
anxieties about the Parliament which one awaits
On the time of the Council, for the [Rapportation]
of Wheat, the Parliament of Burgundy [manquent]
since nearly Sentence, and the officers threatened
yesterday of being exiled to Bourges-[ment] it appears
that the Court would no longer hold itself to that depends;
which put the Parliament in Confusion.

I know nothing, until tomorrow of the News
of the 24th and of the 25th from Paris.

The [Loups] of Bern and the [Boutton]
speak only of the Proceedings that they
make in the Countryside, some for [Jé
crises], and the others to fortify themselves.

A Courier from Switzerland passed yesterday
before me the [Brugger] in the [Alleçes].

Our Letters from Bern teach us
nothing other; However within would appear to be
reported here.

It is certain, it is said that the Emperor
come here in the Month of May. He wants
to see one of our Posts.

Bern.
