First

St. Ildefonso the 28th Decr 1776.

I have just arrived
the News of the arrival of the Entry
of the Vessels which come from
Cartagena, with 2 Million
Piastres for the account of the King;
not yet knowing what they
bring for account of
Individuals, nor that one has
not yet submitted the List of it.
These Vessels brought
the News, of having found the
fleet at Porto Rico.
Mr. de Savalles, who is going to command
the Troops that one destines for
Buenos Aires, departing from here some d.,
is at Callao since some time;
