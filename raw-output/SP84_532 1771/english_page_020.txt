[Right column:]
I have received, Sir, your letter dated
the 15th of this month with the two copies
of the Collection of Laws and Constitutions which
you were so kind as to send to me. I
beg you to accept my thanks for this
obliging attention. I hasten to send
one of these copies to the
President of the Directory of the District
for whom it is destined and to inform him
of the manner in which you have fulfilled his
intentions. He charges me to express to you
his satisfaction and to assure you of his
gratitude.
