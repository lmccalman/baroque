The ordinance concerning the
Royal Corps of Artillery and Engineering,
issued before May 25, 1776,
it contains 155 Pages in folio, and
establishes this Corps by another Edict.
Another one of the same
Date appears, and of the same format, but
which has only 20 Pages in print;
it contains a Regulation for
the Service that the Workers of this
Royal Corps would have to perform
in the Arsenals.

The emperor not wanting to
leave the [Bastelenes] of
Vienna, [on Roi]. [Tel] there is titled
of towers, then the Grand [Caneliers].
Our Politicians are working
on this Journey, and see in it
the renewal of a great
Alliance with France.
