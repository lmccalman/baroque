There will soon appear a supplement to the
Promotion of Land Officers. As for
that of the Navy, it is not yet public.

The Count of Broglie, Brother of the
Marshal of that Name, has just obtained the
General Lieutenancy of M. St Paris, and
the Governorship of the City of Arras.

The Duke of Aiguillon is at Court in
the highest favor of the Monarch.

A Letter from Constantinople of the 19th
December 1771, that the Turks are making the greatest
Preparations to return to
Campaign; that the Grand Seigneur
will go to Adrianople, but that one is very
worried about the [Etendue-Reste].
