That would teach us all the novelty of these consequences.

They are transporting from here to Salinas to hold all the old Cannon Balls, as much from Venice as from Bronte, that formerly if they must leave all this week. It is not yet known whether it is to make Grenades from them to Show, or others to make Field Balls from them.

The Clerks of the Barracks hasten with all possible Diligence, to make Orders arrive from the depths of the Province to have everything ready according to the hours they have received from them.

Saturday the Dragoon of Alhaman received 600 Horses from Seminole, the Cuirassiers received 400.
