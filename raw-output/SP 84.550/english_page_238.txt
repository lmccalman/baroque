I am informed from Cadiz, on
the same date of 21st April last,
that they had destined the Ship
of the Line the Stborough there,
to conduct a good Number
of Officers and Marine Guards,

armed there, and are still being armed;
that they are believed to be for North
America for Buenos
Aires, where it is known that
troops and Munitions
of War will be called. If it would
not be [qu'prudemment], that
General Count O'Reilly
be destined there, to command
the army, in case it comes to
Hostilities. [Ah non au Bon], as
