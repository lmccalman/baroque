Second.

Madrid the 15th April 1776.

M. de Zevallos for the A.
who played in Galicia, the Navil from the 9th to the 10th of the
Current month, and M. de Marin Dussieux the 18th.
It is the Lieutenant General, M. de
Sayas, who exercises the position of
Governor of this Capitule
on an interim basis.

The [Luides] continue here,
for Bigamy with Rigor.
Among the Officers, without Distinction
of Rank, the news of their
Embarkation. Despite the [fardelles]
General Pardons for Vagabonds
and false Abuses, and in the Instant
the Honor that one has had from the King
of Quintus, the King wants that one
act throughout all the Kingdoms,
up to the Number of 46 Thousand Men,
as is said, and in Consequence the
orders have just been dispatched to the
Regiment Corps of Castile, And that
of War.
