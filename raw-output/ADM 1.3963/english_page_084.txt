R 24 May N°2

Paris the 27 May 1776.

Last Sunday
in the Morning M. de Malesherbes beseeched the
King to grant him more leisure which he
had been requesting for so long, the King
determined to do so, and named M. Amelot
as his Successor. The same Day this
new Secretary of State was installed
in the Hands of M. de Maurepas; just
as he takes over the Department
which he takes from M. de Malesherbes,
he also retains for his Title the
Charges of Intendant of Finances,
[un Tiers des Requêtes] will occupy him
until this Title has been [régis
requin]. M. de Malesherbes
left yesterday Morning to go spend
three Days at Rony at Madame de
Senozan his daughter's, he will return Tomorrow
to go Sunday to Versailles;
and it is said that he will continue to attend
the Councils, as simple Minister.
