Paris the 21st May 1776.

                                                The new

Comptroller General has arrived in
Paris. The true Director, or Minister
[naitre] Taboureau, who opened himself
departing to Chanteloup. The same
day at 9 o'clock, the [portail] could
find in favor of the Kings, to whom he had
himself presented, and to the Queen. In the evening the
[Sermonnaire] the Names of the Kings!
carried to His Highness; he arrived
in the Evening in Paris, where he worked
with Mr Vergé, who handed over to him the
Papers concerning the Ministry.
The next day, Tuesday, he was received
Syndic [Semment] at the Chamber
of Accounts.
