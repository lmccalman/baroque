I am not certain of the number of frigates, nor
of their destinations. Last week there was an
bivovac of 10 Men: the Empts hers 4500 and
Santa Merda of 90 Guns, the two former are as
complete frigates as can be in any Maritime
Power. These sailed with sealed destinations without
waiting for each other, and afterwards sailed 20
Cutters, when these are gone the Ships except frigates
& the other departments here are getting ready
are the Ships with the greatest reason. There has
bin already a levy of 6000 Seamen from this
Kingdom of Galicia & Province of Asturias and
4000 from Biscay, and this Port brings over
& make a farther in the Kingdom of Galicia
of 21,000. We are here ignorant of the design
and extent of so considerable an armament
