By 22 April

Paris, August 9, 1788.

A dispatch has just come from America
from the squadron of Korea which had just
arrived there; it reports that the Vengeur
of 64 cannons, commanded by Mr de
Cuerville, after having been battered by
a storm, between the Isle de France
and the Cape, was obliged to put in at
the Isle de Bourbon; the crew was
saved. It is added that Mr de Suffren has
appointed on an interim basis the Commanders
and commissioners in all our
establishments in the Indies.

week; the clerks were occupied there
