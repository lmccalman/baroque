225                    B 23 April N° 1


                        Brest the 8th April 1775.

                                                Our Excellence
is going in nine new of the
Orders has received reinforced the Orders;
he is only waiting to depart, for the Ship
intended from St. Domingue to take him there.
    Mr de Courcy has not yet received his
Own. His Destination is to go to
Guadeloupe. Mr de Roziere in the capacity
of Governor.
    This small Governorship had been given
to that one of Martinique, but Abbé
Terray renounced it to his Nephew, former
Abbé of the Regiment of Flanders.
    There were 2 Ships at L'Orient for
the King, and 2 Flutes in our Port;
Aujours de Montréal, and de Roguelen
command the two Ships;
the two Flutes will be by Messieurs
Lambert and Francatel who embarked
them from Rochefort. The 2 Vessels
are going to Isle de France, and will carry
there Recruits a Number of [Noix] Men.
