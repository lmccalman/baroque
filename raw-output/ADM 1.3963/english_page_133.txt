                                B22 Jan No.1


                    Brest the 2nd June 1778.

        The Renommées have
[en Romanont], they have from the 18th to the 20th,
to go rejoin the Squadron.
Mr. de Monthieu, after having left
the Squadron, indicates to us that one
does not know.
        These two other frigates are
[Arremontées], are the Two
Messrs. de Breuillier and de Périer,
will depart at the same time, the First
for St. Domingue, the Second for
the Windward Islands.
        The Frigate that Mr.
Dornes commands, will also depart at the same time,
one does not know its Destination, having
Packets from the Court to open on
the high seas.
        These Four Vessels all have
Seven Months of Provisions.
        There are 6 Ships, and 2 Frigates
[en Radoute].
                                        B22.
