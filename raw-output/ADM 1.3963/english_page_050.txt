                        Paris the 5th January 1776.

                              [It is reported from Toulon
         on the 28th, that Lafayette set Sail
         on the 24th, to go disembark at Brest.
         In Toulon, they speak only of an
         upcoming great Armament.]

         It is written from Brest on January 5th,
         that thirteen Officers of different
         [Condes], both from this Port, and from
         that of Rochefort, have requested
         and obtained their Retirements; the
         different [RÃ©gions] which are
         granted to them, amount to 40,000 livres.

         There will be an Armament in
         the three Departments, for the
         Duke of Chartres, all will provide
         for a Rendezvous.

         L'Indien will only be able to leave
         Brest, at the end of this Month.
