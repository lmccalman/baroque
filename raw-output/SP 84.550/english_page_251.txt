Havre de Grace the 25 May 1776.

One will confiscate a
fluyt of 22 Cannons there named
l'officieuse, which is quite advanced.
There are in addition Two Corvettes on
slips, of 4 Cannons each one.
When these three Vessels are
armed at Sea, they will be replaced
on the slips by Two fluyts,
la Barbue, and la Carpe.
Two fluyts are being built
at Rochefort.
Two Frigates must be built
at Nantes, if the Project is adopted, to
rebuild the Navy in all
Manners, considering that the new
Grand Master General [Egypte] a
Willingly, to accelerate the
necessary funds.
