Paris, October 24, 1782.

There is nothing new
from our Ports.

The Administrators and dictionaries
of the exceptional language held the day
before yesterday a great Assembly, which they
prorogued to the following day. As they
were not yet Separated yesterday at
About Eleven o'clock in the Evening one is unaware of the
Resolutions that were taken.

It appears decided that Mr. Amelet
will soon give his Resignation, and
that he will be replaced by the Baron de
Breteuil. Mr. Amelet obtains a very
fine Retirement, and the Promise of one of the
first Intendancies that will become vacant
for his Son.

Mr. Dangevilliers has been named
Administrator of Rambouillet.
