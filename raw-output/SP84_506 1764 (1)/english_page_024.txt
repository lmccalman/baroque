Rotterdam Augt 3d 1764


My Lord


I have the honour of your Lordship's letter of the 27 past with the three inclosed proposals from the Lord commissioners of his Majesty's Treasury relating to the contraband trade.

Your Lordship may be well assured that no zeal or activity will be wanting in me, as well in this, as in any other point of his Majesty's Service.
