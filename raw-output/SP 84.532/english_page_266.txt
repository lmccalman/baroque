III.

SAID Superior Council shall be composed of a First President, two Presidents, twenty Counselors, one our Advocate, one our Prosecutor, two Deputies, one civil Clerk, one criminal Clerk, twenty-four Attorneys and twelve Ushers.

III.

ATTRIBUTIONS to the First President......... 6000
to each of the Presidents................... 4000
to each of the Counselors................. 2000
to our Advocate........................... 3000
to our Prosecutor........................ 4000
to each of the Deputies.................. 1000

But [sages] by means of which no case may be rendered by our said Officers, any right under any denomination whatsoever, by title of sessions, spices or otherwise.

IV.

WE WILL that those whom we shall choose to fill the offices of Presidents, of Counselors, of our Advocates & Prosecutors & their Deputies to said Councils be, for this time, for their provisions & receptions to said Offices, exempt from all fees & rights, even from gold mark.

V.

THE Presidents & Counselors of our said superior Councils, our Advocates & Prosecutors to said Councils shall enjoy therein personal nobility, & it shall be transmitted to their posterity, in the case where the father & the son shall have filled successively one of said offices, each for twenty years, or shall have died in the exercise of said office before the twenty years expired.
