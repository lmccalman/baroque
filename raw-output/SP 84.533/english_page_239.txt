N.° 83.

                                        Toulon, October 2, 1775.

                                        The Frigates Pléyade,
Topaze, and Chimère, must be ready
to depart on the 20th of the current month; at the same time,
and they will carry to Cayenne Mr. Savoir, they will
[passeront], [ont], wait, to Paris to put there
      Mr. Julien Petro [longues quil] the [Rambourgs]
with the Commune and the effects of
      three Frigates, while Madame the M.sse des
Graffigny can send herself there for 2 months
more in the [Covient]; having returned here, and
to compare the injustices before the [tien] state;
they have been condemned to a quarantine
of 20 days only.
      Two xebecs which have been [reinis] [Alles],
left the Coast of Provence, are also here
all the guard of this Town in charge;
they are replaced by the Royal Regiment of
two battalions, and for those of the [hamme] of 2.
The Royal Corsican Regiment goes to Avignon
      It is the Regiment of Aquitaine which has
furnished Detachments for the three
Frigates which will depart, which have for
each one for 6 Months of Provisions.
      As for the [Garneaux] of the Arsenal and the
[Portellées] go equally, the Rope is lacking,
and Paris [Augent] all a world.

                                        PARIS
