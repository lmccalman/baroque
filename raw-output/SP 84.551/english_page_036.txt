N° 88.

                                     Paris the 5th July 1776.

                                     Reports from Brest
dated the 28th, that the Renommées, and the three
other Frigates which were in the Roadstead,
have set Sail, the First is going to
join M. du Chaffault, and the
three others will carry Munitions
of War to our different
Colonies.
     Provisions arriving from all
parts in this Port. They are recasting
the Palmier, and are working on the
Sphinx. There are orders for
the Bretagne, finally they are working
Night and Day in our three Ports.
     There is also news from Cadiz, that
the Duke of Chartres went from there to
see Gibraltar, that he was very
well received by the English.
     [La tombe] of St Germain continues
his operations, and reserves all the
objects, he has just made a
