17 22 byt - No 2

Brussels the 26th April 1790.

Campaign of the Equipments
of Duke Charles, to be carried in the Event that
the Court acquiesces to the Request to go
to Vienna.

The following is written from Brandenburg.
The Privy Counselor de Brüthenhoff has received
Order from the King of Prussia, to form
immediately Magazines in
Pomerania, in the Towns of Driesen
and Landsberg situated in the New
March of Brandenburg.

Besides these Magazines it is known that there are
already very Considerable ones which have been
newly formed.

They are in these same Regions
[Ordures de vendeur au Roi, de la Pologne]
and other neighboring Communities, such as
[Poine] of 10 Years of Galley Slaves.

General de Pelen — is, ordered to the
[Peleguns] with Regiments of Cuirassiers,
to go the two Dragoon Regiments
of Altens and that of Polkititz and to publish there
a Manifesto, of which the Content is still unknown.
