Wed 27 Feb 1778-

Paris, January 19, 1778.

No. letters from Toulon,
of the 10th report, that the frigate l'Higuette,
returned from the Levant to this Port,
should depart again on the 18th to go
disarmed to Brest. They continue
to change the Crews, and they will not be able
to begin building there, until in
Two Months.

The armament prepared at Brest,
and at l'Orient, for the French
Indies, is ready to depart.

Nothing transpires however
of the operations of M. de St. Germain,
