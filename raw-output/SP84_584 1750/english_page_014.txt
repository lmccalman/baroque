Rotterdam may: 22/13 1780

My Lord Duke

Two placaerts and a publication relating to the Herring fishery were issued last week by the States general.

The first absolutely forbids all Subjects of this Republick entering into any foreign Service as Mariners or fishermen &c & in taking dens, boats, particular tides or private merchants Service for the Service of the Baltick - & merchants or for the fishery without a particular licence of their High Mightinesses or of one of the Boards of Admiralty.
