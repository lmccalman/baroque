Brest the 22nd December 1779.

We find two
always two troops, has embarked
the 2nd Battalion of Normandy two
at the Union and the Seine; they set
Sail without day of [Large]. They
embark the 3rd Battalion of Artois
On 3 large Merchant Ships;
and we have the Cybet, the Gloria and
the Belle Poule.

They embark at Monsieur the 2nd
Battalion of the Royal Regiment
Vessels two three large Merchant
Ships also. The Destinations
Are Mysteries. We are
always in the Expectation of
Peace or of War; this last
appears preferable.
