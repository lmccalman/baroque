Brussels the 29th February 1724

The Chancellor of Brabant
made on Friday to the assembled States
the Petition in the Name of His Sovereign
for half a Million per Year for
Twenty Years; the States separated
to give their Response next
Thursday.

All the Convents of the Peers, except
those of the Four Mendicant Orders,
have Order to give in the
Course of the Month of March a Specific
Statement of all their Goods and
Revenues, Under Penalty of Confiscation;
