Vienna, December 19, 1772

In my first
of the 12th present I departed on the subject of the Place of
Poland where our Merchants had
distributed in advance, but what
they were doing without the consent of
foreign Merchants. In that
time, there arrived [livres]
through the said, [Kmdergfaleau],
30 Boats of Grain, which have been
stopped at the Customs, and one has
excepted the ordinary Riggs, which is
30 florins per [Reine].

In this week one has sent
a Circular Letter to all the
Ambassadors and Foreign Ministers,
asking them to know, to what extent
they had [resterances] on
