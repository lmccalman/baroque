N°22.

Brest, October 2, 1776.

The Squadron of Mr.
chiffeult, composed of 2 Vessels,
3 frigates, 4 Corvettes, and 2 Cutters,
[moueuenües] in the Roadstead on the 26th
September, from the next day they
proceeded with the Disembarkation
of the Staff. M.[onsieur] Duqhaple,
sent to the Court, departed today
for Paris.

The ordered Armament on which
they are working, will be in 8 or 9 Days
eight [tours d'allét] in the Roadstead;
but the Commanders of the
[bagues Batiuent] are not yet
named, nor the Forces
& Seamen arrived. These are
[l'Infernne fort], the Magnifique,
the Intrépide, the Eveillé, the Protée
the Roland, and the Bizarres. The
