                    Toulon the 28th April 1776.

                              The Squadron awaits
its Orders in the Roads to depart.
Mr de la Grange is made Second
Captain of the Provence, in the Place
of Mr de Troussemaunes, who
commands the Flegueuse.
    According to the Itinerary sent to
your Commander, the Duke and the
Duchess of Chartres will arrive
Tomorrow Evening.
    There will be before the Duchy [fa]
a Simulacre of Naval Combat,
between the Sultane and the Flegueuse.
The Arrival of this Prince attracts an
infinite World in this City.


                              Paris.
