13 a Jenp 1785

Versailles, December 26, 1784.

I was reassured here today;
I have been since Friday. I only write to you
to express the small
worry that you were in
not seeing anything arrive from me this
post. Things are still
in the same state.

The Count of Maillebois had the [foi]
today; he will leave in five
or ten days for Holland

Here is a list that I find on
a table, that [je forme] has not yet;
I am going to have it copied.
