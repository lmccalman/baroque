H 272 two

Paris, December 17, 1784.

No Courier has arrived
for three or four days; and the
Campaign Regulation has not yet
come out of the War Office;
it will appear with the Letters to the
Colonels, who are suspected to soon
receive the Order to rejoin
at the end of the Month of January.
     All that one learns from Vienna
and from the March of Imperial Troops,
makes one presume the Preparations, which
must also carry our Men
toward the Low Countries. It is certain
that a Courier has been sent to
Marshal de Broglio, who is still
in the Land at Broglio, however one ignores
to what extent one has
destined the Command of the
