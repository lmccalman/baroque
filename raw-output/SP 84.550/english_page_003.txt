Rotterdam this 2nd January 1776.

My Lord,

         I have the honour to send
you enclosed the Account of
my Disbursements, for the Quarter
of Correspondences made here
the 25th December last, amounting
to £276. 4. 4 [p'r l'annulet] Holland,
to which add the Charges of this day,
makes £276. 16. 4. Sterling, to which I join
the receipt [sommé] I will take the
liberty to draw, by the next
Post, on Mister Williams
Davis of the Treasury.
         I make my Wishes, My Lord, for
your Health and the augmentation in
the honour we had to enter,
that it makes you [propre] in all
regards, and recommend myself,
and the increase to your Protection.
         I have the honour to be with a
profound Respect
                    My Lord,
                         Of Your Excellency the very
Mle de Jufter.              humble & obedient Servant
                         Th. Walters
