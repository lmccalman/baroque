The Count of St Germain had
taken as Secretaries of Mr. Ca...no
Sieur Olivier, who had been so of
Messieurs de Monteynar, and de Muy
he has just dismissed him, with a
[Parfum] of 3600 livres only
He has sent to Toulouse, to fetch
the Secretary of the Marquis of
Cavamen, to put him in the
Place of Sieur Olivier.

Mr. Meslin, First Clerk
of the Royal Treasury, is made one of the
First Clerks of War,
in the Place of Mr. Busnieres,
who has retired.

Ordinance for the dress
and Equipment of the troops, Officers
to the officers to wear Plumes
on their Hats, and renew
the old prohibition, on wearing
Laces, [Fouraux], and in
the Garrisons.
