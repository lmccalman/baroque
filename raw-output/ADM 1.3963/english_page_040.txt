Reg 27 fol. N°2.

Paris the 29th January 1776.

The letters from Toulon
carry according to Reports 2nd for
a grand Monument.

Those from Brest of the 24th Explain
that the 23rd of the present Month received entry
into This Port.

The [adresphir], commanded by
Mr De Dampierre, has returned from the
Islands to this Port.

The Governorship and Marine of [Baize]
which belonged to the late duke of Polignac has
been given to the Count of [Besançois]
his Grandson.

Mr de Sartines has transmitted
what he had received from Mr de
[Plesme] from Mr de Maurepas
regarding different objects concerning
the Navy, and a Response to be made
in the Offices. Duke of
Chartres has been named Squadron
Chief of the Naval Armies.
