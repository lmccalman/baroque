                    Brussels the 25 February 1718.

                    The King has advised to break up
all the Swiss Regiments before commencing
for the Reception of the loan in Spring,
because this Journey will not take place; d'Avert,
it is said, also doubts that the King will send me.
                    One cannot measure the object of
this future War, but here are the Conjectures
of our Politicians.
                    The Emperor, in concert with the King of
France will divide a Part of
Belgium between Them, or else the King with
the Empire and the King of Hungary, with
his absolute Authority.
                    Others maintain, that the Project is, to
pass the Crown of Belgium to the
Duke of Saxe Lorraine.
                    There is no agreement on the Profit
that the Swiss will have from these Armaments.
                    Others assert that there is the grand
Project to subdue the bonds of all those
who possess them in Europe, and give
freedom to all the States; that for this
purpose the Helvetic and Prussian States
will have concerted with Them, and that the
Helvetic troops will join
with those of Russia by the Danube,
