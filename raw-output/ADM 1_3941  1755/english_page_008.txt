ordered for Friday an
Assembly of all the Bishops
who are in Paris, at
Cardinal de la Rochefoucault's
for the purpose of having their opinion on
the Response of the archbishop.
    The Bishops numbering
27 assembled around
four o'clock in the afternoon and
did not separate until half past
eight; the two
Cardinals, the archbishop of
Cambray, and the Bishop of Bayux
went the day before yesterday to
Jugny to communicate to
the archbishop the Result of
the assembly of the previous day, and to have
on that his opinion and his Consent,
whereupon the two
Cardinals went yesterday
to Versailles to bring the
Result of everything to the King, one
awaits news of it.
                        The abbot
