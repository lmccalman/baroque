The Marshal de Richelieu leaves well
only the first Week of Equipment,
[confoit] the advance minimant, that this
will only be two Mars, he sends under enfes
til to make his [Gournation] to the Parliament
before the Vacancy.

The King has just elevated to the Prince de Beauveaux
the Command of Languedoc, and H. H. H.
My Lord the Count of Clermont that of Angoumois;
it is an Effect of 40 Thousand livres of Income.
One is astonished that the King has not returned this
Command to the Duke de Fitz-James,
to whom 80 Thousand livres of Pensions are given
since it was taken from him, one still expects
that he be filled elsewhere, Belisle is or is
preparing to go take his Govt of Auvergne.

Let us say that the Corsairs who have carried ley
with the Pinnes, and that after, the [methores]
will come happily, the first Example
does not make them retract, one does not doubt
that his Majesty will finally take from the Prince
de Beauveaux the Company of Guards
of the Body.
