Brest the 18th June 1778.

        The Frigates the
named one, the Four, that of Ostignette,
the Alexandre, the Royal order; the
first ones will [fumer] to the Squadron of
Mr Du Chaffault, the [vue chaffron
enflèche], which is unknown; the three
others go to our various
colonies.
    There is an order to keep ready to be
armed, for the first of April,
the Vaillance, the Ville de Paris,
the Duc de Bourgogne, the Eveillé,
the Protée, the Dauphin Royal,
the Intrépide, the Robuste, the Bizarre,
the Magnifique, the Actif, the Roland,
the Alexandre, and the Invincible,
with 7 frigates.
    The vessels, the Ville de Paris,
the Alexandre, the Bien, the Dauphin
Royal, the Magnifique; and the Duc de
Bourgogne are already armed.
                                    On.
