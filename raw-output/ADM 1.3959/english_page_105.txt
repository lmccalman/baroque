                                    Madrid the 21st September 1772.

                                            [SEAL/STAMP]

                    That of the two frigates the St. Theresa
                and the Medea, of an [antenine] of 32 pounds.
                    A Spanish Uniform brought
                to Cadiz, for Account of the Company
                of Negroes, 60 Thousand Piastres. - They
      &        write to me, that despite this Dispatch, the
                Company is coming to an end, not being able
                to meet the Commitments.
                    They speak at [l'Emburg] of the arrival
                of the Grand Duke, and of the [Santé Duschesse]
                of Tuscany, very soon at
                Arlon however, that the King will go to their
                Meeting as far as Cartagena.
                    There is News, that the Emperor
                of Morocco has made the Consul
                of England leave His Kingdom.
