Paris the 26th March 1770.

A letter from Toulon
of the 19th of this, that there is no appearance that one will arm
only one Bark this Spring.

The 2nd Vessel of 64. that one puts at the Shipyard,
are the Caton and the Robuste, the latter is in
the Dock and one has Begun to see [annerer le veniz]
named.

The Vessels at March, the Pluton the Marseillois
and Victoire, and the Robuste in Refit, the 64 Mr Sartine sends
to Calais, they take 9 Certified Carpenters,
of which one is Painter and [Suédilien], and another Surgeon,
and Apothecary. These people are sent there on behalf of
Robuste, by M the Duke of Choiseul Minister.

The [Chev Romelie] of other Ports.

The Commander of [Cervisi] has assembled a Command
of 30 Millions to the King, such as has been requested.

This last of [nantes Pays], is increased by
many Troops, they have several, and however
of [Cippayutti], they will be found in a little while.

There are among others [d'egaliers], it is that truly
to see some, there are 29 in Toulon. To break, and
Navigate it is [miser intents] all then with our count.
to carry it to [la Merague] to be completed there by
one mister Fourcron, one intends the [Vengleanee]
of this [Be] to Control it, in place of Couret
de La Beraudiere de Ville [Marque] Intendants for the
the same [Vailieur]. One my way to [receudre]
money to those who have it, through increase
the Funds.

Malesherbes
