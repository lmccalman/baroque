18th March

Paris February 21, 1782.

It is assured that Twenty of our
best Vessels of the Squadron of
aid will go to disarm at Toulon
The King's intention is to equip
in this Port, a respectable army
to give appearance at the first
Appearance of Rupture.
Construction is being recommenced
at Brest.
Mr. d'Estaing comes to the Waters
of Caleix and he is expected here in
Two to Fifteen Days.
Mr. de Rochambeau was very
well received by the King. It is by the
Frigate the Gloire that the General
came; Mr. the Baron de la Valette
came and remains his Province
