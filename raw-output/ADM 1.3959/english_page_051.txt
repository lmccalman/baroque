184.

The Grand Seigneur comes after the Reis
carried the goods to Mte-Rey, and the
[reprimes].
    While awaiting troops from [fila] which is
in Bohemia, the Emperor increases
the Garrison of Koeniggratz, and has
this Place garrisoned.


    Brussels the 30th March 1772.

    Besides a ship already
departed from here, taking the Route of the
Dord, and from there it will ascend the Rhine
as far as Coblentz, with all sorts of
Munitions of War, the named Jean
van Es, Boatman, has undertaken further
to transport as far as Coblentz
3 Thousand Cannonballs, Cannon Balls,
a great quantity of Grenades,
and some small Pieces of Cannon,
and Gun Carriages &c.
