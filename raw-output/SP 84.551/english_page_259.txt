N° 74.

Paris the 9th September 1778.

It is reported from
Bordeaux on the 2nd that the Maréchal
de Mouchy, Commander in
Guyenne, [feroit brouter la Veille]
to the Parlement, would have held there in the
name of the King, a Lit de Justice.
He would have had registered
by Authority, the famous Edict
for the Liberty of Wine
Commerce; which causes much
Distress to the Inhabitants of
Bordeaux, who can no
longer sell their fixed Wines
to Foreigners.
The Parlement of Paris assembled
last Friday, pronounced
an Arrest by the Conclusions
of the Procureur General, which
