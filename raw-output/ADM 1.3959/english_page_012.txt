Benevento,- and the Jesuits will not

The Nuncio in France has been waiting every
Day for six Months for the Hat;
he has disposed of his Furniture, his Residence
is leased, he is obliged to leave it,
this distresses him,- one understands nothing
of the Pope's Conduct.

Brussels the 5th March 1772.

All the Fathers who are
found in Roermond, Hazeracht,
Flanders, the Land of Waas, Brabant,
and other Provinces, are applying to the
Commissioners for the Account of His
Imperial Majesty.
