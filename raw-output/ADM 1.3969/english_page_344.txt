that Mr Gallera made at St Pierre Vilenel,
by having his property Buildings purchased,
to use the Wood for the Construction
of new ones. He has actually sold
to the Americans a Frigate of Thirty
Thousand Quintals, constructed of old
Wood, and on the first Day he must
launch a similar one, for the same
Buyers.
