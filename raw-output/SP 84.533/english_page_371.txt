                                        Toulon the 5th December 1775.

                                                    The Regiments have just
left Careening, three Letres armed for War,
three frigates armed as flutes, and two avions
Those from Brest will be, considering that they were found
almost all the Planking worm-eaten considerably.

There is reason to believe that the Hull of St. Benard
will have the same Fate.

The Regiment of Auvris will furnish the
Detachment of the Sultane, and the Regiment
of la Fare that of the Mignonne.

The Dutch War [Professeur]
who came detained here, has left.
