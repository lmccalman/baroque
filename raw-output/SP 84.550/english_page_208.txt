N° 98.

                    Paris the 6th May 1776.

                    Our Letters from [deelbruck]
only speaking of the brigantine of the
named ones; one awaited the Manner
of them full Moon; and one hopes not
to be able to [paruiser] this [Bataude] with
M. de Monteil: but I judge by
the courier from [Recuvoir]
     In right of [touton] of the 28th April,
that the evacuation has been renewed voluntarily
in the [Ruëde] by contrary Winds.
The Rhine prevented being able
to provide to Madame the Duchess
of Chartres, the Simulacrum of a
Naval Combat between Two Brigantines,
and water fire boxes. The [Rheder]
that the Marine corps gave,
is neither more perfect, & had been poorly
ordered, poorly was poorly executed &
no one has been content with it.
     M. de Malesherbes has proposed to
want to leave, it is true they say,
to travel to Italy. The
                                King,
