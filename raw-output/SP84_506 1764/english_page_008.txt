The Means the most likely to unrake the Setlers
to embark in the Governments Ships would be, to
make the freight some thing under that taken
by Individuals.

Good Provisions, good Accommodation for the People
and their Baggage, which is often very bulky;
and fresh Provisions from the time of their
embarking to their going to Sea. I deem it as
often, as well as while at Sea amongst
the Passengers, to furnish equal quantity of
Liquor and of Victuals to each Cargo only at
Demand.
