M^r of July 29, Our Minister
of the Indies, goes [viter] in Spain,
he has all the Confidence of the Kings, who
has just named him Governor
of the [Ingrennes] Council and Chamber of
the Indies, with full Authority, and the
general Supervision; which
gives him many Envious ones.
This Minister of the Indies very [letandas]
and merits very great Praise,
His first Object by which he goes,
is that henceforth the Americans
Be treated like all the
other Subjects of the King, and that they
Be equally utilized employed
in all the Charges, Employments
and Dignities [qu'elanques], According
to their Merits, whether in the Civil,
Military, and Ecclesiastical,
alternating with Two.
And to encourage them, it
is affirmed, that he has proposed to the King
to create a Company of
