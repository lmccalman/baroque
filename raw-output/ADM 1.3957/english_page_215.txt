                                                the 9th June 1781

                        Toulon the 24th March 1770.

                                                We have received from the Court
the Nomination of Officers for the Armament
ordered, M. de Rives will command the total
Squadron on the Provence, the Solitaire will be
commanded by M. Cambray, the Magnifique
by M. d'Héliand, the Artésien will be by M.
de Vienne, the Duquin by M. de Montredon, the
M. Chambaurain, and another by M. des
Chatelliers, the Tempérante by M. de Selles and
who will have by default the arms in this Port.
M. les Rives will have the Honors of Chief
of Squadron.

These Armaments had for terms,
is preceded for the Artilliste, commanded
by M. d'Entraigés, the Sage for M. de
M. Lagarde, and the Redoutables for M. de la
Roze, the Delphin for M. de Guillem.
There, we have [lombarde] to the arms that one can
[des font leur effet], [rendement] is not
good enough.

We have two Vessels out of
Careening, the Destín of 74, and the Citoien of 64.
The Sailors go into [fourie], there is
much to do.

                                                Onn.
