                                                N. 10 Oct. N.23

                    Paris the 2nd October 1772.

                    [OVAL SEAL/STAMP]

                                        We have received letters
that everything is tranquil there, and that they enjoy
in the state where they found themselves the day of
departure, for the Conduct of the Combat
of Extramoz.

The [Plénipos] aspires for the Return
of the Vessels from Brest, the [ses bâts] of
Marines, and for that of a part of the
Provisions, more brilliantly than ever
which gives great Pleasure to the Inhabitants
of this City, and of the Surroundings. He has had the
Obligation to their Illustrious Mr. the Counselors
who [leur les Memoires les donnèrent pour]
to the King. For the Marines of [?]
& the Isle of the interest of the [Magaciles], he received
the Province of C. Fourrier.

It is the Comte de Mercy, Ambassador
from Vienna, who was the first to receive the
news of the proximity of the Congresses
of Varkamp for the Reunion of this
City, who has refused to these Demands
of the [RC.] of the Porte [ne arrivé]

[A tant] a false [tableau], for the
[telecommages] of in the Gazettes of the
                                        [Sienne].
