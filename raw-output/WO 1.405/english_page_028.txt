Council Off.
July 8 & 9, 1759

as to an invasion of naval armament only
truely will have heard they are waiting

number of ships in this department arrived

report upon this whole state and to put
then into a situation of being fitted out
tomorrow morning. The two ships which
arrived from Bordeaux with the transports
are kept in commission, and the others the

Brest. Orders came down after five
