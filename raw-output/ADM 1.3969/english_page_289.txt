Madrid the 8th January 1784.

The King has just
received important and pleasant
News from various Places
in America, but especially from
Havana, and from Mexico,
which promise us the arrival
in all of next Month
of 25 to 30 Million Piastres
of which Two Thirds for the King,
From the Recruitment of Africans or
young Negroes, & all at once from the War.
