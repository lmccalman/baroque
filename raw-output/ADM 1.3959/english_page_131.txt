                                                    Bj 24 bis. N°1

                    Brest the 1st of October 1772.

                                        We are awaiting here the
Duke of Brittany, who comes to see
the Port, before the Estates open
at Nantes.
            The Count d'Estaing has delivered to all
the Majors an Extract of his
Instructions. This General had read
Rome the 18th. He has given to all the
Officers printed Cards, in
which each will put the Time
of service, the Campaigns he will have had,
the wounds, the Rewards,
that he has received: but he does
not wish to hear speak of or
of Instruction, nor to the Vessels
2, 18, and 74, of the Ordinance.
            One must arm the Géné. D to go
   ✓    de Bougainville, to go into
        the North to seek a Passage. They
   ✓    will have all him 2 Off.rs of 18 Cannons,
        commanded the Norman, Lieutenant.
   ✓            And 6 Longboats for the Solas.
