Vienna, March 30, 1776.

It is claimed that Baron de Swieten reported these past days that Doctor des Rays of Prague was not, nor even his sons' new Possessions in Poland, but as the Court of Vienna made known that what It wanted to return to the Republic was only of pure Goodness, and without entering into other Considerations; It must now lend itself to the Restitution, promised despite that It is duped by the King of Prussia, and to better conceal this pretended Magnanimity, it is reported that the raising and truce of Austrian Productions, by which is included the Sale of Salt, is such an important Object, that the Revenue from the Lands to be restored, [n'y]
