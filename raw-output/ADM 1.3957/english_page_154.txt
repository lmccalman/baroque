                        Brussels the 19th April 1770.

                                General Ferrani
leaves next Week for Vienna -,
and the Queen of Poland leaves tomorrow with
the Character of First Engineer -
        The Regiment of Wolfenbuttel will arrive
here in the Morning, to hold Garrison in this City,
it will receive the Remount Horses at the end
of the current [month].
        I am told these Days, that the Company
of Ostend found itself in arrears of 600 Thousand
florins, and that it had paid no
Interest for two Years, which has disposed
some Interested parties to reclaim their
[Cepiteux]: but they were answered that
the Interest would be increased by 2 and 1/2 per
Cent, and that the Interest already
due would be paid:   With which they were satisfied -
