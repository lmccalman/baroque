                Paris the 5th November 1784.

    Finally the Courier from Brussels has
arrived, he informs us that an order from
Vienna has just been received on the part of
last [entredi] Her Imperial Majesty
orders the formation in the Low Countries of
Stores for 80 Thousand Men;
and while waiting for such an Army
to be assembled, she sends to these
Provinces, to shelter them from
all Insult, 40 Thousand Men, who
come from Bohemia in three divisions
under the orders of Generals d'Alton
Langlois, and Buren. These three Corps will
find themselves reunited on the 18th December;
the greater part of their Route
