     It is assured that the fleet that
the King of Naples has had armed,
to go join that of the King
of Spain, which must bombard
Algiers, has set sail at
the end of last month; it is composed
of several vessels of 54 cannons
two frigates, two xebecs, two
packet-boats, and six other vessels
loaded with munitions of war
and provisions; moreover a frigate
of 24 cannons which will serve as hospital
belonging to Mr Farina.
