Rotterdam March 29, 1785.

My Lord,

I have the Honor to send You
enclosed herewith the Account of the Draft of
the correspondent from Madrid for [forfour] Part
due the S. of the Current [month], & amounting to 497 £ Sterling,
of which the Half is £ 48. 13s. 6d. Sterling;
for which Sum I will take the Liberty
to draw by the next Post, On the
Treasury, to the Order of Messrs H.S. Montagne.

I have the Honor to be with profound Respect

My Lord,
