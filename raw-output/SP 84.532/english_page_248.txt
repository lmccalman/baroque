Mr. Guy and First Clerk of the
War, having the title of Intendant
of the Armies of the King, the retirement at this
Easter, the King has made him 24 Thousand
Livres of Pension, he will not be
replaced.

vacant by the Retirement of the Count
de Brouil, is given to the Marquis
d'Oysonville, son.

the Marshal de Broglier had,
is given to the Marshal de Biran,
