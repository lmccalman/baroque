The Court is informed, that a contagious
Disease is causing so much Havoc in
the Place of Gibraltar, that one had been
obliged to draw a Cordon, to prevent
all Communication by Land.
And it is added that orders have been dispatched
along the entire Coast, to admit to
Commerce there, no Vessel that
has touched at Gibraltar. However it is
claimed that one had sent Medicines
and Refreshments of all kinds
for the recovery of these brave People.
Mr. d'Almodovar has already begun
by taking Leave to proceed to
the embassy in England, and
will not delay in departing.
Our National Bank
finished here the day before yesterday, and the
Directors will assemble every
Day for the Distribution of Shares.
