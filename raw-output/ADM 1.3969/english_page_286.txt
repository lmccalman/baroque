The Count de Grasse departed
last Monday for St Domingue, where
he was preceded by Messieurs de
Vaudreuil, and de Bougainville.

The Duke of Croissy, Ambassador
of His Britannic Majesty in
France, arrived in London
last Tuesday, he is lodged at the
Baron Royal, while waiting until he
has found a Hotel.

They took advantage in Lyon of the Departure
of an extraordinary Courier who
was coming to Paris to give
Details of the Aerostatic Machine
of One Hundred Feet in Diameter, which
contains Sixteen Cubic Feet: The 13th of the
Current month, is the Day fixed for the
Departure of this Machine; it must
come to Paris, if the Wind permits it.
