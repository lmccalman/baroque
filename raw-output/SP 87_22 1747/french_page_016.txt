Whitehall 25th March
275

Sir

What I wrote you with regard to Mr. Wotherd's Allowance by the last Post was what Occurred to me upon the first face of that Affair; but by a Letter I reed from him to Day, I find he has higher Expectations for his Services, so that I beg His Royal Highness will be pleased to do therein what he may think proper.
