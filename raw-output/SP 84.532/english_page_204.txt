Here is a little Anecdote that may
give you Pleasure. The King received in
the Dispatches of the Minister of Foreign
Affairs a Letter from the King of Sweden
to Mr. de Choiseul. His Majesty read it
and said it must be sent to Choiseul
that will give him Pleasure. Here it is:

I send my Children to France
in preference to any other Country, because
you are there. I flatter myself that they will be
respectful.
