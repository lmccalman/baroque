Whitehall 18th Octr 1743

Sir
In Answer to your
Letter of the 11th Inst. I am to
inform you, that the King
would have you engage with
Your Correspondent at Madrid
for another Quarter from the
1st of Decr next, to which
time He is already paid, &
You will accordingly draw
as usual, on Mr Orme of
the Treasury for the Money
necessary for this Service,
giving me Notice of the
time of your Drawing
any Bills for this purpose.
I must observe, that the
legence of this Intelligence
seems very high, but the
