Paris the 18th October 1783.

The News from our Ports
reports, that they are beginning at Toulon the
supplies of equipment for the arming
of a Squadron; The Vessels do not
need Careening, because they are
all sheathed in Copper.

Barely have the Barges come from Riga
anchored at Brest, The
discharge their Board, that they received
orders to go to Bayonne to seek
near the Construction Woods
that they find in this Port.

Friday the Express dispatched by
Mr. de Guichen with the Judgment of
Mr. de Vigny, arrived here, its
contents are unknown. Nothing will take place
until after the Council of War
of L'Orient, which has begun
the Sessions the 10th of this Month.
