Family, by the force of Gifts and Presents, has
succeeded in establishing a Commerce between
our Republic and Morocco; and that
for the Display of their Vessels, they
will hoist the Flag of France, having
paid a certain Duty to the King of France
for this Permission.

Various [Ragotants] of Languedoc are
constructing a Brig, and a Tartan, of
2500, at St. Mille Seintheux, for their
Commerce in the Levant.

Two Neapolitan Galleys captured
on the 6th of the Current month, a large [Galcotte] from
Tunis, commanded by a Renegade
Neapolitan, who lost his Life in the
Combat, by a Cannon Shot, which
carried off his Head. The Victors
took 80 Prisoners.
