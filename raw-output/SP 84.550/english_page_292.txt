Second.

Madrid, May 25, 1776.

On the 24th of the current month,
passed in our vicinity, the Swiss Regiment
of St Gall going to Old
Castile, where that of Reding, also Swiss,
and already arrived did likewise [aurant] in
garrison at Faro. That of Foreign
Volunteers is equally on the march
to return there. As well as to
little by little make the greater part of this
Army approach the borders
of Portugal, both in Old Castile
and in Extremadura.
    The latest letters from Barcelona
report that they had parked a quantity
of tents there, a large artillery train,
with all the necessary [supplies], field
horses, etc.
