14 27 January 1773

Bouton the 2. June 1776.

We work without

Respite on the Refitting of all the Vessels
of the Port. Our Politicians make a Thousand
☞ Arguments, but there are no other
Laws in this Conduct, than
Necessity and Fear of the Plague.
The Chamber which is in quarantine,
√ would have been Entered into the Port on the 6th,
to disarm there.
We are preparing the Sentine, and the Aurore
for the Levant, the 1st commanded
√ by Mr de Corbin, and the Second by
Mr Despremeaux.
A Levy of Workers has been made
☞ for the Arsenal, but especially in
Rope-makers, and in coopers
The Bakery works Samevigo.


Paris
