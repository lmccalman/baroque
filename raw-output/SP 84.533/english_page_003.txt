Rotterdam this 2nd July 1795.

Milord

I have the Honor of sending you
herewith enclosed the Account of my Disbursements
for the Correspondences Rendered.
of the Quarter of April, May and June
amounting to Pounds 1887. 16. Stup: and Current
of October, November, and which in place of the Exchanges
of Messrs. Jacob Leers 94. Stup: 8 grs.
I would take the liberty of drawing for
the sd: Sum by the next Post,
on Mister William Davies of the
Treasury, for my draft payable 21 Days
after date, to the Order of Messieurs
William Davies on and Company;
sending at the same time to Mister
Dobbie my Agent in London the sd:
Sum.
Upon what Milord R. Hoffer did me
the Honor of telling me, I have drawn on
Sieur Le Comte, and would have much desired
that it had been remitted to order for the said
Correspondent, than his [Roturiers]
of Lafforg.

Milord Halifax
