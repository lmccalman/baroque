Paris the 10th May 1775.

A response will be rendered
a Response to the Protest of the Princes
of the Blood, it exposes the Nullities, and the
numerous souls they have abused, and
in part the response will be changed afterwards;
that which they all give, finally this
Response makes Councils.

There will be a Parliament of Letters
Patent, which will address all Princes,
Lords and to have their Titles examined
Same Rights of the Parliament, but not
to be deprived of them: This will throw
consternation among the Great.

There are approximately 1500 Edicts, which approved
400 Governors, and calls for restoring 100
Charges of Protestant Lawyers.

The Councillors and Members of
the old Parliament are convoked,
the Lord will see 6 days in their
[Maybailles] and Study. He will always
held to 6 to intervene.

The new Code was registered
last Friday, and sent to Rouen
printers, to put it in
great tour.
