It is certain that France cannot enter into the Quarrel of the Spains against the Portuguese [enfreignit] Moderator; to avenge Affairs.

The King will do the next
the Review of the 3 Greeks transferred
to Paris, in the Plain of
Sablons. He will make them come out of beautiful,
there will be a prodigious World
to see a Company of
each of these Regiments, with
the Unique Manual.

On the 17th of this Month, at 3 Hours
in the Morning, there was felt on the Isle
of Retz, a violent Shock
of Earthquake,
which however did no other
Harm, than to greatly frighten
the Inhabitants.

Gaillard
1762
