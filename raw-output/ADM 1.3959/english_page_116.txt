                                                        the 20 October 1772

                        Brest the 3 october 1772.
[STAMP/SEAL]                                    They have changed Port,
it is no longer Regiment, it is Port that you
have at present, and they have no Regard for the Ranks
of the Corps such as Battalions, Companies at
full Strength. It is necessary to make Known
the new Terms, until yesterday's Day
by Divisions, Brest is Roadstead, Divisions
of the Army, Rochefort and Bordeaux, that
of Central city, Bayonne, that of
that of the Colonies, Toulon, the Captains
of Vessels have until now in the Port with
only the Collar of the Division, which is that
of the oldest Brigade.
Construction of the Navy, the Captains
of Vessels Employed Commanding the Stores
of the Navy in port & Supposed for
Divisions, and to the Intendant missing,
those present and absent. Mr. d'Orvilliers
who was present, told these Gentlemen
to place themselves by Order of seniority,
but the [Règle] of [Regà], who had not made
his Embarkation, placed himself at the head of the
others, & those of the Navy protested
equally by Detachment of Divisions,
they were named by an Officer, in
