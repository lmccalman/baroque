The present and first of February we have deliberated
On the apportionment and account of common funds,
And on the payment of common debts of this
community. Whereupon after having heard the Sieur
the Mayor, it has been resolved to apportion and levy
upon all the inhabitants the sum of two Thousand Five
hundred ninety livres, following the roll of
apportionment made in consequence. And there shall be proceeded
to the recovery of the said sum by the Sr Syndic
by virtue of the present deliberation. Done in
assembly held at the accustomed place in presence of
Messrs the municipal officers and several inhabitants.
The said day month and year as above.

Deliberated further that all the inhabitants shall pay
their tailles and impositions before the next St Martin's,
under penalty of constraint and execution,
Whereof act shall be drawn up in good form according to
usage. And the present register shall be signed by
Monsieur the Mayor and we Clerk to serve
and avail what is reasonable. Thus done and
deliberated the said day and year.
