May it please his Majesty the late King, and
be acceptable to Majesty, his Happy Kingdom
of the present Majesty the King, that at this
same time he attends to Your Lordship,
(as they would more particularly do, with
one happy at his Majestys future) their
concern for the late his Majesty, the whole
Nation, and their Majesties testamony,
to assure You at the same time of the
respectfull wish they have of his
happiness, most gracious Majestys present
their Republic, and that they shall
study to serve them by every method
in their Power.

I likewise has the honor, to
deliver to Your Lordship the
Prince of Orange, and the Princess of
Nassau Weilburg, the Town of
Leyden, Notoriuson which they
command with all possible Respect and
Regard, and beg that by their
moving their Majesties honour for the King.

I shall not fail to inform
the other parts of Your Lordship.

I am instructed
