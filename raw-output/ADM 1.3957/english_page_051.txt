The Comptroller General who will bring
from 4 to 5 Hundred of Finances upon a good Footing,
is occupied with Reforms, one has just [devos] made
for his Million per Year in the Two
Departments of the Ministers, but more
that of War than by the others. - also
for Two Million and a half [ber] the Bridges
and Roads.

The Tax upon the Provinces will procure nearly
50. Millions less to pay, one that
will also diminish the Revenues of the Bureaus of
Municipals of the Cities. one works to
make a Reform of the Household of the
King upon the different Parts of the Household
of the King.

Besides that [l'entree] the Reforms that
will procure as one had Written before
The Diminution of more than 30. Millions
per Year upon the Expenses, the King has just established
a new Tax of 2 sols 6 deniers per livre
upon all the tickets of Entry, upon the Product
of which one will form a Loan to reimburse
in part 50. Millions which are spent, by
Anticipation upon the current Year 1770.

The King has declared moreover in Parliament
that his Intention was to recover the
the alienated Domains, to Reunite them [aux soy],
