Madrid the 20th May 1784.

The latest news from
Cadix reports that two ships of the
line and two frigates of the King were to
depart to join our expedition
to Majorca, others to depart
from Cartagena for the same destination;
all under the orders of Mr. Barcelo;
and it is believed that by St. John's Day, all
will be ready to set sail for Algiers
if the weather permits.

The News from Turkey
inform us of the arrival of the
King's frigate l'Enfant, departed from
Montori[?] on the 28th of January last and
arrived on the 30th of April, with 348,087 hard
piastres in gold and in coined silver;
197 untanned hides, 350 arrobas of
tallow, and other effects: And of the arrival
from Havana of the frigate the

Balager
