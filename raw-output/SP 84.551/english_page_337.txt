They have made in all our Ports a
launch of Vessels for [Octomme] beyond
Brest.

There is something new
at the moment which will [prevôtés] for
a man. The Number of Officers
in account more reduced frighteningly
[Judit] to 620. Approximately from England on
Board the Vessels. They are seeking
in the Merchant Marine the most
excellent Pilots, to become officers
in the Royal Navy. Which displeases
our Officers greatly, but too bad! It is necessary.

Our Minister of finances, who
has been in danger from gout, is much
better for the past two days, it is not believed
that he can be on the Journey to
Fontaine Bleau.

The Estates of Brittany have opened
their Sessions on the 30th of September, of 3 [aprit]
the 1st of the [Rebellion], to be in
1829. On October 6th the Commissioners
of the King requested 2,400,000 livres
