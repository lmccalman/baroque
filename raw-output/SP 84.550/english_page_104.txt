The famous Theron is dead,
drowned in Debts; it is said that Linguet
will take his place in the Compilation
of the sheets of the Literary Year.

The Shows, with the Exception
of the Jubilee, will only be interrupted
on Easters and Sundays:

Monsieur and Madame the
Dukes of Charles will depart,
on April 3rd, for Bordeaux,
from there to Toulouse, Montpellier,
and Toulon.
