Frumes the 18th November 1770.

They are expecting at Dunkirk
two Squadrons of the Queen to
patrol along the Coast,
to watch for suspicious Vessels,
but upon their Arrival one of the Five
Battalions of Garrison must
make Place for them.

They have also begun to extend
the Chain at the Entrance of the Port, to
prevent the Entry of Vessels
suspected of the Plague.
