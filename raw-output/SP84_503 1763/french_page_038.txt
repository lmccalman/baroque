Whitehall Dec'r 2. 1768.

Sir,
I am desired by the Earl of
Sandwich, to send you His
M[ajesty's] pleasure to both the
Houses of Parliament, upon
the deplorable murmurs of the
Manufac[ture]rs with the numbers,
which of thousands to return
with their Petitions upon
his occasion.

You will likewise receive
by next Packet of the Lords &
Commons upon the subject of
His Debt, which have, for so
many months, swarmed in this
Capital, in defiance of old Acts of
Government, & to the great
Reproach of this Country, —
especially in the Eyes of —
Foreigners

Resolutions, from the beginning
of this session, came to both
Houses by so large & respectable
a Majority, upon every point,
