194.

J'ai l'ageac is a town and its Church has a
[de Dix Meurice Cap y puelle], charged with [Parqua]
for the Government of this Colony, which
is claimed to be relative to the Arrangement
agreed upon between the Courts of Madrid and
London.

There arrived today at Rochefort
in this Bay, a French Ship with
40 Cannons of 24 pounds of Balls, for my
Assurance.

I am informed here of the unfortunate News
of the Shipwreck of the Spanish Galleon
the Assumption, departed from here in the Month of
February 1710, with a Cargo of the
Value of 32 Millions of Livres for
Peru. It perished on the 27th of July last
On the Coast of Chile.
