145.

Paris this March 1773.

There is an order from the Council
to establish two new Chambers of Inquiry of the
Court of the Commission in the Chambers. It will
take the Councilors from there.

There is talk of several Fiscal Edicts
brought to Parliament.

1st. To establish a Tax of one Hundredth, so that
the visit be undone, and that it be done on March 1, 1788.

2nd. An increase in Duty on stamped
Paper intended to compensate people
[inconnus] for the Diminution that there has been
in the tax on Rolls.

3rd. [Il me Ecole de Melle desous] on all
[demadulis] since 1783. There are many.

4th. Objects of the increase for each
council of the Studies.

5th. It will be required to make from Beaune, in the Duchy
of which they are [fermants], to permit Many
the Investigators have a [valeurant] tax on
[Royle] will not pay at all, and of others
there are 2nd It suits them [touay] that the age
of sixteen years suits them well.

6th. It is a matter of making the Bourgeois pay
Outside of Paris as much as the Bourgeois
must reserve from their lands. That none
of these Taxes fall upon the People
