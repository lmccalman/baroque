                    Paris the 9th July 1784.

                                        There is still something new
at our doors.
                    M. de Jonoffier departed on the 6th in
haste for Constantinople.
                    The departure of the Bey of Tunis is, it is said,
fixed for the 15th of this month.
                    They continue to say that
Prince Henry of Prussia is expected here. There are
people who claim that he has a
secret commission at our Court; regarding
the alliance treaty to be formed
between France, Prussia, Sweden, and
Holland.
                    The Viscount de Polignac, ambassador
of the King in Switzerland, has requested his resignation,
the King has granted it, and has already disposed of
this position in favor of the Marquis de
Vergennes, formerly his ambassador
to the Republic of Venice.
