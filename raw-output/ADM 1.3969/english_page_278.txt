14 45 Nov No 4

Madrid the 27th October 1782

News is rare
here in this Season.

The Count of Reunith Ambassador
to Vienna has just left for Vienna with
good Memoranda, on the Bavarian affair

The Prince of Nassau solicits
at Versailles and at Madrid for his [rente]
of [Polde] by the Republic of Holland
of Prussia at Antwerp, to separate it
in Spain.

A singular
Affair has just arrived here, here it is:

The Count of Gersdorff, envoy of
Saxony at this Court, having wounded
the honor of Mr Faber Secretary
of Legation of the King of Prussia, he
called him to his Service; the latter
proceeding to write him several
Notes to demand satisfaction;
but seeing himself more and more

despised
