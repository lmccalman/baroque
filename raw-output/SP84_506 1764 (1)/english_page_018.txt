The Shareholders of the Company of the Indies held on the 27th their General Assembly, and named 30 Syndics, who are the Duke des Durot, the Marquis de Castries, the Count d'Herouville, the Marquis de Saussey; Mr Boisson Counselor of the Parliament, [Régisseurs] & Comptroller de la Beaujon, Deputies of Commerce Paris, Rouen, Dupleix du Havre, Paris, Duperrey Rouen, Begouëns brothers Deputies of Bordeaux, Lieurrey de Lille, Guynet, and the Moines Shipowners of Rouen. These Two Syndics will choose Four Directors who will do all the work, and will give Account twice a Week to the Syndics.
