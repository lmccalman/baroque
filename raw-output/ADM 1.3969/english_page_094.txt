Cadiz April 29, 1782

It is reported from Lisbon on the
4th Galvens and a Brigade to go
carry to Vera Cruz the Regiment
necessary for the exploitation of the Mines:
these vessels will take in return
the Silver, and the Fruits that one says cannot
be carried away by the Two
Ships that were expected from Havana
Tomorrow Mr. de Corbova left the
Command of the Fleet.

Don Solano will raise his Flag on
the principal Vessel of the Squadron that he
must bring back to Cartagena.

Don Corno will do the same for
that which he leads to Galicia.

Mr. D'Estaing, our Governor will leave
on the 10th or 12th of next Month to
go to Versailles, it is announced for him
will be named Minister of War.

The Prince of Kaunitz, Ambassador
of His Imperial Majesty to our Court is
[en deffois son l'Envoi] He wanted to see our
Port, before the Russians had left it
