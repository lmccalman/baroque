          On the 25th of last month arrived at
Cadiz, a brigantine from Havana
loaded with sugar, leather, tobacco, campeachy wood,
&c.

          On the 27th said arrived at Corunna
the frigate the French, departed from
Monterieu on the 17th of March last,
with 369,169 strong piastres in gold
and in silver, 5464 untanned hides,
1402 arrobes of tallow and other effects.

          The King has just named
captain of the Flemish company
of the guards of the Corps, vacant by
the death of the count of Beaunonville,
the young prince of Masseran, to
reward in the son the merits
of such a worthy father.
