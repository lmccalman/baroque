La Gauvrier who had been sent to
Spain to engage this Court to make
a further Sacrifice, has been back
for three days. Nothing is known
of what he brought, but one has every
reason to believe that the Dispatches are
very favorable to an Accommodation.
    It appears that there is a great
fermentation in Minds; there
are running violent Verses against
the most respectable persons,
[qu'on n'assemble les energies].
