we lack Salted provisions, there are not
enough to equip two Vessels, be-
cause the Ships that bring them
have put in. We detested the Deaf ones
here, but the continual Rains
are contrary; it has been two Months
since we have seen the Sun.

The Bizzarre and the Dauphin Royal
are going to the Orient; there are orders
to disarm them after their Provisions, and
to keep them on their Ballast, ready to
receive 6 Months of victuals.
