Madrid, February 19, 1776.

One has the honor to write to the
to aid the Militia Regiments:
by [entren] at 9 Six Thousand Men,
to complete our Infantry,
and remove by this Reason the
quintos, which this Nation has in
horror, and which would not have
failed to cause troubles
in the different Provinces.
Despite our confidence in that
to Agriculture and to the Arts,
as I [vous] the Advantages
that one owes to those who remain
to fall for the Service, one
has found [profine], so much has and
great the Need of the Nation
for the Service.
