A Merchant Ship, departed from the Indies after the
[falle] for the King, and was charged at [Helleslan] for
our Colonies by the Messrs. the Provinces of the Country.
It arrived the 23rd of December at Lisbon, where it
discharged 148 Men whom it sent back to
Nantes in another Vessel.

The Affair of Belle Isle will not be found judged
before the Vacations, which begin
the 4th of September next.

Brussels the 1st of April 1773

The Commissioners appointed
to watch over the old Hospital. One requires
Letters from us others on the 16th of April from the Magazines
or Granaries of all the Abbeys, and all the Grains
that will be found there more than what is needed for the
subsistence of one Year, will be transported
to the Market, to be sold for the Profit of the
said Abbeys, which will lower the Price, for
the Constitution of the Peoples
