My Lord Duke

every thing is quiet here and I believe 
that the caution, and the good precautions that 
are taken will prevent any farther disturbance 
from the Mob; but the Burgers persist in 
demanding the Sale of places and the giving the 
income of the Post office to the Town to make part 
of her quota to the State; this is intended to be put 
to the Magistrates in the stronger manner by 
several of the Burger companies; but the day 
before yesterday an other Petition was handed 
about to several of the Burger guard Rooms, 
which besides the two above mentioned demands 
contains several others of so extravagant a 
nature, that it leads me to believe that it was

continued -
His grace the Duke of Newcastle                19
