Hague October 30th 1761

My Lord


The melancholy News of the
Death of Her late Gracious Majesty had been
just conveyed here on this day se'ennight
morning, by one of the whispering
Newsmongess usual about that departement
to England, and was afterwards confirmed
by the Daily Post, which brought the
News of the 25th Instant, notwithstanding
which, We had flattered ourselves a
little, till the arrival of Severals, Who
foreigner who deliver'd by an hault Worthy
Pastors of Your Lordships Letters of the
27th Instant, conforming that our Gracious
and worthy of Ever's Tuately lov'd Sov-
ereign has been Snatcht by her, Lan-
to another more Lordship where this
occasion, she had too long an Happiness
