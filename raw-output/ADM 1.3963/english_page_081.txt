18 may 1776

Second

Madrid le 2. May 1776.

I have just received
news of the arrival, Dated
the 27th of last Month, that on the 22nd of said month,
entered into this Port the Two
Frigates of the King, la Stalagérie, and les
Plages des Cabines, commanded
by Mr. de Laguere, with a
small merchant brigade, which
had been Jointly been
destined for Algiers, with a
Cargo of Contraband:
[confectionnér] Matines, Wood
for Construction, Arms, Powder,
Sails, Cordage, &c, and having
on board an Ambassador,
who is said to be the General of the
Navy of the said Kingdom,
and George Mauves, who are
                                                currently there
