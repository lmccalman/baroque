N° 78.

                                             Paris the 23rd September 1775.

                                             The King will return
Friday from Lunéville to the Hunt at
Brunelles, will dine there at the Model-farm
and will go in the Evening to the Comedy at St. Antoine-basse,
[pour jusqu'au ce Soir].

The Departure of the Court for Fontainebleau
is scheduled for the 7th October, that is to say
that it will be held for two Days Only,
instead of three Days as it is
suitable: & which will equally
[relateront] this Journey.

The Lodging of Ladies [sur un Séjour
aviso] the one next Day a Representation
[table]. There will be of it [S'il] of
19 or 20. All that will be in a state to make
[Pouvoir élever] of a Council of [Regarions]
which is established at Caen, or at Bourges.

This one will take place on the 5th October.
The First President, M. de la Cour, having
to go to the Court, he will be replaced for the
Session in this Commission by The President
[et Selectus] who will replace him for these
First Proceedings, and M. de Monthemin
Intendant and Counselor for the Intendancy,
M. Hugucet Boulier Resident at the Council
of Clermont, two [fayt] Intendancy
of the Province.
