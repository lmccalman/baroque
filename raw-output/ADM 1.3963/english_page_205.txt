K.f.O.n.C.104

Paris September 22, 1778.

The Evolution Squadron
returned on the 19th to the Port of
Brest, all the Crews in good
condition, they are disarming a Part
of the Crews, which has always
been doubled, and it will still await
in the Roadstead the Orders of the
Court. They are arming additionally in
this Port 3 frigates, 2 Corvettes
come to Le Havre, and 2 flutes
at Rochefort. That is until now
all that is new,
no other Armament,
but they are in a state to make
one of 30 Sails in very little
time. They expect to have
by this a Promotion
for the Navy.
