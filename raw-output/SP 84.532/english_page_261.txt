This for the term of return has been read In Session;
it brings 300 Attorneys to Paris;
but as they die, one
will be able to put 4 others in the places
where there will be Council, and they will be received
on the Purses.

The Lawyers to the Council have by a
Declaration the Competition with --- --
Lawyers to the Parliament, that is to say
that except for the 70 that are already there, those
in Parliament are Admissible and even
Legal Experts. And so that Crimes
do not remain unpunished, the King relieves
the High Judicial Lords of the Costs
of Criminal Procedure, [l'Isle] will place on
the Royal Judges the Offenses committed
in their Jurisdiction. There is approximately
the Content of the Edict, which should perhaps
be printed this Morning.

One appears very content with this
