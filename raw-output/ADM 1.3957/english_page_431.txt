Furnes the 25th October 1770.

                Nothing new is
happening in Dunkirk; there arrived
on the 18th a [la quiéenne] from Bristol; but
it is solely to reinforce
the Battalions along the Coasts, to
ensure that no Vessel leaves
the Port, nor Bark from [Drondre].
     As for the War, it is not wished for
in these quarters, and one would
even very much desire that it take place
only between Babylon and Spain.
     The Disease of large [Bétais] is spreading
more and more in Flanders.
