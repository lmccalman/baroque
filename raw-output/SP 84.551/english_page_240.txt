Madrid the 22nd August 1776.

The Papers of
the Russian Embassy, [mort voici près]
in my previous ones, have not
appeared until now; the Secretary of
Legation, who has been extraordinarily
afflicted to the Point that he said to his
friends that he was a lost Man,
affects today much
tranquility, by spreading the
rumor that [ce n'a fort que] Papers
of Mr. Pâque.
