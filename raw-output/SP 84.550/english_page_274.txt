The King has just named the
Knight of Croy, Field Marshal,
and the old Marquis of
Crus, formerly Viceroy of
Mexico, as General Commander
of all the newly
discovered Lands, on the Northern Coast
of Mexico, and to be discovered, Namely,
California, Sonora, New
Galicia, and New Biscay; so
they say, with 20 Thousand [Gros d'une]
of Salary.

At St. Laurent died yesterday
the Count of Fuentes, formerly
Ambassador of Spain in
France.
