N°. 70.

                                    Brest the 20th April 1773.

                                    They are naming the Chevalier de
Villeblanche, commander for the King in St. Domingue
the Vessels for Martinique which must pass there
and Guadeloupe for the Service that

The [Père] by M. de Bevers, [admis petit pris]
[théâtre] by M. de la Martelliere, for
M. Domingues.

The [Caphin] by M. Dardeur, to go to
make Observations at the Azores, with
the Cape Verde Islands, subsequently to S. Domingue.
If time permits him several Members
of the Academy of Sciences will accompany him.

There are moreover two large fleets destined
for the Greater Islands, the Captains are not
named, those who must command them
have preferred the two Vessels that are being prepared
at Lorient.

The Frigate which conveys M. de Nort,
arrived the day before yesterday from S. Domingue.

The one [avec donne] of M. Doneau, destined for
Quebec, and which will return here.

We learned yesterday of the Promotion
of 9 Officers in our [corps].

                                                        Paris
