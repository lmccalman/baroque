                    Paris the 9th July 1776.

                    Our Letters from Toulon
of the 30th of June, and those from Brest of the 3rd of July,
speak only of the preparations being
made in these Ports, and say nothing
extraordinary.

This Week the Order is awaited to
begin new Lottery. No more
talk of borrowing money
and [a les questions].

The Controller General still
makes nothing appear, [afin] the
finances.
