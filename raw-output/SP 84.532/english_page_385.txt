I departed from this Bay on the 25th of the day, &
a [Rayueblas] Swedish, of which it is said that the
Destination is for [Basse nos Mycos].
There is which is embarked an Officer
of the Spanish Navy, charged with
Packets for the Governor of this
Colony, which are claimed to be relative
to the Arrangement settled between the Courts
of Madrid and of London.

There arrived today from Boston
in this Bay, a [Jamois] Ship with
40. Cannons of 24 Pounds of Ball, for
our Arsenals.

We learned here the disastrous News
of the Shipwreck of the Spanish [Salaire]
the Anglumma, departed from here in the Month
of February 1770. with a Cargo
of the Value of 30 Millions of Livres
for the River, it was lost on the 27th July
last on the Coast of Chile.
