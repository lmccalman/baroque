Mr Duclos, Perpetual Secretary
of the French Academy, died
of a chest affliction.

There are Letters from Constantinople, and
of the 18th of January, they do not announce
the Peace concluded, but one must finish
for the Preliminaries.

These Letters add, that it is
certain, that the three Allied Powers
are provided, and absolutely in accord
to remove definitively from the Throne.

There is from Vienna, that I learn:
that they chase from the Convents of
Constantinople, and from Petersburg,
which Muslims powerfully have
Amnesty, and the immediate Reparation
of [Appanages] for their Regions of the Turks,
of the Powers garrison everywhere your
chains to [Religieblande] Russians.

the Vistula, and makes pass there
