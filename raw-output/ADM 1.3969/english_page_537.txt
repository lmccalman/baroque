17. 20 Janry


Paris the 21st January 1785.

     There is no more talk of
war here. And word comes from Versailles
that there is no more question of
war, than if it had never
been about to take place. Thus it is hoped
that everything will be arranged soon.
We shall see.

     The departure of
the armament for India has been delayed, which
was being prepared at l'Orient. It is believed that
they want to add other vessels to
those already ordered for this purpose.
The war has so greatly devastated the
Province of India, that our troops
lack many things
