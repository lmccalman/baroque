Rotterdam the 5th November 1781.

My Lord

The Correspondence from Madrid announces
drawn His name for the Treaty dated the 5th
of last September. I Suppose by
the Company of the Company of the
Indies. The amount to Lgs. S. Sterling,
of which Half is Lgs. S. Sterling, for
which Sum I would take the Liberty
to draw on Mister William Davids
of the treasury, by the next Posts
at 21 Days from Date, to the orders of
Messieurs William Davidson & Company

I have the honor to be with a profound
Respect
                                My Lord


[Veües tout Soin blas elle]
[Jos thlenfauts Son entrée]
                                M. Wolters.


Lord Garlies.
