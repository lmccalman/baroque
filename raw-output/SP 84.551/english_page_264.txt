Paris the 18th September 1776.

There is nothing interesting
from Brest this Week.

The Regiment of Touraine has been
to the Country, against the Conduct of the Regiment
of Lyonnois which did not Report the Cellars
of the English of Montlieu, in the time
of the Disorders in the same affair
but that it had been offered by the Council.

Nothing is actually happening in
our Finances; Credit and public
Confidence are being restored every Day in
turn. The Shares of the Company
