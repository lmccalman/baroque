I have had an interview with Mr. Le
Jeune Receiver of the Post Office of the Province

Your Grace a report he has he has been

upon the change of the course of the letters

Mr Le Jeune assures me, that they have
no copy of the Contract with the Post Office
of England and seems to think, that it proba-
bly is now, that the Post Offices of this
Country are managed for the Sovereign, it might
be thought the more necessary to consult it,
as the former was only transacted with
