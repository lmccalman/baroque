Toulon, June 2, 1776.

Work is being done to
finish the Refitting of all the Vessels
of the Port. Our Political matters are null,
uniformly, but there are no other
objects in this Capital, which occupy
perhaps, that it has all divinely.

The Chimere which was in Quarantine,
will have its entry into the Port on the 6th.
to disarm there.

They will repair the Jardin and St Honore,
for the Levant, they are commanded
by M. de Bordes, and the Semole for
M. Degoinousse.

A Review of Workers was held
for the Arsenal, which comprised three
Orders and its coopers.

The Bakery is working a great deal.

Paris
