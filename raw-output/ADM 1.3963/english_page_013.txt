Premier

Madrid the 21 December 1778.

You will have seen by
my last, that if it is for lack of
Matters that I did not write to
you, I do so today, to
respond to your Letter, and to
assure you, that it will not depend
on me to give you
News, in order to maintain
our Correspondence and avoid
by this Means the Complaints that
you make to me on the Occasion
of my Silence.

The Fleet must depart in the
Month of April next, one has
already begun to load
the Vessels which are destined for it.
