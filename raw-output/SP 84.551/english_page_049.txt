                    Toulon the 4th of July 1778.

                              The Courier from Lier
brought the order from the Court
to fully arm a Squadron to
augment the Vessels of the Navy.
        The [ampli] called the order of Armament,
and the Command to the Chevalier
d'Espinouze, of the St Sevore Brigade;
this Squadron must conduct
all the Vessels to put out; And if it is
in this State, it will be armed,
and would increase the best to the
Navy, and of Caronnes, for the
[caronnes] later to arm and
for you.
        The Instructions of the [Borgne Vilain],
have arrived here, and this Morning it has
been conducted to the great Roads, to
set sail most likely Tomorrow,
if the weather permits.
        The [Chofats] of the other [Ouisfeaux]
are exact.
        One always speaks of an Ordinance,
and one does not see it arrive.
                                        Paris
