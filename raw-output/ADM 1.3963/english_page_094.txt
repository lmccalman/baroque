Havre de Grace the 21st May, 1778.

They confirmed here, a
Fleet of 22 Cannons, named
the Africaine, which armed itself until now
between Two [Berquettes]
by the Chamber of 4 Cannons,
each one. As soon as these three
Vessels are armed at Sea,
they will be replaced on the Shipyards
by two Vessels, the Barbary, and the
Corsica.

They constructed Two Fleets, at
Rochefort.

They must construct Two Frigates
at Nantes, if the Project is taken, to
rebuild the Navy in a Grand
Manner, given that the New
Comptroller General, will [prochera]
willingly, to increase the
necessary funds.
