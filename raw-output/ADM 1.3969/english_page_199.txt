                    Genoa the 29 July 1783.

    The Two Vessels that Mr Castellain
of St Pierre d'Arena had built, were
launched at Sea the 7th of the Current month; they carry
34 Pieces of Cannon each: this Individual
proposes to have them loaded for
America, and to go there Himself;
to sell, and load his Merchandise
in exchange for Productions of that Country.
    For Account of the King of Spain,
three Frigates of 36 Pieces of
Cannon each are being constructed.
    The Cambiaso Family, very wealthy, is having
4 Turbines constructed, of the Capacity of 600
Quintals, but without Cannons. It is having
work done still on others, at Sesti,
