Letters from the Empire continue
to represent the Museum of the Elector
of Bavaria as very dilapidated;
which will bring about, as I
previously announced to you, a Scene
that will develop the character of
the Emperor, and the Knot of His Connection
with the King of Prussia; but it is
only after the Death of His Mother that the
former will show himself entirely
in the open.
