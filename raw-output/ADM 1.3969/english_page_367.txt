14 26 June


                    Paris, June 1st, 1784,

              One is currently occupied
here only with the King of Sweden; this Prince
employs his mornings visiting the
establishments and public monuments,
in the evening he goes to spectacles, or into
society.
              Count Pignatelli, ambassador
of Naples, is going to leave to go take
the native air, the Secretary of embassy
remains charged with the Affairs of the court
during his absence. The Queen has
charged Mr. Pignatelli to present
to her Sister the Queen of Naples all
that there is most new,
most elegant, and most sought-after
in fashions and in jewels.
              The position of [l'aumoniere]
of the City has just been erected into
