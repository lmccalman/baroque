you have heard what I have received from
Bergenopzoom this morning; all officers letters from that
place and from the lines give us but very little hopes
of the town's holding out much longer; yet I am told
their a general who knows the town very well is of
opinion their is may hold out for six weeks longer.

The french had erected a Battery at Busquier
which raked the Bankes of the River on that Side;
but a Battery raised by the Beseiged has quite Silenced
the formers.
