Whitehall 2d December 1745

My Lord

Having received a Letter from Mr Walters
at Rotterdam, Dated the 10th Instant N.S., - - - -
giving an account that four large Transports
laden with arms ammunition and some Men - -
were said to be ready to sail from St Malo the
14th of last Month, and only waited for a
man of war to Convoy them, and that they
were intended for Great Britain. We send
your Grace an Extract of the said Letter, for
His Majestys Information. We are.

My Lord

Your Graces

most humble Servants
