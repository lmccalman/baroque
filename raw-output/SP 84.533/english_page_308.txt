Madrid the 05. October 1775.

                    I am informed from
Cadix, that the King's Frigate the Junon,
on which will embark Mr.
Buccarelli, New Viceroy of
Mexico, had departed from Havana
for Vera-Cruz, the 10. August p.
     All the Troops of the Battalion of Ultonie
departed with the Convoy from Havana,
has arrived at Cadix, with the exception
of the Company of Grenadiers.
