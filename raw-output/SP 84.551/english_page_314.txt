Paris the 27th September 1776.

The Evolution Squadron
arrived on the 19th in the Brest Roadstead,
all the Crews in good Condition;
they are disembarking the Masters of the
Crews, who were aboard the Iris,
& this Squadron will await in
the Roadstead the Orders of the Court.
More are requested in this Port
3 Frigates; 2 new Corvettes
at Havre, & 3 Flutes at Rochefort.
That is until now all that
is New, no other
Movement; but they are in a State
to make one of 30 Sails in
very little time. They expect
to see appear a Promotion
for the Navy.
