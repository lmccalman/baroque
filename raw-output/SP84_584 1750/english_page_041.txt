Rotterdam Feby: 23d 1813

My Lord Duke,

I have had an information that Hastie
has obtained the King of Prussia's assent for
the establishment of a Company in London to
trade to the Coast of Bengal; and I have
collected the following particulars about the
affair.—

1st Hastie is to go with the first ships and to
form a Prussian Factory and settlement upon
that coast where he is well known.

Clifton, brother to Sir Robert Clifton is
appointed Commander of the said force, which
action we to consist of one Company only; he
goes out with the first ships; and he is much
esteemed here, and has orders to raise men, &
put the fort in this country. — There has one
