                                                    18 10bre 1721

    By the New Correspondent.

                    [STAMP/SEAL]

                    Rochefort, September 20, 1721.

                            The orders have arrived
here, and delivered with haste

                    For Martinique
            The Bordelois of 56 Cannons
            The Legueris . frigate
            L'Ambitieux }
            La Renomée } Corvettes
            The Perle . flute.

                    For St Domingue:
            The Clement of 56 Cannons
            The Aurore . frigate
            The l'Hippolite Corvette
            La Claire . Barque.

        The Object of this Armament is [des
jours] to transport the troops which are
in Depot at the Isle of Rhé, destined to
go the independent Companies in
the Colonies and restore order there.
        The same Vessels will bring back
the land troops to France.

                                                    [signature mark]
