Toulon the 28th June 1778.

They feared

[L'Observale] for this Port, but they will read of
receiving orders to arm the Frigates,
which will be commanded by Mr de Martonne
Captain of Vessel, and the Blanche, by
Mr de la Grandville, Captain of Frigate;
these two Frigates are destined to go
into the Levant, to relieve two Frigates
departed last last.

There is likewise an order to arm four Xebecs
& three of the Cameleon, for the bay of the
Brabancons, the first commanded by
Mr de Boudes, and the second by, Mr des
Pautes, both Lieutenants of
Vessels.

There is likewise an order to arm
as chaser four Vessels of the King, which
will be commanded by Pilots,
to go fetch Wheat at Civita-
Vecchia, and transport it to Havre-
