Section 5.

Madrid the 30th July 1776.

He has been Squadron Commander,
Don Michel Buston, has been elevated to the rank,
that he was received there, King of Spain,
with all the Distinctions and Honors
possible, and even extraordinary,
as much at the Court, as from the Ministers
and from the Nobility. One would have, without
doubt, been surprised by the unexpected
Approbation of this Squadron on
[le hoc] and even of 4 large Vessels
of 70 to 80 Cannons, and of some
Frigates, before the New Castle of [la Cor],
that which one orders, the Packet of
the Squadron continuing July order
at the Mouth of the Tagus.
One assures, that the Portuguese
Minister has avowed, and affects
to ignore the Hostilities, of which there is
