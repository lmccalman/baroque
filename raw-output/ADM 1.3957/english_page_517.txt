Brest, November 22, 1770.

He Orders and authorizes the Sr
Lieutenant Vessel of About [d'Environ de Cr], who would not have
a lower Battery, and the Pleubet the Seine.
Messrs the Corps d'Innemond to the Vessel;
and Messrs the Vaucauleurs Captain de Boulais,
le Forche. They are destined for the Coasts
of Brittany, with an Escort of About
500 men of Troops.

The Degleris commands for Messrs de
Cucy, returns to the Port, or will occupy
strongly the Crew with the Armament of the [Enterior],
all our Vessels and Brigades, all
the Crews, which are not Employed
on the Scepter have been Reviewed successively,
and returned to the Battalions.

The Chevaliers de Roux has just Obtained
1200 livres of Retirement Pension, Messrs
Fontenay Ensign of Vessel, has had
only a Letter from the Minister, which promises
the Satisfaction that one has with his Services.

The [Envoy] for the purchase [s'affecteusement]
in this Port to what it can Serve
for Education, the first [Intendance]
promised for a long time.

Rennes.
