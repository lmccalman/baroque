546.

The payment from Normandy provided
to the Detachment by the two Frigates
being put into Armament. Mr. le Durfost
having obligated the Clerk of Commerce at
Cordon, has remitted them to Mr. de Courcy.
These two frigates will go to Risle on the 6th November.

Several [Brégalles] have been purchased from
their Major Port. No vessel arrives to us
from outside for
the supplies.

There has been Wood in Cubic Feet for
Twelve large [Baffreurs], and Eight Pieces
of Construction for Remainder sold.

35. Uniforms, good or mediocre,
and 5. Elegant [davour], the Bien-aimé,
the Victory of 74, The Mars, O Indian,
and The [Astizonnaire] of 64.
