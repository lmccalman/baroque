While awaiting the arrival of M. des
Augny, M. Bertin signs all urgent
Dispatches.

The same day last Sunday,
M. de Serres, Intendant of Commerce,
was named Intendant of War,
to have the Inspection, the Intendancy of
Valenciennes.

The same day again, M. de St.
Germain, who was only Secretary
of State, entered the Council as
Minister of State. It is affirmed today,
that he will have under him a Council of War,
composed of 4 Marshals of the Camp, of an
Intendant, and of 4. Commissioners.

It is likewise affirmed, that there will be a
Council, for the Controller General, and that
M. de Maurepas will not be the Head of it.
