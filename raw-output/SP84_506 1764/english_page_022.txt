Paris the 5th October 1764.

Word came from Rochefort
that several [Rediateurs], Doctrinaires, and [in
Manclair], naval officers, who had
been placed in the Royal Prison & [l'eau forte]
of Cayenne, left the Prison on the 18th
of last month.

The Chevalier Turgot named Governor
of Cayenne, has arrived at Rochefort where he
will embark on a frigate which will be armed
expressly to convey him to America.

It would have been decided definitively
for the Fate of the East India Company
that is to say, for the Measures to be taken
to continue its Commerce. it is
however very [de telles], that It will form
new Establishments in
Bengal and the Coromandel Coast, while
preserving the Commerce of China
[Moyent] to the Isles of France and Bourbon
It encounters many Difficulties

[en foire] to the King, and which His Majesty had
at first accepted, but the Thing having been placed under
greater Examination, does not appear to suit
