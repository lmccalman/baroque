476.

They hoped for the King the faithful and
good affection while waiting to show it
in the Deferred office.

They await the appeasement of the Discord
between the King of England and his Parliament,
so as not to take place when it should.
The Duke of Choiseul arrived last year
to the King for the Council which would try to
Choose.

The King went yesterday from Choisy to Versailles
where he spent the night & all day yesterday. The
[bordannes], all the Court found themselves recently
returned to Fontainebleau.

Mr. le Nourier du Paty, advocate general
to the Parliament of Bordeaux, there holds much
Hope & he has destroyed enough of it.
The eldest son of Mr. le Nourier is garrisoned at
the Castle of Pierre Encize in Lyon, they have
removed the Religious, of whom they could have assured
the Safety, in the rest to [Maderans] of women
[vivés] against the Duke d'Aiguillon, who
is found to be making [Moëliés].

The Chevalier Forestuex at Chatelet,
having the [Demy gareauces], [pouvés]
On the Mount of his Province, is accused of no
longer returning there. The Court [assuy] Reproaches him for
this thing, while awaiting his true [gistés].

The Chevalier de Raysseuilte has authorized
the Commands in Marseilles & in the Ports
for the Court, without saying anything about the [Lizes] of his
Voyage.
