finally one was advised to Request
the Augmentation of 12. Hundred Thousand Livres;
and that the Hundred Thousand [repoitants] should
be turned into less surprising, and one made
a Deputation on this Subject to the
Commissioners of the King.

   Few Days after were shown not
authorized to grant the Diminution that
one requested, but that one had to make
a Memorial to this effect, to speak of it.
This Memorial is made, and left the [Coutre]
for the Court.

   One has granted this Matter, the Twentieths
as ordinarily.
