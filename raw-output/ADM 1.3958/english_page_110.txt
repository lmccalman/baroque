141.                                                          R 25 track N°1.


Toulon, the 22nd February 1775.

                                    Monsieur de Boynes,
     In accordance with the order that I received on the 17th
of this month announced in the first place by
Letters given for the Ports of Civrac;
the Destination of the D is to be transported
on board [envoyé] of the Countries, which are awaited
from Paris in 15 hours, and to return it
then into the Kingdom, to go there
as far as Spain and the. Country the. It has been
established here that the two vessels which
have the [redevenir], are those of the R
on board which depart 4 Cabin boys.
     There are besides the fr., [d'armier] and frigates,
[Soutien], [d'Envoyeante], which carries 12s.
and the Magicienne which carries 8s. in
Batteries. The Destination is not known.
These Armaments are not
yet named.
     Vessels are being prepared to
go to Corsica to take the Regiment
of the Count of Provence. The Count of
[Sonnaces] to the army, when one knows
the Count of Provence will pass there.

                                                                        [signature/flourish]
