The Governor of Marriage of Our
Infant Don Louis, arrived the 27th
of the Current (month), at a Village, named
Casarubios del Monte; on which
Day is fixed the Departure of the Court
of Aranjuez, to return here.

The Duke of Char has, after two
Days of Stay at Gibraltar,
returned from it the 12th of the Current (month) to
Cadiz. The Rural Festival
that the French Nation gave him
at Islana, was of the
most brilliant.
