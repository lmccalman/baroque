206.

Pen in hand from within the Castle
of Cracow, he came to my aid
promptly.

According to our Letters from Barbier
of March 22, they were not yet
making the Recall of the Counts of
Marbeufs. The Bandits always steal
the march; to this effect they are making
great roads everywhere from one
town to another.

The King has made a considerable
Grant of a Part of the Noblois to the
Count of la Marche, and will be able
one day to draw from it immense
Revenues.
