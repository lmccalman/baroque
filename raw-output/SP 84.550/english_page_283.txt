It appears already confirmed, that
the Infant Don Louis, brother of the King,
is going to marry on the ... of the 25th of next
Month, with the Niece of the Countess
of Mrs Stuart; but she will be
regarded only as a Private person,
more Children who will be born from this
Marriage, will never be recognized
as Princes of the Blood, or it must
be certified. The spouses are going
to live in Talavera, a Town 20 Leagues
from Madrid.
You are right, to complain
of my sluggishness; I have been occupied mes
by a defense; that I have been
obliged to make, but I will
have the honor my Correspondence
the most [juivie], whenever
there will be Matters.
