Madrid the 24th of February 1783.

He has just had published
the Ratification of the Preliminaries of
our Peace with England: And at the
same time has dispatched Couriers to
all our Ports, and Departments of Marine,
both of the Ocean, and of the Mediterranean
with Order to disarm and to make cease
all Hostilities, and to dispatch at the same
Time, with the same Orders, for
all the States of the King in America.
