Pondicherry the 25th September 1770.

     The Triton brought us
the Decline of the Company, and following
the Consternation in the Colonies, they are making
a considerable Reform in the
Employees of the Company, which affects
no one's rights, but unfortunately
for Mather, and integrity is lacking.
We only needed War
to finish us off.

this Town, according to the Plan that was formed
