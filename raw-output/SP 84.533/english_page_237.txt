    To judge by outward appearances; it
still appears that our Court is
in agreement with that of St Petersburg,
they give each other Marks
of Friendship; recently the Russians
have sent back a Number of Deserters
from the Imperial and Royal Troops,
Under the Pretext that they will not
be punished.
    According to the News that arrives
daily, Archduke
Ferdinand continues His Journey
in perfect Health.
