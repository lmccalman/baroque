The King has just made a
small Promotion of Six Brigadiers
and a quantity of Ranks in the Army.

The Count of Aranda has
frequent Meetings with the King,
it is said that he will return to serve
as Ambassador in Paris, in a few
Months.

All the News that we
receive from Andalusia, Valencia,
Aragon, and Catalonia speak
only of Misfortunes and
Disasters without Remedy, caused
by the heavy Rains, almost
continuous for Four Months,
which have caused terrible
Floods; one has never seen
in this Country Rains of such
long Duration, with no appearance
of stopping yet.
