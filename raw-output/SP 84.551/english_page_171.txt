Paris the 18 August 1776

They report from Brest
that there are at this Moment 18 large
Vessels, and 6 frigates masted,
and ready to be armed, they continue
to work on what remains.

A Vessel the Victoires has departed
from L'Orient for Toulon, loaded
with Construction Wood. [On
Bien armé et hors de même, do
que fa Caronne pas achevée.]

They say that the Duke of Chartres
is ill at his [Mez], they said [fe
débarquer] at [Le. Ferran], and [Par
équipages] will bring him back to Paris.

Eight [Bâtimens] Clerks of
War left the Offices
last Sunday, with
Pensions stronger than They were
[L'aprevoar].

The Sieur de le Chappes is charged by
the Government of the [feüille], with a
journal
