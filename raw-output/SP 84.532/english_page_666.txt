Private Letter

                                    Paris, June 30, 1775.

                                                        On the new
Subject of the Creations of Controllers of Domains, the
Secretaries of the King have their objections,

that were demanded of them last Year,
and that they had Difficulty finding the
6 Money at [Epoux] Hundred, and that their
finance by this Means did not profit them
at percent, they will do no less this
time.
