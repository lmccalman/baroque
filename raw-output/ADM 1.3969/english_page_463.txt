The Court of Spain is in the
Deputation, & [Mêlé] of the Twin Infants
has not recovered forming to his
Brother, he estimates as well as The
two [M'en conforme], caused by
Teething.

An extraordinary Courier from
Madrid, gives us News
of the Vessels from Lima. One [faulo] has
put in at Rio Janeiro, it was in
a pitiful State, having been beaten
by the tempest & by Cape Horn.

It needs long & considerable
Repairs. A Convoy Vessel
was Signaled to it in Distress, during
the Hurricane, & that it [elevé] find
way back, not being able to risk
continuing its Route in the State
where it was. One hopes that it will have
put in at Chile.

arrested by the Comptroller General,
