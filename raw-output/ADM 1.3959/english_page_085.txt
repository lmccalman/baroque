                                                          B 3014. N° 1

By the New Correspondent.

                    Brest the 7th September 1778.

                                        Louis d'Haudemer
All in letters is already noted, except
the battalion of Agenois planned, and the
brigades of St Phar and de Roche Velle
which can only be here upon the arrival of the
boats. Already disembarked here ___
The officers of Brest [mainanés]
for this Destination are left, Masters to
exchange with those of Rochefort,
to whom this might suit, Subject
to Regard for the Regiments to which they belong.
Which already announces a very great
Relaxation of the order announced
last year.

Count d'Estaing Commanding the army,
as has been said, That it not carry out the
operations of Guinea and of England
of the King that is claimed from [Cordeliée].
His officers and the Marines of six not
all assembled yesterday at three [blancs]
after Midday; where unanimously
                                                          approved
