The Duke of Aiguillon profited
marvelously to Support Argenson,
that he wants the Promotion of
relatives at Court, or that one
finds the Graces that the Ministry
has conceived for him, already, that he can
justify it.

The Abbé Baudeaux, Author
of the Loan & periodical,
entitled, The Ephemerides of the
Citizen, having advanced in
his Work, that the Farmers
of the Poissy Treasury were
brigands, & having demonstrated it,
These [averoient] him, attacked him
in regulated Justice. [Battus raplà]
for the Farmers, and the Abbé
Baudeaux pleads himself
his Cause, with the greatest
Applause at the Châtelet,
[la partie l'avenir], and Saturday,
the Cause is postponed [d'bordy portaine].
