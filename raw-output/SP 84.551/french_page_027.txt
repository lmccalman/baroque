Will you please Sir, to favour me
with an Answer, to let me know if
I am inform'd the 13 th of London is
confirm'd to the Concern of one of the
Navy, or not; and if yes, if he charg'd
upon the Bill of Loading of it, when
ship'd, by others with the Reseipt
of Charges on the 1st from Germany
till on Myboard; if not, if I be
make an offer of it to the Dutch
& African Company, and the
Admiralty. The respectfull
of your Answer as soon as
possible, I have the honour to be
with due Respect

Sir

yours most humble
most Obedient Servant

L. D. Hake
