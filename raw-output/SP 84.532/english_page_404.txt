11°. We do not believe that the
Parliament of Paris wanted to enslave the
Throne, by putting a price of fifty
thousand écus on the head of a Cardinal, Prime
Minister, & by stealing two
hundred thousand écus worth of effects from the houses
of the King's Servants, to raise I know not
what army of lackeys against his legitimate Sovereign. We do not believe that
Louis XIV had many obligations
for his Crown to the Coadjutors, Quatre-
sous, Quatre à l'homme, Bussi, Elbeuf, Flan-
quetaut, Martineau, Crépin, Perrot &
Cadet, who signed these banditries.

12°. Let us add to all these truths
known in Europe, that apart from the La-
moignons, the Maupeoux, the Mole, &
about twenty noble houses, which
have served in the Sword & in the Robe,
all the rest is composed of people whose
grandfathers were our Farmers,
or Commissioners at the gates. This is what
we can furnish as proofs to HIS MAJ-
ESTY.

13°. We know how to obey the King, this is
another difference which exists between the
former Parliament of Paris & us.
