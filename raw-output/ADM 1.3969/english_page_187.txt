The Sea rose on the coasts of
China, in the Month of October
last, to an excessive Height,
and it covered entirely during
several Days, the Isle of Formosa,
in such manner that the unfortunate
Inhabitants all perished, and the
Sea in withdrawing, made of
all the Habitations, nothing but a
Heap of Ruins.
