Fine amicable Regency, does not prevent
the Ministers from being summoned
by order to Toulon, and Mr. Talbot,
[Mandant] of this Port, has just departed
to go there.

The Letters from Vienna and Holland,
and even from Berlin, claim that the
King of Prussia has been won over, and that he
will not meddle in this Quarrel;
this Neutrality would be monstrous or
politic, as would that of the King of Sweden.
