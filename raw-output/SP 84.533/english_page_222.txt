They have likewise made Mr d'Amiens understand,
speaks through honor to the Intendancy of Roanne;
Mr. De Dugay, Intendant of Brittany, speaks
in [Piemble], the latter is a man of the Constitution,
and by the State he was in the service of Brittany;
the other one will not have the same similarity there.

Twenty-eight Members of the former Parliament
of Paris, have been asked to liquidate their
goods within three Days; named ones have already
been reimbursed, the same will be done for
those who present themselves.

The King has given learned Tomorrow at Cherisy, and
they claim to Crown him for Versailles Saturday;
they speak of the King's Ceremony of the
Consecration, would be held on Sunday, past
giving the Veil to Madame Loyau;
the [Perse] performed the Ceremony in the Name of the Pope,
and Mr. the Bishop of Senlis, pronounced the Discourse
of Profession. The King returned Today
this Tuesday.

They await the Chancellor of Rouvre, who
has just been [sury] the Chapter of Combourbe
to S. Archbishop of [Ispirois], the Pope having
to make a Promotion on the 27th.
