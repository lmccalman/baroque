Rottd. Decemr 26th 1783.

My Lord Duke

I am honored with your Grace's
letter of the 16th with the enclosed speeches and
addresses, and I have the honour by this to
congratulate your Grace upon this happy
opening of the Session, which carries along
with it so great a prospect of being attended
with good effects.
