15 to Nov. 1782

Paris the 20th October 1782

One will continue to observe,
that it is more than probable that
the next winter without hostilities
on the side of Turkey, the Divan by
its Negotiations, and some measures
to which It submits, has succeeded
in averting the storm which threatened it.
However one would not guarantee
that in the next Spring, It would not be
the first to confront it. One will be
[honoreur] of this new Arrangement
to some powerful Mediations, they
will not have served little, without doubt,
but one claims that the Truth is,
that if Russia had had the funds
for two Campaigns only, if
the Emperor had not feared to irritate
