After the Basques done, the King will go
to St Denis, to see Madame Louise
Sister Thérèse of St Augustine, the Magdalene
will not enter into the Houses. She who
will penetrate and wait to see him at Madame Adélaide's
She will see there only a lady of honor and her
doctor at Versailles, it is to have an extraordinary
and unworthy Council! L. S. Renard.

Affairs in Rome are going very swiftly,
everything departs from the Pope at present; the Bucer
et rara domini, against which all the
Jansenists have concerned themselves, has not been
promulgated this Year on Holy Thursday; and
Following the Custom, the Romans very pleased
with this, regard this Affair as
a relaxation to their Debts, pretensions
and to their Pretensions which are.

The Parliament of Bordeaux is the Only one
which has not accepted until now the [Contexte] and
the Second Vingtieme; but also all that
which it imposes on the Province, they want
it to obey.

P.S. The Assembly of Peers and [Jeux]
the Commissioners does not hold only to that.
