At Calais there are among ships, of
Militia and French units at home, namely
the Walloon and Spanish Guards, who
remain quartered at Port St. Marie
at Brest, until further notice.

of 86 Cannons, and of the frigate St. Theresa

they arrived at Cadiz, and remain confined there
as I marked to you in my
previous [letter]. The 2 Battalions of Marck
will be commanded by their Lieutenant
voyages to America, in a light
✓ a Ship at [la Barrouette] for

There was also still no order

About 5 thousand Navarrese have
withdrawn into the Mountains, to
escape from the orders of the Junta;
which one does not fail to [enquieter lans].
