Rotterdam this 2nd April 1776.

Mylord,

     I have the Honor to
send you enclosed the Account of
Merchandise, for the
quarter of Correspondences
made here the 25th March last,
the amount to £2375. Current
Money of Holland, which
at the Exchange Rate of this time,
makes £321.19. Sterling, for
which Sum I will take
the Liberty to draw, for the
next Post, on Mister
William Davis of the Jefferies.

I have the Honor to be with
a profound Respect

                    My Lord

                         Your very humble &
                         very obedient Servant

Earl of Suffolk.                    M. Wolters.
