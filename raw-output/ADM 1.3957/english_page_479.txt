The Count of St. Germain, who has been in
Denmark, is recalled to Turin; the
sentiment which prevailed in this trial
that employed him, is that he is an excellent
officer.

They name the Young Officer of
the Household of the Count of Provence, of whom
we have spoken in the gazettes,
Monsieur Léman, a young man filled
with merit whom one calls Launage.

At Rennes the regiment of Lion is organizing,
the guard ceaselessly of the Queen [fuhues].
Sieur Faveau [en souche] that Madame
the Dauphine and Madame Adélaïde, but
this is what one cannot have positively.
Madame the Dauphine, and Madame
Adélaïde [de bois Cires à Baillés], is
all alone and the Son of Monsieur.

They send word to me from Versailles, that a crowd
of people at the cause of the Castle at Brie, at the
Carriages whose Horses had taken
the Bit in their Teeth, put [naye] to avoid
this Magistrate, but she got off
with only the Fright, the P. Carmelites had
made them descend from their carriage, it did not
come, also I do not promise it to them
as one says. Pure trifle.
