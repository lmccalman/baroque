Sir                                Rotterdam de ouberiles, 1788.

        I take the liberty to send you
enclosed the account of my Disbursements
for hired Carriage for four Quarter due
the 5th of last Month together with a
quarter of my Allowance to the said Date
amounting to £149.4.., which at the Currency
of Exchange of this Day, makes £ 137. 15. 8ffs.
        According to what was settled
I have in Consequence of the Permission
which you have been pleased to give me
by your Command contained in Mr
Howard's letter, minus for the next Post
for the addition upon William Davis Esqr
of the Treasury, by my Bills payable
28 Days after Date to the order of Mr
James Crawford, adding at the same
time my separate Receipt to Mr Davis.
        Mr Jaminik Correspondent's 3d son
will come in about the Middle of this
Month.
