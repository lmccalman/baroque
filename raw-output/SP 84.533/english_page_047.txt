One has nothing delayed on the Ministry of the King,
the Reform there will be considerable, the Military
Employees and the Officers will not suffer from
this Reform, but one will count some Soldiers
to send them back to Agriculture in the
Provinces; but moreover to send them back to the Corps
when one wishes.

The King organizes by a new Ordinance
a House of men for the old Military men
who will have served without Reproach. He will place
this House at Versailles. The ordinance on Regiment
