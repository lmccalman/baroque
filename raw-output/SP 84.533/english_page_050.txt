From Port au Prince the 20th May 1775.

The Corvettes departed from Brest,
commanded by Mr. de Chanlate, Naval
Lieutenant, arrived here a few
days ago, with the agreeable News
of the concluded Peace. It brought
the Ordinance for the Reform of a third
of the Legion, which has not yet
[suppléé] the Fears that the Colonies
have of War. Commerce has greatly
contributed to maintaining it.
