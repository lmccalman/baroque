Paris, October 10, 1770.

                                                 The Monuments
increasing in our Part, all the
[forgera] of a great Armament. The old
is simple of Quarry of all Cities;
at Rosay on the new hill toward the hotel
at the side of Segron, with Bourgerons, in
place of the last ones that late M... They still await
Carpenters from St Malo and from
Nantes, and Borers from St Omer.
      The Workshops of all kinds that
are working with us, number 2800. we have
enough men, we cannot
have more than 34. They are having
Stone started by the convicts, to compose the [Dîr
Sey] and more than 200 Laborers. They are making
Provisions of all [kinds].
      They have armed 4 Corvettes for our Colonies
the first has departed, the second command
Commanded by M. de traitement, has
just arrived and departs for Le Havre commanded
by M. la thannoir has departed, to pass
to [l'Orie]. all that which is even for the port
the 2 vessels that are there in Careening.
      We lack many things for
the armament, and especially arms, and
[forçons], we would need 40 of them, we only have
3, and we would need to count 7 Supports
for [chaines Manches].
