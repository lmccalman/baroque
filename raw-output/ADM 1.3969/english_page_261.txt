Madrid the 24th September 1782.

Don Bernard de Galvez
Lieutenant-General of the King's
Armies, and [d'arrière] at Court.
M.Bernard [en vivant] here the day before yesterday
his Departure for Cartagena being
fixed for next Monday from where he will
embark for [s'Alicoque], to
prepare there all the necessary for
another Expedition against Algiers,
for next Summer.

The Ambassador of France has just
left for Paris. Mr. Bourgogne
remains here as Chargé d'Affaires
during his absence. The Two
Ministers of Russia, and of Sweden,
are equally about to depart.
One expects at any moment here,
the Count of Rechteren, as
Ambassador of Holland.
