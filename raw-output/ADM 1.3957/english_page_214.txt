Brussels the 23rd March 1740.

Last Tuesday two
an Assembly of the States, Duke Charles there
announced His upcoming Departure for Vienna
and that He approached to Be back at the end
of next September, in taking Leave
of the said States He recommended to them to
maintain the Rights of the Court and the
Privileges of the People with the same Zeal and
and to govern with Gentleness.

His Royal Majesty being awakened, the
States sent him two Deputies, of
whom each one carried a Purse with 4000. Ducats
Ducats to defray the Journey, but
His Royal Highness sent them back, saying
that he had so many honest People in the
City of Brussels, who would need it
to Subsist.
