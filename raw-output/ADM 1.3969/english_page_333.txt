Paris the 5th March 1781,

It is reported from Toulon, that
on the 18th of this Month there arrived in that
Roadstead, Two Dutch Warships,
forming Part of the Squadron
coming from Texel, one of these Vessels
was partially dismasted; they declared
that off the Coast of Majorca, a
Third Vessel, which was sailing
in Convoy with them, experienced a
Terrible Gale, in which
it perished with all Hands.

It is learned that Prince Henry
of Prussia is to depart for Petersburg.

It is expected here for the Middle of
next May, the Count of Aranda
returning from Spain.
