Rotterdam this 2nd April 1778.

My Lord,

                I have the Honor to send you
enclosed the Account of the expenses of
the Correspondents of Mulvill, for
the Quarter due the 5th June last
amounting to £146. 13. Sterling;
of which the Amount is £172. 7. 6. Sterling,
for which Sum I shall take
the Liberty to draw by the next
Post, Upon Mister William Davis
on behalf, to the order of
Messieurs William Danielson
& Company.
                I have the Honor to be with
a profound Respect,
                                My Lord

                                Your very humble and
                                very Obedient Servant
Earl of Suffolk.                M. Watters.
