from Kellevoe I have received the
good news, that Her Majesty,
passed there this day at Noon
in the King's Yacht is, direct for
Rotterdam, & that Her Majesty,
is expected here to Morrow.

The Circular from General
Conway to Mr Wolters, of the 8th
about the happy Event of this
Marriage, I shall leave to Mr
Wolters to answer him self,
He being at Kellevoe & in waiting
the Arrival of Her Majesty there

I have the Honour to be
most Respectfully
Sir
                    your most humble and
                    most Obedient Servant
                    L.C. Hake
