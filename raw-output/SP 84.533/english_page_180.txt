Brest the 25th September 1725.

Overseas Armaments

The Port de Long which is done. [Mille Chelices]
councilor The Regulations of [Mutres],
goes Commissary Commissioner to
Cayenne, Maillard de Mes le [Connigh] [tire]
The Navy, goes with the same. [Beaulhis]
to the Isle de France to relieve Mr. Desvaux.
he will leave with the New Commander
Mr. de [Fervaig].

Mr. de [Besvard] [il esparte Domestres];
he will decide an Increase in
Fortifications, [La feu] come four
Battalions to work on it.

Mr. de Beyne promises to come here
in the Spring, it will result in a Good
for the Navy.

It is from the 15th March to the Officers,
that ruins them, they [trafx ineut Conn]
Lieutenants and for [lent] of Small.

They have made of L'Orient a Department
of Commissioner, that relieves our
Intendants.
