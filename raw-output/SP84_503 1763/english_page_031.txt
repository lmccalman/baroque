Being truly, that Your
Correspondent will with
good the Opinion you have
conceived of His Inclination
& Ability to your exact
Information of the frame
Army Navy & Finances,
which has induced His
Majesty to continue this
Allowance. The Administe
being as you rightly suppose
to furnish their Share of the
Expence for this Intelligence
you will settle with that
Office the Method of
receiving the Money that
shall be due from them.
     I am &c
          Sandwich
