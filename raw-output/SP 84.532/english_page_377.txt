Rotterdam the 6th April 1793.

Milord

I have the honor to send you
enclosed the Account of my Disbursements
for the Ransom of the Crews of the Swedish
vessels, Seized at Brest in the year 1792
Amounting to 14500, which at an Exchange rate
of this Day, makes Â£.638. 13. Sterling.
According to the ordinary Custom, if the
rule, I shall take the liberty to draw
for the said Sum by the posterior Posts
Upon Mr. William Davies of the
treasury, by my draft, payable at
25 Days from Date, to the order of Messrs.
William Davidson & Company,
while notifying you at the same time & Mr.
Othisman Rysot [Ecoqueur] for the said
Sum.

I have the honor to be with a profound
Respect

Milord

Your very humble, and
very Obedient Servant
M. Wolters

Milord Halifax.
