     Ziffer Bey has been deposed, and Haglan Bey
whom the Sultan has named Capitan Pacha
in His Place, does not wish to accept this Charge
he continues to remain at Lemnos at the Head
of the troops who have driven the Russians
from this Island, and who have ravaged it at Candia
     The Ottoman fleet is still here,
there is not a Single Vessel in a State to
Sustain a Combat, and If the turks do not
take Other Measures, the Russians
will do all that they wish.
