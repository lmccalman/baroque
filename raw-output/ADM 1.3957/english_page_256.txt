9. July.

Paris the 2nd July 1770.

The Letters from Toulon
report that from the Detachment of 2 Frigates and
2 Chebecs, the Commanders have been named,
they will depart on the 18th or 20th July.

The Insurance Company is doing well
with confidence in Paris & will employ
much provisionally, which it will [Petter], which
is a precaution.

The Courier from Luxembourg to Brest has been [Raffolé].
It had been established, by Spain named the tax
the Line Uniform, but it does not appear
that this is confirmed.

The Insurance Company, it has Raised
the Funds, which will cause a Decline, prevents
sending the funds, and does great Harm to the Reign,
it is necessary that all this [decoede], and Russia
profits from it.

It is not yet said where the Squadron will go
from America which was here, because there was
a Request at Clermont.

There is no Movement in the Courts,
in relation to the North, That of France says Only,
it has had published all if the Affair with Russia
On the Dardanelles and the Ottomans, but funds
they must occupy themselves with it, the Legations of Russia
& London will depart approximately On the 4th Service
both often, it needs some time yet
