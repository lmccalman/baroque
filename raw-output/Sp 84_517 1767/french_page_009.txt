Rotterdam 9bre 10. 1768.

Sir


I have the acknowledgement of the honour of
your circular with the most perfect sense
of her Majesty's happy delivery of a Prince
you wished, and want I take the liberty to
present to you my warmen congratulations.
I have the honour to be with great respect

Sir
                Your most humble
                and most obedient servant

                        R. Wolters.
