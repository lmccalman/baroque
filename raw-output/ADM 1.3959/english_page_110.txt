                    Daily report the 3rd October 1772.

[SEAL/STAMP IMAGE]
                                        It is the Marine Regiment,
of Four Battalions,
which came to relieve the Garrison of
Dunkirk.
        As a precaution, that the [Colenie] with
the Buildings, would not be finished. since.
one is occupied with placing Pilings
furnished with Planks, on the Side of the Basin,
which leads to the Citadel.
        One is still working in the
[Mocre], and at the new Lock of
Nieuwport.
