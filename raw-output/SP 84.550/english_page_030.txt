First

Madrid the 25th December 1773.

You will have seen by
my last, that if there were made some
withdrawals that I would not write
to you, I do it today, to
respond to your Letter, and to
add to you that it does not [deproche] 
of me to give you
News, in order to maintain
our Correspondence and to have
by this Means the Plants that
you pass me. I Occupy you
with my Selenea.

There must arrive a
Month of April next, they have already
begun to load the
Vessels which are destined there. One
