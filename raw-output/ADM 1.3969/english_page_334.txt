18. 03 March 1781

Paris the 18th March 1781.

There is no interesting
news from our Ports.

There were quite lively Debates
in the Council of State last
Sunday, principally between the
Count de Vergennes, and M. de Calonne.
A Company of French Merchants
had made an agreement with the English
East India Company, to have at
Chandernagor, all the Goods
of Bengal, by paying it ten per
Cent profit; this arrangement
had had the approval of the Committee of
Versailles, and some other Ministers
and it was only a matter of having it
passed by the Council of State, when
