A 15 Rincoli

Paris the 1st March 1781.

They write from La Rochelle the 21st
from Rochefort, that the hurricanes cer-
tainly that were experienced, caused much
Misfortune. More than 40 have been buried.
Corpses cast up on the Coast at La Rochelle,
there were also many Debris from
Vessels, of Planks, of Masts.
The Patache which goes from La Rochelle to
The Isle of Réh, has sunk with 25.
Passengers, only one person saved himself.

They write from Potage, that during
the last Hurricane, there perished in
the Garonne, a prodigious number
of Persons, more than One Hundred Corpses
have been found in this River.
