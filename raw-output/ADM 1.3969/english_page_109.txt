Madrid the 26. May 1782.

It is not doubtful that
the Preparations which were continuing to
be made at Cartagena announced an
imminent Expedition against Gibral-
tar. The Orders of M. Barcelo which could
have no other Object, than that
of a Bombardment, to destroy
their Shipyards, and their Sailors; un-
less the King and the Regency
admit the Conditions of
Peace, which have been offered to them.

I am informed from Malaga
of the 26th of the Current month, that all the
Gunboats, and Bomb vessels
from Gibraltar and a quantity of Vessels
loaded with Munitions, had departed
for Cartagena, with the Brevet Chief
M. Barcelo.
